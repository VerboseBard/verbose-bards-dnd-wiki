# Big Tusk Arena

The [Big Tusk](../../places/big-tusk.md) Arena is the combat venue associated with [Big Tusk](../../places/big-tusk.md).

## Description

The notes frame the arena as a way to survive a bounty problem by fighting as a sanctioned combatant. It is one of the clearest examples of the campaign turning violence into institutional opportunity.
