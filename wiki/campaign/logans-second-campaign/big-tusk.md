# Big Tusk

[Big Tusk](../../places/big-tusk.md) is a major settlement in the Second Campaign notes and one of the recurring future names in the imported material.

## Description

The notes describe a bounty being placed on the party and note that if they make it to [Big Tusk](../../places/big-tusk.md), they can become fighters in the [Big Tusk](../../places/big-tusk.md) Arena. If they become champion, the bounty is dropped.
