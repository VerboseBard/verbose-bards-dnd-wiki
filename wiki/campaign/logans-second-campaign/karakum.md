# Karakum

[Karakum](../../places/karakum.md) is the north city that anchors this Second Campaign's trade and guild structure.

## Description

It is a satyr city and the center of multiple institutions. In the notes, [Karakum](../../places/karakum.md) is described as the place where trade must pass through the merchant and shipping guilds.
