# Merchant Gild

The [Merchant Gild](../../places/merchant-gild.md) is the trade monopoly that controls goods moving through [Karakum](../../places/karakum.md).

## Description

All trade is routed through the [Merchant Gild](../../places/merchant-gild.md). The notes describe it as a monopoly with strict fees, outside-trade penalties, and heavy consequences for bypassing their system.
