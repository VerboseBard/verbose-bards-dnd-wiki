# Shipping Gild

The [Shipping Gild](../../places/shipping-gild.md) controls wagons, ships, river transport, and inland porting in the Second Campaign notes.

## Description

All shipping or porting of goods inland or on the rivers and inland sea must be run by a member of the [Shipping Gild](../../places/shipping-gild.md). They work in tandem with the [Merchant Gild](../../places/merchant-gild.md) and can blacklist people who ignore them.
