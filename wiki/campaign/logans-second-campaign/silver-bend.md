# Silverbend

[Silverbend](../../places/silver-bend.md) is the northern city of [Karakum](../../places/karakum.md) in the Second Campaign notes.

## Description

It is a satyr city. The notes describe women wearing saree and men wearing kurta pajama and sherwani. The city has two temples, a large library, a bazaar, and flowers everywhere.
