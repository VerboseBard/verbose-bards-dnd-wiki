# Logan's Third Campaign

Logan's Third Campaign is a later [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) campaign arc that begins roughly twenty-four weeks before [The Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md) and, by user chronology, ends with it.

## Description

This campaign belongs to the final pre-[Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md) stretch of the [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md), much later than [Logan's Second Campaign](logans-second-campaign.md) and much closer to the world's collapse.

The surviving material currently comes from [Vail](../people/vail.md)'s notes. Those notes show a campaign built around ranger investigations, magical drug trafficking, ancient observatories, pre-elvish inscriptions, weather collapse, undead battlefields, and a gathering magical crisis that points directly toward [The Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md).

Because the surviving notes are character-centered and not yet a complete campaign archive, this page should be treated as a structured placeholder rather than a finished full-campaign summary.

## Overview

The early arc follows ranger operations against the Blackthorn bandits and the alchemist Vernon Anatydes. What starts as a drug-running investigation expands into a much larger conspiracy involving shipping houses, teleportation circles, blood magic, hidden bosses, and figures embedded in city institutions.

The middle of the campaign deepens that structure through:

- Valarondir ranger politics
- Wroat and Riverbreak smuggling leads
- the butcher and Dorothy thread
- [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s hidden involvement
- ancient telescopes and inscriptions
- observatories, keys, beacons, and artificial-sun style machinery

By the later notes, the campaign has become openly apocalyptic. Entire regions are at war, undead armies are fighting across ruined land, magic and weather are behaving unnaturally, and multiple factions are trying to seize or redirect a reservoir of world-changing power.

## Major Threads

- Solovian ranger operations and internal politics
- the Vernon Anatydes drug network
- McGinty shipping infiltration
- butcher / Dorothy / [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) conspiracy
- ancient observatories and pre-elvish runes
- beacon activation and weather collapse
- undead war corridors and cult activity
- the fairground or sanctuary-city magic problem near the [Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md)
