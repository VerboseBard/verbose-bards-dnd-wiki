# Aether Sea

The Aether Sea is the vast magical expanse that surrounds the Core of [Unity](../places/unity.md).

## Description

It is neither air nor water, yet behaves as both. Flying ships sail its currents, navigating stabilized ley flows much like oceans. Where the magic settles, trade routes form. Where it surges, storms tear vessels apart.

The Aether Sea enables transportation deeper into the [Upside-Down](../places/underdark-upside-down.md), movement of goods and people without teleportation, and access to regions unreachable from Topside.

It also enables piracy, smuggling, sabotage, and war beyond surface law.
