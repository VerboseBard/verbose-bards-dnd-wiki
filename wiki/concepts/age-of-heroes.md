# Age of Heroes

The Age of Heroes is the popular name for the early [Seventh Age](seventh-age.md) after [the Concurrence](the-concurrence.md).

## Description

Scholars may call it the Age of [Concurrence](the-concurrence.md), but ordinary people call it the Age of Heroes because it is the era when the world was stitched together again and adventurers became the people who made that stitched world function.

The name reflects the rise of the [Adventurer's Guild](../factions/adventurers-guild.md), the exploration of dungeons, and the growing belief that renown, not bloodline, could build a future.

The close of the [Moon Stone Collectors](../campaign/moonstone-collectors.md) arc gives the phrase a more mythic reading. After [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) claims the master goo and [Sovereignty](../people/sovereignty.md) becomes whole in the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md), the age is framed as one shaped by individuals becoming more than they were expected to be, breaking assigned paths, or taking back limited control from fate, duty, and outside manipulation.
