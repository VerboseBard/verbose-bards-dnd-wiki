# Ancient Ice Hydra

The ancient ice hydra is a newly awakened northern threat near the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Nature

Ice hydras are rare hydras associated with cold, storms, and breath weapons from multiple heads. Older ice hydras can also gain spell-like control over winter weather.

Like other hydras, severed heads can regrow unless the neck is sealed with fire or acid. Fire is especially important because the party expects cold damage to be ineffective.

## Awakening

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), the grove's hunters explain that an ancient ice hydra has awakened from long hibernation as the thaw changes the north.

It kills an infected hyo-jak, blocks the eastern pass, and collapses the northeastern pass. The grove's animal-handlers judge it too hostile for ordinary negotiation.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 14](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-14.md), the party intends to leave for the hydra in the morning, but a [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) child-rescue detour costs them sixty-five hours. They return to the route afterward, stopping at a hunting cabin still one to two days from the hydra's territory.

## Defeat

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 15](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-15.md), the party tracks the ancient ice hydra to the pass. It ambushes them from beneath the snow and uses cold breath, ice armor, petrifying frost screams, and grappling heads.

When badly hurt, it calls its mate: an ember hydra with fire breath, lava armor, and fiery meteor-like attacks.

The party kills both. [Paul](../people/paul.md) sears the ice hydra's heads and throws its body onto the ember hydra. The ember hydra's heads are frozen, blasted, and destroyed with holy water. A luck check confirms there is no nest yet.
