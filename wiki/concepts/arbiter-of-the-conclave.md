# Arbiter of the Conclave

The Arbiter of the Conclave is a proposed secular oversight role offered to [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) by a private faction within the [Conclave of Churches](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md).

This office is distinct from the ordinary ecclesiastical role of Arbiter in the [Ecclesiastical Order](../factions/ecclesiastical-order.md), where Arbiters are paladin-like enforcers of divine judgment.

## Purpose

The role would give a non-divine voice formal authority to hold gods, churches, and divine servants accountable from inside the Conclave's political structure.

## Junvara's Terms

[Junvara](../people/junvara.md) refuses divine empowerment or divine items tied to the office. He is willing to consider a voice at the table, but only if the role can become a broader adjudicator structure rather than a single individual standing alone against the churches.

He suggests that other non-divine voices, such as [Jarth](../people/jarth.md), may be needed to avoid making the office another single point of failure.
