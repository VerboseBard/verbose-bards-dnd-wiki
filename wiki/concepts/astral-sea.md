# Astral Sea

The Astral Sea, also called the land or sea of dreams in older notes, is a grey void realm where ordinary land is rare, unstable, and often made of dangerous living flesh.

## Description

Unlike the [Aether Sea](aether-sea.md) around [Unity](../places/unity.md), the Astral Sea is its own vast realm with open three-dimensional movement in every direction. Navigation is therefore brutally difficult. Up, down, overlap, and depth matter as much as cardinal direction, and every serious ship or city needs a master navigator.

Stable places to stand do exist, but many natural masses are too dangerous to live on long term. They are instead used as hunting grounds or harvesting sites for rare plants and creatures. Everyday life depends on ships, floating strongholds, teleporting cities, and whatever infrastructure a faction can build in open void.

## The Six Sectors

Older notes divide the Astral Sea into six major sectors or power blocs:

- [Gith'yanki Empire](../factions/githyanki-empire.md)
- [Giff Compendency](../factions/giff-compendency.md)
- [Hells Corner](../factions/hells-corner.md)
- [Karrains Corporation](../factions/karrains-corporation.md)
- [Thri'Kreen Sentorium](../factions/thri-kreen-sentorium.md)
- [Dargains Daggers](../factions/dargains-daggers.md)

These borders are not perfectly flat or clean. One sector can overlap another vertically because control in the Astral Sea is volumetric rather than planar.

## Native Dangers

The Astral Sea is home to dangerous creatures and biologically repurposed war-beasts, including:

- Theks
- Somalygots
- Krakars
- beholders and spectators
- phase spiders
- occasional behirs and manticores

Many factions survive by harvesting or weaponizing these creatures rather than merely fighting them.
