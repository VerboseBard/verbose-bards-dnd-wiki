# Death's Kiss

Death's Kiss is a blessing given by [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) to [Junara](../people/junara.md).

## Effect

The blessing can supersede a death event near [Junara](../people/junara.md). When [Vis / Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) is swallowed and killed by the serpent avatar in the [Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout](../places/silver-bend-coastal-cave-hideout.md), Death's Kiss activates.

[Vis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) hears or feels [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s childlike presence, returns to life, and the death is redirected into the serpent instead.

## Significance

The blessing proves that [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s affection is not symbolic. Even diminished and far from the battlefield, her divine nature can interrupt death itself.
