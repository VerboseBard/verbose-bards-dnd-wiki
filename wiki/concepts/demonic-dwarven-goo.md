# Demonic Dwarven Goo

Demonic Dwarven Goo is the necrotic demonic infection tied to the corrupted dwarven artifacts, the ancient undead outbreak on [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md), and the newly thawing northern dangers.

## Origin

The substance began as part of dwarven undead-control experiments. According to records interpreted by [Julara](../people/julara.md), a lesser demon using the false name Elman helped the dwarves bind demonic energy into a substance that could animate and control living or dead bodies.

That demon later became [Zarvok the Torturer](../people/zarvok-the-torturer.md).

The dwarves used the goo to create controllable shock troops through control amulets. A variant escaped control before [the Breaking](the-breaking.md), spreading through bodies and helping cause a massive undead outbreak.

## Properties

The goo appears as black or brown viscous sludge. It can infect animals quickly, driving them mad. Humanoids and other people may resist for a while, but repeated exposure eventually allows the infection to take over.

The substance is resistant or immune to many damage types. Fire destroys it most reliably, while lightning can harm it less effectively. When burned or expelled, it can release a psychic scream.

## Modern Return

The ice of [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md) preserved the infection for centuries. As the world thaws after [the Concurrence](the-concurrence.md), old battlefields, frozen bodies, and buried creatures begin releasing it again.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), the party sees the goo dripping from an infected hyo-jak, crawling through snow toward their camp, infecting goats, and nearly taking root inside [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md).

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 14](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-14.md), [Paul](../people/paul.md) studies [Dwarven Rune-Light Crystals](../items/dwarven-rune-light-crystals.md) and considers whether their ancient light runes could be altered into radiant anti-goo devices.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), [Wisdom](../people/wisdom.md) reveals that [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) began as a unique offshoot of the goo altered by [Fey](fey.md) magic. The party also learns that a master ooze remains in the old dwarven control room, bound by cables and magical chains.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), the party carries a living [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) cutting into the old [Gateway to the Empire](../places/gateway-to-the-empire.md) facility and inserts it into the control apparatus. [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) converts the master ooze and takes command of the wider network.

The result prevents the goo from remaining a free-spreading apocalyptic infection. It also places the old undead armies, infected bodies, and goo nodes under [Viscus](../people/viscus.md)'s control, including roughly 500,000 undead soldiers preserved in frozen lakes and old dwarven systems. This makes [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) an unexpectedly major power.
