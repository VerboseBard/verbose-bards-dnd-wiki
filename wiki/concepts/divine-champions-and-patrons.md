# Divine Champions and Patrons

This page collects the patron-and-champion pairings that show up in the [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) / Conclave material.

Some of these figures are confirmed in session transcripts, while others are note-derived references that have not yet been expanded in a session summary. When those pages exist, this page points to them for easy browsing.

## Champions

- [Seraphiel](../people/seraphiel.md) - champion of [Solarius](../people/solarius.md), an aasimar holy warrior themed around dawn and radiance.
- [Terrence of Tyr](../people/terrence-of-tyr.md) - champion of [Tyr](../people/tyr.md), a justicar devoted to law, order, and retribution.
- [Archivist-General Callis Verdan](../people/callis-verdan.md) - champion of [Oghma](../people/oghma.md), a tactical lorekeeper and planner.
- [Braelen of the Verdant Hand](../people/braelen-of-the-verdant-hand.md) - champion of [Chauntea](../people/chauntea.md), a nature guardian and healer.
- [Arcturus Vaelen](../people/arcturus-vaelen.md) - champion of [Velmora](../people/velmora.md), a solemn deathbound elf tied to the Grave.

## Patrons

- [Solarius](../people/solarius.md) - the Dawn Father.
- [Tyr](../people/tyr.md) - god of justice.
- [Oghma](../people/oghma.md) - god of knowledge.
- [Chauntea](../people/chauntea.md) - goddess of life.
- [Velmora](../people/velmora.md) - goddess of death.

## Notes

The [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) material and the Conclave champion material both use the same broad pattern: divine patrons empower mortal champions, and those champions act as practical extensions of the patron's domain.

This is not the same thing as ordinary clerical worship. In these notes, a champion is often a direct political and military instrument as much as a believer.
