# Dragonborn of Saharun

The Dragonborn of Saharun are tied to ancient experimentation involving dragons and yuan-ti.

## Description

Their origin marks [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) as a world shaped by old magical intervention. Dragonborn identity there carries both cultural and biological history.

## Campaign Role

The Dragonborn of Saharun are part of the wider world briefing and connect [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) to divine politics, sealed gates, and [Alen](../people/alen.md) in her role as the [Raven Queen of Saharun](../people/raven-queen-of-saharun.md).
