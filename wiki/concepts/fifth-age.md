# Fifth Age

The Fifth Age is the era before the [Sixth Age](sixth-age.md) and the [Concurrence](the-concurrence.md), ending with [The Breaking](the-breaking.md).

## Description

The Fifth Age is the last full age before the world is shattered into the more familiar post-[Breaking](the-breaking.md) history. It should not be confused with the later [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md), which begins at the end of the [Sixth Age](sixth-age.md).

As more source material is imported, this page will hold the summary of the era itself and the campaign threads that occur during its closing years.

The imported archives currently include [Logan's First Campaign](../campaign/logans-first-campaign.md), represented by [Quill](../people/quill.md)'s notes and beginning about three weeks before [Logan's Second Campaign](../campaign/logans-second-campaign.md); [Logan's Second Campaign](../campaign/logans-second-campaign.md), which current notes place roughly three years before [The Breaking](the-breaking.md); and [Logan's Third Campaign](../campaign/logans-third-campaign.md), which begins about twenty-four weeks before [The Breaking](the-breaking.md) and runs into it. Older divine-political references from the era also still matter to later ages.

Partial archival notes also survive for earlier divine politics, including a named [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md) and regional worldbuilding for places such as [Opaal](../places/opaal.md).
