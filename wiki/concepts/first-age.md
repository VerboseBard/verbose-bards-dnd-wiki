# First Age

The First Age is the Age of Creation.

## Description

In the oldest surviving truth, the world was built as a game by supreme creator deities. These powers were the original players of creation rather than the lesser gods later worshiped by mortals. They shaped the world, set its rules in motion, and created the lesser gods who would become known inside the world itself.

Most of these supreme creators are no longer known by name. The names remembered by mortals and ancient spirits, including [Big Shad](big-shad.md), belong to the lesser divine order created within the game-world.

By the end of the [Second Age](second-age.md), most of the supreme creators had lost interest and moved on, leaving only one caretaker to watch the world.
