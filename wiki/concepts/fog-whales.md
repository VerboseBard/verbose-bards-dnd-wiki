# Fog Whales

Fog Whales are enormous, powerful creatures that swim through the fog of [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md).

## Description

They are majestic and overwhelming, carrying an aura of scale and presence rather than simple hostility. Seeing one up close makes the party feel the magnitude of the fog's living mysteries.

## Campaign Role

The first clear Fog Whale omen appears above [Sin](../places/sin.md) in [Session 3](../sessions/session-3.md), when lightning in the fog reveals a massive whale-like shape swimming through the cloud layer. The Driftvale-native reading treats it as a favorable omen.

A Fog Whale later appears near the party as they debate investigating [the crashed ship](../places/crashed-ship.md). Its presence grants inspiration and reinforces the idea that the fog has its own strange ecology and omens.

The omen repeats later in the After the War material. In [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), a whale appears overhead as the party nears the final approach to [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md). The party gains inspiration again, and [Alistair Hooley](../people/alistair-hooley.md) soon recognizes the landmark from his family's stories.
