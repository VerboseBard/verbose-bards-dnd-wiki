# Fort Victory Corruption Ring

The Fort Victory Corruption Ring is the network of bribes, artifact skimming, illegal fog expeditions, and officer-level abuse surrounding [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md) and [Sin](../places/sin.md).

## Structure

The ring involves corrupt officers under [Colonel Marrow Vance](../people/colonel-marrow-vance.md), scavengers operating in the fog, artifact buyers, and town power brokers such as [Mistress Selyra Vex'ryn](../people/mistress-sila-vexry.md) and [Rurik Valdren](../people/rurik-valdren.md).

## Exposure

The party helps expose part of the ring by bringing back murdered scavengers and identifying [Lieutenant Garrick Hume](../people/lieutenant-garrick-hume.md)'s distinctive tracks.

## Aftermath

The [Inquisition](../factions/inquisition.md) executes the old command staff, freezes accounts, discharges corrupt soldiers, and attempts to reimpose military order.
