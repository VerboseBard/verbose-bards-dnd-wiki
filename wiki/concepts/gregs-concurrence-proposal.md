# Greg's Concurrence Proposal

Greg's Concurrence Proposal is an in-world technical report prepared by [Greg](../people/greg.md) on the integration of magical resonance across multiversal realms.

## Purpose

The proposal describes a theoretical framework for achieving [the Concurrence](the-concurrence.md): the unification of disparate worlds without causing planetary destruction or uncontrolled dimensional collapse.

[Greg](../people/greg.md) frames the problem as both scientific and metaphysical. The report combines wormhole theory, quantum entanglement, electrical frequency synchronization, magical resonance, ley-line mapping, and ritual calibration.

## Abstract

The report argues that dimensional convergence requires more than brute-force planar alignment. Each participating world must be prepared so that its metaphysical frequency can harmonize with the others.

The central idea is to integrate magical resonance with advanced wormhole theory, using the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) as the central nexus of convergence.

## Operational Methodology

[Greg](../people/greg.md) proposes specialized teams of mages, sorcerers, and other arcane practitioners crossing dimensional boundaries to prepare each world.

Their work has three major stages:

- Dimensional survey: Map each world's ley lines, ley nexuses, and major magical energy sources.
- Conduit establishment: Build magical conduits at optimal sites, allowing energy to flow back toward the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).
- Resonance calibration: Fine-tune the frequency and amplitude of each world's magical resonance until all participating worlds hold stable coherence.

## Theoretical Framework

The proposal suggests that electrical frequencies can be synchronized with wormhole constants and metaphysical coefficients to induce harmonic resonance in spacetime.

When paired with ritual invocation and arcane incantation, that resonance could allow separate dimensional planes to align gradually rather than collide catastrophically.

[Greg](../people/greg.md) also references [Professor Hazard](../people/professor-hazard.md) sequences as a method for fine-tuning dimensional convergence. These sequences would allow iterative adjustment of conduit phase coherence, reducing the risk of planetary ecosystem disruption or collapse.

## Importance

The proposal gives a non-cataclysmic model for the [Concurrence](the-concurrence.md). Rather than smashing worlds together, [Greg](../people/greg.md)'s method seeks to tune them into shared stability.

It also helps explain why ley lines, the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), and post-[Concurrence](the-concurrence.md) transport infrastructure are so deeply connected. The [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) is not merely a travel hub; in this model, it is the central anchor that allowed the worlds to converge without tearing themselves apart.
