# Guild Rank System

The [Adventurer's Guild](../factions/adventurers-guild.md) uses a star-and-medallion ranking system to measure experience, access, and trust.

## Description

Bronze is the entry tier. Silver represents established field competence. Gold is for highly experienced adventurers.

Each color has four stars. The party begins this arc as Bronze 3-Stars.

## Bronze Tier

- 1 Star: Levels 1-2
- 2 Stars: Levels 3-4
- 3 Stars: Level 5
- 4 Stars: Level 6

## Silver Tier

- 1 Star: Level 7
- 2 Stars: Level 8
- 3 Stars: Level 9
- 4 Stars: Level 10

## Gold Tier

- 1 Star: Levels 11-12
- 2 Stars: Level 13
- 3 Stars: Levels 14-15
- 4 Stars: Levels 16-17
