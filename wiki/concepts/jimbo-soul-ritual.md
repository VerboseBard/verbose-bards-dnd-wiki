# Jimbo's Soul Ritual

Jimbo's Soul Ritual is the linked resurrection lattice using [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul as an ideal component in [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return.

## Structure

[Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s attack on the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md) used the crystal's position as a [Ley Lines](ley-lines.md) nexus to activate multiple rituals at once. [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul was divided into four fragments and embedded into the working.

The party receives three divine artifacts from [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md) through [Vis / Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md):

- A guide artifact that pulls the group toward the next ritual site.
- A containment artifact that receives recovered [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragments.
- A transit artifact that carries the group down the ley-line path between sites.

## Ritual Phases

The ritual works in three linked phases:

1. Soul Resonance
   - Known fragments create the tracking link.
   - The ritual can locate other fragments across space, planes, and magical anchors.
   - Without at least one fragment, the rest of the lattice cannot be found reliably.
2. Extraction
   - Each fragment is embedded in a container, construct, spell node, or other anchor.
   - The fragment must be removed without destroying the host.
   - This is why brute-force damage is dangerous during the mech and temple encounters.
3. Reconstruction
   - Once recovered, the fragments can be recombined into a restored identity.
   - The final result depends on the number of casters, the quality of the fragments, the surrounding magic, and the relevant ritual DCs.

## Rescue Options

At each site, the party must keep the ritual stable. They can either rewrite the spellwork to route around [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul or capture and insert a replacement soul.

Spell rewriting is harder and risks damaging the fundamentals of magic if it fails. Soul substitution is somewhat easier, but requires a powerful captured soul and changes [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s final state. Replacing [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) with a weaker or less ideal soul weakens [Lucius](../people/lucius.md), which paradoxically can make the final battle harder because the gods may have less authority to intervene directly.

## Fragment Thresholds

- One fragment prevents [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) from being trapped in eternal ritual torment.
- Two fragments allow [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) to be put to rest.
- Three fragments allow reincarnation without memories.
- Four fragments allow full resurrection.

## Fragment Use

The fragments are not just sitting in storage. [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s working uses them as active components and power sources.

- In the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), one fragment powers a huge mech guardian.
- Other fragments are held in the ritual lattice to stabilize [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return.
- The more fragments are used this way, the less of [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) remains intact.
- If a fragment is destroyed during extraction, that piece is gone permanently.

The campaign treats [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul as finite. Every lost fragment leaves him more damaged and makes the final outcome worse.

## Failure States

If the party fails to extract a fragment safely:

- 1 fragment lost: personality damage and memory gaps.
- 2 fragments lost: major instability and partial identity loss.
- 3 or more fragments lost: [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) cannot be fully restored, only transformed into something new.
- Core fragments lost: resurrection fails entirely.

This is why the party must recognize the fragment, avoid brute-force destruction, and separate the soul from the host structure carefully.

## First Site

At the [Desert Well Ritual Site](../places/desert-well-ritual-site.md), [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) and [Eve](../people/eve.md) successfully exchange a captured djinn soul for the first [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragment. [Veilen](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md) remains behind to maintain the ritual after the lich operator is killed.

## Second Site

At the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), the party discovers that the site contains more than the expected active ritual fragment. A huge mech guardian is powered by a soul-cage structure holding another [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragment.

[Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) and [Jack Kenway](../people/jack-kenway.md) pull the soul cage out of the mech, collapsing the guardian and recovering the second fragment.

The active ritual fragment is recovered after [Winrah Holm](../people/winrah-holm.md)'s mind-control crown is removed and a Conclave champion takes over the spell. The party uses Hellspire's captured soul as the substitute. With Constance's control-panel work reducing the difficulty, the extraction succeeds by one point and recovers the third fragment.

## Final Site

After the third fragment is secured, [Vis / Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md) opens a direct path to the [High Temple of Rexel Kar](../places/high-temple-of-rexel-kar.md). The fourth and final fragment remains ahead, with less than half an hour before [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return completes.

Inside the final chamber, six serpent-head nodes hold [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s remaining anchors, blades, and armor. The party destroys or reroutes several of these anchors to lower the ritual difficulty, spends two Wish spells to reduce it further, and uses a captured Hellspire soul as the replacement component.

The final extraction succeeds. All four [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragments are recovered, making full resurrection possible.

Removing the last fragment also completes [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s consolidation. The remaining [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) anchors collapse into the spell, forcing his scattered fragments into one destroyable manifestation for the final battle.

In that sense, [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s rescue and [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s destruction are linked. The same ritual network that weaponizes [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) also gathers [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) into a form the party can finally kill.
