# The Kreen War

The Kreen War is the recent existential conflict between the linked worlds and the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md).

## Description

The war begins with [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) invasion pressure through gates and hidden routes, including the [Gorbin Ruins](../places/gorbin-ruins.md) on [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md). The [Unified Armed Forces](../factions/unified-armed-forces.md) mobilize across worlds to repel them.

## Late War Shift

After gate disruption, some [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) begin behaving differently. The party discovers [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) who protect civilians and can be reasoned with, undermining simple extermination doctrine.
