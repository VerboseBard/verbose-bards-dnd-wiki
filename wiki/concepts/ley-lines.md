# Ley Lines

Ley lines are reality-stabilizing magical structures activated after the [Divine Gate](divine-gate.md).

## Description

Ley lines appear to consume roughly half of all magical potential to maintain reality. Where their energy concentrates or bleeds off, it may generate [dungeons](dungeons.md) and other magical anomalies.

## Campaign Role

Ley line theory explains why teleportation is becoming harder, why refined ether is valuable, and why artificery may be the only path back to older heights of magical power.

[Greg's Concurrence Proposal](gregs-concurrence-proposal.md) describes ley-line and ley-nexus surveys as the first step in preparing worlds for controlled dimensional convergence. In that model, ley structures identify where magical conduits can safely anchor each world to the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).

During [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](jimbo-soul-ritual.md), [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s agents redirect ley-line flow through ritual facilities. At the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), lines are visibly bent back into the site to feed shields, towers, lair attacks, and ritual machinery.

In [War of the Gods, Session 24](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-24.md), the ley-line project is explained as an active reconstruction chain rather than a remote theory. The [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) is the center node, the elven nexus is already active, and a desert-world node has begun to come online, making three linked positions in a row. The remaining worlds still need their own activations if reality is to be pulled back together without catastrophic overlap.
