# Lucian Soul Fragments

[Lucian](../people/lucian.md) soul fragments are pieces of [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s essence embedded in anchors, serpent avatars, and tainted souls.

## Anchors

[Lucian](../people/lucian.md) placed parts of himself into weapons and other objects. Destroying or consuming these anchors weakens his return, but interacting with them is dangerous.

## Serpent Souls

Fully manifested serpent forms can carry or create [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)-tainted essence. When [Eve / Eva](../people/eva.md) consumes the [Mother Superior of Rax'al Karath](../people/mother-superior-of-raxal-karath.md)'s soul, the hidden fragment bites back and tears away part of her essence.

## Safe Consumption

[Belle](../people/belle.md) later teaches [Eve](../people/eva.md) how to trap a tainted fragment in a soul coin and consume future [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) fragments more safely.

When [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s armored crystal fragment is destroyed, [Eve](../people/eva.md) tries to reach for the fading residue. [Vis / Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) stops her long enough for the moment to pass, warning that she should practice on lesser souls before attempting another [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) fragment.

[Junara](../people/junara.md) senses the earlier fragment still trapped in [Eve](../people/eva.md)'s soul coin and trades her for it. [Greg](../people/greg.md) later explains that the coin can be used in a ritual to trace the larger concentration of [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s soul and the ritual lattice holding [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md).

The trace leads into [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](jimbo-soul-ritual.md), a linked series of ley-line rituals that use [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul fragments as components in [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s return.
