# Lucian's Splintered Echo

Lucian's Splintered Echo is the manifestation of [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) that appears through [Bishop Collins](../people/bishop-collins.md)'s trapped soul during [Junara](../people/junara.md)'s [Soul Cage](../items/soul-cage.md) interrogation.

## Manifestation

Instead of answering as [Bishop Collins](../people/bishop-collins.md), the trapped soul reshapes into [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s presence. The echo has enough soul force to appear, speak, answer questions, and destroy the remaining soul fragment before [Junara](../people/junara.md) can preserve it.

## Claims

The echo claims direct control of the Church of [Rax'al Karath](../factions/cobra-cult-of-raxal-karath.md). It explains that [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) once consumed the serpent god and took the worship for himself, but now the same hunger prophecy points toward [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) being consumed or reborn through the cracked worlds.

## Threat

The echo singles out [Vis / Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) as a substitute target for [Greg](../people/greg.md), saying that hurting [Vis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) may weaken [Greg](../people/greg.md).

## Anchors

[Morir](../people/morir.md) later explains that [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) placed essence into anchor blades and that fully manifested serpent avatars can create new anchors. Destroying enough anchors weakens [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s return; destroying all of them may prevent it entirely.

[Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s blade is destroyed during the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) factory strike, removing another anchor.
