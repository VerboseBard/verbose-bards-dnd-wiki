# Lucius's Crystal Trap

Lucius's Crystal Trap was the contingency that used [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s captured soul to bring a fragment of [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) back into the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md).

## Setup

[Lucius](../people/lucius.md) had already wounded the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) Crystal years earlier with a god-killing dagger. During [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s soul interrogation, [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) used that old wound as a resonant weak point.

The trap depended on several hidden pieces:

- [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s soul still carried enough [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) influence to become a speaking vector.
- [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s stored armor remained tied to his soul and could house a [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) fragment.
- The imprisoned nobles had implanted soul contingencies they did not consciously know about.
- The [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) Crystal was the same place where [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) had once been destroyed.

## Activation

When [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) asks when [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) will rise as Rax'al Karath, the answer becomes part of the spell: now.

[Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s voice hits a psychic and soul-level frequency that resonates through the crystal. The prisoners' souls detonate, damaging the crystal's security arrays and freeing the fragment to reach [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s armor.

## Battle Pattern

The fragment manifests in phases. It first uses armor, crystal debris, and defensive reactions. Later it shifts into a gravity-focused form, pulling enemies, reversing gravity, and using the crystal's own structure against the party.

Once the armor is stripped away, the fragment becomes unstable and flickers between physical and spirit states. If left alone, it would have exploded and caused catastrophic damage to the crystal.

## Failure

The trap fails because the party strips the armor apart, dispels key components, counters the crystal-shattering resonance with Words of Creation, and destroys the fragment before its final detonation.

The crystal survives, but it is no longer a perfect divine blind spot.
