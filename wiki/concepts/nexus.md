# Nexus

The Nexus is the convergence point of the major ley lines that stitch together [Unity](../places/unity.md).

## Description

At the exact center of [Unity](../places/unity.md), the ley lines do not merely cross. They collapse inward, folding magic into itself until reality bends under the pressure. This point is the Nexus.

The [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) was engineered directly upon the Nexus to stabilize impossible amounts of magic. Without that containment, teleportation and other high-order effects become catastrophically unstable.
