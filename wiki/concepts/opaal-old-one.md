# Opaal Old One

The Opaal Old One is the deep-sea horror tied to [Mulligan](../people/mulligan.md)'s ancient Commodore legend and the [Commodore Prophecy](commodore-prophecy.md).

Opaal prophecy and later Coral Palace material call it the Destroyer of Worlds. This page uses "Opaal Old One" as the wiki title to avoid confusion with [Lucius](../people/lucius.md), who also carries world-destroyer titles in other lore.

## Ancient Threat

The Opaal Old One is a Cthulhu-like deep horror whose rise once threatened all of [Opaal](../places/opaal.md), not merely the merfolk or sea elves.

At first, the ancient Commodore considered letting it destroy the peoples he hated. Once he realized it could destroy the entire world, he helped unite humans, merfolk, sea elves, and others against it.

This unification happened faster than outside powers wanted. [Lucius](../people/lucius.md), [Alen](../people/alen.md), and Black Viper-linked forces had reasons to prefer a broken world. Their interference around [Aurora](../people/aurora-queen-beneath-waves.md) helped push the Commodore off-world and turned his anti-vampire, anti-Viper campaign into part of the old one's wider history.

## The Wounded Seal

In the final ancient battle, the Commodore used a god-killing knife to strike the old one. Because the plan failed and the creature had bound him, he had to stab through himself to wound it.

That act mixed the Commodore's blood, power, and legend with the old one's wound. The creature was driven away or sealed, but not truly destroyed.

## Later Stirring

When the old one begins to stir again, the [Commodore Prophecy](commodore-prophecy.md) activates. The Commodore's return is therefore both warning and answer: the champion is back because the threat is close.
