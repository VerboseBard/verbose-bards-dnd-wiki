# Possibility Core

The Possibility Core is the power source formerly contained in the third level of the [Old Silverbend Mines](../places/old-silver-bend-mines.md).

## Nature

The core is formed where two miniature ley-line flows meet. It is not a dungeon core or dragon core, but a core of pure possibility tied to the language of creation.

## Kestrel's Song

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) understands the core's true name through the language of creation and sings it. The core enters him, empowering him with greater health, spell capacity, and the ability to sustain two concentrations.

## Collapse

After the core enters [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), the monolith collapses into possibility dust and the green crystals stop growing. Existing god-sight-blocking crystals become a finite resource.

## Dust

The remaining dust acts as motes of possibility. It can be used for rerolls, crafting, spell alteration, or other creative magical work. [Eve / Eva](../people/eva.md) absorbs a large amount of it, further empowering herself and [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s sight.
