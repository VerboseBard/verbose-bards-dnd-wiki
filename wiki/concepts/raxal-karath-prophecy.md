# Rax'al Karath Prophecy

The Rax'al Karath prophecy is a hunger doctrine preserved in Infernal and Abyssal inscriptions beneath the [Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout](../places/silver-bend-coastal-cave-hideout.md).

## Core Images

The murals and text describe mothers eating young, young eating mothers, fangs unhinging, stars and suns being swallowed, and the world cracking like an egg. Blood or yolk spills from the broken world while a serpent feeds on gods and eventually emerges from the shell.

## Lucius's Place

The prophecy records [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) consuming [Rax'al Karath / Rexel Kar](../people/rexel-kar.md), taking the serpent god's worship, and using that stolen divinity to conquer the Hells and Abyss.

The same cycle turns back on [Lucius](../people/lucius.md). In the hidden murals, [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) holds or consumes the serpent, but the next image shows a serpent or new coil behind him, preparing to devour him in turn.

## Current Meaning

According to [Lucius's Splintered Echo](lucius-splintered-echo.md), the cracked worlds are the next egg. The prophecy now points toward [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return in a more dangerous form, unless the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) succeed in ending or consuming the cycle first.

[Morir](../people/morir.md) later clarifies that serpent avatars and [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s anchor blades are practical mechanisms within this prophetic cycle. Each new anchor makes the return harder to stop.
