# Rite of Faded Remembrance

The Rite of Faded Remembrance is a Cir'[Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) ritual used to bind a spirit, preserve memories, and pass those memories into the Chromate Fungus network at the heart of [Viscus](../people/viscus.md).

## Purpose

The rite is used when the Grove needs to preserve a being's essence rather than allow it to pass away cleanly. It is solemn, carefully supervised, and treated as a sacred transition rather than a simple magical procedure.

The ritual is especially associated with [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s restoration, when his memories were shared with the Grove and his spirit was bound into the fungus network.

## Ritual Structure

The rite proceeds in four broad steps:

1. Invocation, where the elders chant over the body and the collar begins to respond.
2. Memory sharing, where spores and magic bring forward the subject's life and identity.
3. Binding, where those memories are carried into the Chromate Fungus.
4. Root joining, where [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) or one of its roots physically seals the connection.

The exact effect depends on the strength of the ritual and the willingness of the body and spirit involved.
