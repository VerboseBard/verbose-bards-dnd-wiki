# Seventh Age

The Seventh Age is the current era of the campaign, beginning with [the Concurrence](the-concurrence.md).

## Defining Features

The age is defined by linked worlds, changing magic, political consolidation, the aftermath of [the Kreen War](kreen-war.md), and a growing market for dungeon and fog artifacts.

The current main campaign sits in Year 11 of this age, not at its opening moment. That means the setting is still recognizably post-[Concurrence](the-concurrence.md), but no longer brand-new. Institutions, wars, and trade systems have had time to harden.

## Tone

The Seventh Age is not stable peace. It is an era where war is winding down, institutions are catching up, and hidden powers are using the confusion to reshape the future.

## Historical Threads Still In Play

The Seventh Age story is constantly haunted by earlier material:

- Fifth-Age material whose full campaign framing is still being clarified
- the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md)
- the late Sixth-Age and [Concurrence](the-concurrence.md)-era fallout of [Moon Stone Collectors](../campaign/moonstone-collectors.md)

Those are not just backstory archives. They are major sources of living consequences for the present campaign.

## Campaign Order in the Wiki

Within the currently tracked campaign material, the [Seventh Age](../concepts/seventh-age.md) is organized with [The Hunt for Black Vipers](../campaign/hunt-for-black-vipers.md) as the first campaign of the age. [Dawn of the Seventh Age](../campaign/dawn-of-the-seventh-age.md) follows as an early age-of-heroes arc, and the [After the War Campaign](../campaign/after-the-war-campaign.md) is currently placed later in the age's chronology.
