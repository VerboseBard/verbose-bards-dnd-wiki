# Sixth Age

The Sixth Age is the era immediately before the [Concurrence](the-concurrence.md).

## Description

The current main campaign does not begin here; it begins in Year 11 of the [Seventh Age](seventh-age.md). The Sixth Age serves as the immediate historical backdrop for featured material such as the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md) and [Moon Stone Collectors](../campaign/moonstone-collectors.md), both of which run through the end of this era and into the [Concurrence](the-concurrence.md).

This is the age of shattered worlds, open divine intervention, and the final crises that lead to the [Divine Gate](divine-gate.md). By the end of the age, divine power is sealed back, the old world structure is remade, and the [Seventh Age](seventh-age.md) begins.
