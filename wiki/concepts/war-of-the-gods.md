# War of the Gods

The War of the Gods was the divine conflict ignited when [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) turned on gods, heroes, and allies alike.

## Cause

For thousands of years, [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) positioned cults, artifacts, rituals, and prophecies. When the moment came, he betrayed the old pantheon and the alliance that helped him rise.

Archival [Fifth Age](fifth-age.md) notes suggest that this older pantheon still operated through organized divine blocs or assemblies shortly before the war, though the surviving record is incomplete and preserves some conflicting names. See [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md).

## Consequences

The war shattered divine dominion and twisted the shape of the world. At its apex, [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) stood close to total victory before the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) struck him down.

The war lasted twenty-one days and killed millions. The fall of the Raven Queen left mortal souls vulnerable to [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) until the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) defeated him and freed the dead from the Hells.

## Legacy

The war ended the age of direct divine rule and set the conditions for the [Divine Gate](divine-gate.md). Even defeated, [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s plans ensured the gods would be sealed away.
