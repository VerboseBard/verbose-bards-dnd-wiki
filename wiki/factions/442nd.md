# 442nd

The 442nd is a military or special operations unit operating from a forward post on the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).

## Role

The unit investigates and fights the [Black Vipers](black-vipers.md). After the attacks on the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) and the [Hell's Bane Heroes](hells-bane-heroes.md), [Captain West](../people/captain-west.md) briefs the 442nd on recon, street work, cell-tracking, and soul rescue objectives.

## Assets

The 442nd deploys teams through portals on large bats and can operate across worlds. Teams three through eight are available as field support during the [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) operation.

## Saharun Deployment

During the cave assault arc, [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) directs three squads to support and backtrack the [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md) operation, three squads to establish safe houses and watch the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) casino lead, and two squads into the [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md).

The jungle deployment becomes alarming: one squad goes silent, and the other appears to be jammed or distorted when trying to report.

[Morir](../people/morir.md) later returns four captured 442nd members alive and unconscious in exchange for a protected meeting and safe departure.

During the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) operation, 442nd teams monitor the casino, identify suspicious meeting participants, locate [Hellspire's Manufactory](../places/hellspires-manufactory.md), and provide overwatch while the party breaches the factory.

During the assault on [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), a 442nd scout acts as the ley-line shuttle operator, carrying reinforcement waves between the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)-side staging point and the active ritual sites.
