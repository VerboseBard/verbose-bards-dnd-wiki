# Abyssal Hand

The Abyssal Hand is a demonic or infernal-aligned network mentioned during the [Moon Stone Collectors](moon-stone-collectors.md) arc.

## Description

The group is associated with hell-side contacts, abducted travelers, psychological torment, illusion, intimidation, and mind control.

Known names tied to the network include [Zarvok the Torturer](../people/zarvok-the-torturer.md), [Lyris the Deceiver](../people/lyris-the-deceiver.md), [Morgrath the Enforcer](../people/morgrath-the-enforcer.md), and [Vaelith the Whisperer](../people/vaelith-the-whisperer.md).

Later material also refers to part of this leadership structure as the Black Fist, suggesting an elite or alternate name within [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s Hell-side command.

## Campaign Role

The Abyssal Hand is the faction the party keeps circling as the mystery shifts from one missing person to a wider infernal operation.

During the Hell assault, Morgrath and Lyris are killed by the [Moon Stone Collectors](moon-stone-collectors.md), leaving the Hand badly damaged.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), that damage proves less simple than it first appeared. A high-stakes trap at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md) reveals disguised hostile figures connected to the Hand, including [Vaelith the Whisperer](../people/vaelith-the-whisperer.md) and a Deceiver-linked presence. The party also learns that some powers they fought in Hell may have been forced imitations or projections rather than true demon lords risking final death.

In the reconstructed [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), the party breaches [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md) and fights roughly twenty-five demons. Vaelith escapes, but the Deceiver-linked enemy is destroyed by divine retribution. Her final words, "Worth it," imply that the fight triggered broader contingency plans across the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).
