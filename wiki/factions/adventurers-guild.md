# Adventurer's Guild

The Adventurer's Guild is the dominant dungeon-delving institution of the [Dawn of the Seventh Age](../campaign/dawn-of-the-seventh-age.md).

## Description

The Guild offers structure, safety, and the promise of success to those bold enough to step beyond the edge of the known. It trains delvers, funds operations, buys loot, and regulates dungeon access.

## Guild Roles

- Guild Members: sanctioned delvers who follow the rules and benefit from Guild support
- Independent Rescuers: high-risk retrieval specialists who can work with or without the Guild
- Unaffiliated [Dungeon Divers](../places/dungeon-divers.md): free agents who operate outside Guild jurisdiction
- Explorers of the Unknown: another label for the free-agent delving tradition
- Opportunists and Outlaws: thieves, mercenaries, and predators who profit from others' work

## Core Rules

- Members must accept assigned missions
- The Guild takes a major cut of profits
- Gear, training, and support are provided
- Rescue teams exist, but they are limited and expensive

## Campaign Role

The Guild is the party's first real social structure in the [Dawn of the Seventh Age](../campaign/dawn-of-the-seventh-age.md) campaign. It gives them rank, resources, and danger in equal measure.
