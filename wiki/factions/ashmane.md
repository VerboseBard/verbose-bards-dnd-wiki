# Ashmane

Ashmane is the dominant cat clan of [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md).

## Identity

Ashmane's prestige comes from memory as much as power. In the old stories, cat folk led the great beastfolk slave uprising against humans before the valley peoples were purchased and preserved by drow. The uprising failed catastrophically, but Ashmane still carries the legacy of having struck first.

That memory gives the clan seniority in the eyes of many valley families.

## Members

Ashmane includes:

- Leonin / lionfolk
- Tabaxi / catfolk
- Eluran
- local lynx-like cat lines

## Current Leadership

The seated elder of Ashmane is [Siv Halven](../people/siv-halven.md), a leonine old-guard leader who favors isolation, discipline, and strict stewardship.

Its primary valley stronghold is [Ashmane Hold](../places/ashmane-hold.md).

Known race speakers include:

- [Siv Halven](../people/siv-halven.md) for the Leonin
- Runa Keld for the Tabaxi
- Leora Svan for the Eluran

## Politics

Ashmane is strongly associated with [the Old Charge](old-charge.md). It treats outside contact as a danger first and an opportunity second, and it resists any easy restoration of the lost rabbit seat.
