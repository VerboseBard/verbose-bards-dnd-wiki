# Ash's Hounds

Ash's Hounds are elite special operations teams organized under [General Ash](../people/general-ash.md) during the late [Kreen War](../concepts/kreen-war.md).

Their formal spoken name in [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md) is `Unified Counter-Kreen Suppression and Rogue Hive Special Operations Group`. `Ash's Hounds` is the common field name.

## Structure

The Hounds are organized into wolf packs: small, highly capable squads trusted with missions too sensitive, ugly, or politically volatile for regular troops. The player party is one such pack.

The first members are selected from surviving [Bloody Fifth](bloody-fifth.md) soldiers after the Driftvale battle and transferred to [Fort Liberty](../places/fort-liberty.md) on [Unity](../places/unity.md). Promotions are immediate: corporals become sergeants, sergeants become lieutenants, and privates become corporals. [Colonel Gandor](../people/colonel-gandor.md), an orc officer whose exact name spelling remains provisional, is assigned as the unit's direct supervisor.

## Duties

Their missions include deep strike operations, [Kreen](kreen.md) capture, experimentation support, village interdiction, and assignments where official reporting is carefully managed.

The Hounds become involved in the mass capture of [Kreen](kreen.md) for magical and biological research. That research eventually produces [Kreen control collars](../items/kreen-control-collars.md), which can suppress [Kreen](kreen.md) violence and visibly gray their skin. Session 2's transcript specifically frames the breakthrough as preventing a collared Kreen from hurting a human; whether that means literal humans or a broader protected category remains open.

## Fate

After the [Norinar](../places/norinar.md) mission, the Hounds are shut down and broken up. The party is sent to [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md) on [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md). The shutdown does not erase their strategic effect: their capture work enables the control collars and supports the dark elf queen's surrender of 100,000 Kreen in the Underdark.
