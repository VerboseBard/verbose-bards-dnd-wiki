# Briarstep

Briarstep is the small-predator clan of [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md), built from fox, Mapach, and mustelid peoples.

## Identity

Briarstep survives through flexibility. It is not the oldest or most honored clan, and it does not command the weight of Ashmane or the sheer numbers of Burrowroot. Instead, it thrives by being useful, quick, and difficult to pin down.

That has made Briarstep an important swing bloc in council disputes.

## Members

Briarstep includes:

- Vulpin / foxfolk
- Mapach
- Mustel, including otter-, weasel-, ferret-, and badger-like folk

## Current Leadership

The seated elder of Briarstep is [June Mercer](../people/june-mercer.md), a Mapach leader who knows the temple truth but is not loyal to the old secrecy in the same way as the hardest-line elders.

Its primary valley stronghold is [Briarstep Cross](../places/briarstep-cross.md).

Known race speakers include:

- Kaede Noren for the Vulpin
- [June Mercer](../people/june-mercer.md) for the Mapach
- Wyatt Collier for the Mustel

## Politics

Briarstep often aligns with [the Open Trail](open-trail.md), though individual speakers vary. The clan is cautious rather than idealistic and tends to support whatever keeps it adaptable.
