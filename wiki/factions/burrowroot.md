# Burrowroot

Burrowroot is the broad non-predatory bloc of [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md).

## Identity

Burrowroot is less a single people than a political bundle of many peoples forced into shared representation. It is numerically large and socially important, but its diversity makes it easier for stronger council interests to manage.

The rabbit folk were folded into Burrowroot after the loss of the old Moonhare line's council seat more than a century ago.

## Members

Burrowroot includes:

- Harengon / rabbitfolk
- Cervan / deerfolk
- Hedge / hedgehogfolk
- Jerbeen / mouse-jerboa smallfolk
- Capran / goatfolk
- Tortle / turtlefolk

## Current Leadership

The seated elder of Burrowroot is [Tavero Bellan](../people/tavero-bellan.md), a Cervan elder who favors preserving the present order and resists any easy reopening of rabbit claims.

Its primary valley stronghold is [Burrowroot Warren](../places/burrowroot-warren.md).

Alongside Tavero's formal role stands [Walter Wifflewick](../people/walter-wifflewick.md), an elder Harengon lore master whose authority rests on genealogy, record-keeping, old law, and Moonhare memory rather than the official council seat.

Known race speakers and key local voices include:

- [Beatrix Hooley](../people/beatrix-hooley.md) for the Harengon
- [Tavero Bellan](../people/tavero-bellan.md) for the Cervan
- [Agnes Weller](../people/agnes-weller.md) for the Hedge
- [Milo Penwyck](../people/milo-penwyck.md) for the Jerbeen
- [Isaro Velen](../people/isaro-velen.md) for the Capran
- [Tomas Grell](../people/tomas-grell.md) for the Tortle
- [Mireya Bellan](../people/mireya-bellan.md), Tavero's mate and a controlled hospitality figure
- [Celia Velen](../people/celia-velen.md), a Capran mediator and liaison

## Rabbit Question

Burrowroot's greatest unresolved pressure point is the rabbit question.

The old [Moonhare](../people/edmund-moonhare.md) line once held a separate seat. That line collapsed when [Cedric Moonhare](../people/cedric-moonhare.md) vanished on a trade mission and his father died before the inner temple duty could be passed on cleanly. The council then chose not to restore rabbit standing.

That decision created the quiet restoration cause later known as [the Sixth Seat](sixth-seat.md).

Older rabbit memory also preserves the fact that Harengon caravans once left the valley through the fog on a recurring trade cycle. That travel ended after the last expedition failed to return.

## Politics

Burrowroot is publicly communal and stabilizing, but internally divided. It contains hard isolationists, cautious pragmatists, and rabbit families who still remember what was taken from them.
