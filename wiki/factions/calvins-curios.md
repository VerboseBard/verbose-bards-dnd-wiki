# Calvin's Curios

Calvin's Curios is both a shop and an organization centered on artifact acquisition, banking leverage, and controlled access to fog-recovered objects.

## Leadership

[Rurik Valdren](../people/rurik-valdren.md) appears to be the true power behind the shop. He is a dwarven banker, broker, and artifact dealer whose influence extends far beyond [Sin](../places/sin.md).

## Interests

The organization is interested in valuable artifacts, specialized recovery jobs, and financial arrangements that keep it protected from simple military seizure.

## Campaign Role

The party considers Calvin's Curios as a possible employer but ultimately avoids deeper entanglement once the [Inquisition](inquisition.md) arrives.
