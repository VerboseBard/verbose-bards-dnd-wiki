# Cobra Cult of Rax'al Karath

The Cobra Cult of Rax'al Karath is an old serpent-worshipping religious network tied to the original god later remembered as [Rexel Kar](../people/rexel-kar.md).

## Origin

Before [the Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md), Rax'al Karath was worshiped by yuan-ti, grung, and other peoples of the jungle. He was defeated by [Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md) after she was summoned to the plane, but memory and worship kept the cult alive.

## Lucius's Usurpation

During the thousand years when the worlds were separate, the cult returned to prominence. The worshippers did not realize their god's place had been usurped by [Lucius](../people/lucius.md), who fed on the worship meant for Rax'al Karath.

## Relationship to the Black Vipers

Radical members of the cult supported the [Black Vipers](black-vipers.md). After the hammer fell three years ago, many Vipers fled back to these worshippers. To the cult, the [Black Vipers](black-vipers.md) are the hands of their god.

During the [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) cave assault, cult priests manifest spectral serpent power, transform into large divine serpent forms, and empower themselves by consuming allies or followers.

## Church Structure

The cave cell identifies itself as a church, not a cult. [Initiate Thomas](../people/initiate-thomas.md) claims there may be hundreds of thousands of worshippers, though only around ten thousand active servants or warriors.

Known leaders from the cave operation include [Bishop Collins](../people/bishop-collins.md) and [Deacon Matthews](../people/deacon-matthews.md).

## Prophecy and Lucius

The hidden shrine beneath the cave reveals that the church's hunger doctrine is tied to [Rax'al Karath Prophecy](../concepts/raxal-karath-prophecy.md). [Lucius's Splintered Echo](../concepts/lucius-splintered-echo.md) later claims direct control of the church and says the old serpent prophecy now points toward his return.

## Tactical Notes

[Soul Cage](../items/soul-cage.md) interrogation reveals that the jungle watcher can see nearly everything entering the [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md), but "does not have eyes under the water."

[Morir](../people/morir.md) clarifies that not every Rax'al worshipper is a Black Viper zealot. The jungle society contains millions of people, while the Viper-linked faction is an extremist fringe willing to commit atrocities for the prophecy.

The [Mother Superior of Rax'al Karath](../people/mother-superior-of-raxal-karath.md) attends [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s casino table, confirming that serpent-church leadership remains connected to [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s network even after [Bishop Collins](../people/bishop-collins.md) and [Deacon Matthews](../people/deacon-matthews.md) are destroyed.

## Symbol

Members use cobra imagery. In their religious language, the distinction between cobra and viper does not carry the same meaning, and demonic or celestial categories can blur.
