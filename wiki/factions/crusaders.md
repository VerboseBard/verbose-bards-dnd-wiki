# Crusaders

The Crusaders are militant religious forces connected to the [Conclave of Churches](conclave-of-churches.md).

## Description

Crusaders operate where religious mission, military force, and existential danger overlap. Some wear heavy armor and travel with church delegations or high-ranking religious officials.

## Campaign Role

Crusader-like figures appear among the groups preparing to enter the fog for the [Lost Homeland Mission](../concepts/lost-homeland-mission.md). Their presence means the mission is not simply military reconnaissance; it has religious and political stakes.
