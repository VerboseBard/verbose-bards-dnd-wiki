# Dargains Daggers

Dargains Daggers are the last large, coordinated pirate power in the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md).

## Style

Smaller pirate crews exist everywhere, but the Daggers are noteworthy because they remain organized enough to act like a true faction rather than a loose criminal habit.

They prefer skiff-heavy raiding. Groups of four or more skiffs peck at larger ships, wear them down, and force surrender rather than relying only on boarding duels.

## Reputation

The Daggers are treated as pragmatic raiders. They steal because theft is safer than harvesting many Astral resources directly.
