# Driftvale Fog Walkers

Driftvale Fog Walkers are native or long-established peoples who know how to survive and travel through [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md)'s fog.

## Description

Fog Walkers understand routes, eddies, dangers, and survival practices that outsiders must learn painfully. Some families became separated from ancestral homelands when the fog shifted.

## Campaign Role

The [Lost Homeland Mission](../concepts/lost-homeland-mission.md) is personally important because it may reconnect one party member's heritage to a hidden valley in the fog.

In [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), that possibility becomes a confirmed reality. The party reaches [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md), finds it inhabited and defended, and meets a patrol whose reaction shifts when they recognize kinship in the approaching outsiders. That moment confirms that the fog-walker split was historical, not mythical.
