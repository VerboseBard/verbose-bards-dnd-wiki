# Giff Compendency

The Giff Compendency is the second great power of the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md) and the strongest serious rival to the Gith'yanki in open void warfare.

## Identity

The Giff are large, powerful beings resembling hippos from the Prime Material. They became a major Astral force through their unusual natural affinity for firearms, first as pirates and later as an organized imperial power.

## Culture

The surviving note describes Giff culture as sarcastic, playful, profane, and nomadic. Most are born and raised aboard ships rather than on stable land.

They developed huge mobile platform-ships called Temraps for docking, resupply, and concentration of force. Later, after deciding to become an imperial power, they established a permanent capital called Haerdhome as the one unmoving sign of Giff strength in the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md).

## Politics and Religion

The Compendency is led by a council of eight former pirate chiefs, now Fleet Commanders, with a religious leader acting as tie-breaker.

Their religion is deliberately broad and strange: because they no longer know which god originally made or favored them, they pray to all gods at once or to whichever one seems best for the moment. Some even direct prayers to a lost, dead, or undiscovered god.

The surviving [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md) source note names [Sir Humphrey Puddington](../people/sir-humphrey-puddington.md) as god-emperor of the Giff in the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md), while explicitly noting that he is not actually a god.

## Military

Hit-and-run void warfare is their specialty. Even when they could not win a straight war against the Yanki, they were good enough at raiding, kiting, and evasive assault to remain a serious threat.
