# Gith'Charek

The Gith'Charek are the [Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md)-faithful rider splinter of the Gith'yanki.

## Origin

The broader Yanki empire abandoned or ignored its old pact with [Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md), but some refused that break. Those holdouts kept the rider tradition and the divine relationship alive rather than accept the empire's anti-theistic drift.

## Position

The Gith'Charek are powerful but relatively small in number. They have no stable homeland and are most often encountered near the edge of the Uncharted Void.
