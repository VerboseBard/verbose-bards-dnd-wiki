# Godfolk Alliance

The Godfolk Alliance is the cultural and political bloc native to [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md), including orcs, goblins, hobgoblins, bugbears, and related peoples.

## Culture

The alliance is described as Norse meets Rome. Its people combine shield-wall traditions, harsh ice-world survival, and a highly professional military structure.

The goblins of [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md) are especially technologically advanced, with major underground cities and sophisticated engineering traditions. Hobgoblins, bugbears, and other allied peoples are not limited to simple stereotypes of brute force or stealth; they occupy a broad cultural middle ground within the alliance.

## Concurrence Era

As [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) nears completion, [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md) becomes politically tense. Many people distrust the magical technology being installed across their world, especially because the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md) is also removing the gods from direct leadership.

The alliance's religious leadership sends an emissary to coordinate with the [Conclave of Churches](conclave-of-churches.md), prompting assassination attempts that draw in the [Moon Stone Collectors](moon-stone-collectors.md).
