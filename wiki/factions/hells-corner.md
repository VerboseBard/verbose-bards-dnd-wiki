# Hells Corner

Hells Corner is the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md) sector controlled by devils operating out of an old infernal city fragment.

## Role

Its rulers are primarily slavers, traffickers, and soul merchants rather than conquerors. They buy, sell, bind, and repurpose mortal bodies and souls on an industrial scale.

With the illithids weakened and pushed back by Gith pressure, the devils used the opening to expand their slave empire into the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md).

## Strategy

The notes describe Hells Corner as politically neutral in the pragmatic infernal sense. They do not seek war for its own sake. They seek profit, leverage, and souls. But when attacked, they respond with overwhelming lethality, implying that they may possess more raw power than their lower profile suggests.
