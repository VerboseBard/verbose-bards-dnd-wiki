# Karrains Corporation

Karrains Corporation is a human and dwarven [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md) power built around extraction, logistics, and commercialization.

## Origin

The corporation was founded by humans and dwarves who escaped the [Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md) but could not find their way out of the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md). Instead of treating the void as a prison, they developed new methods for harvesting food, drugs, and dangerous Astral creatures.

## Trade Model

They are especially associated with capturing or processing:

- spectral hounds
- Theks
- Somalygots
- exceptionally rare Astral Dreadnaughts

The notes frame them as one of the greediest sector powers, powerful less through ideology than through supply control and adaptation.
