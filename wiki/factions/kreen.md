# Kreen

The Kreen are insectoid invaders and one of the defining enemies of the recent war. Their society appears to have depended on powerful hive-mind or gate-linked control structures.

## Description

During the war, Kreen were treated as an existential threat. After the gate network was disrupted, many Kreen became disoriented, severed, or changed. Some remained violent, while others became capable of surrender, adaptation, or even protection of non-Kreen civilians.

## Gray Kreen

Military experimentation led to magic that made Kreen unable to attack or harm a protected category of people. Session 2's spoken wording says humans, while Session 3's recap generalizes this to sentient races. These altered Kreen develop gray skin, allowing soldiers to identify them visually.

## Campaign Role

The party's understanding of the Kreen changes sharply in [Norinar](../places/norinar.md), where they find Kreen protecting civilians instead of slaughtering them. That revelation complicates the morality of their mission and eventually feeds into surrender and collar policy.
