# Longbough

Longbough is the Hadozee clan of [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md).

## Identity

Longbough is small compared to Burrowroot and less socially broad than Windcrest, but it is respected for discipline, perspective, and a strong culture of elevated watchfulness. Its people think in terms of height, structure, and duty.

## Members

Longbough includes the valley's Hadozee lineages: gliding apefolk with a flying-squirrel-like movement culture and a distinct old-duty identity.

## Current Leadership

The seated elder and sole major race speaker of Longbough is [Matthias Duren](../people/matthias-duren.md).

Its primary valley stronghold is [Longbough Canopy](../places/longbough-canopy.md).

[Talin Duren](../people/talin-duren.md) is a known Longbough observer and escort figure attached to first-contact events.

## Politics

Longbough is strongly aligned with [the Old Charge](old-charge.md). It treats the valley less as a refuge than as an inherited sacred burden and is among the least willing to relax old boundaries.
