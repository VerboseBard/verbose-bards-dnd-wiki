# Moon Stone Collectors

The Moon Stone Collectors, often shortened to TMC, are an adventuring group whose story becomes important near the end of the [Sixth Age](../concepts/sixth-age.md) and again after [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s destruction of [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s stronghold. Older notes and early wiki drafts sometimes rendered the name as `Moon Stone Collators`; current transcript evidence supports `Moon Stone Collectors` as the readable canon name.

## Early Years

Shortly before [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) completes, TMC thwarts an assassination attempt against a religious emissary from [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md). The emissary is traveling to coordinate with the [Conclave of Churches](conclave-of-churches.md), a gathering meant to answer the gods' coming departure.

The group teleports the real emissary and [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) ahead, then travels by ice-ship toward [Ice Watch](../places/ice-watch.md) with [Zen](../people/zen.md) disguised as the priest. The trap draws a hell-portal ambush, and the party defeats the demons, though [Soda](../people/soda.md) is left infected by necrotic demonic energy.

## Ice Watch and Frostfire

The later [Ice Watch](../places/ice-watch.md) arc pushes TMC into [Frostfire Harbor](../places/frostfire-harbor.md), [Frostfire Tavern](../places/frostfire-tavern.md), and the [Temple of Gorthak's Triumph](../places/temple-of-gorthaks-triumph.md).

[Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) disappears, leaving behind a sterilized room, a hidden bow, and the sense that the local church and temple politics are hiding more than they admit.

The party also learns the names and pressure points of the local infernal network, which the campaign later frames as the [Abyssal Hand](abyssal-hand.md).

## Rescue

They launched a daring strike that was not merely an attack, but a rescue. Among those freed was [Gillesa](../people/gillesa.md), a fragment of the shattered devil soul from which [Julia / Julisia](../people/julia-julisia.md) was born.

## Importance

Their rescue destabilized [Julia](../people/julia-julisia.md)'s long revenge. Once [Julia](../people/julia-julisia.md) learned [Gillesa](../people/gillesa.md) lived, she diverted resources, sacrificed pawns, and lost control of the game.

## Radiant Citadel and the Concurrence

Later Moon Stone Collectors sessions move the party through the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), [Glacier's Edge Theater](../places/glaciers-edge-theater.md), [Frozenheart University](../places/frozenheart-university.md), [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](../places/lady-penny-hathaways-school.md), and the [First National Bank of the GIF](../places/first-national-bank-of-the-gif.md).

[Arthur Saran](../people/arthur-saran.md), [Lady Penny Hathaway](../people/lady-penny-hathaway.md), [Kendrick](../people/kendrick.md), and other [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)-side figures become important as the group moves from tavern investigation into travel permissions, school access, and family negotiations.

[Zen](../people/zen.md) is one of the clearest through-lines in this phase, handling disguise work, infernal artifact analysis, and later the moral and practical burden of [Elisia](../people/elisia.md)'s restraint.

## Egg Rescue and Wendell's Back Door

During the final hours of [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), the group accepts [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md)'s contract to help rescue trapped children and dragon eggs.

The operation takes them from the [Shippers and Delivery Guild Warehouse](../places/shippers-and-delivery-guild-warehouse.md) into the [Dwarven Egg Fortress](../places/dwarven-egg-fortress.md), where they fight [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s corrupted dwarven servants and learn of a hidden route to [Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress](../places/wendells-sixth-layer-fortress.md).

## Hell Assault

The next phase sends the group into [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s fortress with a much larger assault force. [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s strike has already broken the gates and crippled [Wendell](../people/wendell.md), but the fortress remains full of demons, Black Fist leaders, captives, and stolen souls.

After a detour through the [Hall of Sorrows](../places/hall-of-sorrows.md), the party returns to the sixth layer and kills [Morgrath the Enforcer](../people/morgrath-the-enforcer.md). [Lyris the Deceiver](../people/lyris-the-deceiver.md) arrives immediately afterward and is killed in the following fight.

The party frees [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) and [Elisia](../people/elisia.md), then escapes as [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s fortress collapses and the sixth layer of Hell is destroyed.

## Citadel Fallout

After returning to the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), the party learns that three days have passed outside Hell. [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) is physically healed but mentally unreachable, and [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) becomes the center of a religious confrontation when [Cardinal Preceptor Thornvale](../people/cardinal-preceptor-thornvale.md) tries to have her destroyed.

The party accepts responsibility for [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) under a [Collar of Demon Subjugation](../items/collar-of-demon-subjugation.md) until the Conclave can judge her case.

The same downtime reveals deeper dangers: [Julara](../people/julara.md) bargains with [Paul](../people/paul.md), [Mave](../people/mave.md) warns [Juan](../people/juan.md) about the cost of luck, and an invitation in [Lady Seraphina Blackthorn](../people/lady-seraphina-blackthorn.md)'s name leads [Juan](../people/juan.md) and [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) into a trap at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md).

The reconstructed next session confirms that the trap becomes a full battle against more than twenty demons. The party prevents the Deceiver-linked enemy from escaping, but [Vaelith the Whisperer](../people/vaelith-the-whisperer.md) escapes and [Juan](../people/juan.md) is left compromised enough to attack [June](../people/june.md).

The manor victory triggers the Deceiver's sleeper contingencies across the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md). The party returns to [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](../places/lady-penny-hathaways-school.md) and finds [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) overtaken by [Julia / Julisia](../people/julia-julisia.md), the school devastated, and [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) still protected in stasis.

The group flees to [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md), regroups at the [Chuckling Gecko](../places/chuckling-gecko.md), and begins the thawing road north toward the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

At the grove, [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) is restored through the Ritual of Fated Remembrance, while [Julara](../people/julara.md) traces the northern infection to ancient dwarven dealings with [Zarvok the Torturer](../people/zarvok-the-torturer.md). The party prepares next to confront an [Ancient Ice Hydra](../concepts/ancient-ice-hydra.md) blocking one of the passes.

Before they can reach the hydra, a missing-children rescue pulls part of the group through a mushroom circle into the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md). They bargain with the [Glade Mistress](../people/glade-mistress.md), return the children safely, and come back [Fey](../concepts/fey.md)-touched after nearly three days have passed outside.

The aftermath leaves [Paul](../people/paul.md) marked by [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s [Crown of Fools](../items/crown-of-fools.md) for destroying the mushroom circle. The group then reaches the pass and defeats the ancient ice hydra along with its ember hydra mate, clearing the immediate monster threat before the hydras can nest.

The next phase sends the group back into the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md), where they petition the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md) to replace the broken Spring Court link to [Viscus](../people/viscus.md). While the grove is restored, [Paul](../people/paul.md) leaves the party to protect [Julara](../people/julara.md), and the remaining group prepares to take a living piece of [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) into the old dwarven control room.

## Final Legacy

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), the group descends into the old [Gateway to the Empire](../places/gateway-to-the-empire.md) facility and reaches the master goo control room.

They survive the dwarven trap gauntlet, protect the living [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) cutting, and complete the control sequence that lets [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) convert the master ooze. The result neutralizes the free-spreading goo apocalypse but gives [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) command over an ancient network of infected bodies and roughly 500,000 undead soldiers.

The same ending sees [Sovereignty](../people/sovereignty.md) made whole in the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md). The Moon Stone Collectors close their arc by leaving the world with fewer immediate apocalypses, but several very large new powers.
