# The Old Charge

The Old Charge is the old-guard current inside and around [the Sunhollow Council](sunhollow-council.md).

## Identity

Its members believe the valley survived because its elders kept faith with the old duty, sealed dangerous truths, and resisted the temptations of contact, curiosity, and expansion.

For them, the central task of Sunhollow is not comfort. It is containment.

## Leading Figures

- [Siv Halven](../people/siv-halven.md)
- [Tavero Bellan](../people/tavero-bellan.md)
- [Matthias Duren](../people/matthias-duren.md)

## Positions

- strongly stay-put
- strongly protective of temple secrecy
- resistant to rabbit restoration
- hostile or deeply suspicious toward outsiders
