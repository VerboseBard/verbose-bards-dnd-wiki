# The Open Trail

The Open Trail is the pragmatic current inside and around [the Sunhollow Council](sunhollow-council.md).

## Identity

Its members do not advocate reckless openness, but they believe the valley's old rules may no longer be enough to keep it safe. They favor controlled contact, observation, and adaptation over blind repetition of inherited policy.

## Leading Figures

- [Sabine Voss](../people/sabine-voss.md)
- [June Mercer](../people/june-mercer.md)
- [Beatrix Hooley](../people/beatrix-hooley.md)

## Positions

- cautiously open to new information
- more neutral toward outsiders than the old guard
- divided on rabbit restoration, but willing to hear the question
- includes members who suspect secrecy itself has become dangerous
