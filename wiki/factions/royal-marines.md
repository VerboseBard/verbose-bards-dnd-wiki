# Royal Marines

The Royal Marines are Giff military forces offered by [Admiral Gray](../people/admiral-gray.md) during the final push against [Lucius](../people/lucius.md).

## Background

They represent the organized military arm of the [Giff Compendency](giff-compendency.md), one of the six major powers of the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md).

## Black Viper Arc

[Admiral Gray](../people/admiral-gray.md) offers the Royal Marines after learning that [Lucius](../people/lucius.md), the being responsible for the God Emperor's favorite son's death, is returning and moving toward godhood.

The Marines are assigned to reinforcement waves for [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md). They arrive at the first ritual site during the party's short rest after the first [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragment is recovered.
