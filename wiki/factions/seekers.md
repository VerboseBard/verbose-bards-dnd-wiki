# Seekers

The Seekers are a divine-linked group or lineage named in the [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md) source note as the children of [Dione](../people/dione.md) and [Savras](../people/savras.md).

## Fifth Age Divine Context

In the Sages' Assembly list, Dione and Savras are described as gods of foreknowledge and prophecy, ranked second in power, with the Seekers named as their children.

## Later School-Era Role

In the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md), Seekers gather newly awakened people from different worlds and bring them to [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](../places/lady-penny-hathaways-school.md).

[War of the Gods, Session 12](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-12.md) clarifies that the Seekers have sided with the school rather than with Dione directly, making their later institutional loyalties more complicated than simple obedience to a parent deity.

## Name Caution

The word "seeker" is also used generically in some communities, such as [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md), for people who travel outward and bring knowledge back. Those local roles should not automatically be treated as the same faction unless later notes connect them.
