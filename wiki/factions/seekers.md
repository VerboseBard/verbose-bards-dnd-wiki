# Seekers

The Seekers are an order of clerics under the Triumphant of Divination Gods.

## Fifth Age Divine Context

In the [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md), the Sages' Assembly list ties the Seekers to [Dione](../people/dione.md) and [Savras](../people/savras.md), gods of foreknowledge and prophecy. The source note calls the Seekers their children, but current clarification identifies the Seekers as a clerical order serving under the divination gods rather than a simple bloodline.

Not much is known about the order's inner workings.

## Holy Book and Mark

Seekers receive foreknowledge, information, and directions through a holy book that updates with what they need to know.

They can be identified by the holy symbol emblazoned on their forehead. If someone who is not part of the Seeker order tries to copy that symbol, the false mark bursts into flame.

## Later School-Era Role

In the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md), Seekers gather newly awakened people from different worlds and bring them to [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](../places/lady-penny-hathaways-school.md).

[War of the Gods, Session 12](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-12.md) clarifies that the Seekers have sided with the school rather than with Dione directly, making their later institutional loyalties more complicated than simple obedience to a parent deity.

## The Quartet

The Quartet are four identical sisters who all belong to the Seeker order:

- [Seeker Lauren](../people/seeker-lauren.md): found [Jeffrey Wilson](../people/jeffrey-wilson.md) and Anton, then was sent to pick up Zach's character. She rebels against being indistinguishable from her sisters by dyeing her hair pink and wearing pigtails.
- [Seeker Lindsey](../people/seeker-lindsey.md): picked up Tim's character and the triplets. She is prickly and grumpy.
- [Seeker Lucia](../people/seeker-lucia.md): picked up Nate's character and Josh's character. She is awkward and socially uncertain.
- [Seeker Linda](../people/seeker-linda.md): picked up Katie's character and Jaden's character. She is easygoing, kind, and has a good sense of humor.

Known wiki-linked pickup details currently include [Seeker Linda](../people/seeker-linda.md) finding [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md), and [Seeker Lindsey](../people/seeker-lindsey.md) finding [Molokan](../people/molokan.md).

## Name Caution

The word "seeker" is also used generically in some communities, such as [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md), for people who travel outward and bring knowledge back. Those local roles should not automatically be treated as the same faction unless later notes connect them.
