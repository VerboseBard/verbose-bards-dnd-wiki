# Silk Parlor Network

[The Silk Parlor](../places/silk-parlor.md) Network is the information, artifact, and influence web controlled by [Mistress Selyra Vex'ryn](../people/mistress-selyra-vexryn.md) from [The Silk Parlor](../places/silk-parlor.md) in [Sin](../places/sin.md).

## Description

The network uses courtesans, social leverage, magical mental skills, blackmail, and paid scavenging crews. It controls a large portion of artifacts moving out of [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md)'s fog.

## Interests

Mistress Vex'ryn is especially interested in drow-related artifacts. She does not appear to care as much about dwarven objects, suggesting a specific hidden agenda rather than simple greed.

## Campaign Role

The party cooperates indirectly for profit during their [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md) posting, then disengages once [Inquisition](inquisition.md) scrutiny makes further contact dangerous.
