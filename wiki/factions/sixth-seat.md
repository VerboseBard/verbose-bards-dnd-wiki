# The Sixth Seat

The Sixth Seat is the quiet cause to restore rabbit dignity and, eventually, a formal rabbit place within [the Sunhollow Council](sunhollow-council.md).

## Identity

It is not a formal party and does not openly challenge the council's legitimacy. Instead, it survives as memory, resentment, kin-loyalty, and the belief that the valley deliberately used the Moonhare disaster to reduce a people that was too numerous, curious, and difficult to control.

## Background

The old rabbit seat was lost when [Cedric Moonhare](../people/cedric-moonhare.md) vanished on a trading mission and his father, [Edmund Moonhare](../people/edmund-moonhare.md), died before the inner temple burden could be passed on. The remaining council chose not to elevate a replacement and folded the rabbits into [Burrowroot](burrowroot.md) instead.
