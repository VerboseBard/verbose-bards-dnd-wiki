# Thri'Kreen Sentorium

The Thri'Kreen Sentorium is the major Thri'[Kreen](kreen.md) sector power in the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md).

## Scale

The Sentorium fields the largest fleet of fighters, skiffs, and cruisers in the realm, but the notes describe its people as individually weaker and more magically vulnerable than the great powers around them.

That weakness is offset by reproduction and numbers. Astral conditions allow far more hatchlings to survive than they would in other realms, producing explosive population growth.

## Pod Warfare

Young [Kreen](kreen.md) are rapidly trained into fixed social functions. Large military pods are raised together, drilled together, and linked by antenna-based communication that allows shared emotion, command transfer, and coordinated action.

This pod-link is one of their greatest strengths. Their main weakness is mental vulnerability, which is one reason the Gith'yanki have historically been able to keep them under pressure.
