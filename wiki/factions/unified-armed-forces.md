# Unified Armed Forces

The Unified Armed Forces are the combined military structure operating across the linked worlds after [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md).

## Description

The force emerged from necessity: multiple worlds, multiple fronts, and the existential pressure of [the Kreen War](../concepts/kreen-war.md). It fields regular troops, special operations, teleport logistics, quartermasters, officers, and contracted support.

## Campaign Role

The party begins as soldiers in this military system. Their career takes them from frontline operations to [Ash's Hounds](ashs-hounds.md), then to [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md), and finally into [contractor status](../concepts/contractor-status.md).
