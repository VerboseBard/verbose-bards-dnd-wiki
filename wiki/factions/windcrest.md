# Windcrest

Windcrest is the broad bird clan of [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md), made up of many aerial and canopy-adapted peoples.

## Identity

Windcrest is one of the valley's most information-rich clans. Its members see more trails, hear more rumors, and carry more messages than most other peoples simply because of how they move through the valley.

That makes Windcrest politically valuable even when it is not numerically dominant.

## Members

Windcrest includes:

- Aarakocra, including hawk- and eagle-like folk
- Kenku, including raven- and crow-like folk
- Owlin / owlfolk
- Corvum
- Gallus, including rooster- and chicken-like folk
- Luma and other songbird folk
- Raptor
- Strig / large owlfolk
- Seeta
- Sylph

## Current Leadership

The seated elder of Windcrest is [Sabine Voss](../people/sabine-voss.md), an Owlin elder who knows the hidden temple truth but is less certain than most elders that secrecy alone can still protect the valley.

Its primary valley stronghold is [Windcrest Roost](../places/windcrest-roost.md).

Known race speakers include:

- Tarin Vale for the Aarakocra
- Mara Voss for the Kenku
- [Sabine Voss](../people/sabine-voss.md) for the Owlin
- Corin Dask for the Corvum
- Jonah Pell for the Gallus
- Elia Maren for the Luma
- Varek Thorn for the Raptor
- Iona Rusk for the Strig
- Nalene Sor for the Seeta
- Aurel Caine for the Sylph

## Politics

Windcrest is divided. Some of its speakers are isolationist, while others lean toward [the Open Trail](open-trail.md). The clan as a whole is better at gathering information than at acting as a unified bloc.
