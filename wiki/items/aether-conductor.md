# Aether Conductor

The Aether Conductor is a stabilized evolution of the [Arcane Conductor](arcane-conductor.md) that uses a refined Ether Crystal as its power source.

## Description

Instead of requiring spellcasting, the Aether Conductor can be used by non-spellcasters. It is a military-friendly magitech blade line that democratizes magical melee combat.

Each model is a rod that projects a stable arcane blade. The weapon is powered by a socketed aether/ether crystal and can generate a blade without the user casting a spell.

## Shared Rules

### Blade Activation

As a bonus action, the wielder activates or deactivates the blade. Activating the blade immediately consumes 1 charge, regardless of how long the blade remains active.

Each charge sustains the blade for up to 10 minutes, after which the blade collapses.

### Weapon Attacks

While the blade is active, an Aether Conductor counts as a magic melee weapon.

The wielder uses Strength or Dexterity for attack and damage rolls, as normal for a melee weapon. Attacks made with this weapon can benefit from the Attack action, Extra Attack, Sneak Attack, Divine Smite, Fighting Styles, and other features that apply to weapon attacks.

### Aether Crystal Replacement

The wielder can replace the aether crystal during a short rest. Installing a new crystal restores all expended charges.

## Mk I

- Rarity: uncommon.
- Attunement: requires a socketed aether crystal.
- Description: powered by a socketed aether crystal, allowing it to generate a stable arcane blade without spellcasting.
- Pre-imprinted spell: `Fire Bolt`.
- Hit damage: 1d10 fire damage.
- Charges: 4.

## Mk II

- Rarity: rare.
- Attunement: requires a socketed aether crystal.
- Description: refined model with improved crystal stabilization and increased energy throughput.
- Pre-imprinted spell: `Fire Bolt`.
- Hit damage: 2d10 fire damage.
- Charges: 8.

## Mk III

- Rarity: very rare.
- Attunement: requires a socketed aether crystal.
- Description: high-output assault model that channels extreme energy through reinforced crystal housing, trading endurance for devastating power.
- Pre-imprinted spell: `Fire Bolt`.
- Hit damage: 3d10 fire damage.
- Charges: 6.
