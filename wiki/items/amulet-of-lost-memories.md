# Amulet of Lost Memories

The Amulet of Lost Memories is a magical amulet found by [Juan](../people/juan.md) at the bottom of an old dwarven well on the thawing road north of [Frostfire Keep](../places/frostfire-keep.md).

## Properties

The amulet grants visions of past events. These visions can disorient the wearer, potentially causing disadvantage on saving throws or risking exhaustion from reliving memories that are not their own.

In the Part 16 identify scene, [Zen](../people/zen.md) recognizes the amulet as rare pre-Breaking dwarven runecraft using divination and memory-copying effects. It does not require a normal attunement slot. A creature who sleeps with it overnight may seek lost memories from themselves or from previous users, but a failed Constitution save causes exhaustion and prevents the benefits of that long rest.

The amulet also scans and copies at least portions of the memories of those who use it, making it an archive as much as a divination tool.

## Discovery

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 12](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-12.md), [Juan](../people/juan.md) pulls the amulet from the wall of the well. Removing it causes a hidden puzzle socket to appear, revealing additional symbol discs and dwarven riddle text.

## Juan's Vision

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), [Juan](../people/juan.md) uses the amulet while trying to reach a specific lost memory. Instead, he is swept into an old dwarven memory from the [Gateway to the Empire](../places/gateway-to-the-empire.md).

He sees a dwarven mage receive orders to keep knowledge of the escaped infection from returning home. The mage reveals infected black blood and spreads the goo, showing how the outbreak began from the inside.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), [Juan](../people/juan.md) uses the amulet again and sees the infected commander open the cliffside door with the three discs. The vision reveals a deeper control chamber containing a massive gray ooze bound by cables and magical chains.

The ooze impales the commander before he can activate the system that would kill all of it. [Juan](../people/juan.md) learns that the party can still reach that control room and that the discs are the way in.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), that path leads the party to the master goo control chamber, where [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) converts the network rather than destroying it.

## Juan's Fey Memories

[Juan](../people/juan.md) later uses the amulet to pursue memories of the [Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md). He sees a past version of himself returning home to guard a forbidden [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) secret, only for his brother to betray the family duty and give the [Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md) access.

[The Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md) uses the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md)'s reality-shaping power to rewrite fate and exchange the brothers' fortunes. In context with [Juan](../people/juan.md)'s earlier soul-dreams, the vision implies that the [Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md) can move through or alongside a person's dreams, memories, and fate-line rather than acting only from a distance.

That makes the amulet one of the clearest late-campaign sources for the idea that the [Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md) can manipulate personal destiny itself.
