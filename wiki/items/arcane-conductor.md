# Arcane Conductor

The Arcane Conductor is a spell-powered melee weapon that converts an imprinted cantrip into a sustained magical blade.

## Description

The weapon appears as a compact hilt engraved with arcane sigils. When activated, it projects a blade of condensed spell energy shaped by the cantrip infused into it.

- Item type: rod.
- Rarity: rare.
- Attunement: requires the wielder to be able to cast a qualifying cantrip.

## Cantrip Imprinting

As a bonus action, the wielder casts one qualifying cantrip into the Arcane Conductor. This causes a magical blade to manifest for 1 minute.

The blade dissipates early if the wielder dismisses it without an action or imprints another cantrip. Only one cantrip may be imprinted at a time.

## Key Traits

- Counts as a melee weapon for all rules purposes.
- Requires a cantrip imprint.
- Uses the wielder's spellcasting ability modifier for attack and damage rolls.
- Compatible with Extra Attack.
- Damage scales naturally with cantrip progression.
- Requires periodic re-imprinting.

## Approved Cantrips

Only cantrips that require a spell attack roll and deal damage to a single target may be imprinted. The approved cantrips are:

- Fire Bolt
- Ray of Frost
- Chill Touch
- Shocking Grasp
- Produce Flame

## Arcane Strike

While the blade is active, the Arcane Conductor counts as a melee weapon for all rules purposes.

When the wielder makes an attack with this weapon, they make a melee spell attack instead of a Strength or Dexterity attack roll. This attack is part of the Attack action, not the Cast a Spell action.

If the wielder has the Extra Attack feature, they may make all attacks granted by that feature with the Arcane Conductor.

## Damage

On a hit, the target takes damage equal to the imprinted cantrip's current damage dice, and any additional effects of that cantrip apply as normal.

The Arcane Conductor counts as a magic weapon.
