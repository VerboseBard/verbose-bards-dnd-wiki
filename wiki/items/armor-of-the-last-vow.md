# Armor of the Last Vow

The Armor of the Last Vow is damaged lithium holy relic armor and the final known piece of the Last Mercy relic set. It is currently too damaged to be worn safely.

## Statistics

- Item type: armor, holy relic.
- Material: lithium, a lost sacred metal.
- Armor class bonus: unknown.
- Attunement: unknown.
- Upgrade slots: none.
- Properties: unknown until restored.
- Weight: unknown.

## Description

The armor is the final relic of an ancient three-piece clerical set, forged from lithium by an order whose name is lost to time. It is inscribed with sacred sigils of vow and protection, but it has suffered catastrophic damage in some long-forgotten battle or catastrophe.

The armor is far too damaged to be worn safely. The sacred systems within are fractured and inaccessible until extensive specialized repairs are completed.

## Damage Report

- Ruptured plating: multiple sections are torn open or completely missing.
- Deep punctures: numerous piercings, some deep enough to reach the core.
- Fractured structure: load-bearing segments are broken or bent beyond safe use.
- Broken bindings: straps, clasps, and sacred rivets are torn, missing, or degraded.
- Dormant systems: sacred circuits and runes show no response, and power is sealed off.

## Unknown Features

The armor has at least three unreadable or inaccessible features. They cannot be determined until repairs are completed and the power source is restored.

## Power Source

The armor contains a dormant divine spark. The sacred core that once powered it remains sealed and obstructed by damage.

## Repair Requirement

The armor cannot be repaired by ordinary means. Restoration requires lithium-working knowledge, a divine forge, or a sacred repair ritual performed by a sufficiently powerful cleric or artificer.
