# Bartholomew's Hearth Medallion

Bartholomew's Hearth Medallion is the Fourth-Age medallion worn by [Bartholomew Hildebrant](../people/bartholomew-hildebrant.md) when he is recovered from stasis in [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md) during [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md).

## Description

The medallion is identical or closely matching to [Sgt. Jefferson Stone](../people/sergeant-jefferson-stone.md)'s [Hestia's Hearth Pendant](hestias-hearth-pendant.md).

When [the Ssar'Velyn Temple Guardian](../people/ssar-velyn-temple-guardian.md) sees Bartholomew's medallion and Jefferson's amulet together, she calls the match interesting before teleporting the party outside.

## Significance

The medallion suggests that Bartholomew's Fourth-Age assault team and Jefferson's Session 6 altar blessing may both connect to [Hestia](../people/hestia.md), the killed or fragmented goddess of hearth, home, and refuge.

In [Session 9](../sessions/session-9.md), the medallion's significance becomes more than visual. When Hestia's name is spoken, Bartholomew's soul-pain fades, and the hearth presence near his medallion and Jefferson's pendant feels like a campfire in the void. Bartholomew later prays and receives warmth, comfort, and physical strengthening.

Its exact item mechanics, if any, are still not fully written.
