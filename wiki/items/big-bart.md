# Big Bart

Big Bart, also called Big B in the gambling room, is a sentient weapon or weapon-spirit won during the high-stakes game at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md).

## Manor Game

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), Big Bart is wagered at the table as an unusual and opinionated prize. He is not a passive object and speaks with a rough personality.

## Intervention

During [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), [Juan](../people/juan.md) attempts to kill [June](../people/june.md) while still compromised by the Deceiver's influence.

After the failed attack, Big Bart takes control long enough to shut [Juan](../people/juan.md) down. He claims that [Juan](../people/juan.md) has a weak mind, says he has handled the situation, and warns the others that more trouble is unfolding across the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).

Afterward, Big Bart chooses not to remain with [Juan](../people/juan.md). By [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), he has been left with the cook back at the inn, preferring better company and refusing to have his reputation tied to a mind-controlled stabbing attempt.
