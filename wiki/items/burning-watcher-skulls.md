# Burning Watcher Skulls

Burning Watcher Skulls are fiery skull boons gifted by the guardian beyond the Hell transit detour.

## Origin

During [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 6](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-6.md), the party frees a guardian who had petrified himself to seal a dangerous radiant artifact away from devils.

After the party offers him food, reading material, and kindness, he grants burning skulls with the phrase, "May the watcher guide you."

## Combat Use

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 7](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-7.md), [Sorrel](../people/sorrel.md)'s skull transfers to [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) when [Sorrel](../people/sorrel.md) is pulled away by [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s fey summons. The skulls contribute attacks during the battle with Morgrath, and one detonates inside a demon for heavy damage.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 8](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-8.md), another skull detonates inside Lyris's illusion-linked body, breaking her concentration and exposing the real threat.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), the lingering watcher-boon tied to [Denny](../people/denny.md) helps force [Vylra](../people/vylra.md)'s sending-stone warning through the warded manor room at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md). The effort appears to cost the visible fire or vitality of that boon, briefly leaving [Denny](../people/denny.md) as a skeletal ferret.
