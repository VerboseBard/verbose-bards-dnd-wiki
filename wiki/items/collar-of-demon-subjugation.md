# Collar of Demon Subjugation

The Collar of Demon Subjugation is a control collar used to restrain demonic power and aggression.

## Function

The collar limits a demon's powers and prevents hostile action against others. It also has a controller or remote held by the person responsible for the restrained creature.

## Elisia

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), the collar becomes the compromise that saves [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) from immediate execution by [Cardinal Preceptor Thornvale](../people/cardinal-preceptor-thornvale.md).

The party accepts responsibility for [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) until the Conclave can decide her status. [Zen](../people/zen.md) receives the controller, making the collar an important practical and moral pressure point in the following downtime.
