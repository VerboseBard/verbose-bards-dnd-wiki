# Contractor Medallions

Contractor medallions are credentials marking trusted civilian contractors authorized to accept special military work.

## Description

The medallion bears military symbolism, including a shield and crossed weapons on one side and the armed forces emblem on the other. In the session material, the front is described as a shield crossed by a blade and axe, while the reverse carries the unified armed forces symbol built around a [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) head pierced by a blade.

It functions as more than a souvenir. It is documentary proof that the bearer has been cleared, discharged honorably enough to be trusted with sensitive work, and can be called back into dangerous service without being ordinary enlisted personnel.

## Campaign Role

[Colonel Core](../people/colonel-core.md) offers the party contractor medallions after their honorable discharge at [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md). The offer comes immediately after the [Inquisition](../factions/inquisition.md) purges the worst of the fort's corrupt command structure, so the medallions also mark a transition in legitimacy: the party is no longer surviving under rotten officers, but is instead being recognized by the new lawful chain of command.

The medallions open doors to future off-book or difficult military contracts, especially work where the armed forces want deniable specialists rather than uniformed soldiers. In practical terms they represent trust, access, and a path for the party to keep influencing major events without being trapped in barracks life.

By [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), that role is already paying off. The party is operating under these credentials while scouting the route to [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md), keeping military authority without going back under standard enlistment.
