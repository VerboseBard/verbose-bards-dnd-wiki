# Crown of Fools

The Crown of Fools is a [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) curse placed on [Paul](../people/paul.md) after he destroyed the mushroom circle linking the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) to the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md).

## Appearance

The crown appears as living mushrooms growing around [Paul](../people/paul.md)'s head or horns. When active, it shines with [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) light.

## Curse

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 15](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-15.md), three aspects of [Belle](../people/belle.md) mark [Paul](../people/paul.md) for breaking a sacred connection between the grove and the Spring Court.

The curse originally functions like a permanent faerie fire effect: [Paul](../people/paul.md) glows, cannot hide or benefit from invisibility normally, and becomes easier to target.

[Julara](../people/julara.md) later helps [Paul](../people/paul.md) suppress the Constance glow through a blood-circle ritual and potion. Afterward, the crown still exists and flares when [Paul](../people/paul.md) rages.

## Unresolved Debt

The crown is not merely a spell. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md)'s attempt to dispel it only destroys one mushroom before another regrows. The [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) demand amends for the broken bond, but the exact repair is still unknown.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), [Julara](../people/julara.md) first creates a cold-iron amulet that suppresses the faerie-fire effect but cannot remove the crown itself. She explains that the Keeper of the Gates' curse stole 64 years from [Paul](../people/paul.md).

Later in the same session, [Finnian / the Green Man](../people/finnian-green-man.md) of the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md) removes the crown and restores [Paul](../people/paul.md)'s stolen years after agreeing to replace the broken Spring Court link to [Viscus](../people/viscus.md).
