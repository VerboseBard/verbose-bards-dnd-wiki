# Dark Drow Archer's Bow

The Dark Drow Archer's Bow is a dark elf relic and the first known piece of the [Dark Drow Archer Set](dark-drow-archer-set.md).

Forged in shadowed forges of the Underdark, this bow was crafted for the silent hunters of the drow. Each shot is a whisper of death, and every kill feeds the darkness within.

## Statistics

- Weapon type: longbow.
- Material: drowforged darksteel.
- Properties: finesse, heavy, two-handed.
- Attack stat: Dexterity.
- Damage: 1d8 piercing.
- Damage type: piercing / necrotic.
- Range: 150 feet / 600 feet.
- Ammunition: arrows.
- Attunement: required.
- Rate of strike: 1 shot.
- Upgrade slots: 1.
- Set piece: 1 of 3.

## Shadow Shot

Once per turn, when the wielder hits a creature with an arrow, the attack deals an additional 1d6 necrotic damage and the target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be marked by shadow.

## Drow Mark

A marked creature takes an additional 10 percent damage from all attacks. The mark lasts until the end of the wielder's next turn or until the target is reduced to 0 hit points.

## Umbral Veil

The wielder has advantage on Stealth checks made to remain unseen while in dim light or darkness.

## Life of the Hunt

When the wielder reduces a creature to 0 hit points with this bow, they regain hit points equal to their Dexterity modifier, minimum 1.

## Drowforged Detail

Veins of crimson run through the obsidian limbs of the bow. The metal drinks in the light, yet hungers for the life of its foes.

## Origin Note

The bow was recovered from the armory of a fallen drow house. It was found beside a quiver of black arrows whose fletching never wears and whose heads drink deep of the living.

## Power Source

Shadow resonance binds the bow to the darkness of the Underdark. It grows stronger in dim light and weakens in bright sunlight.

## Lore Whisper

> "In the light, we are unseen. In the dark, we are eternal. We do not hunt for glory - we hunt so the darkness may thrive."
