# Demonic Ichor Vial

The Demonic Ichor Vial is the treated glass oil vial [Sgt. Jefferson Stone](../people/sergeant-jefferson-stone.md) uses to collect a small sample from the vat system inside [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md) during [Session 9](../sessions/session-9.md).

## Source

Jefferson finds the ichor in a massive vat chamber while exploring hidden temple spaces after being separated from the rest of the party. The liquid is thick, purple, blood-like, and magically dangerous.

The sample matters because [Mistress Selyra Vex'ryn](../people/mistress-sila-vexry.md) later claims that the immediate corruption source lies outside the temple in a cave south along the valley wall. The vial may become evidence for comparing the temple vat system, the [Dead Lands](../places/dead-lands.md), and any demonic source the party finds later.

## Properties

The liquid burns through leather and gives off a sulfurous, branding-iron-like smell. It can be held in treated alchemical glass.

No final mechanics have been established yet. Until studied by a qualified alchemist, artificer, priest, or arcane specialist, the vial should be treated as dangerous evidence rather than a normal consumable.
