# Divine Sand

Divine sand is the material once contained inside [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) doll.

## Tracking Risk

The [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) use the doll's divine contents as a tracking point until the party removes or blocks the connection.

## Annabelle's Demand

When [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) awakens the doll into Mini G, she demands the return of the sand, saying something dear was taken from him.
