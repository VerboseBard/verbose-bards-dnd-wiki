# Dwarven Rune-Light Crystals

Dwarven Rune-Light Crystals are ancient magical light fixtures recovered from a dwarven tunnel on the thawing road north of [Frostfire Keep](../places/frostfire-keep.md).

## Description

The crystals are large ruby or amber gemstones that still glow after more than a thousand years. They were used like corridor lights in ancient dwarven infrastructure.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 14](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-14.md), [Zen](../people/zen.md) and [Paul](../people/paul.md) study them and identify three interlocking effects:

- A growth rune that shapes the gemstone to the proper size.
- A collection rune that gathers and stores ambient magic.
- A light rune sustained at the crystal's center.

The enchantment is masterful enough that modern crafters would struggle to reproduce it. The runes account for the gemstone's growth and distortion as part of the design.

## Potential Use

[Paul](../people/paul.md) realizes the light rune might be altered toward radiant output, which could make the crystals useful against the [Demonic Dwarven Goo](../concepts/demonic-dwarven-goo.md).

The danger is extreme. Because the crystals have gathered ambient power for centuries, a carving mistake could release catastrophic energy.
