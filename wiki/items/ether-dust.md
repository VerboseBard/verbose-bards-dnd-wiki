# Ether Dust

Ether Dust is the powdered remnant of [Ether Crystals](ether-crystals.md), produced during refinement.

## Description

It appears as a fine, glittering violet powder. When exposed to ambient magic, it may faintly glow or shift in hue. Prolonged exposure to open air weakens the glow but not the potency.

Ether Dust is used in controlled alchemy and spellcraft, but it is also highly dangerous when handled casually.

## Legitimate Uses

- Universal spell component substitution
- Alchemical reagent
- Mana potion refining

## Illicit Use

When ingested directly or mixed into food, drink, or other consumables, Ether Dust acts as a powerful magical stimulant.

For a short time it grants heightened spellcasting, but the aftereffects can burn out magical pathways and create strong addiction.
