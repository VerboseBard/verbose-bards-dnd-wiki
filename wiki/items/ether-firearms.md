# Ether Firearms

Ether firearms are magically powered ranged weapons used by modern military forces.

## Description

These weapons fire energy rather than mundane bullets and often deal force-like damage. Their existence reflects the setting's blend of industrialized warfare, artificery, and post-[Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) magic.

Later experimental systems include the [Ticker](ticker.md), a crew-served heavy weapon, and the [Aether Conductor](aether-conductor.md) line for magitech melee support.

## Campaign Role

The party uses ether pistols, rifles, shotguns, and related weapons throughout their military service. These weapons are standard enough for elite troops but still depend on military access and supply.

In [Session 5](../sessions/session-5.md), the crashed ship's armory reveals a more advanced modular rifle design that can fold into a wrist-sized form and accept augmentation slots, showing how far shipboard weapon tech can push beyond standard military issue. See [Ship Sniper Rifle](ship-sniper-rifle.md).
