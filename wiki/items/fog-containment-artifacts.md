# Fog-Containment Artifacts

Fog-containment artifacts are rare objects used to push back or restrain [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md)'s fog.

## Description

Settlements and bases survive by using artifacts that hold the fog at bay. These objects are strategically priceless because they determine where people can live, farm, build, and move safely.

## Campaign Role

The economy of [Sin](../places/sin.md) and the corruption at [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md) depend heavily on fog-related artifacts and ruin salvage. Control of these objects means control of territory.
