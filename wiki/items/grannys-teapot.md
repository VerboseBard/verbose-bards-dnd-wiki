# Granny's Teapot

Granny's Teapot is an artificer-made self-warming teapot gifted by [Granny](../people/granny.md).

## Description

The teapot warms itself to the proper serving temperature and produces [Granny](../people/granny.md)'s preferred joint-pain-relieving tea. Those unaccustomed to the brew may need to resist its stronger effects or become pleasantly altered.

## Campaign Role

The gift marks a personal relationship formed through tea, conversation, and mutual respect at [The Singing Sword](../places/singing-sword.md).
