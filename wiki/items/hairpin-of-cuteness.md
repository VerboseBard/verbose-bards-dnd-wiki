# Hairpin of Cuteness

The Hairpin of Cuteness is a golden paperclip-like hairpin given by [Belle](../people/belle.md) to [Eve](../people/eve.md).

## Origin

[Belle](../people/belle.md) gives the hairpin to [Eve](../people/eve.md) in a dream after being irritated by [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md)'s criticism of her parenting. The gift is part blessing, part proof of affection, and part [Belle](../people/belle.md)-style rebuttal.

## Appearance

The hairpin resembles one of the shiny paperclips [Belle](../people/belle.md) once wore as decoration, elevated into a divine-fey accessory.

## Meaning

The hairpin matters because it is not just a cute token. It is one of the clearest signs that [Belle](../people/belle.md) treats [Eve](../people/eve.md) as one of "hers" even while everyone around them is still trying to decide whether [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s version of love can ever be trusted.

That makes the item emblematic of the wider [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) material: even comfort gifts are entangled with divine politics, fractured motherhood, and the question of whether tenderness from a dangerous being is safer than open hostility from an honest enemy.
