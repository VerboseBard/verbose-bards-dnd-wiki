# Hat of Wishful Thinking

The Hat of Wishful Thinking is a shape-changing magical hat bought by [Zen](../people/zen.md) at Dudley's Duds on [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md).

## Properties

The hat can change its style, shape, and apparent design into whatever hat the wearer imagines, though it remains fabric or leather rather than true armor.

It also allows the wearer to use guidance as a reaction instead of an action. The item can hold additional enchantments.

## Enchantments

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 12](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-12.md), [Zen](../people/zen.md) takes the hat to Widow Margaret, who adds fire resistance and poison resistance before the party leaves [Frostfire Keep](../places/frostfire-keep.md).
