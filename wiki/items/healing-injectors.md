# Healing Injectors

Healing injectors are compact shipboard medical tools recovered from the [Kex](../places/kex.md).

## Description

These injectors act like fast emergency treatment rather than full medbay reconstruction. They are portable enough for field use and strong enough to get a badly injured party member back on their feet during crisis travel or combat.

## Campaign Role

In [Session 5](../sessions/session-5.md), [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) opens a compartment containing six healing injectors / medjectors as part of the party's first real alliance with the ship.

Working table effect from the transcript:

- restores the user to full hit points
- may temporarily or effectively raise maximum hit points through accelerated ship/nanite healing
- the max-HP interaction is provisional and should be checked against character-sheet notes before hard use

By [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), those injectors are being used in the field after brutal fights, including the ruins and chimera sequence. They help bridge the gap between standard battlefield healing and the much more invasive [Nanite Pods](nanite-pods.md).
