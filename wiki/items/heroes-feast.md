# Heroes' Feast

Heroes' Feast is a powerful magical meal effect sometimes produced by spellcraft, blessing, or exceptional magical cooking.

## Effect

A Heroes' Feast grants resilience, courage, and vitality. In the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md)' case, the feast increases maximum hit points and protects against poison and fear for the day.

## Chuckling Gecko Breakfast

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 12](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-12.md), the kobolds of the [Chuckling Gecko](../places/chuckling-gecko.md) serve an extravagant breakfast that functions like Heroes' Feast because the local watch is paying the bill after the party helped stabilize the teleport yard.
