# Holy-Water Sprayer and Mortar

The Holy-Water Sprayer and Mortar are experimental anti-demon devices created by [Professor Hazard](../people/professor-hazard.md).

## Sprayer

The sprayer is a backpack-mounted holy-water weapon with metal cylinders, rune work, hoses, and a wand-like nozzle.

It can fire holy water in a line or empty itself into a wider cone. Hazard designed it for melting demons at close to mid range and notes that it can also be sprayed upward for an improvised holy-water shower.

## Mortar

The mortar is a spring-loaded launcher built to fire holy-water flasks over distance and obstacles.

The party takes the devices before entering [Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress](../places/wendells-sixth-layer-fortress.md).
