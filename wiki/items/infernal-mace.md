# Infernal Mace

The Infernal Mace is a large demonic weapon recovered from [Morgrath the Enforcer](../people/morgrath-the-enforcer.md).

## Description

The mace is sized for a large wielder and deals heavy bludgeoning force backed by infernal fire. A strong enough wielder can knock targets away with it, which makes it much more useful in the hands of someone who can enlarge or otherwise fight at giant scale.

During the Hell assault and its aftermath, [Paul](../people/paul.md) is one of the few party members physically suited to make real use of it while enlarged through his giant path.

## Recovery

The surviving session material ties the mace to the fortress-phase battles around [Morgrath the Enforcer](../people/morgrath-the-enforcer.md). In practice, it becomes one of the clearer examples of the party turning Hell's own armory back against infernal forces.
