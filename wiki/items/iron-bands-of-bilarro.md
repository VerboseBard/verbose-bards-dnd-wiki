# Iron Bands of Bilarro

The Iron Bands of Bilarro are a magical restraint item purchased by [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) for nonlethal capture work.

## Discovery

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 12](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-12.md), [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) buys the bands while the party is outfitting for the long northern trip toward [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md). The purchase fits his role well: he is one of the few members who consistently thinks about nonlethal control, mounted positioning, and protecting the rest of the party by stopping movement rather than only by killing faster.

## Hydra Battle

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 15](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-15.md), [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) uses the bands against the ember hydra during the mated hydra fight.

The bands briefly restrain the hydra's body, but the creature breaks free through legendary force. The attempt fails, but it shows how close the party came to capturing one of the hydras instead of killing it and how seriously [p'Op](../people/p-op.md)'s control-oriented battlefield style shaped the fight.
