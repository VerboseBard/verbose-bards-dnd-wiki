# Kreen Control Collars

[Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) control collars are magical restraints or alteration systems created after extended experimentation on captured [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md).

## Function

The collars or associated magic prevent altered [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) from attacking or harming protected people. In [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md), the spoken explanation is that a collared Kreen `can't hurt a human`; [Session 3](../sessions/session-3.md)'s opening recap broadens this to sentient races. The exact protected category remains unresolved.

Affected [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) turn gray, making them visually distinguishable from still-hostile green [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md).

## Ethical Status

The collars make surrender policy possible but remain morally fraught. They are part mercy, part prison, part weaponized behavioral control.

They are also a direct strategic result of [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md) capture work and help enable the dark elf queen's surrender of 100,000 [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) in the Underdark.
