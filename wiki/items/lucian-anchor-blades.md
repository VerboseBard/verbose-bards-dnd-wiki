# Lucian Anchor Blades

The [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) anchor blades are weapons containing pieces of [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s essence or soul.

## Purpose

[Morir](../people/morir.md) explains that [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) placed parts of himself into items as anchors. As long as enough anchors remain, [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s return remains possible. Fully manifested serpent avatars can also create new anchors.

## Old Mine Transfer

[Morir](../people/morir.md) gives two such blades to the party during the meeting at the [Old Silverbend Mines](../places/old-silver-bend-mines.md).

## Consumption

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s ancient blades ask to consume the anchor blades. They devour both, causing [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) to scream twice across the psychic level and weakening his return.

During the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) factory strike, [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s blades also consume [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s anchor blade. This further weakens [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) and empowers [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s blades.
