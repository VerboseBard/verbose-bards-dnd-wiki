# Mace of the Last Mercy

The Mace of the Last Mercy is an unknown-origin holy relic weapon, forged from lithium and once carried by a cleric of a fallen divine power. Mercy, protection, and judgment live within its core.

## Statistics

- Weapon type: martial melee weapon, mace.
- Properties: one-handed.
- Damage: 2d8 radiant / bludgeoning.
- Critical: 20 normally; 19-20 against undead, drow-corrupted creatures, spider-abominations, fiends, and void-touched creatures.
- Activation: passive.
- Attunement: required.
- Upgrade slots: none.
- Material: lithium.
- Set piece: 1 of 3.

## Hearth Smite

A number of times per long rest equal to proficiency bonus, when the wielder hits with this weapon, the strike may flare with old divine power and deal an extra 2d8 radiant damage.

Against undead, drow-corrupted creatures, spider-abominations, fiends, or void-touched creatures, this extra damage becomes 3d8 radiant damage.

## Last Mercy

Once per long rest, when the wielder hits with the mace, the wielder may choose one ally within 30 feet. That ally regains 2d8 hit points plus the wielder's proficiency bonus.

## Lithium Relic

This relic is forged from lithium, a lost sacred metal whose crafting method has vanished from the world. The mace cannot be upgraded by ordinary means; it counts as if it already contains the power of two built-in upgrades.

## Set Note

The mace is part of a three-item cleric relic set. The matching items are a holy shield and sacred armor. When united, the relics may awaken greater divine power.

## Campaign Note

This relic set is tracked with the [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md) [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md) discoveries. Its exact current carrier and any final live item-card revisions should be checked against the table inventory before play.

## Destruction and Repair

If destroyed, the mace cannot be repaired by common smithing. Restoration requires a divine forge, forgotten lithium-working knowledge, or direct intervention from a compatible divine source.
