# Motes of Possibility

Motes of possibility are dust-like remnants of the [Possibility Core](../concepts/possibility-core.md) from the [Old Silverbend Mines](../places/old-silver-bend-mines.md).

## Uses

The motes can be spent for rerolls, crafting, spell alteration, and other creative magical effects. Their exact behavior depends on the method of use and the power guiding them.

## Known Effects

Contact with the dust causes unpredictable changes. [Eve](../people/eve.md) absorbs most of the remaining pile and gains new power, while Constance, Zuk, and the party preserve smaller quantities for future crafting.
