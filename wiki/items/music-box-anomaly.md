# Music Box Anomaly

The Music Box Anomaly is a lacquered music box found in the back room of [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md)'s quartermaster storage.

## Description

The box contains two masked dancing figures. When opened, it plays music and casts shadows that appear to reveal glimpses of another place. With enough willpower, a viewer may sense that they could reach through into the vision.

By [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), the box is no longer just a suggestive portal image. It behaves like a dangerous dimensional gate or prison. When opened during a chimera fight, it swallows the creature into the space beyond, resists being shut again, and begins drawing nearby spirits into itself. One party member looking into it perceives an intensely malevolent presence on the far side.

## Campaign Role

The party discovers the box after using a wand of secrets. At first it is treated as a strange sleep aid or curiosity, though its connection to portals, shadow, or [the Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md) remains unresolved.

After the Session 6 chimera incident, that caution changes. The box becomes a sealed liability as much as a tool. It is useful enough to save lives, but dangerous enough that the party explicitly does not want it opened casually again until they understand what realm or intelligence it connects to.

The transcript does not identify the destination. The safest canon statement is that the box opens onto a black hostile space containing or watched over by a powerful malevolent presence. It can imprison or redirect creatures and spirits, but using it risks drawing attention from whatever waits beyond.
