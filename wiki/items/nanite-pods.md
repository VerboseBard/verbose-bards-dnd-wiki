# Nanite Pods

Nanite pods are advanced enhancement chambers found aboard [the crashed ship](../places/crashed-ship.md).

## Description

The pods inject metallic, dust-like microscopic machines into a subject. The process can alter physical or mental attributes, heal wounds, and produce other biological changes.

The pods are part medbay, part enhancement chamber. Ceiling arms can seize and inject a target automatically, and the system appears designed to accept specific nanite cylinders rather than casual experimentation.

## Campaign Effects

One party member is forcibly grabbed by a ceiling arm and injected, gaining major physical enhancement and self-healing nanites. Other party members intentionally use the pods afterward, gaining major attribute increases.

The raw Session 4 transcript already contains the first nanite-pod enhancement sequence. The nanites do not simply heal wounds; they rework bodies enough to justify major stat changes, temporary recovery, and the ability to keep fighting through the rest of the ship.

By [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), the effects are still shaping play. Characters are adapting to the changes in their bodies, and the ship's technology has clearly altered what the party can survive on the road.

## Risks

The process is poorly understood, dangerous, and partly interrupted. Wrong inputs, mixed cylinders, or broken machinery could have produced very different results. The session material strongly suggests that more than a limited number of cylinders at once would have been unsafe.
