# Plate Carriers

Plate carriers are military armor supplements worn over or under other armor.

## Description

Early versions resemble vests added over existing armor, while improved versions include soft armor or under-jacket designs. They can provide significant AC bonuses independent of the wearer's primary armor.

## Campaign Role

The party receives or acquires military plate carriers during service. In [Session 3](../sessions/session-3.md), one character chooses an upgraded plate carrier as a retained special-forces item.

Working mechanics remain unresolved. The transcript discusses an earlier +2 AC carrier and an improved carrier that may be +4 AC total or +2 over the prior model. Older carrier notes may also include crit protection, but the final property package is not locked.
