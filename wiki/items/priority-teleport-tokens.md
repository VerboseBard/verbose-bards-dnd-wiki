# Priority Teleport Tokens

Priority teleport tokens are military-issued passes that allow the holder to skip normal teleportation queues.

## Description

Each token is worth roughly the standard price of a major teleport fare, but its true value is priority. In urgent situations, people may pay far more than face value for immediate access.

The After the War material treats them as military-access passes rather than simple fare vouchers. They are valuable because they cut lines, override delays, and let the holder leave trouble quickly when the normal system would make them wait.

## Campaign Role

The party negotiates eight tokens as payment for the [Lost Homeland Mission](../concepts/lost-homeland-mission.md), dividing them two per member.

That payment matters because the party expects to lose the ordinary protection of uniform and rank the moment they leave the army. The tokens represent emergency mobility at exactly the moment the campaign turns from barracks politics into contractor risk.
