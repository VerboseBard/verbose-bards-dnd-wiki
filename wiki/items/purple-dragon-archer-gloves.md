# Purple Dragon Archer Gloves

The Purple Dragon Archer Gloves are dark elf archer relics and the third known piece of the [Dark Drow Archer Set](dark-drow-archer-set.md).

Crafted from purple dragon leather and stitched with pale spider silk, these ancient archer gloves were made for a dark elf hunter of terrifying precision. Their touch hides treasures, their weave deflects harm, and their magic binds them to the bow and quiver.

## Statistics

- Item type: wondrous item.
- Material: purple dragon leather and spider silk.
- Properties: elbow-length, attuned relic.
- Attunement: required.
- Defense bonus: +4 AC.
- Storage type: dimensional stomach.
- Set role: archer set piece.
- Upgrade slots: none.
- Set piece: 3 of 3.

## Dragonhide Guard

While attuned and wearing these gloves, the wielder gains a +4 bonus to AC.

## Dimensional Stomach

By touching an unattended object, the wielder may store it in an extradimensional space contained within the gloves.

As an action, the wielder may retrieve a stored item into their hand or place it in an open space within 5 feet.

## Web-Hunt Bond

These gloves resonate with the [Dark Drow Archer's Bow](dark-drow-archers-bow.md) and [Webbed Quiver](webbed-quiver.md), stabilizing the set's magic and helping unlock its full potential.

## Spider-Silk Precision

The pale silk seams are impossibly strong and fine, granting perfect flexibility without sacrificing protection.

## Material Detail

Dark violet leather with faint iridescence, supple despite its age. Pale spider-silk stitching runs like web lines along the fingers and forearms.

## Origin Note

The gloves were recovered alongside the Dark Drow Archer relics within the ancient drow temple, hidden in a vault woven with shadow and patience.

## Power Source

Web Hunt resonance carries a lingering drow enchantment linked to bow, quiver, and shadow-hunt techniques.
