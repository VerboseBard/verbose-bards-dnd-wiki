# Ring of Lilith's Favor

The Ring of Lilith's Favor is the renewed bond between [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) and [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md).

## Creation

After [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) severs the old Raven Queen influence from [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md), [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) can no longer feel him and believes he has died. When [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) proves he is alive, [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) manifests a new ring through sheer will and asks for him back.

## Effect

[Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) accepts the ring freely. The bond burns away his vampiric nature and transforms him into an eight-foot black-winged aasimar: the champion of [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s Raven Queen mantle.

Through the ring, [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) is tied to [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s continued existence. As long as she endures, death has much less claim on him.
