# Shield of the Lost Hearth

The Shield of the Lost Hearth is an unknown-origin holy relic and the second known piece of the Lost Hearth / Last Mercy relic set. It is a battle-worn shield forged from lithium and still warm with sleeping divinity.

## Statistics

- Item type: heavy shield.
- Properties: +4 AC, requires attunement.
- Material: lithium.
- Activation: reaction.
- Recharge: proficiency bonus per long rest.
- Power source: dormant hearth divinity.
- Set piece: 2 of 3.

## Hearthwarden Aegis

While wielding this shield, the bearer gains +4 AC. The lithium core reinforces both body and spirit, turning aside blows with old divine force.

## Reflected Judgment

A number of times per long rest equal to proficiency bonus, when the bearer takes damage from a physical attack, spell, or magical effect, the bearer may use a reaction to reflect that damage back to the attacker.

## Lost Divinity

The shield holds dormant power from a god no longer present in the world. Full attunement may awaken deeper abilities not yet understood.

## Set Resonance

The shield resonates with the [Mace of the Last Mercy](mace-of-the-last-mercy.md) and the damaged [Armor of the Last Vow](armor-of-the-last-vow.md), awakening greater power when the set is restored.
