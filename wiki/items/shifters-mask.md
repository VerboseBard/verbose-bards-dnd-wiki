# Shifter's Mask

The Shifter's Mask is an ancient [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) artifact gifted to [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) by [Nicole](../people/nicole.md).

## Origin

[Nicole](../people/nicole.md) recovers the mask from a dangerous temple expedition in [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) with help from [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s uncle. The temple contains forgotten language and iconography tied to [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s people.

## Nature

The mask is made from material similar to [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s own changeling or shifter substance. [Kestrel's Ancient Blades](kestrels-ancient-blades.md) recognize it from an old temple where it was on display before the blades themselves were placed there.

The mask carries a dormant mind or personality that may be awakened later.

## Effect

When worn, the mask adapts to [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) and can fade into his skin, supporting his shifting nature rather than replacing it. As [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) grows stronger, it may allow more extreme object and divided-form transformations.
