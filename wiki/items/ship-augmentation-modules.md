# Ship Augmentation Modules

Ship Augmentation Modules are technological upgrade components recovered aboard the [Kex](../places/kex.md).

## Description

They are physical technological components that fit into augmentation slots on advanced ship weapons and equipment. They are not ordinary enchantments and cannot be replaced by simple spellcasting.

## Session 5

After the hostile combat AI is defeated, [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) identifies four available modules in large avatar/droid bodies:

- one in a bridge-area large unit
- one in a damaged lower unit
- two in the hostile brother/avatar area

Kubix also provides schematics for adapting ship weapons to local magical power sources, including ether crystals, and for installing augmentation modules.

## Session 6 Allocation

Session 6 clarifies the first allocation of those four modules:

- one is installed in [Jose](../people/jose.md)'s [Arcstrike War Gauntlets](ship-forged-gauntlets.md)
- one is installed in [Sgt. Jefferson Stone](../people/sergeant-jefferson-stone.md)'s drone
- one is installed in Jefferson's [Plasma Greatblade](two-handed-plasma-blade.md), unlocking its first critical-range upgrade
- one is held back rather than spent immediately

Installing a module requires specialized tools and either Kubix, one of the ship's workers, or an artificer-level process. Installation takes roughly half an hour, and once installed the module is not freely removable.

The party also chooses not to slow the [Kex](../places/kex.md)'s repairs just to manufacture more modules. That keeps the hidden ship's repair clock more important than short-term loot optimization.
