# Ship Sniper Rifle

The Ship Sniper Rifle is an advanced modular firearm recovered from elite defenders aboard the [Kex](../places/kex.md).

## Working Properties

The Session 5 transcript frames the weapon as:

- an advanced rifle, larger than standard military firearms
- capable of folding or transforming by mental command into a smaller pistol or gauntlet-like configuration
- built with four augmentation slots
- difficult to use without about Dexterity 15 and Intelligence 15
- able to incorporate both Dexterity and Intelligence in its operation

The exact item card remains provisional.

## Campaign Role

The rifle shows that Kex weapon design is modular, intelligent, and more technically demanding than standard [Ether Firearms](ether-firearms.md). It is also one of the clearest reasons [Kubix](../people/kubix.md)'s schematics matter: without translation and crystal-conversion guidance, the party can hold the weapon without fully understanding it.

## Session 6 Limitation

In Session 6, [Alistair Hooley](../people/alistair-hooley.md) is still one Intelligence point short of full attunement.

Until that threshold is met, the rifle is limited:

- it remains stuck in rifle form
- it uses only base damage
- it does not gain the advanced critical effect
- Alistair cannot add proficiency to attacks or damage in the normal calculation

This makes the rifle a future unlock rather than a fully online weapon at the moment the party leaves the [Kex](../places/kex.md).
