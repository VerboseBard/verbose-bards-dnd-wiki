# Shroud of Invisibility

The Shroud of Invisibility is a dark elf assassin relic recovered in [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md). It is a standalone relic rather than part of a set.

## Statistics

- Item type: wondrous item, cloak / shroud.
- Material: spider silk, shadow-thread, and dark drow steel clasps.
- Properties: hooded, lightweight, shadowbound.
- Activation: bonus action.
- Duration: up to 1 minute.
- Uses: proficiency bonus per long rest.
- Attunement: required.
- Upgrade slots: none.

## Veil of Unseen Steps

As a bonus action, the wearer becomes invisible for up to 1 minute. The effect ends early if the wearer attacks, casts a spell, or dismisses it.

## Shadowwoven Silence

While the shroud is active, the wearer gains advantage on Stealth checks and their movement is unnaturally quiet.

## Predator's Ambush

The first melee attack the wearer makes before the invisibility ends has advantage.

## Ghost-Cling Weave

The shroud bends light around the wearer, clinging like smoke and making the body difficult to track.

## Origin Note

The shroud was recovered from an ancient cloaked dark elf assassin in the temple stasis chamber. It was found alongside the [Twin Bloodsteel Daggers](twin-bloodsteel-daggers.md) and [Widow's Bracer](widows-bracer.md).
