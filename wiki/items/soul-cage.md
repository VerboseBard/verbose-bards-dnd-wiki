# Soul Cage

Soul Cage is the spell and containment method [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) uses to trap the souls of slain enemies for questioning or tactical use.

## Query Soul

A trapped soul can be asked questions and must answer truthfully to the best of what it knew in life. The answers may still be incomplete, cryptic, or broken if the soul itself is damaged.

## Cave Interrogations

After the [Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout](../places/silver-bend-coastal-cave-hideout.md) battle, [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) questions the souls of [Deacon Matthews](../people/deacon-matthews.md) and [Bishop Collins](../people/bishop-collins.md). The Deacon provides fractured but useful information about the [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md). The Bishop's soul becomes the vessel for [Lucius's Splintered Echo](../concepts/lucius-splintered-echo.md).

During the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) factory strike, [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) captures [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s soul after [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) drops him and the Anti-Magic Field is released.
