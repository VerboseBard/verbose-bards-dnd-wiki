# Tainted Soul Coin

The tainted soul coin is the soul coin [Eve](../people/eve.md) uses to imprison the [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)-tainted fragment she accidentally consumes from the [Mother Superior of Rax'al Karath](../people/mother-superior-of-raxal-karath.md).

## Creation

After [Eve](../people/eve.md) realizes something is wrong inside her soul, she offers the taint the power of a soul coin, then forces it into the coin rather than allowing it to use the coin to form a new body.

## Significance

The coin becomes a prison for the fragment and proves that [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) soul fragments can be contained if identified quickly and handled with sufficient will.
