# The Ticker

**Official designation:** Mark IV Chrono-Repeater

The Ticker is a brutal, clockwork heavy firearm whose report can be magically suppressed so that the battlefield hears only ticking and feed clacks.

## Description

The gun is a crew-served magitech heavy weapon with a rotating three-rod heat cradle around the barrel. As it fires, the mechanism advances like a metronome.

When overheated, rods drop from the assembly and the weapon must be serviced before it can keep firing.

## Core Rules

- Heavy firearm
- Requires heavy firearms proficiency to use well
- Can be mounted or fired unmounted by a very strong operator
- Fires in three-round bursts
- Supports aim, jam clearing, rod replacement, and crew-assisted reloads

## Heat Rod System

The Ticker uses heat rods as thermal sinks and firing stabilizers. Rods can be replaced, cooled, or repurposed later.
