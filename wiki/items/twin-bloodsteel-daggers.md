# Twin Bloodsteel Daggers

The Twin Bloodsteel Daggers are dark elf assassin relics recovered from an ancient cloaked assassin in [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md). They are standalone relics rather than part of a set.

## Statistics

- Weapon type: paired daggers.
- Material: blood steel.
- Properties: light, finesse, thrown.
- Attack stat: Dexterity.
- Damage: 1d6 + Dexterity modifier each.
- Damage type: piercing / necrotic.
- Range: 20 feet / 60 feet.
- Attunement: required.
- Rate of strike: twin blades.
- Upgrade slots: none.

## Life-Stealing Edge

On every successful attack against a living creature with one of these daggers, the attack deals an additional 1d6 necrotic damage. The wielder regains hit points equal to the necrotic damage dealt.

## Flurry of Blows

A number of times per day equal to proficiency bonus, the wielder may use their action to make four fast attacks with these daggers.

## Paired Predator

When both daggers are drawn, they balance perfectly for relentless close-range assault and flow unnaturally fast from one strike into the next.

## Bloodsteel Hunger

Blood steel drinks light and clings to violence. These blades seem almost eager when blood is spilled.

## Vein-Taker

Against bloodied or surprised targets, these daggers threaten critical strikes on a 19-20.

## Origin Note

The daggers were recovered from the corpse of an ancient cloaked dark elf assassin found in the temple stasis chamber. They were found alongside the [Shroud of Invisibility](shroud-of-invisibility.md) and [Widow's Bracer](widows-bracer.md).
