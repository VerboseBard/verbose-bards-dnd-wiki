# Webbed Quiver

The Webbed Quiver is a dark elf relic and the second known piece of the [Dark Drow Archer Set](dark-drow-archer-set.md).

Woven from the silks of monstrous spiders and the hide of shadowed beasts, this quiver feeds the hunger of the hunt. Within its depths, the web never runs dry.

## Statistics

- Item type: wondrous item / quiver.
- Material: spider silk, spider skin, and purple dragon leather.
- Properties: set piece, arrow generation, specialty ammunition.
- Attack stat: none.
- Damage: +2 arrows generated.
- Damage type: piercing.
- Range: uses bow range.
- Attunement: required.
- Rate of draw: endless draw.
- Upgrade slots: none.
- Set piece: 2 of 3.

## Endless Draw

Whenever the attuned wielder reaches into the quiver, it generates a +2 magical arrow ready to be drawn.

## Specialty Uses

The wielder may choose a specialty arrow a number of times per long rest equal to proficiency bonus.

## Sticky Arrow

On a hit, the target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or become restrained by magical spider silk.

A restrained target may use its action to repeat the save, breaking free on a success. If it fails to break free on its turn, small spiders swarm from the silk and deal 2d8 damage each turn until the target is freed.

## Spider Bite

On a hit, the target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes an additional 2d6 poison damage and is poisoned for 1d4 rounds.

## Void's Call

On a hit, the target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it becomes trapped within its own mind until the end of the wielder's next turn.

While trapped, the target automatically fails saving throws and ability checks. Taking damage does not end the effect. `Lesser Restoration` or similar magic can end it early.

## Webbed Detail

Layered spider hide, stitched in pale silk over purple dragon leather. The quiver feels faintly warm, as if something inside still lives.

## Origin Note

The quiver was recovered alongside a dark drow steel bow from the corpse of an ancient dark elf archer in the temple stasis chamber.

## Power Source

Web resonance spins arrows from lingering silk-magic and predatory drow craft.
