# Admiral Gray

Admiral Gray is a black Giff admiral connected to the [Giff Compendency](../factions/giff-compendency.md) and the [Royal Marines](../factions/royal-marines.md).

## Black Viper Arc

During the final push against [Lucius](lucius.md)'s resurrection, Admiral Gray contacts [Junvara](junvara.md). He says it has come to his people's attention that the being responsible for the death of the God Emperor's favorite son is returning and ascending toward godhood.

Gray offers access to the [Royal Marines](../factions/royal-marines.md) for the operation. [Junvara](junvara.md) accepts their aid and folds them into the allied reinforcement waves moving toward [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md).
