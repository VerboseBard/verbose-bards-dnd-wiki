# Agnes Weller

Agnes Weller is a Hedge speaker and elder civilian voice within [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

## Role

Agnes is maternal, frugal, and relentless. She asks practical questions about food, disease, and children, keeping council talk grounded in survival.
