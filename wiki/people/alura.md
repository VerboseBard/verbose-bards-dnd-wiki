# Alura

Alura is the Love fragment of the rival celestial-demonic sister-being shattered by [Lucius](lucius.md).

## Sister-Fragment Role

Alura is one of the six known fragments that eventually become part of [Sovereignty](sovereignty.md).

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), [Julia](julia-julisia.md), the Conquer / Power fragment, reaches Alura and tries to merge with her. [Elisia](elisia.md), the Justice / Protection fragment, intervenes from within the mindscape to warn Alura that Julia means to consume her.

Love and Justice briefly merge to resist Conquer, but Julia wins dominance over the struggle. The result is not simply Julia alone. It becomes the early Sovereignty state, with Love and Justice still present inside the growing whole.
