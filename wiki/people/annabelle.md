# Annabelle

Annabelle is a living doll-like protector whose power rises in response to threats against children.

## Role

Annabelle appears when [Mave](mave.md) and [Gregario / Shaq](gregario-shaq.md) are guarding [Lilith](lilith.md) in [Junvara's Magnificent Mansion](../places/junvaras-magnificent-mansion.md). She judges the protection insufficient and moves [Lilith](lilith.md) under her own care at [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md).

## Protective Aura

At the dragon school, Annabelle's presence creates a broad protective aura. Its strength depends on the danger facing children: the more children under threat, the more terrifying her defensive power becomes.

## Mini G

Annabelle awakens [Lilith](lilith.md)'s [Gregario](gregario-shaq.md) doll into a small sentient protector called Mini G. She also demands the return of the divine sand removed from the doll, implying that the sand remains personally important to the awakened protector.

## Lucius Warning

After [Lucius's Splintered Echo](../concepts/lucius-splintered-echo.md) manifests during a [Soul Cage](../items/soul-cage.md) interrogation, Annabelle warns the party not to bring that presence back into the school.

## Alen Severed

Annabelle later removes the raven tattoo from [Gregario / Shaq](gregario-shaq.md), severing the remaining command structure tied to [Alen](alen.md), who is also the [Raven Queen of Saharun](raven-queen-of-saharun.md). This confirms [Alen](alen.md) is gone beyond return and that her Raven Queen mantle is shifting toward [Lilith](lilith.md).

## Lucius at the School

When [Lucius](lucius.md) flees to [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md), he tries to exploit Annabelle's protective limits. He swears that he means Annabelle and the children no harm, then demands someone in the school who is no longer a child.

Annabelle refuses to treat that oath as absolution. She snaps the party to the school, creating the opening for [Mave](mave.md) to strike [Lucius](lucius.md) down.

## Concurrence Rescue

During [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 5](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-5.md), Annabelle gathers children, porters, and living stuffed protectors at the [Shippers and Delivery Guild Warehouse](../places/shippers-and-delivery-guild-warehouse.md).

She contracts the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) to help rescue trapped children and protect the evacuation. Her psychic connection to endangered children gives Constance the location needed to open the portal to the [Dwarven Egg Fortress](../places/dwarven-egg-fortress.md).
