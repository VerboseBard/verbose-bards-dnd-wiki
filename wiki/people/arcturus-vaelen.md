# Arcturus Vaelen

Arcturus Vaelen is an elven champion of [Velmora](velmora.md), goddess of death.

## Role

Arcturus is deathbound, solemn, and tied to the Grave domain. His presence fits the pattern of a champion who embodies finality, remembrance, and the careful handling of death.
