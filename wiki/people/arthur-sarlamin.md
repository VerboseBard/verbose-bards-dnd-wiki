# Arthur Sarlamin

Arthur Sarlamin is a [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)-connected businessman and the father of [Nicole Sarlamin](nicole.md).

## Role

He hires the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) to find [Nicole Sarlamin](nicole.md) after she wanders off into the city's performance and tavern circuit.

Arthur presents himself as a man with useful contacts, including ties to the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), the docks, and the banking network.

## Residence

Arthur stays in the [Eastern Tower](../places/eastern-tower.md) while the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) approaches.
