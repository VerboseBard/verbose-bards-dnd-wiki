# Aunt Beitris

Aunt Beitris was a beloved older woman in Constance's village who became an unofficial aunt to many children and a true refuge for Constance and Gadzooks.

## Kindness

Beitris regularly fed Constance, gave her safe nights away from home, and showed her the sort of ordinary affection the Coggeshall house denied her. Before dying of old age, she also passed on her prized cookie recipe to Constance alone.
