# Aurora, Queen Beneath the Waves

Aurora is the merfolk queen tied to [Mulligan](mulligan.md)'s ancient life as the Commodore.

## Relationship with the Commodore

Aurora began as an enemy, captive, or political hostage in the Commodore's war against the merfolk and sea powers. Over years of fighting against the [Opaal Old One](../concepts/opaal-old-one.md), the relationship changed into alliance and love.

She became the Commodore's queen and soul-bonded partner. Their daughter is [Mira of Opaal](mira-of-opaal.md).

## Blood Binding

Aurora and the Commodore share a blood binding. As long as one remains alive, the other can return after death. Their injuries and healing can echo across the bond, allowing wounds and restoration to manifest across both bodies.

This bond is central to the [Commodore Prophecy](../concepts/commodore-prophecy.md) and to the later Coral Palace rebirth material.

## Coral Palace Rebirth

During the Moon Stone Collectors Elsewhere material on [Opaal](../places/opaal.md), Aurora recognizes that the seal against the old one is failing.

In the Coral Palace, she enters a sealed chamber with a pentagram etched into the coral floor. Tentacles rise from the ground, and Aurora uses a blood ritual to reinforce the bond and call the Commodore back.

The scene shows Aurora driving a blade into her own heart, then growing young again as wounds close and power surges through the sea. The exact cost to Aurora is not fully settled in the wiki: session summary language suggests sacrifice, while the scene imagery suggests rejuvenation or transformation rather than simple death.
