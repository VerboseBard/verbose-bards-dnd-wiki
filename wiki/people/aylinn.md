# Aylinn

Aylinn is a godlike but unascended power associated with darkness, retribution, and punishment.

## Role

In the [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md), Aylinn is explicitly described as having the power of a god without fully ascending.

## Campaign Echoes

Aylinn is important in the early [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md), especially around the desert-world and mine ritual material. There, her influence suppresses awareness of other deities until it is broken or diminished.

She is also part of the instability threads running through [Hawk](hawk.md)'s and [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s notes.
