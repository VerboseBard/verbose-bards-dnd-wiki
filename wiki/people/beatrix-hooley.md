# Beatrix Hooley

Beatrix Hooley is the Harengon speaker within [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md) and one of the most important living rabbit voices in [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md).

## Description

Beatrix is a long-eared rabbit woman with silver at the muzzle, well-kept coats, and the posture of someone raised to remain composed while being judged.

## Role

She has spoken for the Harengon for twelve years, but unlike the old [Moonhare](cedric-moonhare.md) line, she does not hold a formal council seat. Rabbit concerns must pass through Burrowroot instead.

In the Burrowroot first-contact material, Beatrix is the current rabbit speaker under Burrowroot: courteous, composed, and emotionally controlled while the return of [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md) threatens to reopen old grief.

## Household

Her mate is [Rupert Hooley](rupert-hooley.md), a Harengon household organizer. They have four children.

## Temperament

Beatrix is courteous, politically alert, and more wounded than she allows herself to appear. She is more neutral than hostile toward outsiders and is one of the clearest voices adjacent to [the Open Trail](../factions/open-trail.md) and [the Sixth Seat](../factions/sixth-seat.md).
