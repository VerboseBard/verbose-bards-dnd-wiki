# Bernard Weller

Bernard Weller is a Hedge root-cellar keeper in [Burrowroot Warren](../places/burrowroot-warren.md).

## Role

Bernard is suspicious and protective of food stores. He is a practical pressure point for questions of scarcity, secrecy, and whether outsiders are a drain or a danger.
