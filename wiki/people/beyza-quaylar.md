# Beyza / Quaylar the Druid

Beyza was [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s mother from [Palewood](../places/palewood.md) on [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md).

## Personas

Beyza was her home persona: a half-elf woman with long dark brown hair, green eyes, and simple clothes.

She was also known in town as Quaylar the Druid, a human woman in her late fifties with grey hair, hazel eyes, and an intense demeanor. Quaylar was one of [Palewood](../places/palewood.md)'s town personas, and Beyza was the third person to use that identity.

## Role in Kestrel's Story

Beyza died when [Kestrel](kestrel.md) was seventeen. After her death, Kestrel left home and spent about two years traveling from town to town, playing music in pubs and inns.
