# Bishop Collins

Bishop Collins was a senior field leader of the [Cobra Cult of Rax'al Karath](../factions/cobra-cult-of-raxal-karath.md) at the [Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout](../places/silver-bend-coastal-cave-hideout.md).

## Cave Battle

[Initiate Thomas](initiate-thomas.md) identifies Bishop Collins as the leader responsible for the cave operation. The larger serpent avatar in the final battle is believed to have been Collins after his full transformation.

## Soul Cage

When [Junara](junara.md) questions Collins's trapped soul, the soul no longer behaves as Collins alone. [Lucian's Splintered Echo](../concepts/lucians-splintered-echo.md) manifests through it, claims control of the Church of Rax'al Karath, answers several questions, and then destroys the remaining soul fragment.
