# Bishop Octavian Ironheart

Bishop Octavian Ironheart is the [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md) church authority who hears the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md)' report after the missing wagon and abducted travelers incident.

## Role

He confirms that the wagon reached the city empty, arranges [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) access for the party, and warns that the correct portal expert is not immediately available.

Ironheart is practical rather than theatrical. He gives the party a travel token, keeps the process moving, and tries to keep the church from losing face as the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) closes in.
