# Bram Rusk

Bram Rusk is a Strig watch commander of [Windcrest](../factions/windcrest.md).

## Role

Bram represents Windcrest during the next-morning escort to [Hollowmere](../places/hollowmere.md) and keeps pressure on the party through watchful official presence.
