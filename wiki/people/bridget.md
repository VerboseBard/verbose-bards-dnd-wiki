# Bridget

Bridget was the goddess of music, art, and beauty in the old pantheon.

## Role

In the surviving [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md) notes, Bridget is described as roughly third in power.

She is distinct from both [Maeve, Queen of the Fae](maeve.md), the older goddess of magic, and [Maeve, the Schoolmistress](mave.md), who later rose into Bridget's place.

## Death and Succession

Bridget was killed by [Lucius](lucius.md).

After her death, [Maeve, the Schoolmistress](mave.md), the half-elven paladin from the [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) campaign material and the school that later launched the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md), rose into Bridget's divine role.
