# Archivist-General Callis Verdan

Archivist-General Callis Verdan is a half-elf champion of [Oghma](oghma.md).

## Role

Callis is a tactical archivist and lore-driven commander whose authority comes from foresight, knowledge, and the ability to make information usable under pressure.
