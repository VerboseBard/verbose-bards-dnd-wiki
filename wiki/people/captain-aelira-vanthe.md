# Captain Aelira Vanthe

Captain Aelira Vanthe is an elf assault captain of the [Bloody Fifth](../factions/bloody-fifth.md) in [Session 1](../sessions/session-1.md).

## Role

She delivers a grounded speech to the soldiers before the trench assault, contrasting with more manipulative grand speeches from higher command.

She is calm, precise, and unflinching. Unlike [General Faelar Grimward](general-old-blood-and-guts.md), she is present in camp, in the rain, and near the killing edge. She leads from just behind or within reach of the front rather than from total safety.

## Status

She leads the first wave, is critically wounded or downed by Kreen fire, and is stabilized in actual play. She is not confirmed dead.
