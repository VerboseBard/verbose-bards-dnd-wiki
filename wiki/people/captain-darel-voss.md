# Captain Darel Voss

Captain Darel Voss oversees the [Adventurer's Guild](../factions/adventurers-guild.md) operations in the [Caldrith's Gate](../places/caldriths-gate.md) region.

## Description

He is Silver 4-Star, charismatic, calculating, and fiercely loyal to guild procedure. He knows he has capped out unless someone above him retires, but he still runs a tight ship.
