# Captain Donner

Captain Donner is a human captain at [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md), around middle age, with a worn but maintained uniform.

## Role

He briefs incoming civilians and soldiers on [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) fog rules, artifact law, and the dangers of unauthorized exploration.

## Key Warnings

- Civilians must not enter the fog.
- Fog artifacts must not be touched, bought, stolen, or moved before clearance.
- Cleared items are sold through [Calvin's Curios](../places/calvins-curios.md) or the quartermaster.
- Soldiers may have to retrieve dangerous things but should not tamper with them.
- Halfling vampires were released or encountered by a patrol that ignored procedure.
