# Captain Hail

Captain Hail is an [Inquisition](../factions/inquisition.md) captain at [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md) during [Session 4](../sessions/session-4.md).

## Role

Hail interviews the party after they declare the murdered fog-team bodies. He is formal and cautious, but not hostile once the party cooperates.

## Key Actions

- Takes charge at the gate after the party declares the bodies.
- Asks whether the bag of holding contains anything explosive or dangerous before ordering it emptied.
- Orders the bodies inspected.
- Interviews the party about their patrol and evidence.
- Is present when [Lord Inquisitor Boss](lord-inquisitor-boss.md) confirms their truthfulness with the [Truth Lantern](../items/truth-lantern.md).
- Thanks the party for their useful report.
