# Captain Havlin

Captain Havlin is a Leonin patrol commander of [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md), likely aligned with [Ashmane](../factions/ashmane.md), and the first official local authority to challenge the party during [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md).

## Description

Havlin is large, scarred, warrior-built, and visibly ready for violence without seeming eager for chaos. His first-contact posture is wary but controlled rather than reckless.

## Role

He leads the patrol that intercepts the party after they emerge from the fog into the valley. Havlin initially treats the group as a security threat, questions whether [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md) vouches for them, and allows the encounter to soften only after the party proves more cautious than hostile.

In [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), Havlin is part of the Sunhollow escort that brings the party from [Hollowmere](../places/hollowmere.md) toward the [Dead Lands](../places/dead-lands.md), stopping where local law and danger prevent the valley folk from continuing deeper.
