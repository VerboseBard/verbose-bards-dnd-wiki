# Captain Rovan Halven

Captain Rovan Halven is a Leonin of [Ashmane](../factions/ashmane.md) and the security lead for the next-morning escort from [Burrowroot Warren](../places/burrowroot-warren.md) to [Hollowmere](../places/hollowmere.md).

## Role

Rovan maintains official control over the party during the escort. His presence keeps the movement formal, watched, and firmly under Ashmane security expectations.
