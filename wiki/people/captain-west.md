# Captain West

Captain West is an officer of the [442nd](../factions/442nd.md).

## Role

Captain West briefs the team after the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) attack the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) and kill two members of the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md). He frames the operation as reconnaissance, counter-cell work, and a rescue mission for stolen souls.
