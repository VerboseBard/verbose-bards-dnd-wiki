# Cardinal Preceptor Thornvale

Cardinal Preceptor Thornvale is a high-ranking servant of the Dawn Father active on the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) after the destruction of [Wendell](wendell.md)'s sixth layer.

## Role

Thornvale appears during the triage aftermath in [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md). When a healer reports [Elisia](elisia.md)'s presence, Thornvale denounces her as an abomination: a demonic creature clothed in celestial light.

He is zealous, charismatic, and dangerous in a crowd. His speech nearly turns the relief and mourning of the triage area into a mob against [Elisia](elisia.md) and the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md).

## Conflict with the Party

[Juan](juan.md) answers Thornvale with a parable about two dogs born from the same blood but shaped by different lives. [Lady Seraphina Blackthorn](lady-seraphina-blackthorn.md) then intervenes, arguing that the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) is not Thornvale's church and that free will matters.

Thornvale accepts a temporary compromise only after [Elisia](elisia.md) is fitted with a [Collar of Demon Subjugation](../items/collar-of-demon-subjugation.md). He states that the party will be responsible if she commits dark deeds before the Conclave decides her fate.

## Guardian

Thornvale is accompanied by [Morus the Unchained](morus-the-unchained.md), a large angelic guardian who warns the party not to threaten the Holy Father.
