# Caroline Chuffnell

Caroline Chuffnell is a Harengon midwife of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

## Role

Caroline is warm and protective. She sees [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md) first as someone's child and only second as a political problem.
