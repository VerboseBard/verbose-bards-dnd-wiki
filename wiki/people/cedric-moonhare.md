# Cedric Moonhare

Cedric Moonhare is the lost rabbit heir whose disappearance more than a century ago helped erase the rabbit folk's independent standing in [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md).

## Role

Cedric was tied to the old Moonhare line that once held a distinct rabbit place in the valley's political order.

He vanished on a trading mission outside the valley. When his father, [Edmund Moonhare](edmund-moonhare.md), died before the inner temple duty could be passed on cleanly, the remaining council chose not to restore the seat and instead absorbed the rabbits into [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

## Legacy

Cedric's disappearance became both a real family tragedy and the excuse used to reduce rabbit authority. In the present era, any sign that the line survived would have major consequences inside the valley.
