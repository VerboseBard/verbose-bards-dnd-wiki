# Celia Velen

Celia Velen is a Capran weaver, household mediator, and civilian liaison of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

## Role

Celia is practical and socially skilled. She explains local customs, prevents misunderstandings where possible, and joins the next-morning escort to [Hollowmere](../places/hollowmere.md).
