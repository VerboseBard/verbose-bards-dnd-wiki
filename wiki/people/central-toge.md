# Central Toge

Central Toge was one of the figures responsible for [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s death during the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md)' attack.

## Captured Soul

[June](june.md) later used a soul cage and ancient ritual to capture Central Toge's soul. This shifted [Lucian](lucian.md)'s resurrection from inevitability toward possibility and conflict.

## Impersonation

[Kestrel](kestrel.md) later impersonates Central Toge at the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md) to draw out [Dark Star](dark-star.md). The deception succeeds well enough that [Dark Star](dark-star.md) reveals the true meeting location at [Hellspire's Manufactory](../places/hellspires-manufactory.md).
