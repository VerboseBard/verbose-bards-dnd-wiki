# Clara Penwyck

Clara Penwyck is a Jerbeen baker and mother of three in [Burrowroot Warren](../places/burrowroot-warren.md).

## Role

Clara is kind, curious, and anxious about the outside world. She can show the civilian stakes of outside contact without holding formal authority.
