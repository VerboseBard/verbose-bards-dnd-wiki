# Colonel Core

Colonel Core is the orc officer who assumes command of [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md) after the [Inquisition](../factions/inquisition.md) purge.

## Role

Core restores formal military discipline while trying to keep the [Inquisition](../factions/inquisition.md) within procedure. He is pragmatic, legalistic, and willing to use the party if they remain useful and clean.

He should stay distinct from [Colonel Gandor](colonel-gandor.md), the earlier orc supervisor of [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md), unless the table later confirms a merger or retcon.

## Key Actions

- Publicly announces the charges against [Colonel Marrow Vance](colonel-marrow-vance.md) and other corrupt officers.
- Takes command after their execution.
- Reviews the party's paperwork and finds their official earnings legal, including nearly 12,000 platinum in documented payouts.
- Asks the party about the real power structure in [Sin](../places/sin.md).
- Limits further Inquisition pressure on the party unless new concerns are raised through proper channels.
- Offers the party [Contractor Status](../concepts/contractor-status.md) after honorable discharge.
- Sponsors the [Lost Homeland Mission](../concepts/lost-homeland-mission.md).
