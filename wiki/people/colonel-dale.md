# Colonel Dale

Colonel Dale is a fit orc officer commanding the professional outpost near the old [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) gate-region.

## Role

Dale represents professional military discipline in contrast to corrupt [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md). His site is fortified, alert, and defended by snipers, tickers, and magical traps.

## Key Events

- In [Session 3](../sessions/session-3.md), informs the party that their orders have been changed for political reasons.
- Explains that controversy around [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md) and [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) experimentation has made them politically inconvenient.
- Offers to spend political capital to keep them only if they re-enlist.
- Warns them to watch their backs.
