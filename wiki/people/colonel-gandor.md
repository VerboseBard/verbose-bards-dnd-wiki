# Colonel Gandor

Colonel Gandor is the orc officer assigned as the direct supervisor of [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md) in [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md).

## Name Note

The transcript drifts through a table nickname or mishearing before settling on `Gandor`. His exact spelling remains provisional. He is introduced as a major and then promoted to colonel when the Hounds are formed.

## Role

Gandor runs operations for the Hounds under [General Ash](general-ash.md). He is the officer the unit reports to after Ash's formation speech and the practical commander attached to their missions and debriefings.

## Distinction

Gandor should be tracked separately from [Lieutenant Rethiel Kaine](lieutenant-rethiel-kaine.md), who remains active as a hardline field lieutenant during the same session, and separately from [Colonel Core](colonel-core.md), the later Fort Victory commander, unless the table later confirms a merger or retcon.
