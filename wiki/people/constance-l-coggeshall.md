# Constance L. Coggeshall and Zuk

Constance L. Coggeshall and Zuk are dragon-bonded partners associated with a new school for children who believe they can bond with dragons.

## Roster Snapshot

- Level 20
- Half-Elf
- Fighter / Gunslinger / Artificer / Artillerist

## Constance's Origin

Constance is from the [Elven Lands](../places/elven-lands.md), not [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md). The Elven Lands are the world where the party later returns to fight the undead invasion and where Constance's dragon-keeper bloodline was rooted before the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md).

Constance was born into the Coggeshall line, the ruling bloodline of the Keepers of the Dragons. She was born under a dark prophecy and with one leg malformed, which caused her father to immediately reject her as a bastard or a divine curse rather than acknowledge her as his daughter.

Her childhood was defined by isolation, neglect, and abuse. She was hidden away as a family disgrace, pushed into a shed at the edge of home life, and treated as something between embarrassment and omen. That rejection shaped both her stubborn independence and her need to build what the world refused to give her.

The hatred around her birth was not only emotional. Without the second prophecy and the dragons' support for her survival, Constance might have been killed as a child or allowed to die soon afterward. The family feared that the hostile prophecy meant she would destroy everything tied to them, but the counter-prophecy and the dragons' recognition kept that fear from becoming a death sentence.

Her bond with Zuk is therefore not just heroic destiny. It is a reversal of everything her family said she was not allowed to become. The child they treated as cursed became one of the defining dragon-bonded figures of the age.

A Seeker found Constance and Zuk before rescuing [Greg](greg-p-smith.md) from the isolated goblins who had captured him on the Elven Lands. This makes Constance and Zuk Greg's first non-goblin contacts after he was pulled into the campaign world.

## Family

Constance's family history is bitter even before the later wars:

- [Cornelius Coggeshall](cornelius-coggeshall.md), lord of the house and head of the dragon keepers, disowned her at birth and refused her public affection or recognition.
- [Genevieve Coggeshall](genevieve-coggeshall.md), her mother, loved her quietly and secretly brought her comfort, gifts, and encouragement in the shed.
- [Lucian Coggeshall](lucian-coggeshall.md), her eldest brother and heir, brutally abused her throughout childhood and spread stories that she was a cursed changeling.
- [Josephine Coggeshall](josephine-coggeshall.md), her older sister, treated her with contempt and later raised her own children to despise Constance's disability.

Constance also survived because a few people in and around the village saw her clearly:

- [Aunt Beitris](aunt-beitris.md) gave her kindness, safety, and a treasured cookie recipe.
- [Old Man Herbert](old-man-herbert.md) recognized her talent for tinkering and taught her how to care for a disabled dragon.
- [Cousin Lucy](cousin-lucy.md) helped soften village opinion and gave Constance real acceptance.
- [Josiah](josiah.md) hero-worshipped her and adored Zuk.

## Prophecy

Two competing versions of the same prophecy followed Constance's childhood.

One frames her as a hated harbinger: a malformed child of dragon blood who will bring betrayal, darkness, and death. In the harshest family reading, she was the child who would destroy everything tied to the Coggeshall line. That version reflects the family's fear and the village's cruelty more than any objective truth.

The other treats her as a scarred but defiant figure whose bond with a likewise scarred dragon may let her break fate instead of submit to it. It is also the prophecy that helped keep her alive. Because the dragons supported her and the second prophecy left open a path other than ruin, the Coggeshall line could not simply execute the feared child and call it duty.

That version better matches the life she actually builds: painful, dangerous, world-shaping, but not reducible to the hatred that named her cursed.

## School Announcement

[Eve](eve.md) finds a pamphlet announcing Constance and Zuk's school while leaving [JJ and Jackson's Teleportation Service](../places/jj-and-jacksons-teleportation-service.md). The school appears to be under construction and built around dragon-bond potential.

## Black Viper Arc

During the Black Viper hunt, Constance and Zuk's school becomes a refuge for thousands of children, dragons, eggs, and bonded souls. [Annabelle](annabelle.md) brings [Lilith](lilith.md) there for stronger protection.

Zuk can read nearby thoughts unless blocked and helps route the party to protected spaces. Constance reacts with overwhelming draconic fury when she understands that the school could become a target for [Lucius](lucius.md)'s return.

Constance also offers to repair [Vis / Jarvice](vis-jarvice.md)'s damaged runes in her workshop.

Constance's lava-fed workshop becomes the site of major upgrades before the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) operation. Zuk also provides food, polymorph rings, mental routing, and an unwise but educational squid breakfast for [Vis](vis-jarvice.md).

At the old mine, Constance and Zuk collect some [Motes of Possibility](../items/motes-of-possibility.md) after the [Possibility Core](../concepts/possibility-core.md) collapses.

During the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) operation, Constance and Zuk negotiate indirectly with [Vince Gambino](vince-gambino.md), then detect [Charlie](charlie.md)'s soul prison inside [Hellspire's Manufactory](../places/hellspires-manufactory.md). They join [Vis / Jarvice](vis-jarvice.md) in a draconic breach from above and help kill Hellspire before he can use his prepared defenses.

After the island fight, Zuk confronts [Gregario / Shaq](gregario-shaq.md) for attacking Constance while under the spider queen's pheromone control. The sparring match becomes a blunt warning that old vows and repressed desires can be dangerous if left unexamined.

During [Lucius's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucius-crystal-trap.md), Constance and Zuk are the first to face the armored fragment. They damage weak points with rifle and pistol fire, survive [Lucius](lucius.md)'s gravity manipulation, and afterward spend hours restoring the crystal's gravity, air, transport, and monitoring systems.

After [Lilith](lilith.md)'s dragon bond destabilizes the school, Constance and Zuk return to help stabilize [Lilith](lilith.md) and her newly hatched dragon, Greggy. [Gwyne](gwyne.md) then introduces them to the [Flight of the Ancients](../factions/flight-of-the-ancients.md).

There, [Tiamat](tiamat.md) reveals that dragons were once guardians of reality and asks Constance and Zuk not to stop [Lucius](lucius.md)'s final return too early if doing so would prevent his permanent destruction. [Tiamat](tiamat.md) brands them with the [Brand of the Flight of the Ancients](../items/brand-of-the-flight-of-the-ancients.md) and names Constance the future Mother of Dragons for this world.

Before the assault on [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), Constance releases [Charlie](charlie.md)'s soul at dawn rather than keep her bound in hope of resurrection. At the [Desert Well Ritual Site](../places/desert-well-ritual-site.md), Constance and Zuk join the first-wave assault, use dragon-form mobility and firearm support, and help breach the locked ritual chamber with Knock.

At the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), Constance and Zuk track recent movement through the swamp, contribute to the stealth approach, and fight from the air after the shield breach. Constance shoots down [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) on the upper levels while Zuk uses dragon-form melee to knock defenders from the structure.

On the second floor, Constance examines the ley-line control machinery and realizes it can dampen or redirect the power flow, but avoids altering it before the active ritual fragment is secured.

When the upstairs ritual turns dangerous, Constance stays at the controls with Zuk. She siphons power, disables the shield, stabilizes flow channels, and turns off the room's attack system, lowering the difficulty of the third fragment extraction.

At the [High Temple of Rexel Kar](../places/high-temple-of-rexel-kar.md), Constance and Zuk enter the lower temple with the party. Zuk is briefly petrified by a serpent gaze before being restored by a Conclave champion.

## Earlier War of the Gods Fallout

In the extracted [War of the Gods, Session 18](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-18.md), Constance and Zuk survive [Wendell](wendell.md)'s direct mental assault only to carry the aftermath onto the rescue ship. Constance is left emotionally wrecked and deeply destabilized, while [Charlie](charlie.md) becomes one of the few people able to ground her after the violation.

The same session also shows older Rose warning Constance not to accept power from gods, demons, or fey no matter the pain, then immediately departing to join her sisters. That warning lands directly on the same fault line already opening around [Belle](belle.md), dragon cores, and the party's splintering loyalties.

In [War of the Gods, Session 20](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-20.md), the fallout escalates into a permanent transformation. Zuk abandons restraint, drags Constance from the doomed ship, and pulls future draconic power backward through their bond rather than risk losing her a second time.

That decision turns their partnership into something far more extreme than ordinary dragon-bonding. Zuk reveals that in another timeline Constance was his future mate, and the emergency act leaves them effectively sharing one soul across two bodies. Their power, survival, and future now overlap in a way that later dragon-scale lore makes much easier to understand.

The same session also returns them to Constance's homeland in the middle of [Wendell](wendell.md)'s assault, reuniting her briefly with Zuk's mother and with her brother [Lucian Coggeshall](lucian-coggeshall.md), whose contempt survives even a siege.

In [War of the Gods, Session 22](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-22.md), the social cost of that return fully lands. Constance is dragged before her father and the allied officers, where [Lucius](lucius.md) publicly uses the hostile version of her birth prophecy to paint her as the family's betrayer. Lucy then reveals that a second prophecy was spoken the same night, one that frames Constance and Zuk not as doomed monsters but as scarred figures who may rise above hate and choose their own fate.

The same session also turns the homeland visit into tragedy. The dragon hatchery is raided, eggs are stolen, children go missing, Constance's mother is killed defending the site, and much of her blood family falls back into open hostility. Yet the session does not leave her there: the fallen dragons and her mother are restored, Zuk's mother stands with her, and the dragons themselves largely accept her even while many humans do not.

In [War of the Gods, Session 23](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-23.md), Constance and Zuk carry that pain straight into the first phase of the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) siege. After receiving [Mave](mave.md)'s blessing and warning, they intercept [Wendell](wendell.md)'s undead dragon before it can reach the school and destroy it with overwhelming force, proving again that when the school or the eggs are threatened they become one of the party's fastest and most decisive response tools.

In [War of the Gods, Session 24](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-24.md), that response continues without pause. After destroying the undead wyvern, Constance and Zuk rejoin the main fight at the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), add heavy ranged and draconic pressure against [Lucius](lucius.md), and help hold the field long enough for the god-killing blade to land. When [Lucius](lucius.md)'s hourglass releases a larger surge than the party can safely absorb, Zuk helps [Gregario / Shaq](gregario-shaq.md) route the overflow back into the crystal. The same aftermath also reveals a hidden safeguard from [Mave](mave.md): Constance's necklace can be used to summon [Belle](belle.md)'s concealed daughters from hiding if the worst ever happens.

## Concurrence and the Dragon Eggs

During the final [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) arc, Constance and Zuk learn that [Greg](greg-p-smith.md)'s rewrite threatens to force dragons and other high-power beings out of mortal reality. Constance chooses to alter that limit during the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), protecting Zuk, their dragon family, and her own future draconic nature.

At the [Shippers and Delivery Guild Warehouse](../places/shippers-and-delivery-guild-warehouse.md), Constance and Zuk use the peak of the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) to open a portal to the [Dwarven Egg Fortress](../places/dwarven-egg-fortress.md). When they see the stolen dragon eggs, both enter dragon form and lead the rescue.
