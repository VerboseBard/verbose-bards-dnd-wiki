# Dark Star

Dark Star is one of the most dangerous surviving [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md), deeply tied to the murders of [Charlie](charlie.md) and [Jimbo](jimbo.md) and to the restoration plan for [Lucian](lucian.md).

## Viper Operator

Dark Star is part of the Viper cell that exploits the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), steals [Lucian](lucian.md)'s old Umbra arsenal from Hell, and then pivots immediately into the forest-side strike on [Jimbo](jimbo.md) and [Charlie](charlie.md).

By the time the party catches up with his name during [The Hunt for Black Vipers](../campaign/hunt-for-black-vipers.md), he is already regarded as one of the core surviving enemies: fanatical, highly mobile, and personally committed to [Lucian](lucian.md)'s return.

## Profile

According to [Morir](morir.md), Dark Star is obsessed with destroying the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) and bringing [Lucian](lucian.md) back at any cost.

He does not operate as a straightforward thug. He uses false meetings, remote projection, timed contacts, and overlapping church and criminal connections. By the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) phase, he is meeting with nobles, cult figures, merchants, and other power brokers on the same night.

## Blade and Anchors

Dark Star carries a blade with demonic essence and an eighth-tier power. [Morir](morir.md) warns that the blade can open portals and that Dark Star is extremely dangerous in one-on-one combat.

That weapon is more than a personal tool. It is one of the [Lucian](lucian.md) anchors that has to be removed if the return plan is going to fail.

## Big Tusk Lead

[Morir](morir.md) reveals that Dark Star is expected at the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md) in [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) two days after the old mine meeting.

At the casino, Dark Star does not simply walk into an ambush. He appears through projection and uses the meeting to test [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s impersonation of [Central Toge](central-toge.md). Once sufficiently convinced, he reveals that the real meeting is at [Hellspire's Manufactory](../places/hellspires-manufactory.md).

## Defeat

During the factory breach, [Junara](junara.md) reaches him with Anti-Magic Field, [Kestrel](kestrel.md) seizes his blade before it can be drawn, and [Gregario / Shaq](gregario-shaq.md) drops him with Stunning Strike and Quivering Palm. [Junara](junara.md) captures his soul afterward.

Dark Star's blade is consumed by [Kestrel's Ancient Blades](../items/kestrels-ancient-blades.md), destroying another [Lucian](lucian.md) anchor.

## Soul Interrogation

Inside the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), Dark Star's soul is questioned about [Winrah Holm](winrah-holm.md), [Jimbo](jimbo.md), and [Lucian](lucian.md)'s resurrection. He reveals that Winrah has gone beyond his control and that [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s soul has been woven into the resurrection lattice but not fully consumed.

The interrogation is a trap. [Lucian](lucian.md)'s features bleed through Dark Star's soul, and the final answer activates [Lucian's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucians-crystal-trap.md). Dark Star's soul is overwritten and spent as [Lucian](lucian.md)'s fragment escapes into the armor stored inside crystal security.
