# Dead Lands Ghost Druid

The Dead Lands Ghost Druid is the corrupted ancient elven spirit who guides the party through the [Dead Lands](../places/dead-lands.md) in [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md).

## Nature

She was once the druid caretaker of the sacred woods around what is now [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md). She tied her life to the forest, and when the forest was corrupted, she was corrupted with it.

Her memory is damaged, her sense of time is unreliable, and she is not fully trustworthy in the ordinary sense. Even so, she gives the party one of the clearest surviving accounts of the valley's older history.

## Lore Given

The ghost explains that the valley was once a meeting place of all elven peoples, united under one god, before [Lolth](lolth.md)'s corruption of the holy ground.

Her account links:

- the Second-Age fey and giants war
- the fey curse that transformed giants into dwarves
- the dwarves breaking the world and bringing in the first dragon
- the first dragon remaining on a mountain for two ages
- Lolth later allying with that dragon and [the Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md)
- Lolth killing a god, stealing divine power, and corrupting the treaty halls
- [Hestia](hestia.md)'s killed or fragmented divine power, recognized through [Sgt. Jefferson Stone](sergeant-jefferson-stone.md)'s amulet

She fades before the final temple gate.
