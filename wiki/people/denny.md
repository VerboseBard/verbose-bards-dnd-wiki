# Denny

Denny is [Vylra](vylra.md)'s undead companion and reluctant party mascot during the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md)' [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) downtime.

## Origin

Denny begins as a raised enemy corpse affected by shark-like teeth and strange necromantic growth. He becomes more persistent and capable than an ordinary temporary undead servant, leading the party to treat him as a recurring companion rather than disposable magic.

## Personality

Denny has no normal speech, but he communicates through gestures, exaggerated posture, and physical bits. He learns card tricks badly, offers awkward handshakes, and accepts [Vylra](vylra.md)'s orders with a strange amount of character for a zombie.

## Elisia

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), [Vylra](vylra.md) polymorphs Denny into a large soft fox-like creature to comfort [Elisia](elisia.md) on her first night outside Hell. [Elisia](elisia.md) falls asleep holding him.

Later, Denny is polymorphed into a ferret for the trip to [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md). When [Vylra](vylra.md)'s sending-stone warning nearly fails, Denny's linked watcher-boon helps force the message through, leaving him briefly as a skeletal ferret full of buzzing bees.

In the reconstructed [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), Denny helps in the manor fight and badly mauls the tooth-themed assassin, leaving him nearly toothless.

## Finale

By [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), Denny has begun learning to cook at [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md), mostly favoring soups, sauces, and soft foods due to his single remaining tooth.

In the [Gateway to the Empire](../places/gateway-to-the-empire.md) trap gauntlet, he is sent ahead and immediately triggers fire, lightning, and stun traps. He survives, then waits behind with cooking supplies while the party continues deeper.
