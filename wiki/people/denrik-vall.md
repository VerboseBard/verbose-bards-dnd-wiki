# Denrik Vall

Denrik Vall is one of the [Caldrith's Gate](../places/caldriths-gate.md) guild receptionists.

## Description

He is a stern tiefling who speaks in clipped phrases. He is known for an encyclopedic memory and intolerance for heroic swagger.
