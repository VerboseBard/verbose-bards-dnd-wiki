# Dione

Dione is one of the old gods of foreknowledge and prophecy.

## Role

In the [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md), Dione and [Savras](savras.md) are collectively described as second in power.

The Seekers are identified as their children.

## Campaign Echoes

In [Quill](quill.md)'s [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) notes, Dione appears as the halfling seer and oracle of Wilbrix. Her manor contains magically enlarged internal spaces, a craftsmen's corridor, and a strange time-displacement aftermath: Dione experiences centuries while only days pass locally, and Quill's party experiences months in suspension while almost no local time passes.

In [Hawk](hawk.md)'s [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) notes, Dione blesses [Hawk](hawk.md) with knowledge and later appears through dreams and prophecy-warning material. That makes Dione one of the cleaner divine through-lines between early party notes and the larger council structure.
