# Dorothea Hooley

Dorothea Hooley is a Harengon elder cousin in [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

## Role

Dorothea is dignified, emotional, and afraid to hope. She is a family mourner whose reaction to [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md) can carry the grief of the lost rabbit stories without turning immediately political.
