# Edmund Moonhare

Edmund Moonhare was the last recognized rabbit elder to hold the old Moonhare line's standing in [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md).

## Role

Edmund belonged to the line that once held the rabbit seat before that standing was erased and folded into [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

When his heir vanished on a trading mission beyond the valley, Edmund died in an accident before the inner temple duty could be passed on securely. That combination of loss gave the rest of the council an opening to refuse the rabbit line a replacement.

## Historical Importance

Edmund's death marks the political fracture that later feeds [the Sixth Seat](../factions/sixth-seat.md) and the broader rabbit grievance against the present council structure.
