# Elder Tomas Halvek

Elder Tomas Halvek is the village elder of [Norinar](../places/norinar.md).

## Role in Norinar

During the [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md) operation, Tomas briefly resists or wakes from sleep magic inside the inn. He believes the military has finally responded to a request for help, negotiation, or surrender.

He asks why the military did not accept the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) surrender.

## Aftermath

After the operation, Tomas accuses the military by implication, asking why they came in violently when the village had sent word that the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) wanted surrender.
