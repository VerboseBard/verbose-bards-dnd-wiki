# Experiment 69

Experiment 69 is the official name for the figure often shortened in session notes to "69." He is tied to [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md), [Belle](belle.md), [Mummy](mummy.md), dungeon cores, and the birth of [Eve](eve.md).

## Origin on Driftvale

Experiment 69 was found on Driftvale in a strange research lab. The lab was not originally native to Driftvale; it appeared there as part of the detritus and displaced material pulled in from other realities.

That origin makes Experiment 69 part of Driftvale's wider pattern of anomalous wreckage, impossible technology, and cross-reality debris.

## Mummy

Mummy begins as a goldfish. Experiment 69 picks Mummy up along the way, and Mummy becomes his friend.

After Mummy receives [Belle](belle.md)'s blessing and becomes sentient, Mummy is considered Experiment 69's brother.

## Belle's Plan

Experiment 69 and Mummy grow in power by consuming dungeon cores. Once they reach the threshold Belle needs, she uses them in two linked but different reproductive workings.

Belle begins by framing herself as Experiment 69's patron and creator. Because Experiment 69 has a childlike mind and adores the one who gave him sentience, he calls Belle Mommy. Belle never corrects that title, but she does not allow him to call her Mother and does not consider him her child. She cares for him more like a favored pet or useful creature than a son.

As he grows stronger, Belle shifts the language from service to purpose. She grooms Experiment 69 and Mummy to accept their own death and consumption as a transformation into something higher, like a butterfly emerging from a chrysalis.

[Alen](alen.md) helps arrange the cores and conditions that accelerate the process. Experiment 69 becomes the foundation for [Eve](eve.md), while Mummy is used in the separate vampire-birth working that produces [Lilith](lilith.md).

This transformation becomes important to the later [Hunt for the Black Vipers](../campaign/hunt-for-black-vipers.md) arc.
