# Experiment 69

Experiment 69 is the official name for the figure often shortened in session notes to "69." He is tied to [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md), [Belle](belle.md), [Mummy](mummy.md), dungeon cores, and the birth of [Eve](eve.md) and [Lilith](lilith.md).

## Origin on Driftvale

Experiment 69 was found on Driftvale in a strange research lab. The lab was not originally native to Driftvale; it appeared there as part of the detritus and displaced material pulled in from other realities.

That origin makes Experiment 69 part of Driftvale's wider pattern of anomalous wreckage, impossible technology, and cross-reality debris.

## Mummy

Mummy begins as a goldfish. Experiment 69 picks Mummy up along the way, and Mummy becomes his friend.

After Mummy receives [Belle](belle.md)'s blessing and becomes sentient, Mummy is considered Experiment 69's brother.

## Belle's Plan

Experiment 69 and Mummy grow in power by consuming dungeon cores. Once they reach a certain threshold, Belle uses them as building blocks to impregnate herself.

This happens with help from [Alen](alen.md), who had ascended to godhood and taken the [Raven Queen of Saharun](raven-queen-of-saharun.md) mantle, though she distorted what that mantle originally was.

As a result, Experiment 69 and Mummy become part of the divine birth process that produces Eve and Lilith. In later family language, the twins are Belle and Alen's daughters, while Belle is the one who carries and births them.

This transformation becomes important to the later [Hunt for the Black Vipers](../campaign/hunt-for-black-vipers.md) arc.
