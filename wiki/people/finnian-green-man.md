# Finnian / the Green Man

Finnian, also called the Green Man or the Leprechaun, is a powerful [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) figure tied to deals, merchants, swindlers, and gamblers.

## Fifth Age Divine Assembly

In the surviving [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md) source note, Finnian appears as the first named figure in the Sword and Purse Assembly.

The note identifies him as "Green Man or Finnian the leprechaun" and associates him with deals, merchants, swindlers, and gamblers.

## Summer Court

Later material identifies the Green Man as ruler of the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md). He is a leprechaun-like Fey king whose authority comes through bargains, trickery, accumulated power, and formal court politics.

During the [Moon Stone Collectors](../campaign/moonstone-collectors.md) finale, the party petitions him to replace the broken Spring Court link to [Viscus](viscus.md). He accepts, removes [Paul](paul.md)'s [Crown of Fools](../items/crown-of-fools.md), restores Paul's stolen years, and confirms that he kept [Wisdom](wisdom.md) safe for roughly a thousand years.
