# Gaston

Gaston is a fox-kin weapon master at [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](../places/lady-penny-hathaways-school.md).

## Role

Gaston appears during the early school phase of the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md), where he is tied to weapons training and the school's dueling culture.

[Kestrel](kestrel.md) practices rapier work with Gaston during the early academy sessions.

## Family

Gaston has a sister named [Monic](monic.md).
