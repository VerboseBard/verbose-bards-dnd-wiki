# General Ash

General Ash is the human general who forms [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md) after the battle in [Session 1](../sessions/session-1.md). He is older, with ash-gray hair, and speaks bluntly rather than ceremonially.

## Role

Ash understands that the [Kreen War](../concepts/kreen-war.md) is not truly over when the [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) gate closes. He predicts that isolated [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) will adapt, form new command structures, and become dangerous again unless hunted down.

He creates [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md) as a special-operations force for rogue hive suppression. The formal name spoken at table is `Unified Counter-Kreen Suppression and Rogue Hive Special Operations Group`, but Ash himself accepts the shorter field name.

## Key Actions

- Promotes survivors pulled from the [Bloody Fifth](../factions/bloody-fifth.md).
- Establishes pack/wolf-pack operations.
- Provides better equipment, food, quarters, and training.
- Frames the surviving soldiers as skilled improvisers rather than expendable bodies.
- Assigns [Colonel Gandor](colonel-gandor.md) as the Hounds' direct supervisor.
- His program's capture work eventually enables [Kreen Control Collars](../items/kreen-control-collars.md) and the first mass Kreen surrender.
