# General Old Blood and Guts

`Old Blood and Guts` is the nickname of General Faelar Grimward, the commander of the [Bloody Fifth](../factions/bloody-fifth.md).

## Reputation

He is known for wave tactics and enormous casualty rates. Soldiers assigned to his command expect to be used up.

His doctrine is brutally simple:

- bodies are cheaper than equipment
- pressure breaks lines
- pressure requires blood

He defines doctrine, authorizes mass assaults, allocates ether and manpower, and treats catastrophic casualty ratios as normal.

## Role in Session 1

His command culture frames the party's first battle: success is bought with bodies, and survival is almost accidental.

He appears by portal, projection, or formal address rather than entering combat zones himself. He gives speeches, then leaves.
