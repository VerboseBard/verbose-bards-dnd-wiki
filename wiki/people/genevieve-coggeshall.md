# Genevieve Coggeshall

Genevieve Coggeshall is Constance's mother and the only member of the immediate family who openly loved her in private.

## Secret Kindness

Because [Cornelius Coggeshall](cornelius-coggeshall.md) treated Constance as proof of disgrace or divine punishment, Genevieve could only comfort her daughter in secret. She waited until Cornelius slept or buried himself in work, then slipped out to the shed to spend time with Constance.

She brought small gifts and knickknacks, recognizing Constance's talent for tinkering long before the rest of the world found it useful.
