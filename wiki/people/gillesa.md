# Gillesa

Gillesa is one of the six fractured aspects of the rival devil shattered by [Lucian](lucian.md). She is a sister-fragment of [Julia / Julisia](julia-julisia.md).

## Imprisonment and Rescue

[Julia](julia-julisia.md) believed Gillesa had been destroyed ages ago. In truth, Gillesa survived as a prisoner until the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) rescued her during the chaos after [Belle](belle.md)'s destruction of [Wendell](wendell.md)'s stronghold.

## Importance

Gillesa's survival changed [Julia](julia-julisia.md)'s priorities. [Julia](julia-julisia.md) had focused on revenge for millennia, but the rediscovery of part of her own soul made her sacrifice patterns and lose control of her long-laid game.
