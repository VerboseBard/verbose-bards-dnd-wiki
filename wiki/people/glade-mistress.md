# Glade Mistress

The Glade Mistress is a powerful [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) noble encountered in the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md).

## Nature

The Glade Mistress appears as an ethereal woman seated beneath an ancient tree, attended by dryads and guards. Her realm is beautiful and ordered, but it follows [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) rules of debt and hospitality rather than mortal assumptions.

She does not present herself as cruel. She shelters the missing children, feeds them, and uses song to soothe them and bring them into phase with the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md). However, that care creates a debt that must be paid.

## Bargain

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 14](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-14.md), the party takes on the children's debt:

- [Vylra](vylra.md) pays years of life.
- [Paul](paul.md) accepts a loss of personal strength.
- [p'Op](p-op.md) offers an original ballad in her honor.
- [Vylra](vylra.md) gives up his ring of fire protection.

After the debt is settled, the Glade Mistress blesses the children and grants the party twenty-four hours of protection against surprise and improved defenses.
