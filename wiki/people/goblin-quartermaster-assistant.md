# Goblin Quartermaster Assistant

The goblin quartermaster assistant is a small green goblin who effectively takes over quartermaster duties after the head quartermaster at [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md) is arrested.

## Role

He provides supplies, including magical paper and ink, and offers strange barter deals.

He is also the one who opens the door to one of the campaign's strangest buried lore threads, because his ghost-clearing side job leads directly to the hidden back room, the [Second Age Bound Spirit](second-age-bound-spirit.md), and the [Music Box Anomaly](../items/music-box-anomaly.md).

## Side Deal

He agrees to help the party keep or ship military gear after discharge if they:

- clear a ghost from the back room
- bring him the biggest spoon they can find

That deal matters more than it first appears. It turns a quartermaster favor into one of the main bridges between the [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md) corruption plot and the campaign's deeper old-magic story.

## Hidden Function

He is also collecting strange objects for [Rurik Valdren](rurik-valdren.md)'s future mission, including an odd book, nutmeg, a fancy pillow, liquor, and the spoon.
