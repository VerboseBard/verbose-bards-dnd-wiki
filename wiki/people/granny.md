# Granny

Granny is an elderly tiefling associated with [The Singing Sword](../places/singing-sword.md).

## Role

She brews medicinal tea for aches, pains, and long walks in the fog. She appreciates manners and proper tea service.

## Gifts

- In [Session 3](../sessions/session-3.md), she gives the party a small bag of tea after a character respectfully cleans dishes.
- In [Session 4](../sessions/session-4.md), she gifts a [never-ending teapot](../items/grannys-teapot.md) of her brew.

## Warning

Granny warns that once the party loses uniform protection, [Mistress Selyra Vex'ryn](mistress-sila-vexry.md)'s people may try to kill them.
