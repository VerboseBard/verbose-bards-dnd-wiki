# Greg

Greg is a figure associated with the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md), the hidden systems of the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), and later ascension to godhood.

## School and Library Thread

In the earlier [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md) material, Greg is already one of the strangest and most system-facing members of the party.

At the school, he stands out as someone who is repeatedly hard to wake, book-bound, and partly detached from normal student life while still being central to whatever is really happening behind the scenes. By [War of the Gods, Session 8](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-8.md), he is deeply involved in [Junara](junara.md)'s crisis around THE BOOK, altered memory, and soul damage.

By [War of the Gods, Session 9](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-9.md), Greg is the person who can still turn catastrophe into logistics. When the Emerald Palace council erupts in fire, he uses a ring to open the escape route to the library and gets the party out alive.

## Architect of Worlds

Greg is also known as the Architect of Worlds. Because divine restrictions prevent him from directly intervening, he finds ways to "not help" while very obviously helping. He and [Belle](belle.md) construct [Vis / Jarvis](vis-jarvis.md), a new warforged body for the longtime voice in Greg's head, then sends [Vis](vis-jarvis.md) to aid [Junara](junara.md) and the others.

Greg can perceive through [Vis](vis-jarvis.md) within a broad radius, making [Vis](vis-jarvis.md) both an ally and a direct line back to Greg.

In the extracted [War of the Gods, Session 17](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-17.md), Greg learns that his old "three questions a day" library access came from an illicit magical tap engineered through [Jinx](jinx.md). That same session sharpens his role as an analyst rather than only a builder: he uses the library to reconstruct [Wendell](wendell.md)'s pressure-based anti-divine strategy and realize that information itself is now contested terrain.

In [War of the Gods, Session 19](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-19.md), Greg finally gets a fuller explanation from [Jinx](jinx.md) about why that illegal line matters so much. The real prize is not just ordinary world-to-world teleportation, but genuine inter-universe movement beyond the normal structure of the Gate. [Jinx](jinx.md) also hands over a massive research dump, effectively advancing Greg's own gate and transit work by roughly a hundred hours at once.

In [War of the Gods, Session 20](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-20.md), Greg's proximity to the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)'s hidden infrastructure grows even more obvious. [Veil](veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) admits him into the crystal's central control chamber, [Jinx](jinx.md) is still working there under heavy strain, and one of Greg's old bodies is literally delivered back to him. At that point he is no longer merely a researcher circling the system from outside. He is becoming one of the few mortals trusted to move near its hidden core.

In [War of the Gods, Session 23](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-23.md), [Jinx](jinx.md) pushes a major systems update into Greg while the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) comes under direct siege. That update gives him full portal-construction knowledge within the current reality and ties his spare chassis into a continuity backup. By then Greg is no longer just clever. He is operating on infrastructure scale.

In [War of the Gods, Session 24](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-24.md), Greg crosses one more threshold from analyst to operator. Using the crystal-link and systems access [Jinx](jinx.md) fed him, he reaches [Veil](veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) during the siege, takes the fight onto the outside of the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), and spends one of his precious library favors to force the god-killing blade into [Lucian](lucian.md) when ordinary tactics stall out.

Greg is credited with [Greg's Concurrence Proposal](../concepts/gregs-concurrence-proposal.md), a theoretical report describing how magical resonance, ley-line conduits, wormhole theory, electrical synchronization, and [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)-centered calibration could allow worlds to converge without catastrophic destruction.

During the final [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) sequence shown in [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 5](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-5.md), Greg's role as Architect becomes literal. His rewrite of reality pushes him beyond the mortal side of the Gate and into godhood. He leaves behind a body or chassis and a personal knife for [Junara](junara.md) before ascending.

## Ascension and Limits

By the time of the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md)' strike, Greg had ascended to godhood. Even so, he could do nothing to prevent the deaths and soul-taking that unfolded directly.

During the Black Viper hunt, Greg contacts [Jarvis](vis-jarvis.md) through a non-divine mechanical link while [Jarvis](vis-jarvis.md) is inside the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md). He explains [Belle](belle.md)'s warning about god-blinding crystals: [Lucian](lucian.md) originally designed them, and while they can protect mortals from divine sight, they also created the blind spots that made divine betrayal possible.

After [Lucian's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucians-crystal-trap.md), Greg breaks his own intervention rules to tell [Junara](junara.md) and [Jarvis](vis-jarvis.md) that [Eve](eva.md)'s [Lucian](lucian.md)-tainted soul coin can trace the main ritual and [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s soul. Greg frames the choice as rule-breaking in service of saving his friend.

Greg also apologizes to [Vis](vis-jarvis.md) for creating a new life without recognizing his individuality. He separates [Vis](vis-jarvis.md) from [Jarvis](vis-jarvis.md) and makes [Jarvis](vis-jarvis.md) the Avatar of the Architect so the final ritual can proceed without risking [Vis](vis-jarvis.md)'s original soul.

Before the assault on [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), Greg speaks through [Jarvis](vis-jarvis.md) to brief the party. He explains the linked ley-line rituals, the three divine artifacts, the four fragments of [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s soul, and the danger that failed interference could damage magic itself.
