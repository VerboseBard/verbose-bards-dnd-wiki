# Gregario / Shaq

Gregario, also called Shaq, is a powerful monk-like protector entrusted with [Lilith](lilith.md).

## Role

Gregario protects [Lilith](lilith.md) in [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md) after the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) target her. He flees through the ruins of her mother's palace, deflects blades and bullets meant for her, and kills multiple assassins before reinforcements arrive.

## Relationship to Lilith

[Lilith](lilith.md) calls him Kagari or Gregario in childlike forms of address. She trusts him deeply and refuses to leave his side unless he allows it. Gregario treats her safety as his highest priority.

## Cave Reinforcement

During the cave assault, Gregario arrives after the serpent avatar's roar stuns much of the party. He attacks the serpent from above and helps finish the remaining scorpion guardian after the avatar falls.

## Hidden Shrine

Gregario explores the deeper shrine beneath the [Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout](../places/silver-bend-coastal-cave-hideout.md). He finds the serpent prophecy murals, preserved vampire body parts, a speaking vampire head, and a blood vessel that tries to enter his body.

After resisting the blood and using holy water, Gregario gains or recovers a raven tattoo matching the one he once bore before [Alen](alen.md)'s death as the [Raven Queen of Saharun](raven-queen-of-saharun.md).

## Annabelle's Severing

At the dragon school, [Annabelle](annabelle.md) removes the raven tattoo and the remaining commands tied to [Alen](alen.md). Gregario loses the old raven-linked powers, but becomes whole without the charge that once defined him.

[Annabelle](annabelle.md) gives him a childlike bracelet that stabilizes him and lets her help protect the part of him that was taken and frozen long ago.

## Dark Star Strike

During the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) factory breach, Gregario reaches [Dark Star](dark-star.md) inside [Junara](junara.md)'s Anti-Magic Field. He lands Stunning Strike and then Quivering Palm, dropping [Dark Star](dark-star.md) before the Viper can act.

## Pheromone Control and Zuk's Warning

During the island battle, the spider queen's pheromones briefly override Gregario and cause him to attack Constant. After the party retreats to the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), Zuk spars with him and warns that vows made without understanding can become damaging.

Zuk frames Gregario's repressed desires and old celibacy oath as a practical danger, not merely a personal issue, and urges him to seek help from [Annabelle](annabelle.md) or another trusted ally.

During [Lucian's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucians-crystal-trap.md), Gregario closes into melee with the armored fragment, burns through [Lucian](lucian.md)'s legendary defenses, and helps finish the phase before the crystal can collapse.

## Champion of Lilith

After the crystal battle, Gregario dreams of [Lilith](lilith.md) crying in ruined [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md) because she believes he is gone. He returns to the school and learns that [Lilith](lilith.md) lost her ability to feel him after [Annabelle](annabelle.md) severed the old [Alen](alen.md) / Raven Queen connection.

[Lilith](lilith.md) manifests the [Ring of Lilith's Favor](../items/ring-of-liliths-favor.md) and asks for him back. Gregario accepts freely. The bond burns away his vampiric hunger and transforms him into an eight-foot black-winged aasimar, the champion of [Lilith](lilith.md)'s Raven Queen mantle.

## Earlier Raven Pact

In the extracted [War of the Gods, Session 18](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-18.md), Gregario is shown already entangled with a raven-linked patron or progenitor before the later [Lilith](lilith.md)-centered development seen in [Sixth Age](../concepts/sixth-age.md) material. That figure negotiates through his raven, clears an old debt, and uses him to pass dragon cores toward 69.

The session also makes clear why later allies mistrust the arrangement: even when Gregario himself remains calm, the thing riding with him can still exert pressure, demand service, and redirect events through him.

## Session 19 Escalation

In [War of the Gods, Session 19](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-19.md), that relationship escalates sharply. When a destabilized dungeon core threatens to kill the party aboard a broken ship, Gregario breaks the feather-linked bargain and negotiates directly with the raven-associated death power behind it.

He accepts a much deeper form of service than the earlier contract implied. The result regrows his missing arm, marks his body with hidden magical meridian-tattoos, and lets him channel or absorb the catastrophic surge from the dungeon core rather than dying to it.

Most importantly, he chooses not to hoard that power. He spreads much of the resulting gain to nearby companions, establishing a pattern that later lore keeps reinforcing: even when Gregario becomes spiritually dangerous, he still tends to use that danger protectively rather than selfishly.

In [War of the Gods, Session 21](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-21.md), the aftertaste of that choice becomes clearer. The party openly debates whether his bargains have really protected them or merely entangled them further, while [Alen](alen.md) continues using private praise and selective affection to isolate him emotionally from the group. The result is not open betrayal, but a growing split between how Gregario understands his sacrifices and how the rest of the party judges them.

In [War of the Gods, Session 22](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-22.md), that split widens. After Rose dies over the active gate nexus, Gregario follows [Alen](alen.md)'s prompting and claims Rose's champion gear from the body. He also receives further private reassurance from [Alen](alen.md) that his future role as protector is not over, even as [Junara](junara.md) openly challenges the logic of his bargains and warns that he may be serving a manipulator while calling it protection.

In [War of the Gods, Session 23](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-23.md), that entire structure breaks. [Alen](alen.md)'s manifested form is killed by [Lucian](lucian.md) over the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), and Gregario collapses with the loss of the patronage and order that had been shaping many of his recent powers. He does not stay down, however. Soul-coin logic and the damage already done to him yank him back in a broken but living state.

In [War of the Gods, Session 24](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-24.md), Gregario becomes one of the only people present capable of surviving and redirecting the massive power release caused by [Lucian](lucian.md)'s hourglass. With Zuk assisting, he routes much of that surge back into the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), helping restore the shields and prevent the fight from turning into a second disaster. The moment matters because it shows Gregario's danger in a new light: even after losing the structure that once controlled him, he is still a living regulator for catastrophically unstable power.

## Jimbo Ritual Warning

Before the first assault on [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), [Mave](mave.md) shares tea with Gregario and warns that failed extraction work could destroy aspects of magic. She asks him not to assist directly in the soul rescue, but to remain outside the working and be ready to stabilize magic if the ritualists lose control.

Gregario joins the first-wave assault at the [Desert Well Ritual Site](../places/desert-well-ritual-site.md), using speed, stunning strikes, and radiant force to break through the defenders while holding back from the soul exchange itself.

At the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), Gregario kicks open the facility door and engages the huge mech guardian directly. After the head is damaged, he looks into the machine and recognizes that its power source resonates like a trapped soul tied to [Jimbo](jimbo.md).

Gregario reaches into the burning core, endures the backlash, and with [Jack Kenway](jack-kenway.md)'s help tears the soul-cage fragment out of the mech. This collapses the guardian and recovers the second of [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s four fragments.

At the [High Temple of Rexel Kar](../places/high-temple-of-rexel-kar.md), Gregario charges down into the lower temple and beats a snake guardian back into [Jarvis](vis-jarvis.md)'s Wall of Fire, finishing it in the flames.

## Concurrence Completion

During [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 5](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-5.md), an Elsewhere follows Gregario back to the alley where his first team died. He watches the worlds complete their alignment around the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), feels the missing part of himself return, and encounters the child he once swore to protect.
