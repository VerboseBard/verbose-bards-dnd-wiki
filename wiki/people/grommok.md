# Grommok

Grommok is the orc god of war named in the surviving [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md) source note.

## Fifth Age Divine Assembly

Grommok appears as the second named figure in the Sword and Purse Assembly.

The current archive preserves only his title and assembly position: god of war, associated with orcs.
