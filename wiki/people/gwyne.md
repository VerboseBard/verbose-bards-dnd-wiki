# Gwyne

Gwyne is Zuk's mother and a powerful dragon elder involved with [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md).

## Role

Gwyne helps watch over the school and its growing dragon horde while [Constance and Zuk](constance-l-coggeshall.md) are away on the Black Viper hunt.

When [Lilith](lilith.md)'s dragon bond destabilizes the school, Gwyne calls [Constance and Zuk](constance-l-coggeshall.md) back and later introduces them to the [Flight of the Ancients](../factions/flight-of-the-ancients.md).
