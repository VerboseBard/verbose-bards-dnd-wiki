# Head Keeper of Viscus

The Head Keeper of Viscus is the caretaker who maintains the symbiosis between [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) and [Viscus](viscus.md).

## Role

The Head Keeper mediates between the living fungus network and the grove's circles. During important rites, they guide [Viscus](viscus.md)'s roots and ensure the ritual remains in balance with the Grove's needs.

## Ritual Presence

During the [Rite of Faded Remembrance](../concepts/rite-of-faded-remembrance.md), the Head Keeper completes the cardinal circle with the three elder leaders and helps direct the root binding.
