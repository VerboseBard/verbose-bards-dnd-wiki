# High Priestess of Leira

The High Priestess of Leira is a religious figure tied to Leira, goddess of illusions and trickery.

## Casino Meeting

She attends [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s table at the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md). Her presence reinforces that [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s network is not limited to serpent zealots and Viper remnants.
