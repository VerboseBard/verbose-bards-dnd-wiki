# Initiate Thomas

Initiate Thomas was a surviving cult fighter captured after the battle in the [Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout](../places/silver-bend-coastal-cave-hideout.md).

## Interrogation

Thomas explains that he is associated with the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) but is not a true Viper. He serves the Church of [Rax'al Karath](../factions/cobra-cult-of-raxal-karath.md).

He names [Bishop Collins](bishop-collins.md) and [Deacon Matthews](deacon-matthews.md) as his superiors. He claims the church has hundreds of thousands of worshippers and perhaps ten thousand active servants or warriors.

## Death

When pressed on the cult's pursuit of children, Thomas begins chanting the hunger doctrine of the serpent faith. [Eve / Eva](eva.md) incinerates him before the interrogation continues.
