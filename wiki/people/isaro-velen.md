# Isaro Velen

Isaro Velen is a Capran of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md) and [Tavero Bellan](tavero-bellan.md)'s assistant or deputy liaison.

## Role

Isaro is practical, smooth, and good with logistics. He handles guest quarters, guards, and communication with other speakers when Burrowroot needs official movement to look calm.
