# Iven Grell

Iven Grell is a Tortle shield guard of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

## Role

Iven joins the next-morning escort to [Hollowmere](../places/hollowmere.md) as a steady ground guard. His presence helps make the escort feel controlled without making it purely Ashmane-led.
