# Jack Kenway

Jack Kenway is the third reincarnation of the Commodore, a legendary sea figure now acting as an unlikely mentor to [Eve / Eva](eva.md) on [Opaal](../places/opaal.md).

## Role

Jack teaches [Eve](eva.md) aboard ship and lets her take command when mercenary vessels pursue them into a storm. After [Eve](eva.md) destroys the attackers, Jack counsels her that family is not an anchor meant to sink her, but a tether that keeps her from floating too far away.

## Philosophy

Jack advises [Eve](eva.md) that if enemies keep coming, she should kill first and pile the bodies high enough that no one considers attacking again. Security, to him, is when enemies fear you more than you fear them.

## Gift

Jack gives [Eve](eva.md) a teleportation pass pendant that lets her travel from [Opaal](../places/opaal.md) to [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) and reunite with [Lilith](lilith.md).

## Return Against Lucian

Before the assault on [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), Jack reflects aboard his ship on [Opaal](../places/opaal.md), his wife, his daughter, and the hunting harpoon artifact left to him. His daughter asks him to help settle their people's old debt against [Lucian](lucian.md), and Jack accepts.

Jack joins the first-wave assault at the [Desert Well Ritual Site](../places/desert-well-ritual-site.md). He fights with the artifact harpoon, using lightning-line throws and swashbuckling precision during the surface and hell-descent battles.

At the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), Jack fights across the mech guardian's frame and helps [Gregario / Shaq](gregario-shaq.md) tear the [Jimbo](jimbo.md) soul-cage fragment out of the machine's core. The act recovers the second fragment and collapses the mech.

On the ritual floor, Jack recognizes [Winrah Holm](winrah-holm.md)'s mind-control crown as the same design he once wore in a previous life. Later, at the [High Temple of Rexel Kar](../places/high-temple-of-rexel-kar.md), Jack cuts through serpent defenders with repeated critical strikes, beheading the stair guardian and helping drop the largest snake.
