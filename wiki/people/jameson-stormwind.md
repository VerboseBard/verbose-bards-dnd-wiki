# Jameson Stormwind

Jameson Stormwind is a wind genasi member of the Circle of Death at [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Role

Jameson is quiet and introspective, treating death as a natural and meaningful part of the cycle rather than something to fear.
