# Jarth

Jarth is the ranger and authority figure associated with the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md)'s security forces.

## Role

Jarth controls access to the crystal's protected interior and coordinates its black-armored guards, staging spaces, and high-security containment areas.

## Black Viper Arc

[Junvara](junvara.md) briefs Jarth on the Black Viper hunt, the threat of [Lucius](lucius.md)'s return, and the ultimatum delivered toward the Conclave. [Junvara](junvara.md) also treats bringing the [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) captains into the crystal as a sign of intended alliance between Jarth's forces and the [442nd](../factions/442nd.md).

During [Lucius's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucius-crystal-trap.md), Jarth is wounded when the prisoners' hidden soul contingencies explode. He survives because the party prevents the crystal from being destroyed.

Before the first assault on [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), the party checks whether Jarth can join the operation. His soul damage is healing, but a poor luck result means he remains unconscious and cannot take the field.
