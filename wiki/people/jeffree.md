# Jeffree

Jeffree, spelling provisional, is a shifter and local fog walker / contractor encountered near [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md).

## Role

He subtly reveals himself as a local by shifting his hand during a handshake with the [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md)-native party member.

## Key Actions

- Invites the party to [Calvin's Curios](../places/calvins-curios.md).
- Hints that the fog tides have been good.
- Serves as an early bridge between the party and local fog-scavenging networks.
