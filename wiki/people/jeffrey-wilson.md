# Jeffrey Wilson

Jeffrey Wilson is one of the school-era figures gathered into the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md).

## Background

Jeffrey comes from a very wealthy family. He is antisocial, bookish, and much more comfortable around texts and study than around ordinary social pressure.

He has odd characteristics connected to a foreign orc background or influence. The exact nature of that connection has not yet been fully clarified in the wiki.

## Seeker Connection

[Seeker Lauren](seeker-lauren.md) found Jeffrey and Anton before she was sent to retrieve Zach's character.
