# Jimbo

Jimbo was a powerful late-[Sixth Age](../concepts/sixth-age.md) ally of the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md), remembered both for impossible survivals and for becoming the central soul target in [Lucian](lucian.md)'s return plan.

## Arrival in the War of the Gods

Before the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) murdered him, Jimbo entered the surviving [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md) record as a major reinforcement figure.

In [War of the Gods, Session 22](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-22.md), he joins the party during the fallout from the hatchery disaster and immediately proves why he matters. He helps resurrect Constance's mother and the five fallen dragons, dramatically improving the party's standing with the surviving dragons even while the wider family and prophecy crisis is still raging.

That session establishes Jimbo as more than a late replacement body. He arrives as a high-end magical problem solver with enough resurrection power to change the emotional outcome of a whole arc.

## Crystal-Battle Sacrifice

In [War of the Gods, Session 24](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-24.md), Jimbo becomes one of the key figures in the battle on the outside of the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md).

When the fight against [Lucian](lucian.md) stalls around the god-killing dagger, Jimbo is the one who physically grabs the blade. Just touching it costs him levels and life force. [Greg](greg.md) then spends one of his precious library favors to force the dagger through Jimbo and into [Lucian](lucian.md), turning Jimbo into the unwilling hinge of the finishing strike.

The backlash disintegrates him during the crystal surge, but [Junara](junara.md) later uses stored diamonds and a powerful scroll to restore him fully. By the end of the session, Jimbo is alive again and openly acknowledges the life debt created by that restoration.

## Death and Soul Taken

Later, during the Vipers' forest-side ambush shown in [Assassination of Jimbo and Charlie](../sessions/assassination-of-jimbo-and-charlie.md), Jimbo is killed after the attackers exploit the chaos of the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) to steal [Lucian](lucian.md)'s old weapons out of Hell.

The killers strike under anti-magic pressure, ranged fire, and soul-cage tactics. Jimbo uses copied magic, invisibility, and time stop to try to recover the fight, but the Vipers finish him before [Junara](junara.md) can intervene.

Jimbo's soul is claimed rather than destroyed, making it an ideal component in [Lucian](lucian.md)'s resurrection spell.

During the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) operation, [Charlie](charlie.md)'s soul is recovered, but Jimbo's remains missing. [Dark Star](dark-star.md) had last held or won Jimbo's soul, but his defeat does not immediately reveal it.

During [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s soul interrogation, [Lucian](lucian.md) reveals that Jimbo's soul has been woven into the spell lattice meant to resurrect him. Jimbo is not fully spent, because the final seal still requires him intact. [Greg](greg.md) later tells [Junara](junara.md) that the [Lucian](lucian.md)-tainted soul coin taken from [Eve](eva.md) can be used to trace the main ritual and improve the party's chance of finding Jimbo.

## Soul Fragments

[Greg](greg.md) later explains that Jimbo's soul has been divided into four fragments inside [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md). Each fragment recovered improves Jimbo's fate:

- one prevents eternal torment
- two allow rest
- three allow reincarnation without memories
- all four allow resurrection

The fragments are also being used as active power pieces inside [Lucian](lucian.md)'s ritual lattice. That means the party is not simply collecting keys; they are extracting Jimbo's soul from a weaponized structure before the remaining pieces are lost.

At the [Desert Well Ritual Site](../places/desert-well-ritual-site.md), [Junara](junara.md) and [Eve / Eva](eva.md) successfully exchange a captured djinn soul for the first fragment. Jimbo is not saved yet, but he is no longer doomed to be endlessly consumed by the ritual.

At the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), the party discovers a second Jimbo fragment powering a massive mech guardian. [Gregario / Shaq](gregario-shaq.md) and [Jack Kenway](jack-kenway.md) pull the soul cage out of the machine, recovering the second fragment. Jimbo can now be put to rest if no further fragments are recovered.

The active fragment at the same facility is later recovered after [Winrah Holm](winrah-holm.md) is freed from a mind-control crown and a Conclave champion takes over the ritual. The party substitutes Hellspire's captured soul and succeeds by a single point, recovering Jimbo's third fragment.

At the [High Temple of Rexel Kar](../places/high-temple-of-rexel-kar.md), the party reaches the final ritual chamber and disables several [Lucian](lucian.md) anchors to make the last extraction possible. With two Wish spells, a replacement Hellspire soul, and a decisive fate-twist in the ritual roll, the fourth fragment is recovered.

All four of Jimbo's fragments are now secured. Full resurrection is possible, though the party does not perform it before the final battle with [Lucian](lucian.md).

If any fragments had been lost, the result would have been permanent damage:

- one missing fragment would have left personality damage and memory gaps
- two missing fragments would have caused major instability and partial identity loss
- three missing fragments would have prevented full restoration
- the loss of core fragments would have made resurrection impossible
