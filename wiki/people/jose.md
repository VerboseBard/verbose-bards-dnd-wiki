# Captain Sgt. Jose

Captain Sgt. Jose is Nate's goliath fighter / monster hunter and one of the core front-line party members of the [After the War Campaign](../campaign/after-the-war-campaign.md).

## Role

Jose emerges in the ship arc as the party member most willing to take command of dangerous technology and then act on it immediately.

He is:

- a goliath fighter / monster hunter who can also speak Elvish
- recognized by the [Kex](../places/kex.md) as acting captain in [Session 5](../sessions/session-5.md)
- heavily associated with the [Arcstrike War Gauntlets](../items/ship-forged-gauntlets.md) in [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md)
- one of the party's main front-line brawlers

## Bloody Fifth Origin

Session 1 notes place Jose in Wave 3, Squad 12 as Nate's veteran / non-acting PC corporal. That puts him in the same early-war survival cluster as [Sgt. Jefferson Stone](sergeant-jefferson-stone.md), [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md), and [Bevar Lurton](bevar-lurton.md).

## Captain of the Kex

After the engineering fight aboard the [Crashed Ship](../places/crashed-ship.md), the [Kex](../places/kex.md) recognizes Jose as its captain. That is more than a title joke. It means the ship's systems now accept him as a legitimate command point while [Kubix](kubix.md) continues repair and recovery work.

In Session 5, Jose orders [Kubix](kubix.md) to assess damage, seal the hull, secure all entrances except the party's access, and begin repairs. He also leaves Kreen skull/axe material with the ship so Kubix can fabricate the gauntlets later carded as the [Arcstrike War Gauntlets](../items/ship-forged-gauntlets.md).

By the start of Session 6, the Kex has accepted the repair order and the gauntlets are ready in the fabrication bay. Jose also begins calling Kubix or the ship interface `Alexa`; this is tracked as a nickname unless later canon formalizes a full rename.

## Session 4 Nanite Upgrade

In the raw Session 4 transcript, Jose is the first party member forced into a ship pod by a ceiling arm. The interrupted nanite process still enhances him dramatically:

- Strength becomes 20
- Constitution becomes 19
- unarmed and improvised melee damage increase by one die step
- proficiency times per day, he can regain 10 hit points as a free-action nanite response, including while unconscious

This is the first clear moment where the hidden ship stops being salvage and starts rewriting the party's bodies.

## Arcstrike War Gauntlets

In [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), Jose receives the completed [Arcstrike War Gauntlets](../items/ship-forged-gauntlets.md). They attune by cutting his skin briefly and connecting to his blood and nanites.

The party installs one [Ship Augmentation Module](../items/ship-augmentation-modules.md) into the gauntlets during initial fabrication.

## Hornet

By [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), Jose is also using [Hornet](../items/hornet-drone.md), a compact Kex-derived reconnaissance and assault drone with a stunning shock discharge.

## Ssar'Velyn Temple Trials

In [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), Jose becomes one of the main reasons the party survives [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md)'s trials.

Jose and [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md) avoid the worst of the temple's initial trap, break into side rooms, and find the stasis chamber holding [Bartholomew Hildebrant](bartholomew-hildebrant.md). Jose opens the pod, helps stabilize Bartholomew, and then carries the crippled Fourth-Age dwarf on his shoulders through the trials.

That improvised setup becomes brutally effective. Jose uses mobility, grappling, shotgun pressure, and Hornet while Bartholomew fights from his shoulders like a mounted axe-and-crossbow attacker. During the second trial, Jose even throws Bartholomew through the air so the dwarf can kill a summoner mid-flight.

In [Session 9](../sessions/session-9.md), Jose is part of the group returned outside the temple after the trials. His role in carrying and fighting with [Bartholomew Hildebrant](bartholomew-hildebrant.md) becomes part of how the party introduces the ancient dwarf to the wider crisis once [Bevar Lurton](bevar-lurton.md) is confirmed missing.

## Music Box Incident

Jose's most important confirmed scene comes in [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), when a chimera is about to overwhelm the party in ancient ruins.

Instead of winning by force, Jose opens the [Music Box Anomaly](../items/music-box-anomaly.md) and uses it like a portal-prison. The box swallows the chimera into whatever lies beyond it, ending the fight instantly but proving that the artifact is vastly more dangerous than anyone thought.

That choice saves the group and simultaneously turns the music box into one of the campaign's most unstable items.

## Sunhollow First Contact

In [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md), Jose's Elvish becomes unexpectedly important during first contact with [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md). [Sgt. Jefferson Stone](sergeant-jefferson-stone.md) tries to teach [Voss](voss-kenku.md), but because Jefferson does not speak Elvish he has to work through Jose, turning the whole exchange into a team effort that still somehow produces profanity-heavy bad instruction.
