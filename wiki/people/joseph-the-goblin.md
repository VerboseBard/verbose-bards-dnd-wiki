# Joseph the Goblin

Joseph is a goblin employee at [JJ and Jackson's Teleportation Service](../places/jj-and-jacksons-teleportation-service.md) in [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md).

## Role

Joseph greets [Eve / Eva](eva.md) when she arrives on [Saharun](../places/saharun.md), offers brochures, explains local services, and gives her a blunt explanation of how people acquire money.

## Family

Joseph claims to have fifty-seven children, many grandchildren, and great-grandchildren. [Eve](eva.md) later returns to pay him in platinum after deciding she is "option one": born into money.
