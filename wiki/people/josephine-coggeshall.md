# Josephine Coggeshall

Josephine Coggeshall is Constance's older sister and the secondborn child of the Coggeshall family.

## Contempt

Josephine treated Constance with disdain and made little effort to hide that she believed the child should have been abandoned to the gods. Her cruelty was colder than [Lucius](lucius.md)'s but still deliberate. At one point she shaved Constance's hair out of jealousy and humiliation.

Later, marriage and children pulled Josephine away from directly tormenting Constance, but she passed that same contempt on by raising her children to hate Constance for her disability.
