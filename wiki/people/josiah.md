# Josiah

Josiah is Lucy's youngest brother and one of Constance's most devoted admirers.

## Hero Worship

As a child, Josiah thought Constance was the coolest person he had ever met. He adored Zuk, wanted a dragon like him, and prayed nightly for a bond of his own.

His admiration gave Constance something rare in her early life: uncomplicated belief.
