# Julara

Julara is a succubus of knowledge connected to [Paul](paul.md)'s cursed summoning compulsion.

## Nature

Unlike a carnal succubus, Julara feeds on the desire for knowledge. Her connection is tied to curiosity, study, and the urge to know more.

## Paul

During the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md)' Hell detour, a feminine voice in [Paul](paul.md)'s mind warns him away from deadly paths and later reveals that she can see through his eyes in some parts of Hell.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 8](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-8.md), [Elisia](elisia.md) identifies that presence as Julara and calls her one of her sisters.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), [Paul](paul.md) bargains with Julara after discovering that the salt for his summoning circle has been stolen. Julara removes the compulsion to summon or contact her in exchange for [Paul](paul.md)'s memories and account of [Elisia](elisia.md)'s rescue.

The bargain leaves Julara able to converse with [Paul](paul.md) more easily and, with permission, watch events through his perspective. She says the payment is the knowledge she gains from witnessing the end of an age through someone close to major events.

Julara also grants [Paul](paul.md) stronger protection against outside mental influence, framing it as a practical defense after she realizes how open his mind has become.

## The City of Bar

Julara helps [Paul](paul.md) identify the [Dwarven City of Bar](../places/dwarven-city-of-bar.md), marking major locations on his map and explaining that the dwarves claimed the ancient giant site after the giants vanished at the end of the [Second Age](../concepts/second-age.md).

## Dwarven Goo Research

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), Julara researches the [Demonic Dwarven Goo](../concepts/demonic-dwarven-goo.md) and connects it to a false-name deal between the dwarves and the lesser demon who later became [Zarvok the Torturer](zarvok-the-torturer.md).

She explains that the goo was originally part of a dwarven undead-control system involving demonic energy and command amulets. A variant escaped control, helped cause an undead outbreak, and was preserved in [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md)'s ice until the thaw began.

## Feywild Observation

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 14](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-14.md), Julara observes the party's unexpected trip into the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) through [Paul](paul.md). She helps with a riddle, is caught by the fairy gatekeeper, and fails her own riddle, causing [Paul](paul.md) to suffer a [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) navigation curse.

Despite the danger, she treats the experience as a rare chance to study [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) magic, curses, and living planar ecology.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 15](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-15.md), Julara helps [Paul](paul.md) understand the consequences of destroying the mushroom circle. She identifies [Belle](belle.md) as a triune [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) queen and goddess tied to Spring, Mischief, and Dreams.

She also researches hydras for the party, explaining that ancient ice hydras are beastlike, regenerate fiercely, and are vulnerable to anti-regeneration tactics such as fire or acid.

After [Belle](belle.md) marks [Paul](paul.md) with the [Crown of Fools](../items/crown-of-fools.md), Julara finds a ritual that suppresses the crown's constant glow, though it does not remove the curse.

## Departure with Paul

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), Julara helps [Paul](paul.md) suppress the [Crown of Fools](../items/crown-of-fools.md) with a cold-iron amulet and warns him that the [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) age curse has stolen 64 years of his expected life.

She offers ways to restore or extend his life, including a full bond with her. [Paul](paul.md) refuses that deeper bond, but when [Luca](luca.md) arrives with orders to kill Julara or free [Paul](paul.md) from her, [Paul](paul.md) leaves the party to protect her.

[Wisdom](wisdom.md) later suggests merging with Julara to wield the celestial artifact, but the party recognizes this would override Julara's will.

## Final Merger

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), after [Viscus](viscus.md) converts the master goo, the knowledge and wisdom fragments are brought together in the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md).

Julara merges with [Wisdom](wisdom.md), also called Sophia, as part of the final restoration of [Sovereignty](sovereignty.md). The scene leaves [Sovereignty](sovereignty.md) whole in the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) rather than destroyed or ascended.

This means Julara's independent state after the finale is no longer the same as her mid-campaign connection to Paul. She is now one of the completed parts inside Sovereignty.
