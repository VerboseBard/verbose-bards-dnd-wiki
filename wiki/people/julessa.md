# Julessa

Julessa is the Lust for Carnal Pleasures fragment of the rival celestial-demonic sister-being shattered by [Lucius](lucius.md).

## Sister-Fragment Role

Julessa is one of the six known fragments that eventually become part of [Sovereignty](sovereignty.md).

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), after [Julia](julia-julisia.md), [Elisia](elisia.md), and [Alura](alura.md) have merged into the early Sovereignty state, the merged being finds Julessa imprisoned in a labyrinth in the second layer of Heaven.

Julessa recognizes herself in the being before her and is absorbed willingly or with little resistance. After this, Sovereignty carries Conquer / Power, Justice / Protection, Love, and Lust for Carnal Pleasures.
