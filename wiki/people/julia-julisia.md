# Julia and Julisia

Julia is the Conquer / Power fragment of the rival celestial-demonic being whose soul was shattered by [Lucius](lucius.md).

Julisia is not merely an alternate spelling. It is the name used after Julia merged with or consumed [Elisia](elisia.md), showing that more than one sister-fragment was present within the same being.

## Origin

Long ago, [Lucius](lucius.md) used a divine artifact to shatter a rival celestial-demonic being into six fractured aspects. Each aspect carried a piece of the original soul. Julia was one of those aspects, representing Conquer or Power.

[Lucius](lucius.md) made the mistake of doing an enemy a small harm rather than ending her completely. For a thousand years, Julia survived at the edge of his empire, watching, infiltrating, and waiting.

## Revenge

When [Lucius](lucius.md)'s resurrection neared, Julia triggered a sleeper cell in the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md): the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md). The Vipers believed they were acting for [Lucius](lucius.md), but Julia used them as a lure and distraction to draw the attention of [Lucius](lucius.md)'s enemies.

## Shift in Motivation

After [Belle](belle.md)'s attack on [Wendell](wendell.md)'s stronghold, the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) freed [Gillesa](gillesa.md), a sister-fragment Julia believed destroyed. Confronted with part of her original soul, Julia diverted resources and lost control of the larger game, allowing new possibilities to emerge.

The Conclave later reveals that Julia was the hidden player who set much of [Lucius](lucius.md)'s resurrection game in motion. Her goal appears to be forcing all of [Lucius](lucius.md)'s soul fragments into one destroyable form so the being who shattered her can be erased permanently.

## Julisia

In the reconstructed [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 11](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-11.md), Julisia appears through or over [Elisia](elisia.md) after the Deceiver's contingencies strike the school. The party finds [Elisia](elisia.md) with a second face superimposed over hers, standing over [Phlukk](phlukk.md)'s protected stasis pod.

This marks the point where the table begins using Julisia to show a merged identity rather than a single isolated fragment. Julia is still the Power / Conquer fragment, but Julisia indicates Julia plus Elisia within the same body or identity. [Julara](julara.md) later frames the situation as [Elisia](elisia.md) becoming closer to the original whole, though not necessarily by consent.

## Sovereignty

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), several sister-fragments merge into [Sovereignty](sovereignty.md): Julia as Conquer / Power, [Elisia](elisia.md) as Justice / Protection, [Alura](alura.md) as Love, and [Julessa](julessa.md) as Lust for Carnal Pleasures. The merged being argues that the actions of one severed fragment should not define the whole.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), the remaining knowledge and wisdom fragments merge with [Sovereignty](sovereignty.md) in the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md). The completed [Sovereignty](sovereignty.md) is treated as a new whole rather than a simple return to Julia, Julisia, or any other single fragment's identity.
