# Junara

Junara is a high-level spellcaster and strategist associated with the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md), active both in the late [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md) and in the later hunt for the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md).

## War of the Gods Role

In the earlier late-[Sixth Age](../concepts/sixth-age.md) campaign material, Junara is already one of the party's main interpreters of magical systems.

He is central to the THE BOOK crisis, the soul-parasite purge, and the library-driven logic work that lets the group understand [Wendell](wendell.md)'s plans. During the desert-world and school phases, he is repeatedly the one willing to read too far, ask the dangerous question, or stare directly at magical infrastructure until it gives up an answer.

By [War of the Gods, Session 17](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-17.md), Junara is helping turn the [Thornwall](../places/thornwall.md) church-dungeon from a simple hazard into a readable system. In [War of the Gods, Session 19](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-19.md), he is part of the group trying to survive the dungeon-core catastrophe while still extracting meaning from it.

In [War of the Gods, Session 21](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-21.md), he becomes the person most willing to enter the newborn dungeon alone. He reaches the true core, recognizes that it may be conscious rather than merely mechanical, and persuades it to come willingly instead of destroying it. That choice changes the party's understanding of [Dungeons](../concepts/dungeons.md) entirely.

In [War of the Gods, Session 22](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-22.md), Junara uses the library to check [Wendell](wendell.md)'s own research history, helping confirm that ley lines, dungeon cores, [Alen](alen.md), and [Lucian](lucian.md)'s timetable are all part of the same larger project.

## Abilities and Style

Junara uses teleportation, demiplanes, soul magic, anti-divination protections, and a magnificent mansion. He is precise, severe, and pragmatic, but shows tenderness toward [Lilith](lilith.md) by protecting her and offering her a gift.

## Black Viper Hunt

After the Vipers kill [Charlie](charlie.md) and [Jimbo](jimbo.md), Junara interrogates a captured soul with methods harsher than a soul cage. He learns that the Vipers have fallen back to [Saharun](../places/saharun.md), that [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s soul has been taken, and that Viper cells are tied to [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md), [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md), and mercenary activity on [Opaal](../places/opaal.md).

During the bridge fight in [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md), [Lilith](lilith.md) gives Junara [Death's Kiss](../concepts/deaths-kiss.md), a divine blessing that can redirect death from one creature to another at the moment death occurs.

During the cave battle, [Death's Kiss](../concepts/deaths-kiss.md) activates when [Vis / Jarvis](vis-jarvis.md) is killed inside the serpent avatar. Junara then captures the fallen avatar's soul and later uses [Soul Cage](../items/soul-cage.md) interrogation to question [Deacon Matthews](deacon-matthews.md) and [Bishop Collins](bishop-collins.md), accidentally giving [Lucian's Splintered Echo](../concepts/lucians-splintered-echo.md) a vessel through which to speak.

Junara negotiates with [Morir](morir.md) at the [Old Silverbend Mines](../places/old-silver-bend-mines.md), recovering captured [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) members and learning the names of several surviving Viper threats. He then moves the hunt toward [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) and the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md).

In [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md), Junara negotiates with [Vince Gambino](vince-gambino.md) for noninterference, then leads the lower-level factory strike. His Anti-Magic Field strips [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s defenses long enough for [Kestrel](kestrel.md) and [Gregario](gregario-shaq.md) to end the fight before [Dark Star](dark-star.md) can act.

During [Lucian's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucians-crystal-trap.md), Junara destroys the unstable fragment before it can complete its soul explosion. Afterward, he trades [Eve](eva.md) for the [Lucian](lucian.md)-tainted soul coin that [Greg](greg.md) says can trace [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s ritual lattice.

Junara later meets a private faction within the [Conclave of Churches](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md). They offer him the title of [Arbiter](../concepts/arbiter-of-the-conclave.md), but he refuses any divine item or enhancement and asks that any oversight role include more non-divine voices than his alone.

During [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), Junara accepts Conclave champions and military reinforcement, including [Royal Marines](../factions/royal-marines.md) offered through [Admiral Gray](admiral-gray.md). At the [Desert Well Ritual Site](../places/desert-well-ritual-site.md), he captures an air djinn's soul, later captures the lich maintaining the first ritual, and acts as primary ritualist for the first soul exchange.

Junara and [Eve / Eva](eva.md) successfully replace [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s first fragment with the djinn soul, preserving the ritual and recovering one of four soul fragments.

At the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), Junara helps breach the shield and then prevents a dangerous tower-manipulation attempt from crashing a massive component into the ritual system. He later paralyzes the mech guardian's head and rocket pods, stopping its target-lock barrage long enough for the party to disable it.

When [Eve](eva.md) finds the elevator route to the ritual floor, Junara brings Callus into position so the champion can potentially take over the active ritual. Junara then enters the ritual chamber with Steel Wind Strike, damaging the defenders while deliberately avoiding the ritual operator.

In the reconstructed [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), Junara arrives as reinforcement during the battle at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md). After the Deceiver-linked enemy is killed, he moves to secure the killer of [Jimbo](jimbo.md). [Juan](juan.md), still mentally compromised, attempts to stab [June](june.md), but the attack fails.
