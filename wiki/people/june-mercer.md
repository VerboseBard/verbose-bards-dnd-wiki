# June Mercer

June Mercer is the Mapach elder of [Briarstep](../factions/briarstep.md) and one of the seated elders of [the Sunhollow Council](../factions/sunhollow-council.md).

## Description

June is a compact raccoon woman with one torn ear, clever hands, and practical clothes full of hidden pockets. She usually looks friendlier than she is.

## Role

She has held the Briarstep seat for eleven years. June knows the truth of [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md), but she is not loyal to the old secrecy in the same way as the valley's strictest elders.

## Household

Her mate is Wade Mercer, a male Mapach storehouse master with a reputation for hearing every rumor early. They have four grown children.

## Temperament

June is warm on the surface, unsentimental underneath, and politically slippery when she needs to be. She is better described as neutral toward outsiders than friendly toward them.
