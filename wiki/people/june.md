# June

June is one of the figures connected to the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md).

## Role in the Long Shadow

June arrived moments too late to save [Charlie](charlie.md) and [Jimbo](jimbo.md) from the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md). Later, using a soul cage and ancient ritual, June captured the soul of [Central Toge](central-toge.md), one of those responsible for [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s death.

## Ebonwing Manor

In the reconstructed [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), June survives [Juan](juan.md)'s mind-controlled attempt to kill him with the dagger after the Deceiver-linked enemy is destroyed.

The attack fails by a narrow margin, and June catches [Juan](juan.md)'s hand. The session ends on that unresolved standoff.
