# Kaithra Mally

Kaithra Mally is a goblin tracker and hunter of the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Role

Kaithra rides with the grove's hunting parties and helps patrol the thawing wilderness around the valley.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), she arrives with other hunters on giant arctic foxes while burning trails of [Demonic Dwarven Goo](../concepts/demonic-dwarven-goo.md) through the snow.

She recognizes [Vylra](../places/kuran-grove.md)'s return as part of a familiar pattern: trouble in the wilds, then [Vylra](vylra.md) showing up nearby.
