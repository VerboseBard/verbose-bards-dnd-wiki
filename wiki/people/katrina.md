# Katrina

Katrina was a Fourth-Age wood elf cleric who fought in the old assault on [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md).

## Session 8 Memory

In [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), [Bartholomew Hildebrant](bartholomew-hildebrant.md)'s recovered memory begins with Katrina trying to heal him in an ancient temple chamber after their assault team has already been devastated.

Katrina is badly wounded herself, but she still knows Bartholomew, trusts him, and chooses to save him when she cannot save everyone. A later memory phrase frames the choice plainly: it was him or Oscar, and Oscar was already out of spells.

She is killed by an invisible assassin soon afterward. Her death becomes one of the emotional anchors of Bartholomew's survival, because it leaves him with pain, loss, and rage as the forces pulling him back from the void.
