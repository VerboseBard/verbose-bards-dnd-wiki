# Keck Heft

Keck Heft is the Head of the Circle of Rebirth at [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Role

Keck manages renewal, rebirth, and the Grove's necromantic revival practices. He is calm, focused, and practical, with a strong interest in how memory and knowledge can be carried forward through the Grove's living systems.

## Ritual Presence

During the [Rite of Faded Remembrance](../concepts/rite-of-faded-remembrance.md), Keck stands with the other elders to bind Pluck's memories into the Chromate Fungus.
