# Kendrick

Kendrick is a blade singer and stage partner tied to [Nicole](nicole.md) in the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) arc.

## Role

He performs with [Nicole](nicole.md) at [Glacier's Edge Theater](../places/glaciers-edge-theater.md), where the pair draw a huge crowd and use music as a form of public magic.

Kendrick and [Nicole](nicole.md) read as a long-term partnership rather than a one-night fling.
