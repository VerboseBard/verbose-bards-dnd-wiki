# Krol

Krol is an ally operating with [Junvara](junvara.md), [Kestrel](kestrel.md), and [Vis](vis-jarvice.md) during the hunt for the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md).

## Role

Krol helps track [Gregario / Shaq](gregario-shaq.md) across the cliffs of [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md) and joins the bridge fight against cobra-marked Vipers.
