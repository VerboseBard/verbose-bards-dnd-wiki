# Kubix

Kubix is the shipboard AI of the [Crashed Ship](../places/crashed-ship.md), later identified as the [Kex](../places/kex.md)'s assistant and technical intelligence.

## Name Note

The Session 5 transcript drifts through table variants like `Tubix` and `Kovix`; `Kubix` is the stabilized wiki spelling.

In Session 6, Jose starts calling Kubix or the ship interface `Alexa`. The table jokes about whether the AI or the ship has been renamed, but Jose says he mainly wants to call the interface Alexa and will think about the ship's formal name. Until later canon locks this down, `Alexa` should be treated as Jose's nickname or command-interface label rather than a replacement for Kubix or the [Kex](../places/kex.md).

## Role

Kubix handles technical, scientific, and practical functions aboard the ship. He can read ship logs, print weapon schematics, manage repairs, and register crew when the ship still has enough autonomy to do so.

## Conflict

Kubix is not the ship's combat brain. A separate security or combat subroutine acts like a hostile brother to him, seizing control during alarms and using the ship's defenses against the crew.

In [Session 5](../sessions/session-5.md), the party helps Kubix regain enough control to shut down the hostile systems, defeat the combat avatar, and turn the ship into something closer to an ally than a prison.

Kubix offers two possible solutions: destroy the reactor, which would stop the hostile brother but also wipe out Kubix and most ship utility, or defeat the avatar in engineering and preserve the Kex.

## Repairs and Fabrication

By [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), Kubix has shifted from desperate trapped intelligence to active ship steward.

He:

- estimates an initial 41-45 day repair/sealing window after Session 5
- confirms that major hull and access repairs will take weeks rather than hours
- directs coordinated repair drones and nanite hull-growth systems
- runs the ship's fabrication bay for the party's new equipment
- completes [Jose](jose.md)'s [Arcstrike War Gauntlets](../items/ship-forged-gauntlets.md), first tracked as ship-forged gauntlets, before the party leaves in Session 6
- keeps the [Kex](../places/kex.md) operational enough to remain a hidden fallback base while the party returns to the [Lost Homeland Mission](../concepts/lost-homeland-mission.md)

Jefferson's Session 6 inspection shows that the repair process is slow but active. Drones spray nanite-rich material across breaches, build web-like scaffolds, feed metal stock into the damaged area, and let the hull regrow at a microscopic scale.

The party also learns that access to the shuttle bay is blocked until repairs open the route, and that an armory section broke off during the crash and may still be somewhere in the fog.

## Technical Aid

Kubix can print or provide schematics for adapting ship weapons to local magical power sources and for installing [Ship Augmentation Modules](../items/ship-augmentation-modules.md).
