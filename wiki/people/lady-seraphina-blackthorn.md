# Lady Seraphina Blackthorn

Lady Seraphina Blackthorn is an aasimar noble or power broker on the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md). Session audio renders her given name inconsistently, but she is repeatedly identified as Lady Blackthorn.

## Nature

Blackthorn carries angelic heritage with a complicated ancestry that makes her sympathetic to those judged by bloodline rather than action.

[Elisia](elisia.md) describes aasimar like her as rare beings whose heritage can make both celestials and mortals uneasy. Blackthorn herself argues that free will, compassion, and justice matter more than inherited nature.

## Defense of Elisia

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), Blackthorn intervenes when [Cardinal Preceptor Thornvale](cardinal-preceptor-thornvale.md) tries to rally the triage crowd against [Elisia](elisia.md).

She reminds Thornvale that the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) has laws and authorities beyond his church, and she supports [Juan](juan.md)'s argument that nurture and choice matter.

## Rumors

At the memorial for the fallen, Blackthorn privately tells [Zen](zen.md) that someone has spent years damaging her reputation. The rumors claim she is involved in illicit backroom deals, underground sin, and corrupt private gatherings.

She asks the party for help finding who is spreading the accusations. The timing is dangerous because religious and political power on the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) is shifting after [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md).

## Impersonation

Later that night, an invitation sent in Blackthorn's name leads [Juan](juan.md) and [Vylra](vylra.md) to [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md). The real Blackthorn is at the school when the warning arrives, confirming that the manor event is an impersonation or trap connected to [Lyris the Deceiver](lyris-the-deceiver.md) and the [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md).
