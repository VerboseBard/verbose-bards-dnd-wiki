# Lieutenant Brenic Ashlow

Lieutenant Brenic Ashlow is a quiet, survival-focused wave officer in the [Bloody Fifth](../factions/bloody-fifth.md) Session 1 command notes.

## Role

In the planning structure, Brenic commands Wave 4, the wave that exists mainly to show how little of the unit is left after the first attacks.
