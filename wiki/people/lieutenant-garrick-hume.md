# Lieutenant Garrick Hume

Lieutenant Garrick Hume, spelling provisional, is an elf lieutenant at [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md).

## Personality

Hume is decadent, lazy, and unprofessional when first encountered. He delays a convoy by sleeping late in luxurious officer quarters.

## Party Conflict

In [Session 3](../sessions/session-3.md), the party wakes him with salt water and command voice, humiliating him. He develops a grudge.

## Murder Implication

The party later finds a murdered fog team. Tracks and magical evidence implicate Hume or his team.

Open question: Hume may have personally killed them, ordered it, or been connected through magic.
