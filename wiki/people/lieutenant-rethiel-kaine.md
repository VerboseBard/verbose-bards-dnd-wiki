# Lieutenant Rethiel Kaine

Lieutenant Rethiel Kaine is an elf officer associated with the party's early war service and later [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md). Session 1 transcript notes include a surviving lieutenant nicknamed `Gandalf` by the table, but Session 2 later introduces a separate orc supervisor whose name drifts from `Gandalf` to [Colonel Gandor](colonel-gandor.md). The nickname assignment should remain unresolved rather than merged automatically.

His exact Session 1 fate has a continuity conflict: the existing actual-play chain keeps him alive into [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md), while a later command-hierarchy note lists a Rethiel Kaine as killed in the initial trench engagement.

## Personality and Role

Kaine is hardline anti-[Kreen](../factions/kreen.md). He has seen enough atrocities to view surrendering [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) as threats to be put down rather than prisoners to be protected.

## Key Events

- Survives the events of [Session 1](../sessions/session-1.md).
- In [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md), nearly executes the first surrendered [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) before the party and [Sergeant Quill](sergeant-quill.md) talk him down.
- During the [Norinar](../places/norinar.md) operation, moves to kill a surrendered [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md).
- A child jumps in front of the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) and is shot.
- Kaine is shot with his own pistol during the struggle with [Sergeant Quill](sergeant-quill.md).
- The party helps create a cover story blaming the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md).

## Status

Dead after the [Norinar](../places/norinar.md) operation unless later retconned.
