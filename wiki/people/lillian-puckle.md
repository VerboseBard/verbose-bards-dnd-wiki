# Lillian Puckle

Lillian Puckle is a Harengon seamstress and mourning-cloth keeper of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

## Role

Lillian is quietly emotional and deeply aware of the lost family stories. She is useful when rabbit grief needs to surface through ritual, cloth, and memory rather than direct argument.
