# Lord Inquisitor Boss

Lord Inquisitor Boss, also called Bosh in some accounts, is a tall aasimar [Inquisition](../factions/inquisition.md) authority in white robes or formal uniform.

## Role

He represents the terrifying side of the [Inquisition](../factions/inquisition.md): truth magic, public punishment, and uncompromising suspicion.

## Key Actions

- Uses the [Truth Lantern](../items/truth-lantern.md) to test whether the party was truthful in their report to [Captain Hail](captain-hail.md).
- Publicly executes [Colonel Marrow Vance](colonel-marrow-vance.md) and other corrupt officers/non-coms with light/fire bindings.
- Remains suspicious of the party even after [Colonel Core](colonel-core.md) closes the inquiry.
