# Luca

Luca is a male leonin, a Path of the Zealot frontliner, and a core member of the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md), connected to Gorthak's faith and the party's Hell-side rescue arc.

Older wiki pages and some transcript drift render his name as `Luka`. The current wiki treats [Luka](luka.md) as an alternate spelling and keeps this page as the active profile.

## Faith

Luca is devoted to Gorthak, a god of strength, struggle, and trials. [p'Op](p-op.md)'s own religious awakening begins when he joins Luca's baptism or dedication and unexpectedly feels that same divine power.

At the canon Session 1 final roster, Luca is a Barbarian 14 on the Path of the Zealot. He fills the role of zealous frontliner and divine berserker: a feral holy warrior whose rage is tied as much to faith and conviction as to raw violence.

## Citadel Stand

In the reconstructed events of [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 11](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-11.md), Luca stays behind on the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) while the others flee with [Phlukk](phlukk.md). Luca argues that the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) is worth fighting for and that his calling is to protect those still present.

His testimony later helps keep the Conclave from sending harsher forces after the party.

## Mission Against Julara

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), Luca returns to the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) with a mission from the churches. He has been told that [Julara](julara.md) is a dangerous sister-fragment feeding through [Paul](paul.md) and that she must be destroyed or [Paul](paul.md) must be freed from her.

Luca's arrival helps push [Paul](paul.md) to leave the party rather than risk [Julara](julara.md) being killed or forcibly merged.

## Control-Room Finale

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), Luca joins the party in the old dwarven control room. His zealot rage helps protect the living [Viscus](viscus.md) cutting when gray goo begins forming around a giant skeleton.

During the final fight, Luca nearly destroys the master goo outright, which would prevent [Viscus](viscus.md) from taking control of it. The party restrains, redirects, and eventually knocks him into the goo. He is briefly infected, but after [Viscus](viscus.md) claims the master network, Luca survives and purges the infection through divine force.
