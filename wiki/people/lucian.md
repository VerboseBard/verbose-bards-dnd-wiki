# Lucian

Lucian is the False Savior, Great Deceiver, Light That Lies, and architect of the gods' downfall.

## History

Once a minor devil or demon, Lucian rose by deception near the end of the [Second Age](../concepts/timeline.md). During the war between the fey and the dwarves, he stood victorious when the serpent god [Rexel Kar](rexel-kar.md) was slain, stealing the god's mantle and the worship of his people.

After a divine artifact fell from the heavens and shattered through the plains, Lucian survived and claimed it. With that artifact, he conquered the Hells, then the Abyss, reshaping the underworlds into his throne over four thousand years.

## Role in the Ages

Lucian's lies helped set up [The Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md), the [War of the Gods](../concepts/war-of-the-gods.md), and ultimately the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md). He convinced minor gods, demigods, powerful mortals, and eventually great heroes that the world was already dying and that the dragon guardians had to be sacrificed to save what could be saved.

The heroes killed the last dragon guardians with hope in their hearts and lies in their ears. The world shattered according to Lucian's design.

At the apex of his power, Lucian was struck down by the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md), but his death was not the end of his plan. His true victory was the sealing of the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md), which exiled the gods from the world they once ruled.

## Contingencies

Even after death, Lucian's influence endured through cults, artifacts, rituals, agents, and sleeper cells. His resurrection later became possible, but that inevitability was disrupted by [Julia / Julisia](julia-julisia.md), the fractured devil aspect he had once underestimated.

In [War of the Gods, Session 23](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-23.md), Lucian acts much more directly. He appears over the first [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) siege portals, kills [Alen](alen.md)'s manifested form with the god-killing blade and hourglass combination, and openly frames the crisis in soul-economy terms: if death collapses wrong and the worlds follow, he intends to claim what spills loose.

In [War of the Gods, Session 24](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-24.md), the party reaches him on the outside of the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md). Despite fear effects, layered barriers, and the catastrophic cost of even touching the god-killing blade, they force the weapon into him and survive the hourglass backlash that follows. That death shuts the open hell portals and saves the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), but later records make clear it was still not the absolute final destruction of every contingency Lucian had embedded across ages.

## Rax'al Karath Prophecy

During the Black Viper hunt, [Lucian's Splintered Echo](../concepts/lucians-splintered-echo.md) manifests through [Bishop Collins](bishop-collins.md)'s trapped soul. The echo claims Lucian controls the Church of [Rax'al Karath](../factions/cobra-cult-of-raxal-karath.md), explains that Lucian once consumed the serpent god and took the worship for himself, and says the cracked worlds now serve as the next egg in the hunger prophecy.

The echo confirms that the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) have sworn souls and thousands of lives toward ending the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) for what they did to him.

## Anchors

[Morir](morir.md) reveals that Lucian placed parts of his essence into weapons and other anchors, using a technique connected to a bargain with [Belle](belle.md). Fully manifested serpent avatars can also bring new pieces of Lucian's soul into the world as anchors.

Two [Lucian Anchor Blades](../items/lucian-anchor-blades.md) are destroyed when [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s ancient blades consume them, causing Lucian to scream twice across the psychic level.

## Crystal Trap

During [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s soul interrogation, Lucian uses a soul-level frequency to trigger [Lucian's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucians-crystal-trap.md). He resonates with the old wound he left in the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), detonates hidden soul contingencies in the prisoners, and manifests through [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s armor.

The fragment is destroyed before it can collapse the crystal, but the attack confirms that [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s soul is part of Lucian's resurrection lattice and that the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) itself is part of the return mechanism.

## Resurrection Lattice

[Greg](greg.md) later confirms that the crystal attack activated multiple linked rituals across the ley lines. [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s soul is divided into four fragments inside [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), making him an ideal component in Lucian's return.

The party can weaken Lucian by replacing [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s fragments with lesser or less ideal souls, but doing so may make the final fight harder by keeping Lucian below the threshold where the gods can directly intervene through a loophole in the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md).

By the time the party leaves the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), three of [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s four fragments have been removed from Lucian's lattice. The substitutions weaken the return, but the final fragment remains in play at the high temple route.

At the [High Temple of Rexel Kar](../places/high-temple-of-rexel-kar.md), the party removes the fourth [Jimbo](jimbo.md) fragment while disabling several of Lucian's remaining anchors. The failed lattice collapses inward, gathering Lucian's scattered essence into a single destroyable manifestation.

## Final Destruction

Lucian returns through the mantle of [Rexel Kar](rexel-kar.md) and fights the party, [Caden / Baba Yaga](caden-baba-yaga.md), and eventually [Tiamat](tiamat.md) on a desert battlefield. He survives counterspells, fire vulnerability, massive weapon strikes, poison breath, gravity magic, divine spellcasting, and a 10th-level Time Stop before fleeing to [Constant and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constant-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md).

At the school, Lucian tries to exploit the children as a divine shield while claiming he means them no harm. [Annabelle](annabelle.md) refuses his bargain and teleports the party to him.

[Mave](mave.md) then appears through a tear in reality and drives her reforged god-killing blades into Lucian's back, ending him at last.
