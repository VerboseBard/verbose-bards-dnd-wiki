# Lucius

Lucius is a powerful but unascended figure in the old divine order.

## Role

In the [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md), Lucius is listed among the major powers even though he never fully ascended.

## Bridget's Death

Lucius kills [Bridget](bridget.md), the old goddess of music, art, and beauty.

That death is one of the key succession points behind [Mave](mave.md)'s later rise into [Bridget](bridget.md)'s divine place.
