# Lyris the Deceiver

Lyris the Deceiver is an [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md) illusionist associated with [Wendell](wendell.md)'s Hell-side forces.

## Role

Lyris is a master of illusion and deception who can manipulate appearances and realities to confuse victims. She often appears as a tiefling child.

At the end of [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 7](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-7.md), a failed group luck check brings Lyris up the stairs into the fortress battle after [Morgrath the Enforcer](morgrath-the-enforcer.md) falls.

## Death

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 8](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-8.md), Lyris multiplies herself through illusions, charms frontline fighters, and lashes out with psychic magic. The party identifies the true body, breaks the charm, and [Luca](luca.md) kills her.

## Later Echo

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), an invitation in [Lady Seraphina Blackthorn](lady-seraphina-blackthorn.md)'s name leads [Juan](juan.md) and [Vylra](vylra.md) to [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md). The event is revealed as a Deceiver-linked trap, suggesting that Lyris's death did not end every illusion, echo, or contingency tied to her network.

In the reconstructed [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), that Deceiver-linked presence is killed by [Saraphel of the Dawnfather](saraphel-of-the-dawnfather.md) with divine retribution. Her final words are "Worth it," immediately before contingencies begin to unfold elsewhere on the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).
