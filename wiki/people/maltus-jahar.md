# Maltus Jahar

Maltus Jahar is a half-elf member of the Circle of Rebirth at [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Role

Maltus is a scholar and ritual-minded caretaker of rebirth, fascinated by how the Grove's fungal systems can turn decay into renewal.
