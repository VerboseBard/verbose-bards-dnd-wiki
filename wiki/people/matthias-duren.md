# Matthias Duren

Matthias Duren is the Hadozee elder of [Longbough](../factions/longbough.md) and the sole major clan speaker for the valley's Hadozee lines.

## Description

Matthias is a rangy glider with long dark membranes, bark-stained fingers, and a severe stillness that makes him seem always half in judgment.

## Role

He has held the Longbough seat for fourteen years. Matthias knows the full temple truth and treats [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md) as a sacred burden rather than merely a political secret.

## Household

His mate is Adelheid Duren, a female Hadozee rope-maker and tree-bridge planner. They have three adult children.

## Temperament

Matthias is intense, disciplined, severe, and deeply sincere. He is strongly anti-outsider and closely aligned with [the Old Charge](../factions/old-charge.md).
