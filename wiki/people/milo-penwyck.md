# Milo Penwyck

Milo Penwyck is a Jerbeen smallfolk speaker and civic helper within [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

## Role

Milo is nervous, earnest, and braver than he sounds. He is useful for showing the smaller Burrowroot families trying to participate despite pressure from stronger voices.
