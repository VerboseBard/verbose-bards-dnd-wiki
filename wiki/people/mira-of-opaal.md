# Mira of Opaal

Mira of Opaal is the daughter of [Aurora, Queen Beneath the Waves](aurora-queen-beneath-waves.md), and [Mulligan](mulligan.md), the Commodore reborn.

This is a different person from [Mira Quentis](mira-quentis.md) of [Caldrith's Gate](../places/caldriths-gate.md).

## View of Her Father

Mira knows the Commodore as her father, but she struggles with the public legacy attached to him. To much of the world, Molokan / Mulligan is a cursed betrayer who sided with [Wendell](wendell.md) and helped bring catastrophe to the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).

Aurora does not excuse everything he did, but she insists that he loved them and that his choices were shaped by survival, vengeance, and the ability to see through divine and political lies.

## Queen Beneath the Waves Material

The Moon Stone Collectors Elsewhere scenes place Mira in the Coral Palace on [Opaal](../places/opaal.md), walking with Aurora through the Pink Coral Halls as the old seal begins to fail.

Her questions about Molokan frame the reader's understanding of the Commodore as both savior and betrayer.
