# Mireya Bellan

Mireya Bellan is a Cervan of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md), [Tavero Bellan](tavero-bellan.md)'s mate, and a household matriarch in [Burrowroot Warren](../places/burrowroot-warren.md).

## Role

Mireya arranges hospitality for guests in a way that is graceful, formal, and controlled. Around the party, she can make confinement feel polite rather than openly hostile.
