# Morgrath the Enforcer

Morgrath the Enforcer is one of the [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md) leaders tied to [Wendell](wendell.md)'s Hell-side forces.

## Role

Morgrath is a brute-force demon with immense strength and a fearsome presence. The [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md) uses him to ensure that captives do not escape.

During [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 7](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-7.md), he confronts the party inside [Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress](../places/wendells-sixth-layer-fortress.md).

## Death

Morgrath is weakened by the broader assault on the fortress, then caught in [Zen](zen.md)'s Hold Monster after spending his legendary resistance. [p'Op](p-op.md) uses the paralysis window to deliver a devastating burst of radiant lance strikes, and Morgrath is killed.
