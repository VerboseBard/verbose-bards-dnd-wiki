# Morir

Morir is a [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) defector who shifts from enemy operative to reluctant intelligence source during the hunt for [Lucius](lucius.md)'s surviving network.

## Earlier Role

Morir is part of the broader Viper cell still active after the murders of [Jimbo](jimbo.md) and [Charlie](charlie.md). By the time the party meets him directly, he is no longer acting like a loyal ideologue. He is acting like someone who wants out before [Lucius](lucius.md)'s return consumes everyone still tied to those oaths.

Before defecting, Morir operated on the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) as an underworld fixer and odd-jobs contact with ties to [Tony](tony.md)'s delivery and information network.

## Hostage Meeting

Morir captures a [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) jungle team and uses them to force a protected meeting with [Junvara](junvara.md).

At the [Old Silverbend Mines](../places/old-silver-bend-mines.md), he returns the unconscious [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) members in exchange for a conversation and safe departure. The meeting is not friendly, but it is honest enough to reshape the whole hunt.

Morir claims he entered the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) through soul-bound oaths without fully understanding the demonic structure behind them. Once his handler dies, he becomes functionally free for the first time and starts trying to survive the fallout.

## Intelligence Provided

Morir's information becomes the bridge from [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) fallout into the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) and jungle strikes. He reveals:

- [Sir Hellspire](sir-hellspire.md) holds [Charlie](charlie.md)'s soul and uses it for design insight
- Hellspire has a primary workshop in the [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md) and manufacturing in [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md)
- [Dark Star](dark-star.md) is obsessed with restoring [Lucius](lucius.md) and destroying the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md)
- [Winrah Holm](winrah-holm.md) has become a powerful anti-god speaker and serpent-church leader
- [Dark Star](dark-star.md) will appear at the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md) in [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) in two days

Morir also gives the party blades containing pieces of [Lucius](lucius.md)'s essence, making him one of the few defections in the setting that produces immediately actionable strategic value.

## Motive

Morir is not portrayed as noble. He wants to remain free, avoid being killed by the party, and avoid being reabsorbed into [Lucius](lucius.md)'s hierarchy if the resurrection succeeds.

What makes him useful is that his hatred for the surviving Viper leadership appears genuine even when his self-interest obviously is too.
