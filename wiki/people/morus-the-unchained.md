# Morus the Unchained

Morus the Unchained is an angelic guardian serving alongside [Cardinal Preceptor Thornvale](cardinal-preceptor-thornvale.md).

## Appearance

Morus appears as a large winged figure in plate armor and a white cloak. When tension rises, his wings spread and his presence pushes the crowd back.

## Role

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), Morus steps forward after [Vylra](vylra.md) physically intimidates Thornvale during the argument over [Elisia](elisia.md). He warns that there are consequences for touching the Holy Father and signals that the confrontation could become a fight.

The standoff is defused by the arrival of the [Collar of Demon Subjugation](../items/collar-of-demon-subjugation.md), preventing immediate violence.
