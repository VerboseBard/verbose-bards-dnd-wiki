# Mother Superior of Rax'al Karath

The Mother Superior of Rax'al Karath is a yuan-ti or serpent-cult leader who attends [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s casino table.

## Casino Meeting

She appears during the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md) operation alongside nobles, religious agents, and disguised contacts. She agrees to attend the later island after-party arranged by [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s [Central Toge](central-toge.md) impersonation.
