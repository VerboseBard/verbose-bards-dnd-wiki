# Mulligan

Mulligan was the original leader of the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md), a major Fifth-Age figure whose betrayal helped open the path for [Lucius](lucius.md)'s final war.

## Role in the War

Mulligan fell to Lucius's influence and allowed evil forces into the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), causing catastrophic loss of life. His party eventually killed him, then pursued Lucius and struck the deceiver down on the great crystal.

## Maeve's Ascension

Before his betrayal fully defined his legacy, Mulligan also played a critical role in the ascension of [Maeve, the Schoolmistress](mave.md).

After [Bridget](bridget.md) was killed by [Lucius](lucius.md), Maeve was badly damaged. [Belle](belle.md) and Mulligan tried to keep her from dying, but Belle was pulled into direct battle with Lucius at the school. Mulligan carried Maeve to the school's temple shrine and used a dungeon core, soul coins, and the released divine magic from Bridget's death to stabilize her and help her ascend into Bridget's place as goddess of knowledge, art, beauty, and learning.
