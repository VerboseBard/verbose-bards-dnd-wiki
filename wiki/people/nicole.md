# Nicole

Nicole is a white-haired musician and recurring ally tied to both [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s late-[Sixth Age](../concepts/sixth-age.md) story and the broader [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) school-and-performance circles.

## Early War of the Gods Presence

Nicole appears in the school-era [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md) before the later Black Viper rescue arc.

In [War of the Gods, Session 9](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-9.md), [Kestrel](kestrel.md) meets her in an upstairs performance space where the two of them fall into a long shared musical exchange. [Maeve](mave.md) and Nicole's father Arthur are both present by the end of it, turning the moment into something halfway between courtship, worship, and public recognition.

In [War of the Gods, Session 11](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-11.md), Nicole is part of the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) pub-crawl social web that keeps the party tied to the city's living culture even while infernal labor disputes and Black Viper pressure are rising around them.

## Music

Nicole trains with [Kestrel](kestrel.md) at one of his [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) music-shop identities. Her music is powerful enough to release tension and emotion in those who hear it.

## Shifter's Mask

During the Black Viper hunt, Nicole gives [Kestrel](kestrel.md) the [Shifter's Mask](../items/shifters-mask.md), an ancient [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) artifact she recovered from a dangerous temple expedition with help from [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s uncle and her wolfen guards.

Nicole offers to help with the final rescue of [Jimbo](jimbo.md), though doing so would require sneaking away from her household.

Nicole later joins the reinforcement wave for [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), arriving at the first ritual site with her wolfen guards during the party's short rest.

## Moon Stone Collectors Note

Later [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) material also mentions a Nicole who performs with [Kendrick](kendrick.md) and is described as [Arthur Saran](arthur-saran.md)'s daughter. It is not yet confirmed whether that Nicole is the same person as this page or a different character with the same name.
