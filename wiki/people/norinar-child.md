# Norinar Child

The [Norinar](../places/norinar.md) child is an unnamed or not-yet-confirmed eight-year-old girl central to the tragedy of [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md). Planning notes may have named her Mira Talren, but actual-play transcript confirmation is still open.

## Role

She appears to have befriended or communicated with the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) in [Norinar](../places/norinar.md). The keep later contains a classroom-like space where a child had been teaching [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) the alphabet.

## Death

During the [Norinar](../places/norinar.md) operation, she jumps in front of a surrendered [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) when [Lieutenant Rethiel Kaine](lieutenant-rethiel-kaine.md) attempts to kill it. She is shot and dies.

## Significance

Her death marks the moral center of the [Norinar](../places/norinar.md) incident. It suggests the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) relationship with the village may not have been simple mind control.
