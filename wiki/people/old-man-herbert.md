# Old Man Herbert

Old Man Herbert was the village gardener who first recognized Constance's talent for tinkering.

## Mentor

Herbert encouraged Constance's gifts and helped turn survival-minded improvisation into real craft. He also taught her how to care for a bonded dragon with a disability, which later mattered deeply for her life with Zuk.

He died when Constance was still young, and the loss hit both her and Gadzooks hard.
