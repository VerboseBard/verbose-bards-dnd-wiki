# Oscar

Oscar was an orc wizard in the Fourth-Age assault on [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md).

## Session 8 Memory

In [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), Oscar is found dead in [Bartholomew Hildebrant](bartholomew-hildebrant.md)'s recovered memory of the temple assault. His burned corpse remains in the same chamber where Bartholomew wakes beside [Katrina](katrina.md), shattered spider eggs, dead drow, and dead giant spiders.

A later memory from Katrina implies that Oscar was out of spells when she had to choose whom to save. That detail frames him as part of the same doomed strike team rather than a random corpse in the room.
