# p'Op

p'Op is a male grung and one of the core defenders of the [Moon Stone Collectors](../campaign/moonstone-collectors.md).

## Role

At the canon Session 1 final roster, p'Op is a Fighter 14 / Bard 1 built as a cavalier defender and mounted tactician.

He rides [Soda](soda.md), a giant honey badger, and is known for using Hold the Line and Unwavering Mark to protect allies while remaining practical, steady, and loyal.

## Moon Stone Collectors

Across the surviving session material, older shorthand often calls him p'Op, but p'Op is the canon spelling.

He is one of the party's defining mounted frontliners. He helps anchor the Hell fights, lands devastating radiant lance bursts against [Morgrath the Enforcer](morgrath-the-enforcer.md), uses the [Iron Bands of Bilarro](../items/iron-bands-of-bilarro.md) for restraint work, and stays central to the party's battlefield shape even when the narrative focus is elsewhere.

His bond with [Soda](soda.md) is one of the campaign's most emotionally immediate hooks from the start, especially after [Soda](soda.md) is nearly lost to infernal necrotic infection during the opening ice-ship ambush.
