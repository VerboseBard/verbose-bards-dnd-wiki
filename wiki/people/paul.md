# Paul

Paul is a male minotaur and one of the main front-line powers in the [Moon Stone Collectors](../campaign/moonstone-collectors.md).

## Background

Roster and note material describe Paul as an unusually small or compact minotaur with giant ancestry. He wants to understand giants not just academically but personally, because learning their lost ways is tied to his own growth, identity, and power.

The same notes connect him to [Phlukk](phlukk.md) long before the campaign. The two grew up around each other, got into trouble together, and carried that history into the party. Paul is one of the clearest examples of the campaign's recurring pattern that old friendship can survive even when the world keeps changing shape around it.

## Role

Roster snapshot: Level 15 minotaur, Barbarian, Path of the Giant. Player: Intuitivemoose.

He is the party's heavy bruiser and tank: a towering juggernaut who grows when enraged, dominates space physically, and solves many problems by refusing to be moved.

## Scholarship and Temptation

Paul is not only a bruiser. He is one of the party members most actively pursuing lost giant and dwarven knowledge.

At [Frozenheart University](../places/frozenheart-university.md), in the library, and later with [Professor Hazard](professor-hazard.md), he compares maps, studies runes, and helps trace possible giant-city locations buried in the ice. That research becomes one of the party's cleanest links between old giant history, lost dwarven settlements, and the later goo crisis.

At the same time, Paul is one of the characters most vulnerable to outside leverage. A cursed book leaves him with a compulsion to summon a succubus, and that problem eventually pulls him into direct contact with [Julara](julara.md).

## Moon Stone Collectors

Paul appears repeatedly across the surviving session material as one of the group's most durable and consequential members.

He helps hold the line in the opening [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md) escort, studies the infernal gauntlet and other dwarven relics, becomes deeply entangled with [Julara](julara.md)'s bargains, and later becomes the direct target of [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) punishment after destroying the grove's mushroom-circle connection.

During the Hell and fortress phases he is one of the main shock troops. He enlarges, absorbs hits, swings oversized weapons, and helps break multiple hard fights open by force. The same giant path that makes him physically dominant also frames his most important quiet scenes: map study, translation work, and the attempt to understand what the old worlds were before everything shattered.

His bargain with [Julara](julara.md) matters because it is not only power for power's sake. In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), he trades access, memory, and perspective to end a compulsion and to get clearer answers about what the party has touched. That agreement later lets [Julara](julara.md) see through him more easily and binds his personal story directly to hers.

That punishment manifests as the [Crown of Fools](../items/crown-of-fools.md), a visible curse that becomes one of Paul's defining late-campaign burdens. [Julara](julara.md) later suppresses the crown's faerie-fire effect with a cold-iron amulet and reveals that the Keeper of the Gates' curse stole 64 years from Paul. The crown is ultimately removed, and those years restored, when the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md) repairs the broken link to [Viscus](viscus.md).

In the hydra battle, Paul is one of the main reasons the party survives. He grows to enormous size, sears heads shut with fire, smashes through ice armor, and eventually throws the ancient ice hydra's body into its ember mate. That sequence turns him from "big hitter" into one of the battle's defining problem-solvers.

Paul eventually leaves the active party rather than allow [Julara](julara.md) to be hunted through his connection to her. That departure is in character with the rest of his arc: when he finally chooses, he chooses to carry risk on himself instead of letting it roll downhill onto everyone else.

Later, Julara merges with [Wisdom](wisdom.md) into [Sovereignty](sovereignty.md), which changes the practical shape of Paul's departure but does not erase why he made the choice.
