# Paxton

Paxton is [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s uncle from [Palewood](../places/palewood.md).

## Description

Paxton is known as a tall human man with short dark hair and dark eyes.

He is a guide and gloomhaze explorer. He traveled widely when he was younger, but became more present in [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s life as Kestrel grew older.

Paxton gave [Kestrel](kestrel.md) the twin shapeshifting blades that became [Kestrel's Ancient Blades](../items/kestrels-ancient-blades.md).
