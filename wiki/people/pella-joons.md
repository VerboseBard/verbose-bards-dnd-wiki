# Pella Joons

Pella Joons is one of the [Caldrith's Gate](../places/caldriths-gate.md) guild receptionists.

## Description

She is an elderly human woman with a sweet smile and a dagger under the desk. She has been here longer than the council and knows every secret hallway and adventurer nickname.
