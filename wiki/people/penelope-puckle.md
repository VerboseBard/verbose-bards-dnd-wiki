# Penelope Puckle

Penelope Puckle is a young Harengon of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md) who serves on the first-contact patrol in [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md).

## Description

Penelope is curious, socially open by local standards, and especially interested in [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md) and the idea of a world beyond the fog.

## Role

Among the patrol, Penelope most clearly represents the rabbit-side hunger for stories, movement, and lost kinship. Her curiosity contrasts sharply with the more guarded tone set by older or more security-minded members of the patrol.

During the next-morning escort to [Hollowmere](../places/hollowmere.md), Penelope functions as a rabbit witness and runner, keeping the rabbit emotional thread present while official security controls the party.

In [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), Penelope remains part of the escort to the edge of the [Dead Lands](../places/dead-lands.md), where the party moves beyond the locals' permitted path into the forbidden temple approach.
