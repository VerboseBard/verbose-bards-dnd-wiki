# Perrian Halfstar

Perrian Halfstar is a small frost-gnome or halfling-associated druid of the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md), known for working with the grove's giant arctic foxes.

## Role

Perrian helps train and manage the foxes used by grove hunters. She is tied to the grove's rebirth traditions and speaks comfortably with animals and hive-like companions.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), Perrian rides with the hunters who meet the party outside the grove and warns them that infected undead may not remain obedient if raised.
