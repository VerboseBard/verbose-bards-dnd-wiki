# Phlukk

Phlukk is a male owlin and one of the core members of the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md), though a long stretch of the campaign revolves around rescuing and restoring him.

## Role

At the canon Session 1 final roster, Phlukk is a Rogue 12 / Blood Hunter 1 who serves as assassin, scout, and chaos agent.

In the opening mission, the party finds that Phlukk has already been teleported ahead with the real emissary as part of the fake-priest escort plan. Soon after, the larger campaign finds that he has left behind a note, payment threads, and a hidden bow, but not a clean explanation of where he went.

His room has been sterilized so thoroughly that it feels as if someone removed his presence on purpose.

## Hell Rescue

By [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 6](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-6.md), the party confirms that Phlukk is held in the Dark Hole, [Wendell](wendell.md)'s sixth-layer Hell fortress.

The rescue becomes part of a larger assault after [Jimbo](jimbo.md) and [Charlie](charlie.md) are killed and their souls are stolen by forces using demonic dwarven artifacts.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 8](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-8.md), the party frees Phlukk from [Wendell](wendell.md)'s prison. He is alive but comatose or near-comatose, and [Elisia](elisia.md) warns that his condition is precarious.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), healers confirm that Phlukk's body is whole but his mind is unreachable. [Zen](zen.md)'s deep mental inspection finds memories but no active presence. The school places him in magical medical stasis until a mind healer can return.

In the reconstructed [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 11](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-11.md), Phlukk's stasis shield survives the attack on the school. Blood runs down the outside of the barrier, but the protected body inside remains intact.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 12](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-12.md), [Vylra](vylra.md) leads the party toward the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md), hoping its chromate fungal network can help Phlukk's absent mind.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), Phlukk is restored by the grove's Ritual of Fated Remembrance. The ritual replaces or repairs missing memories with fungal-spore memory work and leaves a fragment of [Viscus](viscus.md) inside his skull.

Phlukk wakes with no memory of the long rescue arc. From his perspective, the last clear memory is dropping off the priest before the trap, then waking in the grove.

## Viscus Connection

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), [Viscus](viscus.md) reaches Phlukk in dreams. Phlukk experiences the grove through [Viscus](viscus.md)'s roots, plants, bees, undead workers, and mushroom circles.

This reveals that Phlukk carries enough of [Viscus](viscus.md) to communicate through the network. It also means that if the party's living [Viscus](viscus.md) cutting is destroyed before reaching the goo control room, Phlukk may be one of the people capable of sacrificing himself to preserve [Viscus](viscus.md) while stopping the master goo.

Phlukk also lets [Paul](paul.md) leave the party after [Paul](paul.md) explains that he cannot let [Julara](julara.md) be killed.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), Phlukk's truesight helps the party navigate the dwarven trap gauntlet. His still-new instinctive spellcasting also causes trouble when he tries to reincarnate a long-dead giant skeleton, stirring gray goo around the bones instead.

The incident is contained, and Phlukk survives to see [Viscus](viscus.md) claim the master goo network.
