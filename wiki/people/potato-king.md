# The Potato King

The Potato King was a halfling king, old ally of [Belle](belle.md), and member of the pre-[Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md) circle that later became entangled in divine trials, soul claims, and the war around [Lucius](lucius.md).

Some current notes tentatively call him Tolman, but that spelling still needs confirmation.

## Relationship with Belle

Belle treated the Potato King as one of her closest old companions: a friend, party member, and almost-brother from before the Breaking. She tried more than once to convince him to ascend toward godhood, but he refused.

That refusal made his death much more important. If he would not become divine and remain beside Belle willingly, then his soul could still become the thing she kept.

## Death and Soul Claim

During the attack and assassination tied to [Lucius](lucius.md)'s larger divine war, the Potato King was present and vulnerable. Belle was not aware of the attack beforehand, but when the moment came, she faced a choice: save him, or allow him to die and claim his soul before [Dione](dione.md) could take him.

Belle chose not to protect him.

This did not make her the direct killer, but it became one of the core accusations against her. Her inaction let her claim the soul of an old friend, weakened another divine line, and gave her enemies a way to argue that she had acted selfishly even without personally causing the assassination.
