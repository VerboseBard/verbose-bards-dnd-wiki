# Professor Hazard

Professor Hazard is a dwarf artificer and scholar at [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](../places/lady-penny-hathaways-school.md).

## Role

Hazard is notable because he is a rare dwarf who is not part of [Wendell](wendell.md)'s corrupted dwarven forces. He dislikes [Wendell](wendell.md) and expects that [Wendell](wendell.md) would eventually need to be dealt with.

During [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 6](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-6.md), Hazard equips the party with experimental anti-demon tools before their dive into Hell.

## Scholarship

Hazard helps [Paul](paul.md) compare old giant maps with his own pre-[Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md) material. The comparison reveals that a lost giant city may be buried inside a glacier near [Sorrel](sorrel.md)'s grove on the ice world.

He gives [Paul](paul.md) beginner texts on dwarven-to-common translation, rune work, enchanting, artificery, forging, metallurgy, chemistry, and blacksmithing.
