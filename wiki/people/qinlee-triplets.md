# Qinlee Triplets

The Qinlee triplets are Daisy, Violet, and Rose Qinlee, three sisters from [Opaal](../places/opaal.md) tied to [Bowing Reed](../places/bowing-reed.md).

## Prophecy and Awakening

The triplets are a major anomaly. The chance that all three sisters would awaken to powers should have been considered impossible if not for a gnomish prophecy spoken over them by their old nursemaid.

## Sisters

- [Daisy Qinlee](daisy-qinlee.md) is the strongest of the triplet sisters. She has a keen eye for details, insight into people, and a mischievous streak that leads her into practical jokes that sometimes go a bit too far.
- [Violet Qinlee](violet-qinlee.md) is the most outgoing of the sisters. She loves music, talking to other people, and movement. She is very fast and gifted with magic.
- [Rose Qinlee](rose-qinlee.md) is not the sharpest tool in the shed and has a bit of a temper, but she sees the best in her friends and is loyal to a fault.

## Seeker Pickup

[Seeker Lindsey](seeker-lindsey.md) is associated with picking up the triplets during the early [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md) gathering phase.
