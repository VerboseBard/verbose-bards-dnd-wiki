# Quentin Riddle

Quentin Riddle is a Harengon retired scribe and name-keeper of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md).

## Role

Quentin is proud of names and records. He becomes especially interested if [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md) mentions Clementine Riddle or other lost family-name threads.
