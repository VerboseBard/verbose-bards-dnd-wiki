# Raven Queen of Saharun

The Raven Queen of Saharun is [Alen](alen.md), the death queen associated with [Saharun](../places/saharun.md).

## Role

[Alen](alen.md) ruled [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) before ascending during or after the [Kreen War](../concepts/kreen-war.md). Her ascension contributes to a major power vacuum and is one of the reasons [Saharun](../places/saharun.md)'s later political and religious structure is so unstable.

## Identity

This is not a separate person from [Alen](alen.md). "Raven Queen of Saharun" is the throne-name or divine title associated with [Alen](alen.md)'s death sovereignty.

That distinction helps reconcile several campaign threads:

- [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) material emphasizes [Alen](alen.md) as a vampire queen pursuing death and godhood.
- [Sixth Age](../concepts/sixth-age.md) and [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) material more often use Raven Queen language when dealing with [Saharun](../places/saharun.md), [Lilith](lilith.md), and [Gregario](gregario-shaq.md).
- Later inheritance language about [Lilith](lilith.md)'s possible ascension refers to [Alen](alen.md)'s former mantle, not to a different goddess.

## Mantle

After [Annabelle](annabelle.md) severs [Alen](alen.md)'s remaining influence from [Gregario / Shaq](gregario-shaq.md), the Raven Queen mantle shifts toward [Lilith](lilith.md). [Lilith](lilith.md) is not yet fully ascended, but the threat of [Alen](alen.md) reclaiming or consuming that future is removed.

## Conclave Conflict

The [Conclave of Churches](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md) refused to recognize her worship because she was alive and ruling on the mortal side of the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md). [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) cut ties afterward.
