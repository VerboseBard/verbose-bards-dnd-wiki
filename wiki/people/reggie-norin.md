# Reggie Norin

Reggie Norin is a Vulpin associated with [Briarstep](../factions/briarstep.md) who serves on the Sunhollow first-contact patrol in [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md).

## Description

Reggie presents himself as clever, observant, and only partly forthcoming. He understands more than he first lets on and is later revealed to be following the conversation through magic rather than ignorance.

## Role

Reggie becomes one of the first locals to grasp the scale of the valley's danger once the party explains how outsiders would value the valley's ether crystals. He is a useful early example of a valley local whose suspicion of outsiders coexists with real practical intelligence.

Session 7 also shows Reggie as one of the first locals to bridge the language gap through magic. He admits he has a little magic that helps with languages, and his translation work lets the party communicate more directly before formal council magic takes over.

When Jefferson shows processed outside ether, Reggie can feel the difference between local raw crystal and the party's refined or altered crystal. His reaction helps establish that Sunhollow's people know crystals have value, but not the full military/economic meaning outsiders will assign to them.

In [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), Reggie accompanies the party toward the [Dead Lands](../places/dead-lands.md). At the edge of the forbidden approach, he warns them not to trust everything the dead say, then says goodbye using the profanity [Jose](jose.md) helped teach [Voss](voss-kenku.md).
