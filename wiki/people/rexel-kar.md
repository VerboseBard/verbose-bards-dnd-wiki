# Rexel Kar

Rexel Kar, also known to his cult as Rax'al Karath, was a serpent god slain near the end of the [Second Age](../concepts/timeline.md).

## Significance

When Rexel Kar died, [Lucius](lucius.md) stood victorious and stole the god's mantle and the worship of his people. This was the moment [Lucius](lucius.md) stopped being merely a creature of the Hells and became a god in all but name.

## Cultic Memory

Before [the Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md), Rax'al Karath was worshiped by yuan-ti, grung, and other jungle peoples. He was defeated by [Tiamat](tiamat.md) when she was summoned to the plane, but memory and worship preserved his cult.

During the era when the worlds were separate, the cult returned to prominence without realizing that [Lucius](lucius.md) had usurped the god's place and was drawing power from the worship.

## Prophetic Cycle

The hidden cave shrine reframes Rexel Kar's defeat as part of a larger cycle of hunger. [Lucius](lucius.md) ate the serpent god and became stronger, but the [Rax'al Karath Prophecy](../concepts/raxal-karath-prophecy.md) suggests that what eats may also be eaten. The cracked worlds may now be the shell from which the next serpent or [Lucius](lucius.md)'s altered return emerges.
