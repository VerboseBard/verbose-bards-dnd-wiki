# Rupert Hooley

Rupert Hooley is a Harengon of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md), [Beatrix Hooley](beatrix-hooley.md)'s mate, and a household organizer in [Burrowroot Warren](../places/burrowroot-warren.md).

## Role

Rupert is polite, anxious, and protective. [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md)'s arrival visibly shakes him because it threatens to reopen the rabbit families' buried political and emotional history.
