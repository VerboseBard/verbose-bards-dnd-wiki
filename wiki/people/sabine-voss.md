# Sabine Voss

Sabine Voss is the Owlin elder of [Windcrest](../factions/windcrest.md) and one of the five seated elders of [the Sunhollow Council](../factions/sunhollow-council.md).

## Description

Sabine is a pale-feathered owl woman with silver-ringed eyes and a habit of becoming perfectly still while others reveal more than they mean to.

## Role

She has led Windcrest for sixteen years. Sabine knows the full truth of [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md), but unlike the hardest old-guard elders, she privately questions whether secrecy alone is still protecting the valley.

## Household

Her mate is Dorian Voss, a male Owlin record-keeper and night-scout. They have two adult children.

## Temperament

Sabine is observant, dry, careful, and politically difficult to read. She is cautious rather than welcoming toward outsiders and is often associated with [the Open Trail](../factions/open-trail.md), though she still takes the old temple duty seriously.
