# Saraphel of the Dawnfather

Saraphel is a champion of the Dawnfather associated with the [Conclave of Churches](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md).

## Conclave Champion

Saraphel is previously named among the Conclave champions sent to aid [Junvara](junvara.md) during the assaults on [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md).

## Ebonwing Manor

In the reconstructed events of [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), Saraphel enters the battle at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md) after a strong luck result brings reinforcements to the party.

Saraphel kills the Deceiver-linked enemy with a blade of divine retribution, snuffing her out of existence before she can escape.
