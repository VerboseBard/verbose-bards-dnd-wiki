# Savras

Savras is one of the old gods of foreknowledge and prophecy.

## Role

In the [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md), Savras and [Dione](dione.md) are collectively described as second in power.

The Seekers are identified as their children.
