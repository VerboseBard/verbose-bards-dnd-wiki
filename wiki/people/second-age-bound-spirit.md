# Second Age Bound Spirit

The Second Age Bound Spirit is an ancient drow-like or elven woman found beneath [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md)'s storage area in [Session 4](../sessions/session-4.md).

## Known Facts

- She is from the [Second Age](../concepts/second-age.md).
- She was buried alive or imprisoned in a coffin of pain.
- Her soul was bound by ritual.
- She does not remember her own name.
- She remembers the being who bound her: [The Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md).
- Her protector became the hostile ghost the party destroyed.
- She can learn or bridge language supernaturally when the party makes contact with her.

## Lore

She remembers an older cosmology of four creator-gods:

- Creator
- Sculptor
- Painter
- [The Big Shad](../concepts/big-shad.md)

She fought in an ancient war with giants and recognizes Goliaths as descendants of giants.

## Current State

She senses that the land and magical flows are wrong, broken, and sick. She also senses the [Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md)'s power nearby. After spending too much power, she fades back to sleep.

Later campaign events make her warning feel more credible rather than less. The ruins, divine remnants, and dimensional anomalies encountered after [Fort Victory](../places/fort-victory.md) all point toward the same conclusion: the world around [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) really is layered with much older damage than ordinary military history can explain.
