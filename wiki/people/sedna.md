# Sedna

Sedna is the goddess of sea and sky in the old pantheon.

## Role

She is one of the named figures preserved in the [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md).

## Campaign Echoes

[Hawk](hawk.md)'s [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) notes include Wilbrix unrest tied to Sedna's worship, showing that her cult or church remained politically important in mortal life.
