# Seeker Lauren

Seeker Lauren is one of the Quartet, four identical sisters who belong to the [Seekers](../factions/seekers.md), an order of clerics under the Triumphant of Divination Gods.

## Personality

Lauren rebels against being indistinguishable from her sisters. She dyes her hair pink and wears pigtails so people can tell her apart.

## Known Assignments

Lauren found Jeffrey and Anton. She was later sent to pick up Zach's character.
