# Seeker Linda

Seeker Linda is one of the Quartet, four identical sisters who belong to the [Seekers](../factions/seekers.md), an order of clerics under the Triumphant of Divination Gods.

## Personality

Linda is easygoing and kind, with a good sense of humor.

## Known Assignments

Linda picked up Katie's character and Jaden's character. Existing session summaries identify her as the Seeker who found [Constance and Zuk](constance-l-coggeshall.md) in the [Elven Lands](../places/elven-lands.md).
