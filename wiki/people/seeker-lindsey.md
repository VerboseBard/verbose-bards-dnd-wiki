# Seeker Lindsey

Seeker Lindsey is one of the Quartet, four identical sisters who belong to the [Seekers](../factions/seekers.md), an order of clerics under the Triumphant of Divination Gods.

## Personality

Lindsey is prickly and grumpy.

## Known Assignments

Lindsey picked up Tim's character and the [Qinlee Triplets](qinlee-triplets.md). Existing session summaries also identify her as the Seeker who found [Molokan](molokan.md) on the beach.
