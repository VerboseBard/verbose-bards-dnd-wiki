# Seeker Lucia

Seeker Lucia is one of the Quartet, four identical sisters who belong to the [Seekers](../factions/seekers.md), an order of clerics under the Triumphant of Divination Gods.

## Personality

Lucia is a little awkward and socially uncertain.

## Known Assignments

Lucia picked up Nate's character and Josh's character. She is also tied to [Experiment 69](experiment-69.md)'s arrival during the early mine arc.
