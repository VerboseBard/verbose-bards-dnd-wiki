# Sgt. Jefferson Stone

Sgt. Jefferson Stone is Zach's main-party tiefling fighter and veteran in the [After the War Campaign](../campaign/after-the-war-campaign.md).

The transcript material can blur his name through auto-captioning, but the canon name for this character in the [Seventh Age](../concepts/seventh-age.md) After the War arc is Sgt. Jefferson Stone.

## Background

User canon identifies Jefferson Stone as:

- a tiefling
- an orphan from the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)
- a soldier who joined the military when the war broke out
- a character who began earlier in the campaign arc at corporal-level responsibility before becoming the sergeant the group kept leaning on

The surviving character-sheet material also frames him as a disciplined front-line fighter whose core values include `Fairness`, `Respect`, and `Honor`.

## Recorded Appearance

The currently imported local transcripts do not preserve a full prose physical description of Jefferson's face, horns, hair, or coloration.

What they do preserve is enough to establish that he presents as:

- a tiefling soldier
- a front-line fighter in military gear
- someone physically durable enough to take point repeatedly in war and contractor operations

If a fuller visual description shows up in later notes or transcripts, this page should be expanded with it.

## War and Survival

Session 1 notes place Jefferson in Wave 3, Squad 12 as a tiefling corporal with `Chain of Command` and `Steady Presence` traits. His squad includes [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md), [Bevar Lurton](bevar-lurton.md), and [Captain Sgt. Jose](jose.md), making that squad the clearest early root of the later main party.

Jefferson survives the [Bloody Fifth](../factions/bloody-fifth.md), the transfer into [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md), and the slow moral collapse of postwar [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md).

By the time the campaign shifts into contractor work, he has become one of the party's main responsibility magnets: the soldier who keeps being asked to make choices, hold the line, and absorb the consequences.

## Crashed Ship

In the [Crashed Ship](../places/crashed-ship.md) arc beginning in [Session 4](../sessions/session-4.md), Jefferson helps turn the hidden wreck from a lethal secret into a usable long-term asset.

During the Session 4 ship fight, he helps break the ship's robotic defenders and recovers the [Plasma Greatblade](../items/two-handed-plasma-blade.md) from the elite melee unit.

The raw Session 4 transcript also gives Jefferson one of the voluntary nanite upgrades: a mixed intelligence/charisma enhancement that raises both stats by 5. This should remain tied to Session 4 unless later table notes revise the exact distribution.

In Session 5, Jefferson continues using the plasma weapon through the ship corridor and engineering fights. His command/threat language against the ship defenders supports the party's larger tactic of using Jose's captaincy and contradictory authority claims to destabilize the hostile combat AI.

During the return visit in Session 6, he:

- studies the ship's internal layout closely enough to understand how logical the [Kex](../places/kex.md)'s design really is
- watches the nanite-repair drones rebuilding hull breaches and damaged passages
- helps judge how long the vessel may take to become useful again
- receives the first upgrade to the [Plasma Greatblade](../items/two-handed-plasma-blade.md), expanding its critical range to 18-20
- has one ship augmentation module installed into his drone

## Lost Homeland Mission

Once the party leaves the ship, Jefferson remains central to the [Lost Homeland Mission](../concepts/lost-homeland-mission.md)'s hardest stretch through storm country, ruined passes, and dead-end routes.

He is present for:

- the magical lightning storm in the mountains
- the mutated displacer beast fight
- the chimera attack in the rune-ruins
- the final approach into [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md)

## Hestia's Blessing

Session 6 gives Jefferson one of the campaign's clearest spiritual turning points.

After the [Music Box Anomaly](../items/music-box-anomaly.md) swallows the chimera and reveals something horrific beyond it, Jefferson's blood activates an altar in the ruins. That contact reveals [Hestia](hestia.md), a Fourth-Age goddess of hearth and refuge long thought to have been killed in the [Fourth Age](../concepts/fourth-age.md). Her manifestation feels maternal, sheltering, and profoundly unlike the malign force inside the box.

She leaves Jefferson with [Hestia's Hearth Pendant](../items/hestias-hearth-pendant.md), a necklace marked by an old kitchen, fire, table, and bread. Session 7's recap clarifies that the known blessing can turn a natural 1 into a 20 proficiency times per day and grants a backswing-as-bonus-action style benefit.

In Session 7, Jefferson also becomes the party member most actively thinking about how to protect [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md) from outside conquest. Once he sees the scale of the valley's [Ether Crystals](../items/ether-crystals.md), he considers using [Rurik Valdren](rurik-valdren.md)'s communication stone to create a legal-commercial shield before the military, church, or state can simply seize the valley.

That blessing tied to home, refuge, and remembered care turns him into one of the campaign's strongest living links between war survival and older sacred protection.

## Session 8 Temple Removal

In [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), Jefferson enters the [Dead Lands](../places/dead-lands.md) and [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md) with the party.

When [Bevar Lurton](bevar-lurton.md) follows [Gwen](gwen.md) and the ghost priestess into the temple's emissary path, Jefferson tries to intervene. The temple removes him from the active group by dropping him through the floor. He is absent from most of the survival trials and is later returned by [the Ssar'Velyn Temple Guardian](ssar-velyn-temple-guardian.md), wrapped in a cocoon and healed.

The guardian notices Jefferson's hearth amulet and [Bartholomew Hildebrant](bartholomew-hildebrant.md)'s [matching medallion](../items/bartholomews-hearth-medallion.md), treating the resemblance as significant. This makes Jefferson's [Hestia](hestia.md) connection more than a personal blessing: it is now visibly tied to older Fourth-Age temple history and to survivors of the assault against Lolth's site.

## Session 9 Temple Route

In [Session 9](../sessions/session-9.md), Jefferson's separated path through the temple is revealed. Rather than falling all the way into the intended route, he uses the [Plasma Greatblade](../items/two-handed-plasma-blade.md) to slow himself against the shaft wall and cuts into hidden temple chambers.

He discovers a shattered egg or crypt room, a broken spider plinth, an archive of unreadable old script, and a vat chamber filled with dangerous purple demonic ichor. The ichor burns through leather but can be contained in treated glass, so Jefferson collects a [Demonic Ichor Vial](../items/demonic-ichor-vial.md) for later proof or study.

Jefferson then reaches an office where [Mistress Selyra Vex'ryn](mistress-sila-vexry.md) tells him a mixture of warnings, claims, and likely lies about [Lolth](lolth.md), [Hestia](hestia.md), the valley's blood oath, and a claimed corruption source south along the valley wall. His insight confirms that she is hiding motives and that some of what she says may be false.

After leaving the temple without [Bevar Lurton](bevar-lurton.md), Jefferson contacts the [Inquisition](../factions/inquisition.md) with careful partial truths and then contacts [Rurik Valdren](rurik-valdren.md). This turns his earlier Session 7 idea of using Rurik as a shield for [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md) into an active plan.
