# Sergeant Quill

Sergeant Quill is the human leader of Pack Five, a sister pack that often works alongside the party during [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md) operations.

## Personality

Sergeant Quill has a strong conscience and is disturbed by the treatment of captured [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md), especially after seeing the laboratory experiments in [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md).

## Key Events

- Supports preserving the first surrendered [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) rather than executing it.
- Becomes increasingly uncomfortable with [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) dissection and experimentation.
- During the [Norinar](../places/norinar.md) operation, tries to stop [Lieutenant Rethiel Kaine](lieutenant-rethiel-kaine.md) when he moves to kill a surrendered [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md).
- Kaine is shot with his own weapon during the struggle.
- Quill is unsure whether she acted freely or was influenced.
- The party helps protect her by supporting the story that her mind-shielding amulet malfunctioned.

## Continuity Note

Sergeant Quill is an [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md) NPC and is distinct from the older Fifth-Age adventurer named [Quill](quill.md), unless later notes explicitly connect them.

## Status

Alive after [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md), psychologically compromised, and a major witness/liability.
