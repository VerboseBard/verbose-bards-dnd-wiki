# Sir Calto Grathpar the Sixth

Sir Calto Grathpar the Sixth is an ancient Black Viper veteran believed dead by most of the world.

## Reputation

Before the betrayal and the later war, Calto Grathpar was a celebrated hunter and one of the great old names of the Vipers. By the time he reappears after the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) he is described as a decaying, hooded tiefling or fiend-blooded elder who still carries the presence of a true-born hunter.

## Return

During [Assassination of Jimbo and Charlie](../sessions/assassination-of-jimbo-and-charlie.md), he appears outside Hell to receive the recovered Umbra arsenal and coordinate the wider fallback to [Saharun](../places/saharun.md). His survival confirms that the Vipers still have old leadership and deeper continuity than the heroes initially realize.
