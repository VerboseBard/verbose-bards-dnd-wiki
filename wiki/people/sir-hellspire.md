# Sir Hellspire

Sir Hellspire is a Black Viper artificer, weapons designer, and war-manufacturer.

## Role

[Morir](morir.md) identifies Hellspire as the Viper in the [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md). Hellspire prefers his specialized jungle workshop but also oversees a manufacturing operation in [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md).

## Charlie's Soul

Hellspire currently holds [Charlie](charlie.md)'s soul. [Morir](morir.md) describes an unsettling fascination or obsession with her, and says Hellspire uses her design knowledge to improve his weapons.

## Threat

Hellspire may be less dangerous outside his prepared workshops, but in his own factory or laboratory he is expected to be catastrophic.

## Defeat

At [Hellspire's Manufactory](../places/hellspires-manufactory.md), Hellspire is caught while using [Charlie](charlie.md)'s soul prison. [Constance and Zuk](constance-l-coggeshall.md) and [Vis / Jarvice](vis-jarvice.md) attack from above in draconic form.

He is killed before he can activate his prepared mech suit or full facility defenses. [Charlie](charlie.md)'s soul prison is recovered.
