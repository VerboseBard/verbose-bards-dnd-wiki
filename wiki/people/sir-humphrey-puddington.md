# Sir Humphrey Puddington

Sir Humphrey Puddington is the god-emperor of the [Giff](../factions/giff-compendency.md) in the [Astral Sea](../concepts/astral-sea.md), but the surviving source note explicitly states that he is not actually a god.

## Fifth Age Divine Assembly

In the [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md) source note, Sir Humphrey appears as the seventh listed figure in the Sword and Purse Assembly.

The note is careful about his status: he carries divine or imperial religious titles among the Giff, but should not be treated as a true deity in the same way as the old gods.
