# Siv Halven

Siv Halven is the leonine elder of [Ashmane](../factions/ashmane.md) and one of the most powerful figures on [the Sunhollow Council](../factions/sunhollow-council.md).

## Description

Siv is a tawny lioness marked by age and authority: a greying mane-braid, old claw scars at the throat, and bronze rings worn like old office rather than ornament.

## Role

She has held the Ashmane seat for eighteen years and is one of the clearest living representatives of [the Old Charge](../factions/old-charge.md). She knows the full truth of [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md) and believes Sunhollow survives only if desire is kept below duty.

## Household

Her mate is Haldor Halven, a male Leonin metalworker with a strong memory for clan law and precedent. They have three adult children.

## Temperament

Siv is deliberate, proud, restrained, and accustomed to being obeyed without raising her voice. She is openly suspicious of outsiders and resistant to restoring rabbit political power.
