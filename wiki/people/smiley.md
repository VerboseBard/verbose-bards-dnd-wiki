# Smiley

Smiley is a burned-out dwarf operative used by the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) as a living key for [Wendell](wendell.md)'s hidden back route.

## Condition

By the time of the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), Smiley is barely functioning as an independent person. The other Vipers describe him as hollowed out, obedient, and held together mostly by command structure and artifact bindings.

## Role in the Heist

During [Assassination of Jimbo and Charlie](../sessions/assassination-of-jimbo-and-charlie.md), Smiley wears demonic dwarven bracers and opens the hidden path between the [Dwarven Egg Fortress](../places/dwarven-egg-fortress.md) and [Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress](../places/wendells-sixth-layer-fortress.md). His only job is to get the strike team through and, if necessary, die still bound to the route.
