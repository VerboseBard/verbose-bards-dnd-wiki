# Soda

Soda is [p'Op](p-op.md)'s giant honey badger companion and mount in the [Moon Stone Collectors](../campaign/moonstone-collectors.md).

## Role

Soda is more than transport. She is a combat companion, emotional anchor, and one of the campaign's earliest living stakes.

## Moon Stone Collectors

In the opening [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md) escort ambush, Soda is struck by a demonic blade that leaves necrotic infernal corruption spreading through her body. That injury becomes one of Session 1's central emotional hooks and helps establish that the campaign's danger does not fall only on expendable enemies.

Soda survives, fights on through the later Hell and northern arcs, and remains closely tied to [p'Op](p-op.md)'s identity as a mounted defender. She is also present in later trap and hydra material, where her senses and mobility keep mattering even when other storylines become more cosmic.
