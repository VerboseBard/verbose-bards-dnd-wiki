# Sorkia Peck

Sorkia Peck is the Head of the Circle of Life at [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Role

Sorkia is stern, protective, and deeply committed to the Grove's living harmony. She treats life as something that must be guided, defended, and respected.

## Ritual Presence

During the [Rite of Faded Remembrance](../concepts/rite-of-faded-remembrance.md), Sorkia serves as one of the three elders guiding the ritual.
