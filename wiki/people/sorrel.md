# Sorrel

Sorrel is a female fairy and one of the core members of the [Moon Stone Collectors](../campaign/moonstone-collectors.md).

## Role

At the canon Session 1 final roster, Sorrel is a Bard 9 / Fighter 5 who serves as an aerial duelist and support caster.

She is described as graceful, witty, and performance-driven, mixing swordplay and song while keeping spirits up with humor even when the fights are ugly.

## Moon Stone Collectors

In the opening ice-ship escort, Sorrel becomes one of the most visible personalities on deck. Her seasick complaints, insults, and magical mockery help sell the bait operation while the real priest has already been moved elsewhere.

Later, during the Hell assault, Sorrel is pulled away by [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) summons. Her burning watcher-skull boon transfers to [Vylra](vylra.md), and she is eventually found again in the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md), serving under her grandfather, a brownie adviser to the Green Man.

That makes Sorrel one of the party members most visibly tied to the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) side of the campaign.
