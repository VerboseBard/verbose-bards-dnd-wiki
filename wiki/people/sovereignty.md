# Sovereignty

Sovereignty is the current name for the merged sister-being formed from several fragments of the original celestial demon split by [Lucius](lucius.md).

## Composition

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), Sovereignty first contains four known aspects:

- [Julia](julia-julisia.md), Conquer / Power.
- [Elisia](elisia.md), Justice / Protection.
- [Alura](alura.md), Love.
- [Julessa](julessa.md), Lust for Carnal Pleasures.

Conquer wins the first struggle for dominance, but the merger also changes her. Justice and Love outnumber her within the whole, and the resulting being is not simply Conquer wearing the others.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), the remaining knowledge and wisdom aspects merge in the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md). The restored being becomes something new and complete, though not necessarily identical in will to any one fragment.

Known fragment names and aspects include:

- [Julara](julara.md), the demonic lust for knowledge.
- [Julia](julia-julisia.md), the demonic lust for power, also tracked as Conquer / Power.
- [Julisia](julia-julisia.md), the merged Julia-plus-Elisia state after Julia absorbed or merged with Elisia.
- [Elisia](elisia.md), the celestial justice and protection.
- [Alura](alura.md), love.
- [Julessa](julessa.md), lust for carnal pleasures.
- Sophia or [Wisdom](wisdom.md), the celestial wisdom and knowledge.

## Aspect Mapping

- Power / Conquer = [Julia](julia-julisia.md)
- Justice / Protection = [Elisia](elisia.md)
- Love = [Alura](alura.md)
- Lust for Carnal Pleasures = [Julessa](julessa.md)
- Knowledge = [Julara](julara.md)
- [Wisdom](wisdom.md) = Sophia / [Wisdom](wisdom.md)

Julisia is the transitional merged name used after Julia absorbed or merged with Elisia. It is not simply a misspelling of Julia.

## Goals

Sovereignty says she wants to become whole and learn who she truly was before [Lucius](lucius.md) split her. She argues that Conquer's actions were the acts of one severed fragment, not the whole being restored.

When [Junvara](junvara.md) confronts her, she says she does not seek to conquer mortal realms. If she sought dominion, she would more likely go home to Hell or ascend toward the heavens to learn why the artifact that split her was cast down.

## Current State

The churches feared that if all six sister-fragments recombined, an arch-demoness or arch-celestial-demon level threat would return.

After the final merger, Sovereignty remains in the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) rather than the mortal realm. She is too powerful to safely enter the mortal world and does not ascend into the heavens. Her future nature remains unknown, but the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md)' ending leaves her whole rather than destroyed.

The finale explicitly leaves this as one possible ending rather than a simple victory label. Sovereignty is not shown conquering, but her completed existence is a major supernatural fact for the early [Seventh Age](../concepts/seventh-age.md).
