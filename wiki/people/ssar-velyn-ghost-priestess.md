# Ssar'Velyn Ghost Priestess

The Ssar'Velyn Ghost Priestess is the temple entry figure who confronts the party in [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md) during [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md).

## Role

She appears after the party enters the temple approach and demands to know why males are walking on holy ground. The party fails wisdom saves and is frozen under the temple's authority.

[Bevar Lurton](bevar-lurton.md) kneels and says the party are humble servants of a mistress. The priestess then recognizes [Gwen](gwen.md) as an emissary, sends Gwen and Bevar along the emissary path, and forces the rest of the party into survival trials if they want to leave alive.

## Identity Caution

The transcript compares her appearance to [Mistress Selyra Vex'ryn](mistress-sila-vexry.md), close enough to suggest a sister or mother/daughter resemblance. This is not confirmed as actual kinship. For now, the resemblance is tracked as a visual echo or open question, not proof of identity.

It is also unclear whether the ghost priestess and [the Ssar'Velyn Temple Guardian](ssar-velyn-temple-guardian.md) are separate entities, projections of one guardian system, or different stages of the same surviving temple authority.
