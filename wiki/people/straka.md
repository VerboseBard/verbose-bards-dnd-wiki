# Straka

Straka is the god of second chances, storms, and freedom.

## Role

Straka is one of the named powers in the [Council of Gods of the Fifth Age](../concepts/council-of-gods-of-the-fifth-age.md).

## Origin with Mulligan

Straka began as an air elemental rather than as the being that restored [Mulligan](mulligan.md) to life.

When Mulligan woke on [Opaal](../places/opaal.md) as a corpse returned to life, Straka was hovering nearby. Mulligan had no memory and believed the wind-like being had brought him back. The truth is that Straka had watched him during his previous life as the Commodore and recognized him when the [Commodore Prophecy](../concepts/commodore-prophecy.md) forced him back early.

Mulligan's belief in Straka became powerful in its own right. He worked to raise the elemental into a minor god and eventually bargained with [Bridget](bridget.md) to have Straka accepted into the pantheon structure she shared with [Belle](belle.md). After Bridget's death, Belle became the effective head of that structure.

## Later Importance

Straka remains highly relevant in the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md), especially through [Mulligan](mulligan.md)'s early divine ties and later rejection of them.

The later sessions also make clear that Straka and [Belle](belle.md) were willing to use the party as bait to force [Wendell](wendell.md) into direct motion, a choice [Maeve](mave.md) openly condemns.
