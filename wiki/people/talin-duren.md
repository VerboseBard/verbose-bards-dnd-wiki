# Talin Duren

Talin Duren is a Hadozee guard and old-duty observer associated with [Longbough](../factions/longbough.md) who serves around the valley first-contact sequence in [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md).

## Description

Talin is guarded, security-minded, and noticeably less willing than some of the others to reveal strategic details about the valley.

## Role

His reactions help establish the Longbough style of first contact: disciplined, controlled, and focused on what outsiders should not be told.

During the next-morning escort to [Hollowmere](../places/hollowmere.md), Talin watches [Gwen](gwen.md) and the party's behavior as part of Longbough's temple-protocol caution.
