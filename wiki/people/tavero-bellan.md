# Tavero Bellan

Tavero Bellan is the Cervan elder of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md) and one of the strongest defenders of the current order in [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md).

## Description

Tavero is a tall stag-featured man with polished antler tips, deep-set eyes, and the manner of someone who has spent decades deciding who gets heard.

## Role

He has held the Burrowroot seat for twenty-one years. Tavero knows the truth of [Ssar'Velyn Temple](../places/ssar-velyn-temple.md) and aligns strongly with [the Old Charge](../factions/old-charge.md).

In the first-contact sequence, Tavero wants the party contained and the rabbit issue controlled until the full council can meet.

## Household

His mate is [Mireya Bellan](mireya-bellan.md), a Cervan household matriarch and ceremonial hostess. They have five children.

## Temperament

Tavero is formal, paternal, patient in public, and quietly controlling in private. He distrusts outsiders and opposes any easy restoration of rabbit political standing, believing it would destabilize the bloc he spent years holding together.

By [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md), that instinct hardens into a visible attempt to contain both [Alistair Hooley](alistair-hooley.md)'s return and the old-law leverage wielded by [Walter Wifflewick](walter-wifflewick.md).
