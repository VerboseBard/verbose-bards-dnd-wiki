# Tavi Redfern

Tavi Redfern is one of the [Caldrith's Gate](../places/caldriths-gate.md) guild receptionists.

## Description

She is an energetic halfling who handles paperwork at breakneck speed. She will flirt with anyone and scowl at incompetence.
