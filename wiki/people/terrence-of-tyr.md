# Terrence of Tyr

Terrence of Tyr is a winged justicar and champion of [Tyr](tyr.md) and the [Conclave of Churches](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md).

## Earlier Role

Terrence is one of the Conclave champions involved in the fight against [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), where he reflects a necrotic trap and later helps counter divine suppression effects.

## Judgment of the Moon Stone Collectors

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 12](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-12.md), Terrence catches up with the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) on the thawing road north of [Frostfire Keep](../places/frostfire-keep.md).

He has been sent to judge the party's actions regarding [Elisia](elisia.md), not to haul them back by force. After hearing testimony and examining intent, he judges that they acted in good faith: they protected [Elisia](elisia.md), left her somewhere they reasonably believed safe, and could not have stopped the attack that overtook her.

Terrence warns that the Conclave, the Dawnfather's hardliners, and the newly shifting divine order will make the coming years unstable.
