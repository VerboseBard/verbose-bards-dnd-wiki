# Thessa Moorfield

Thessa Moorfield is the chapter leader for [Brindle Hollow](../places/brindle-hollow.md).

## Description

She is a former ranger with a stiff leg and a sharper tongue. She keeps tight ledgers and tighter standards and hates mess.
