# Thrum Stonegrinder

Thrum Stonegrinder is the hobgoblin innkeeper of [Frostfire Tavern](../places/frostfire-tavern.md).

## Role

Thrum is one of the first stable local contacts the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) find after reaching [Frostfire Harbor](../places/frostfire-harbor.md). He receives the party warmly, helps them settle in during the blizzard, and carries himself like someone who has already seen too many strange travelers to be surprised by one more band of armed weirdos.

He is more useful than a simple bartender. Session notes show him handling messages, rooms, and money left behind in [Phlukk](phlukk.md)'s name. When the party starts realizing that [Phlukk](phlukk.md)'s disappearance is wrong in ways normal tavern gossip cannot explain, Thrum becomes one of the first local people whose reliability makes the investigation possible.

The tavern staff around him, including the formidable kitchen presence of Margaret, reinforce that the Frostfire is not just a rest stop. It is a practical safehouse where information, food, and temporary shelter still function even while the world outside keeps sliding toward crisis.
