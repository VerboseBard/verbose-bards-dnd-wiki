# Tiamat

Tiamat is the mother of dragons, a primordial dragon legend, and one of the first two dragons alongside Bahamut.

## Legend, Not God

Tiamat explains that she is not a god. She is a legend: a being whose story, belief, and draconic nature cause her to return even after death. In her telling, dragons are more than gods because powerful dragons exist across their own timelines rather than only through divine hierarchy.

## Dragon Guardians

Tiamat says dragons once held the ley lines, dungeon-core power, and reality lattice before dungeons replaced them in that role. When [Lucius](lucius.md) and others convinced heroes to kill the dragon guardians and consume their power, they broke the world while believing they were saving it.

## Imprisonment

After Tiamat was killed, [Lucius](lucius.md) knew she would reform weakly. He bound her in the Hells as a guardian of something he feared. She therefore supports gathering all of [Lucius](lucius.md) into one destroyable form so he can be ended permanently and she can be freed.

## Constance and Zuk

Tiamat meets [Constance and Zuk](constance-l-coggeshall.md) through the [Flight of the Ancients](../factions/flight-of-the-ancients.md). She thanks them for preserving dragonkind, warns them not to stop [Lucius](lucius.md)'s return too early if it prevents his permanent destruction, and names Constance the future Mother of Dragons for this world.

Before the first assault on [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), Constance remembers Tiamat's request while choosing to release [Charlie](charlie.md)'s soul and move toward the [Lucius](lucius.md) endgame.

During the final battle with [Lucius](lucius.md), [Constance and Zuk](constance-l-coggeshall.md)'s counterspell work tears open part of [Lucius](lucius.md)'s prison structure. Tiamat is freed from the Hells and crashes into the fight in full legend-form.

Her fivefold roar empowers the party for the final hour of battle. She attacks [Lucius](lucius.md) directly and breathes fire into the vulnerability [Junvara](junvara.md) prepared through Wish-cast Hallow.
