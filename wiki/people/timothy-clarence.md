# Timothy Clarence

Timothy Clarence is a lesser [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) noble and high-rolling gambler drawn into [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s island game.

## Casino Contact

Timothy meets [Dark Star](dark-star.md) through gambling circles and accepts an invitation to a more exclusive game on [Saharun](../places/saharun.md). Under interrogation, he appears terrified, truthful, and unaware of the Black Viper or [Lucian](lucian.md) layers beneath the invitation.

## Soul Contingency

During [Lucian's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucians-crystal-trap.md), Timothy's hidden soul contingency detonates. He did not consciously know about the implanted effect, but the explosion helps damage the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md)'s security arrays.
