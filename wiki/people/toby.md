# Toby

Toby is a champion of [Dione](dione.md), the halfling power of foresight and prophecy.

This page refers to Dione's champion. Other transcript references to people named Toby, including a young server in [Sin](../places/sin.md) and a disguise used by [Vince Gambino](vince-gambino.md), should not automatically be treated as the same person without later confirmation.

## Role

Toby's champion status ties him to Dione's prophetic network and to the halfling divine line surrounding [the Potato King](potato-king.md). That makes him part of the same larger knot of lore involving Belle's soul claim, Dione's possible claim on the Potato King's soul, and the divine-law distinction between killing someone and allowing someone to die.
