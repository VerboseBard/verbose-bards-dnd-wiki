# Tony

Tony is a [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) runner, fixer, and delivery contact who assists the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) during the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) rush.

## Role

Tony appears to be a young but highly capable courier who already knows how the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)'s schedules, banks, and school-side logistics actually work. The party treats him partly as a messenger and partly as a fixer because he is one of the people who can make a crowded city move a little faster.

During [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 4](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-4.md), Tony helps guide the group through the city-side process around the [First National Bank of the GIF](../places/first-national-bank-of-the-gif.md). Notes describe him as handing out a contact card and moving with the confidence of someone already used to important errands.

During [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 5](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-5.md), he delivers [Paul](paul.md)'s ordered candles and other supplies at [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](../places/lady-penny-hathaways-school.md). That small errand matters because it ties him directly to the party's cursed-book problem, their rushed preparations, and the larger institutional network that keeps the school running under crisis pressure.

Later in the same general stretch, Tony is also one of the names the party watches during the rescue and warehouse chaos, which suggests he is involved in more dangerous movement and coordination work than his first introduction implies.
