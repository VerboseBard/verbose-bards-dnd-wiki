# Tuppence Sandwich

Tuppence Sandwich is a Harengon of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md) and a market gossip in [Burrowroot Warren](../places/burrowroot-warren.md).

## Role

Tuppence is an auntie figure who talks too fast and spreads news instantly. She is useful when the table needs the rabbit community's rumor network to move faster than official channels.
