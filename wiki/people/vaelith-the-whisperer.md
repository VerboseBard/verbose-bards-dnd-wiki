# Vaelith the Whisperer

Vaelith the Whisperer is an [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md) leader specializing in mental manipulation and mind control.

## Role

Vaelith can extract secrets, implant suggestions, and help ensure that released captives remain silent about their ordeal.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), Vaelith appears or is revealed among the hostile disguised figures at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md). The manor game turns truths, secrets, and insight into wagers, fitting Vaelith's role as a manipulator of minds and information.

In the reconstructed [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), Vaelith warns the others that reinforcements are nearly there, then escapes through a teleporter or portal.
