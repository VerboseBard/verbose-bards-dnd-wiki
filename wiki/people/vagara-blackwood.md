# Vagara Blackwood

Vagara Blackwood is a firbolg hunter and keeper of [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Role

Vagara is serious, protective, and wary of outsiders. She values action over talk and takes the Grove's traditions seriously.
