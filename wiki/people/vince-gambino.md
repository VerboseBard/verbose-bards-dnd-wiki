# Vince Gambino

Vince Gambino is the dark elf ruler of [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md)'s dominant criminal order.

## Public Role

Vince's organization runs much of [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md)'s order. The city is clean, lawful, and safe by criminal standards because the mob punishes theft, disorder, and unapproved violence harshly.

Vince argues that the difference between a mob and a government is time: if he keeps the peace long enough, he expects [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md)'s order to become legitimacy.

## Meeting with Hell's Bane

When the party seeks permission to act against [Dark Star](dark-star.md), Vince first appears as the bartender Toby. After testing [Junara](junara.md)'s tone, he reveals himself and hears the warning that [Lucian](lucian.md)'s return would begin a new war on [Saharun](../places/saharun.md).

Vince agrees not to interfere if fighting breaks out at the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md), as long as the party avoids direct civilian casualties. He returns [Junara](junara.md)'s money and instead asks for future business ties and legitimacy.
