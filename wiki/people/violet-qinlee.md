# Violet Qinlee

Violet Qinlee is one of the Qinlee triplets of [Opaal](../places/opaal.md).

## Traits

Violet is the most outgoing of the sisters. She loves music, conversation, and movement, and she is especially noted for speed and magical talent.

## Triplet Prophecy

The Qinlee triplets are remembered as an impossibility made real. Their old nursemaid is said to have spoken a prophecy over them, and all three later awakened to powers that should have been vanishingly unlikely in combination.
