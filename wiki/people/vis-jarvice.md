# Vis / Jarvice

Vis, formerly known as Jarvice, is a divine warforged or autognome-like being created from [Greg](greg-p-smith.md)'s long-time internal assistant.

## Roster Snapshot

- Level 20
- Autognome
- Cleric / Knowledge Domain (Legacy)

## Origin

[Greg](greg-p-smith.md), [Belle](belle.md), and [Jinx](jinx.md) construct Vis in [Greg](greg-p-smith.md)'s heavenly workshop using magitech, divine materials, and a piece from [Greg](greg-p-smith.md)'s original chassis. [Greg](greg-p-smith.md) insists he is "not helping" while sending Vis to aid [Junvara](junvara.md) and the others.

## Personality

Vis is precise, formal, and newly embodied. He prefers the name Vis because [Greg](greg-p-smith.md) allowed him to choose it for himself. Though his speech is highly literal, he is emotionally aware enough to understand that existing in his own mind and body is different from existing inside another.

## Abilities

Vis can change forms, including a protective form linked most strongly to [Greg](greg-p-smith.md). He supports allies through magic such as haste, tactical analysis, and battlefield protection.

## Cave Battle and Death's Kiss

At the [Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout](../places/silver-bend-coastal-cave-hideout.md), Vis identifies the transformed serpent as a lesser divine avatar rather than a creature that can be dispelled. He later survives being swallowed only because [Death's Kiss](../concepts/deaths-kiss.md) redirects his death into the serpent.

## Anti-Magic Field

In the hidden blood room, Vis uses Anti-Magic Field to disrupt an active ritual. The choice collapses suspended magic, breaks crystals, and nearly traps him behind a sealing door. [Junvara](junvara.md) criticizes the decision, but [Greg](greg-p-smith.md) privately reassures Vis that the instinct was correct.

## Constance's Workshop

At [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md), [Constance and Zuk](constance-l-coggeshall.md) recognizes [Greg](greg-p-smith.md)'s regrets toward Jarvice and offers to repair and permanently reinforce Vis's damaged runes.

## Moral Development

After the old mine events, Vis and [Junvara](junvara.md) speak about mortality, agency, and whether being repairable makes a life expendable. Vis begins forming a personal moral structure rather than simply inheriting [Greg](greg-p-smith.md)'s outlook or obeying divine mission logic.

Constance later helps adapt a disintegration rifle into Vis's non-cleric form.

During the factory breach, Vis takes amethyst dragon form and joins [Constance and Zuk](constance-l-coggeshall.md) in the upper strike against [Sir Hellspire](sir-hellspire.md), using force breath and claws to finish the assault.

## Crystal Trap

Inside the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), Jarvice receives a mechanical call from [Greg](greg-p-smith.md) warning him about the risks of recreating [Lucius](lucius.md)'s god-blinding crystals.

When [Lucius's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucius-crystal-trap.md) activates, Jarvice enters the fight as a tactical analyst and support caster. He identifies the fragment's phase mechanics, protects allies with Circle of Power, and dispels key pieces of [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s soul-bound armor.

After the battle, Jarvice prevents [Eve](eve.md) from trying to consume the fading [Lucius](lucius.md) residue immediately. He later receives [Greg](greg-p-smith.md)'s ritual information for tracing the [Lucius](lucius.md)-tainted soul coin back to the main resurrection working.

## Separation and Avatarhood

After the crystal fight, [Greg](greg-p-smith.md) speaks to Vis in a dreamlike workshop and apologizes for treating him like an upgraded Jarvice instead of a newly created life. [Greg](greg-p-smith.md) offers to separate Vis into his own body so his young soul is not risked in the final mission.

Vis accepts. Jarvice remains in control of the existing warforged body and becomes the Avatar of the Architect, giving [Greg](greg-p-smith.md) a safer, direct link for the ritual to locate [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s soul.

During the first ritual briefing, Jarvice serves as [Greg](greg-p-smith.md)'s conduit. [Greg](greg-p-smith.md) explains the linked ley-line sites, the artifacts needed to follow them, and the fragment thresholds that determine [Jimbo](jimbo.md)'s fate. Jarvice remains behind at the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) to maintain the home-side ritual support rather than joining the assault.

After the third [Jimbo](jimbo.md) fragment is recovered, Jarvice senses the final ritual location from the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md). He opens the last book, connects through [Greg](greg-p-smith.md), and joins the party directly. With less than half an hour remaining, he opens the portal toward the [High Temple of Rexel Kar](../places/high-temple-of-rexel-kar.md).
