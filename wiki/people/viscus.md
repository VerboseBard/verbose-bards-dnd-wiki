# Viscus

Viscus is the ancient fungal intelligence at the heart of the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md), also called the Tree of Repose.

## Nature

Viscus is not a normal tree or mushroom, but a vast chromate fungal network whose mycelium spreads throughout the grove. The great central mushroom-tree connects the valley, its hives, its caretakers, and its undead labor systems.

The people of the grove treat Viscus as an ancient, benevolent intelligence. It receives knowledge from Seekers who return from the outside world and offers guidance through the circles.

## Seekers

When a Seeker returns, Viscus can take in everything they have experienced. In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), roots bind [Vylra](vylra.md) and pass his memories, questions, and discoveries into the network.

Viscus returns glimpses of what happened while he was gone, including hive joinings, the shock of [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), ley lines awakening, and the grove's changing weather.

## Phlukk

Viscus agrees to help [Phlukk](phlukk.md) after the party brings him to the grove with his mind broken by demonic and psychic damage.

During the Ritual of Fated Remembrance, Viscus drives a root into [Phlukk](phlukk.md)'s skull and leaves a fragment inside. This helps restore [Phlukk](phlukk.md) to consciousness, though the long-term implications are not yet known.

## Origin and Fey Links

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), [Wisdom](wisdom.md) explains that Viscus began as a unique offshoot of the [Demonic Dwarven Goo](../concepts/demonic-dwarven-goo.md). A dying infected host collapsed onto a forming mushroom circle, and [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) magic altered the goo's nature into something new.

After [the Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md), the [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) courts tied mushroom circles to Viscus, creating a power exchange. The destroyed Spring Court circle weakens Viscus until the party petitions the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md) to create a replacement link.

## Master Goo

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), the party carries a living cutting of Viscus into the old dwarven [Gateway to the Empire](../places/gateway-to-the-empire.md) control room.

The cutting is inserted into the control apparatus while the party holds off the master goo and its servants. When the control sequence succeeds, Viscus converts the master ooze and claims the wider goo network.

This gives Viscus control over the ancient infected network, including the undead soldiers preserved in frozen lakes and dwarven tunnels. The mass-infection threat is neutralized, but [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) becomes a power that nations must reckon with.

The finale transcript gives the scale more sharply: Viscus gains command over roughly 500,000 undead soldiers, infected animals and bodies, the old dwarven tunnel infection, and the wider goo network. Viscus is not immediately interested in conquest, but his reach changes the political map of the world.
