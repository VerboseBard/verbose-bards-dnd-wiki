# Vivra Blackwood

Vivra Blackwood is [Vylra](vylra.md)'s mother and a member of the Blackwood family of the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Role

Vivra embodies the grove's overwhelming hospitality: food, family, practical care, and very little patience for anyone being "too thin."

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), she welcomes the party into the Blackwood home, feeds them relentlessly, and tries to feed the unconscious [Phlukk](phlukk.md) before his restoration.

Her cooking, and the Blackwood family's broader feasting tradition, is strong enough to function like a magical blessing for those who fully indulge.
