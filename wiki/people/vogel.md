# Vogel

Vogel is the name or persona associated with [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s father in [Palewood](../places/palewood.md).

## Description

Vogel was known as a human man in his forties with long blond hair and a long beard.

He left when [Kestrel](kestrel.md) was very young because he was being hunted by the town guard. The reason for that hunt has not yet been confirmed in the wiki.
