# Volko Blackwood

Volko Blackwood is one of [Vylra](vylra.md)'s uncles and a hunter connected to the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Role

Volko helps defend the grove from threats released by [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md)'s thaw. He rides with the hunters who burn out trails of [Demonic Dwarven Goo](../concepts/demonic-dwarven-goo.md) and track infected beasts.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), Volko greets [Vylra](vylra.md) after the party survives the infected hyo-jak's trail. He later helps explain the grove's immediate crises: infection spreading from the ice, an awakened [Ancient Ice Hydra](../concepts/ancient-ice-hydra.md), and blocked mountain passes.
