# Voss

Voss is a Kenku patrol member from [Windcrest](../factions/windcrest.md) who appears during [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md).

## Description

Voss is a mimic, a watcher, and one of the session's comic relief figures. He becomes memorable almost immediately when the party's attempt to teach him Common goes exactly as badly as expected.

This Voss is not [Sabine Voss](sabine-voss.md), the Owlin council elder.

## Role

Voss helps establish the tone of first contact: wary, strange, but not without humor. His presence also underscores how language barriers inside Sunhollow are cultural rather than absolute, since several people understand more than they admit at first.

During [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md), Jefferson tries to teach Voss language through [Jose](jose.md), since Jose is the one who actually speaks Elvish. The result is famously unhelpful: Voss learns profanity and introduces himself accordingly.

In [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), Voss is still with the escort as the party is led toward the [Dead Lands](../places/dead-lands.md), making the profanity lesson part of the continuing first-contact fallout rather than a one-scene joke.
