# Vykra Blackwood

Vykra Blackwood is a firbolg druid of [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) and the father of [Vylra](vylra.md).

## Role

Vykra is tied to the Circle of Life and carries the Grove's practical, familial side. He is gentle, protective, and observant, with a strong concern for how the Grove balances life, death, and rebirth.
