# Vymra Blackwood

Vymra Blackwood is the Head of the Circle of Death at [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).

## Role

Vymra oversees mortality, transition, and the respectful release of souls within the Grove. He is quiet, solemn, and deeply reverent toward death as a natural part of the cycle.

## Ritual Presence

During the [Rite of Faded Remembrance](../concepts/rite-of-faded-remembrance.md), Vymra stands as one of the three elders guiding the binding of Pluck's spirit into the Chromate Fungus.
