# Winrah Holm

Winrah Holm is a noble-born anti-god speaker who becomes tied to the serpent church and [Dark Star](dark-star.md).

## Public Persona

For years on the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), Winrah denounces the gods and argues that mortals should control their own destiny rather than remain subservient to divine powers. She works this into a cover identity as a street preacher and confessional-style listener, using anti-authority rhetoric to find the disaffected and quietly filter useful names back to her Viper cell.

## Hidden Alignment

According to [Morir](morir.md), Winrah swears loyalty to the same cause as [Dark Star](dark-star.md) and becomes a charismatic leader or intermediary for the Church of [Rax'al Karath](../factions/cobra-cult-of-raxal-karath.md).

In the Vipers' own prelude, she is one of the four main sleepers who helps steal [Lucius](lucius.md)'s weapons and kill [Jimbo](jimbo.md) and [Charlie](charlie.md).

[Frederick Blackthorn](frederick-blackthorn.md) later explains that Winrah is his sister and that she had long been publicly anti-god before her disappearance. He does not believe she would target nonreligious [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) members, but [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s soul claims Winrah is now more fanatical than the Mother Superior and no longer working under [Dark Star](dark-star.md)'s direction.

[Dark Star](dark-star.md) does not know Winrah's current location.

## Mind-Control Crown

At the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md), Winrah is found maintaining the active ritual for [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md). [Jack Kenway](jack-kenway.md) recognizes that she is wearing a mind-control crown similar to the one once used on Mulligan.

[Kestrel](kestrel.md) takes on the appearance of her brother, [Frederick Blackthorn](frederick-blackthorn.md), and persuades her that her mind is not her own. [Kestrel](kestrel.md) then uses Greater Restoration to break the crown's curse and remove it. The spikes tear free from Winrah's head, but she keeps the spell stable long enough for a Conclave champion to take over.

Once freed, Winrah collapses in shock and tears. The party chooses not to execute her or use her soul, instead planning to send her back under guard.
