# Wisdom

Wisdom, also known as Sophia, is one of the six sister-fragments of the original celestial demon split by [Lucian](lucian.md).

## Nature

Wisdom is the counterpart to [Julara](julara.md)'s knowledge aspect. She is not blind, but wears a blindfold because she sees too much when exposed to strong magic.

She is also called the Watcher, the Oracle, and the Seer. She has been hidden in the [Summer Court](../places/summer-court.md) for roughly twelve hundred years, aiding the Green Man and his court.

## The Weaver

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), Wisdom identifies [The Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md) as her nemesis when she recognizes [Juan](juan.md) as someone marked by him.

She explains that the Weaver may call himself fate, but that true names have power among the [Fey](../concepts/fey.md). Because Wisdom is not Fey, she can speak about the hidden Fey oath that binds those who learn the secret cornerstone spellwork holding the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) together. Her explanation connects [Juan](juan.md)'s missing memories, the Wild Hunt's oath enforcement, and the Weaver's later fate-rewrite into one larger thread.

## Counsel

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), Wisdom explains several major truths:

- [Viscus](viscus.md) began as an altered offshoot of the demonic dwarven goo, changed by [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) magic.
- The [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) courts tied mushroom circles to [Viscus](viscus.md) after the [Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md), creating power exchanges.
- The sister-fragments can be separated only through very specific divine and demonic power.
- Merging fragments creates a new whole whose rights and identity complicate any attempt to split them again.

Wisdom offers a path where she merges with [Julara](julara.md) so they can use the celestial artifact, but the party recognizes that this would override [Julara](julara.md)'s autonomy.

## Final Merger

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), Wisdom enters the Green Man's auditorium after the party succeeds in the dwarven control room. The Green Man confirms that he kept her safe for roughly a thousand years.

Wisdom and [Julara](julara.md) merge as the last pieces of [Sovereignty](sovereignty.md), restoring the sister-being to a new whole within the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md).
