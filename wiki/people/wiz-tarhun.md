# Wiz Tarhun

Wiz Tarhun is a green dragonborn musician from [Kestrel](kestrel.md)'s early life.

## Role

Wiz Tarhun taught [Kestrel](kestrel.md) to play the lute, helping establish the musical foundation that later becomes central to Kestrel's bardic identity.
