# Zarvok the Torturer

Zarvok the Torturer is an [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md) leader known for sadistic psychological and physical torment.

## Role

Zarvok's reputation is built on breaking even strong wills, making him useful to the [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md)'s interrogation and captivity operations.

## Dwarven Deal

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 13](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-13.md), [Julara](julara.md) identifies Zarvok as the demon behind an old dwarven false-name deal. Before becoming known as the Torturer, he helped the dwarves bind demonic energy into the [Demonic Dwarven Goo](../concepts/demonic-dwarven-goo.md), originally meant to control undead shock troops.

That experiment eventually produced an escaped variant that helped trigger an ancient undead outbreak on [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md).
