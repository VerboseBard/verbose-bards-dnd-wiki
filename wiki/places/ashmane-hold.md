# Ashmane Hold

Ashmane Hold is the southern fortified stronghold of [Ashmane](../factions/ashmane.md) in [Sunhollow Valley](sunhollow-valley.md).

## Description

Situated near the lower lake road and closest among the living settlements to the valley's blighted southeastern quarter, Ashmane Hold functions as both a clan seat and a hard defensive line.

Its people are well positioned to monitor movement toward the corrupted lands, the older temple approaches, and any threat rising from the southern roads.

## Role

Ashmane Hold is the principal cat-clan stronghold in the valley and one of the clearest symbols of the cats' enduring military and political weight.
