# Big Tusk

Big Tusk is a city or major settlement on [Saharun](saharun.md), known in this arc for a casino connected to [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) communication.

## Fifth Age

Big Tusk appears in the imported [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) campaign notes as a major settlement with an arena structure and bounty-driven combat culture.

## Sixth Age

No [Sixth Age](../concepts/sixth-age.md) material has been added for Big Tusk yet.

## Seventh Age

A retired or semi-retired Viper contact frequents a casino in Big Tusk about once a month. The casino becomes one of the main leads for finding additional Viper cells.

During the [Soul Cage](../items/soul-cage.md) interrogation, [Lucian's Splintered Echo](../concepts/lucians-splintered-echo.md) confirms that one of the Black Viper assassins connected to [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) and [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s deaths is presently in Big Tusk.

[Morir](../people/morir.md) later clarifies that [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) is expected at the [Longshore Casino](longshore-casino.md), while [Sir Hellspire](../people/sir-hellspire.md) maintains a manufacturing operation somewhere in the city.

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s Ora Gimblewick identity gives the party access to the [Silver Sage Apothecary](silver-sage-apothecary.md) and a manor safe house.

## Order and Crime

Big Tusk is effectively ruled by [Vince Gambino](../people/vince-gambino.md)'s criminal order. The streets are clean, theft is rare, and merchants openly display goods because everyone knows unauthorized crime brings brutal consequences.

Vince frames this as a transitional government: if the order holds long enough, the mob becomes the state.

## Big Tusk Operation

During the casino operation, Vince agrees to keep casino security from interfering with the party's move against [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md), provided the party avoids civilian casualties. The operation ultimately shifts from the [Longshore Casino](longshore-casino.md) to [Hellspire's Manufactory](hellspires-manufactory.md), where [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) and Hellspire are killed.
