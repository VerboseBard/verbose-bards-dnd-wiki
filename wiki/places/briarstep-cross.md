# Briarstep Cross

Briarstep Cross is the eastern road-stronghold of [Briarstep](../factions/briarstep.md) in [Sunhollow Valley](sunhollow-valley.md).

## Description

Positioned between the eastern ether crystal fields, the lake roads, and the approach to the blighted quarter of the valley, Briarstep Cross is built around movement, exchange, and watchfulness.

Its people are well placed to see who is traveling, which routes are still safe, and when the eastern side of the valley starts becoming dangerous.

In Session 7, the party passes through or near Briarstep Cross on the way to the edge of the [Dead Lands](dead-lands.md). They see local livestock and a fishing operation where fruit is thrown into the water to draw enormous lake fish close enough for harpoons and winches.

## Role

Briarstep Cross is the main territorial base of the Vulpin, Mapach, and Mustel peoples under the Briarstep banner.
