# Brindle Hollow

Brindle Hollow is a quiet farming hamlet in the [Caldrith's Gate](caldriths-gate.md) region.

## Description

It is surrounded by wind-blasted meadows and whispering woods. The village is known for its eerie stone circle and its careful, practical relationship with the dungeon economy.
