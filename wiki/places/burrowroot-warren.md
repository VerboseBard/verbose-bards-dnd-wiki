# Burrowroot Warren

Burrowroot Warren is the western clan stronghold of [Burrowroot](../factions/burrowroot.md) in [Sunhollow Valley](sunhollow-valley.md).

## Description

Burrowroot Warren sits near forest edge, road links, and the western ether crystal lands. It is one of the most populous valley settlements, with a heavy presence of Harengon, Cervan, Hedge, Jerbeen, Capran, and Tortle families.

Homes, storehouses, and family compounds tend to spread low and wide here rather than rising high, reflecting the bloc's earthbound and communal character.

By [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md), the party finds Burrowroot Warren full of giant produce fields, wildflower-heavy farmland, and an enormous visible rabbit population. It is the first place where [Alistair Hooley](../people/alistair-hooley.md)'s family name starts to trigger public genealogical attention.

## Political Role

Because the rabbit folk were folded into Burrowroot after the fall of the old Moonhare seat, this settlement carries more buried resentment than its calm appearance suggests.
