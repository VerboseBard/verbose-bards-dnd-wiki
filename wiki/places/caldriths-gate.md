## Seventh Age

Caldrith's Gate is the first major city of the [Dawn of the Seventh Age](../campaign/dawn-of-the-seventh-age.md) campaign.

It is a cutthroat guild city built around dungeon access, trade, and rank. The city is crowded, loud, and organized around the [Adventurer's Guild](../factions/adventurers-guild.md) Hall, chapter offices, inns, supply wings, and routes out to nearby villages.

The city is overseen by [Captain Darel Voss](../people/captain-darel-voss.md), while Vice Captain Selene Draymoor handles the sharp edge of guild discipline and mission assignment.

## Districts and Services

- Guild Hall
- Supply Wing
- High District inns
- Lower Quarter taverns
- Shops that buy dungeon salvage
