# Calvin's Curios

Calvin's Curios is an artifact shop and power center in [Sin](sin.md), run by [Rurik Valdren](../people/rurik-valdren.md). The name "Calvin" appears to be the public-facing brand, while Rurik is the real force behind the operation.

## Description

The shop handles rare and dangerous objects from the fog. Its influence extends beyond [Driftvale](driftvale.md) through banking, artifact brokerage, and old dwarven networks.

Calvin's Curios is not a cluttered curio shop. It is a high-end auction house and black-market exchange for the wealthy, the connected, and the desperate. Rare relics, military-approved surplus, strange salvage, restricted goods, and objects no one should openly own all pass through here if the buyer is acceptable.

The building itself feels more curated than commercial. It is a place where value is performed as much as measured.

## Campaign Role

The party learns that Rurik is not simply a shopkeeper. He has enough power that even [Colonel Core](../people/colonel-core.md) treats him as a risky target. He is involved in the local corruption structure but appears narrower and more businesslike than [Mistress Selyra Vex'ryn](../people/mistress-sila-vexry.md).

He is one of the key people who decide what does and does not move through [Sin](sin.md).
