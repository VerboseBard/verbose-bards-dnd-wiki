# Caravan's Rest

Caravan's Rest is an inn or caravan house in [Silverbend](silver-bend.md), known to [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) from before his current life as [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s protector.

## Description

The establishment serves travelers, caravans, tea, coffee, breakfast foods, sweets, and hearty meals. It contains private rooms and table privacy wards.

[Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) has a shabby personal room or stall-like space there, making it a useful but humble safehouse.

## Campaign Role

After the bridge fight, the group retreats to Caravan's Rest, receives food and privacy, and meets [Maeve](../people/mave.md) when she arrives to take custody of [Lilith](../people/lilith.md).
