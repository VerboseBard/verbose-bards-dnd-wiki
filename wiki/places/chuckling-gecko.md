# Chuckling Gecko

The Chuckling Gecko is a kobold-run inn near the northern frontier route on [Norvindr](norvindr.md).

## Style

The inn is built in a former dragon-sized establishment, but the current owners have rebranded it around geckos and playful dragon disrespect. Much of the decor mocks or parodies dragons while still clearly obsessing over them.

Its kobold staff serve food with high-speed coordination, throwing plates, bowls, drinks, and biscuits through the room while older kobolds quietly use small magic to keep the chaos functional.

## Moon Stone Collectors

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 11](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-11.md), the party retreats to the Chuckling Gecko after fleeing the [Radiant Citadel](radiant-citadel.md).

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 12](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-12.md), the party wakes there and receives an extravagant breakfast after helping stabilize the local teleport yard.
