# Church of the Gods

The Church of the Gods is the Conclave-aligned temple on the edge of [Sin](sin.md).

## Description

Rather than serving a single deity, the church serves the pantheons recognized by the [Conclave of Churches](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md). It is not a grand cathedral, but it is an important stabilizing force in a town where legitimacy is always under pressure.

The building stands apart from the rest of [Sin](sin.md). Taller, sharper, and more deliberate, it looks like something from a more orderly world planted at the edge of a disorderly one.

The church offers ritual, doctrine, counsel, and the public face of Conclave authority in the town.

The roles visible in a small local temple are only the public surface of the broader [Ecclesiastical Order](../factions/ecclesiastical-order.md), whose hierarchy includes doctrinal scholars, truth-seekers, judges, soldiers, missionaries, hidden agents, and divine intermediaries.

## Campaign Role

The church represents one of the few places in [Sin](sin.md) that can claim legitimacy without relying on gambling, salvage, or fear. It is a place for advice, mediation, and public ceremony, but also a place where politics and faith constantly overlap.
