# Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School

Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School is a newly announced dragon-bonding refuge on [Saharun](saharun.md), near old ruins closer to the lost city than [Silverbend](silver-bend.md).

## Purpose

The school is intended for children who believe they can connect with or bond with dragons.

During the Black Viper hunt, it also becomes an emergency sanctuary for thousands of children, dragon eggs, young dragons, bonded souls, and living stuffed protectors.

## Distinction

This school is not the same institution as [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](lady-penny-hathaways-school.md) on the [Radiant Citadel](radiant-citadel.md). Earlier drafts of the wiki blurred the two because both are child-centered sanctuaries connected to major late-campaign crises.

[Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md)'s school belongs to the later [Seventh Age](../concepts/seventh-age.md) [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) material on [Saharun](saharun.md). Penny Hathaway's school belongs to the late [Sixth Age](../concepts/sixth-age.md) [Radiant Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) material.

## Campaign Role

[Eve](../people/eve.md) picks up a pamphlet for the school while leaving [JJ and Jackson's Teleportation Service](jj-and-jacksons-teleportation-service.md). The school hints at dragon legacy becoming part of the post-war world again.

[Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) later moves [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) there when she decides the party's protection is not enough. The school contains hundreds of visible young dragons and stuffed protectors, with thousands more children and eggs under care.

The joy of [Belle](../people/belle.md)-touched orphan children resonates strongly with [Eve](../people/eve.md), who can feel their connection to [Belle](../people/belle.md). The same concentration of children, dragons, and eggs also makes the school a terrifying potential target for [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return.

The school also houses Constance's lava-fed workshop, where the party upgrades several weapons and items before the [Big Tusk](big-tusk.md) operation.

## Lucius's Last Gambit

During the final battle, [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) uses divine Time Stop and teleportation to flee the desert battlefield and reach the school barrier. He attempts to place himself where the gods cannot strike without risking the children under [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md)'s protection.

[Lucius](../people/lucius.md) swears that he means [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) and the children no harm, but demands someone in the school who is no longer a child. [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) refuses the bargain and teleports the party to the school.

[Maeve](../people/mave.md) then appears above [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) and kills him with reforged god-killing blades, making the school the site of [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s final fall.
