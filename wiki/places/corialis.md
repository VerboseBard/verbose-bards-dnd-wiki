# Corialis

Corialis is the ancient sea-elf city of [Opaal](opaal.md) and one half of the twin-city power structure shared with [Wytebluff](wytebluff.md).

## Undersea City

The city sits beneath the water at the base of [Wytebluff](wytebluff.md)'s cliffs. It is home to many sea elves and other aquatic peoples and is surrounded by underwater farms and gardens.

Its architecture rises in spiraling towers, expansive domes, and sweeping arches, giving the city a graceful vertical shape even below the surface.

## Gateway Role

Corialis contains the connection point through which offworld visitors arrive, making it one of [Opaal](opaal.md)'s main portals to the wider setting.
