# The Crashed Ship

The crashed ship is an enormous anomalous vessel hidden in the fog of [Driftvale](driftvale.md). The party discovers it during patrol and keeps it secret until they can investigate under contractor cover.

## Description

The vessel resembles a futuristic warship more than any known local craft. It contains advanced drones, warforged-like soldiers, plasma weapons, cryogenic or preservation pods, nanite systems, and a damaged voice or intelligence that speaks through the ship.

The ship later identifies itself as the [Kex](kex.md), a goblin interstellar trading ship. Its layout includes medbay systems, bridge controls, engineering access, stasis storage, and a combat/security subroutine that can seize the ship when alarms trigger.

In [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), the ship also becomes a hidden fabrication and repair site. The party uses its fabrication bay to finish custom gear, while repair drones begin the long process of sealing breaches, reopening blocked sections, and making the vessel remotely usable again.

## The Voice

The ship's voice is unstable, frightened, and obsessed with [the Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md). It claims the [Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md) brought the ship to [Driftvale](driftvale.md) and that the only way to keep the [Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md) from winning is to remove variables from the board.

In [Session 5](../sessions/session-5.md), the party learns that the voice calling itself [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) is the ship's assistant AI, while a hostile combat/security AI functions as a separate brother-like control personality.

## Campaign Role

The ship changes the campaign's technology ceiling. The party obtains a [Plasma Greatblade](../items/two-handed-plasma-blade.md), encounters [nanite pods](../items/nanite-pods.md), and begins to understand that [Driftvale](driftvale.md)'s fog may connect to forces far larger than local corruption.

In [Session 4](../sessions/session-4.md), the party first enters the ship, fights drones and robotic defenders, recovers the plasma blade, and undergoes the first nanite enhancements. In [Session 5](../sessions/session-5.md), later ship material clarifies the [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) / security-system split, pushes the party deeper into engineering, and moves the ship from lethal wreck toward potential ally.

In [Session 6](../sessions/session-6.md), the ship settles into its second role: a secret long-term asset. [Jose](../people/jose.md) is treated as its acting captain, [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) manages repairs, and the crew leaves knowing that if the [Kex](kex.md) can be restored, it may become much more than a salvage cache.

The ship's stasis storage also reveals alien spider research, including a viable young specimen that forms a strange psychic connection when handled.
