# Dungeon Cemetery

The Dungeon Cemetery is a dangerous site explored during [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md). It contains dungeon-spawned threats, [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) activity, and evidence of how unstable dungeon magic can twist a location.

## Description

The cemetery is linked to rat-like dungeon creatures and hidden [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) survivors. It reinforces that dungeons are not normal ruins: they generate hostile organisms and magical consequences from unstable energy.

## Campaign Role

The party enters the cemetery expecting enemy action and instead encounters a more complicated picture of [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) survival, dungeon ecology, and civilian risk.
