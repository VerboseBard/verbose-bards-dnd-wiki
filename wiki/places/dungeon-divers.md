# Dungeon Divers

The Dungeon Divers are the sanctioned adventuring guild structure in the [Karakum](karakum.md) notes.

## Fifth Age

The guild building opens into a large circular chamber with multiple lower levels below. It is described as the structured alternative to freelance dungeon running.
