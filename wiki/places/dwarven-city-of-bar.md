# Dwarven City of Bar

The Dwarven City of Bar is an ancient city marked on [Paul](../people/paul.md)'s map during [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md). The map originally appears tied to the giants, but old records identify the later site as dwarven.

## History

[Julara](../people/julara.md) explains that Bar was either a giant city or a settlement built over a giant outpost. The dwarves claimed it at the end of the [Second Age](../concepts/second-age.md) after the giants disappeared and held it for more than three ages.

The city is now associated with the ice-covered region the party previously explored. [Julara](../people/julara.md) notes that glaciers and ley lines can hide or reveal very old secrets.
