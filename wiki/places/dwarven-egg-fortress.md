# Dwarven Egg Fortress

The Dwarven Egg Fortress is an ancient ruin used by [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s servants to hold thousands of stolen dragon eggs.

## History

[Juan](../people/juan.md)'s [Divine Soul Coin](../items/divine-soul-coin.md) reveals flashes of the fortress's history. It was once a powerful dwarven stronghold, then fell into long degeneration and became home to cannibalistic, demonic, blood-drinking dwarves.

[Wendell](../people/wendell.md) later entered the fortress through a portal, declared himself a god to its inhabitants, and bent the remaining dwarves into his service.

## Egg Rescue

During [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 5](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-5.md), [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md), [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md), the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md), and allied defenders open a portal from the [Shippers and Delivery Guild Warehouse](shippers-and-delivery-guild-warehouse.md) into the fortress.

[Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) enter as dragons and drive through the center. The [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) hold one flank while [Nicole](../people/nicole.md), [Kendrick](../people/kendrick.md), and [Nicole](../people/nicole.md)'s bodyguards hold the other.

## Back Door to Hell

The fortress contains a hidden route to [Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress](wendells-sixth-layer-fortress.md). The route can be opened with the [Demonic Dwarven Gauntlets and Hammer](../items/demonic-dwarven-gauntlets-and-hammer.md), but doing so requires attunement to a dangerous demonic artifact set.
