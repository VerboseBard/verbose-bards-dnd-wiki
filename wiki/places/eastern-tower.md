# Eastern Tower

The Eastern Tower is [Arthur Saran](../people/arthur-saran.md)'s residence while the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) and [Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) business are in motion.

## Description

It is a respectable high-city dwelling used by a man with enough contacts to move between banking, docks, and school politics.
