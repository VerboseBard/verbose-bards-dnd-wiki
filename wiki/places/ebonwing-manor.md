# Ebonwing Manor

Ebonwing Manor is the site of a secret high-stakes gambling trap during the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md)' [Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) downtime. The session audio renders the name as something close to Ebon Wing or Evan Wing Manor.

## Invitation

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md), [Tony](../people/tony.md) delivers an invitation to [Juan](../people/juan.md) in [Lady Seraphina Blackthorn](../people/lady-seraphina-blackthorn.md)'s name. The invitation offers a seat at a private high-table game and asks for a look at the dagger taken from [Lyris the Deceiver](../people/lyris-the-deceiver.md).

The invitation also hints that [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) would be welcome, suggesting that the hosts have a specific interest in the rescued celestial demon.

## Protected Room

The high-table room is warded against psionics, telepathic communication, and cheating. The protections are described as the sort of work [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) once built for powerful patrons. That detail matters because it means the manor is not just socially exclusive. It is professionally prepared for people who expect magical treachery from other powerful people.

## Trap

[Juan](../people/juan.md) and [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) discover that the Blackthorn hosting the event is not the real Blackthorn. The table includes disguised hostile figures connected to the [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md), including [Vaelith the Whisperer](../people/vaelith-the-whisperer.md) and a Deceiver-linked presence.

The high-table game uses truths, secrets, dangerous objects, and personal cost as wagers. It is there that [Juan](../people/juan.md) learns the harshest truth about his luck and stolen destiny, and where [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) realizes just how many false faces are sitting in the room.

The session ends with the rest of the party rushing toward the manor while [Juan](../people/juan.md) and [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) keep the game going long enough for reinforcements to arrive.

## Battle

In the reconstructed events of [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), the party breaches the manor doors and fights roughly twenty-five demons and disguised enemies.

Vaelith escapes through a teleporter, but the Deceiver-linked enemy is prevented from fleeing. [Saraphel of the Dawnfather](../people/saraphel-of-the-dawnfather.md) kills her with divine retribution. Her dying words, "Worth it," precede the activation of wider contingencies across the [Citadel](radiant-citadel.md).

The aftermath also strips away one of [Juan](../people/juan.md)'s longest-running disguises. Under compulsion and pressure, his apparent form fails and his satyr nature becomes visible to the others. In that sense, Ebonwing Manor is not only a trap house. It is the point where several party secrets stop being sustainable.
