# Elven Lands

The Elven Lands are the pre-[Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) world where [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) are from. They are separate from [Driftvale](driftvale.md).

## Identity

The Elven Lands are the homeland of Constance's people, her dragon-keeper bloodline, Zuk's dragon kin, and the later battlefield where the party returns to fight the undead invasion.

Some notes blur this world with Driftvale, but the two are distinct. Driftvale is the fog-frontier world tied to [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), [Junvara](../people/junvara.md), [Experiment 69](../people/experiment-69.md), and the later Seventh Age campaign. The Elven Lands are Constance and Zuk's world.

## Greg's Arrival

[Greg P Smith](../people/greg-p-smith.md) was transported to the Elven Lands, not Driftvale. After arriving, he was captured by an isolated tribal goblin group that had been cut off from the Gobfolk Alliance since [the Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md).

The goblins who captured Greg were not part of the advanced gobfolk civilization. After roughly a thousand years of isolation, they had fallen into destructive, fearful tribal patterns. Greg was going to be burned at the stake before a Seeker intervened.

The Seeker had already found [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md), which made them the first elf or half-elf and dragon Greg met in the campaign world.

## War and Concurrence

The party later returns to the Elven Lands during the undead invasion tied to [Wendell](../people/wendell.md), the dragon hatchery crisis, [Thornwall](thornwall.md), and the larger [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md).

During the Concurrence, the Elven Lands are consumed into [Unity](unity.md), becoming part of the remade world order rather than remaining a separate world in the old sense.
