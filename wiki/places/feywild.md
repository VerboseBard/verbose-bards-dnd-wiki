# Feywild

The Feywild is a bright, dangerous plane of beauty, bargains, tricks, and unstable time.

See also: [Fey](../concepts/fey.md).

## Fifth Age

No [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) material has been added for the Feywild yet.

## Sixth Age

In the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md)' experience, the Feywild is not simply whimsical. It is governed by rules of hospitality, debt, riddles, names, paths, and appearances that may be fair by [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) standards while still being perilous to mortals.

Mushroom circles can act as gateways between the Feywild and the mortal lands. Time does not pass evenly between the two realms.

## Seventh Age

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 14](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-14.md), a collapsed hole near the [Kuran Grove](kuran-grove.md) hides a mushroom circle that sends part of the party into the Feywild while they search for missing children.

There they encounter:

- A fairy gatekeeper who demands riddles and can impose curses.
- A pond of giant frogs, dragonflies, lily pads, and hungry water.
- A powerful noble known as the [Glade Mistress](../people/glade-mistress.md).

The party returns after rescuing the children, but sixty-five hours have passed outside.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 15](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-15.md), [Paul](../people/paul.md) destroys the mushroom circle after returning. The act breaks or wounds a bond between the [Kuran Grove](kuran-grove.md) and [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s Spring Court, leading [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s aspects to mark him with the [Crown of Fools](../items/crown-of-fools.md).

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), the party returns to the Feywild through another [Viscus](../people/viscus.md)-guided portal and visits the [Summer Court](summer-court.md). The Green Man agrees to create a replacement link to [Viscus](../people/viscus.md), and three weeks pass outside while the party is gone.
