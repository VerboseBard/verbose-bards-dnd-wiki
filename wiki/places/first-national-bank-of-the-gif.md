# First National Bank of the GIF

First National Bank of the GIF is the [Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) branch bank the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) use to store most of their money.

## Description

The bank is run by hippo folk, uses magical signature verification, and can hold both funds and items across branches.

## Campaign Role

The party opens a platinum-level account here so they do not have to carry their entire fortune around while the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) is imminent.
