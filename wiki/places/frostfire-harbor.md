# Frostfire Harbor

Frostfire Harbor is the winter port where the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) first reach [Ice Watch](ice-watch.md)'s wider civic district.

## Description

The harbor is guarded, practical, and suspicious of unusual travel problems. Its authorities immediately notice when the party arrives carrying dangerous artifacts.

## Campaign Role

The party is sent onward from the harbor to the temple district, which turns the harbor into a gateway rather than a destination.

## Ten Years After

If the thaw continues, the harbor becomes a less certain ice port and more of a seasonal transit point, with routes shifting as the northern climate destabilizes.
