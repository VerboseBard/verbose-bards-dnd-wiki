# Frostfire Keep

Frostfire Keep is a northern frontier outpost on [Norvindr](norvindr.md), built around a surviving ancient dwarven defensive tower.

## Description

The keep sits near the edge of the great ice plains and serves adventurers, hunters, soldiers, miners, and supply caravans heading into the dangerous north.

Its most distinctive feature is a melted spiral of stone: the remnant of an old dwarven tower or wall section struck by dragonfire more than a thousand years ago. The site belonged to the dwarven defenses raised against ancient undead and abyssal threats before the [Breaking](../concepts/the-breaking.md).

## Frontier Role

Frostfire Keep marks the edge of more controlled territory. North and northwest lie tundra, thawing lakes, frozen forests, platinum deposits, monsters, and ancient ruins emerging from the ice.

The keep also has a teleport yard. During the thaw, the yard becomes dangerously muddy, and the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) help reinforce it with stone magic.
