# Frostfire Tavern

Frostfire Tavern is the inn where the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) rest during their [Ice Watch](ice-watch.md) arc.

## Description

The tavern is warm, busy, and familiar to locals. [Thrum Stonegrinder](../people/thrum-stonegrinder.md) runs it like someone who has already seen every kind of winter trouble.

[Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s missing-note mystery begins here, and the rooming situation becomes a clue of its own when the party finds his space sterilized and his bow hidden away.

## Ten Years After

The tavern survives by adapting to a less stable winter economy. As [Ice Watch](ice-watch.md) changes, places like Frostfire Tavern become the kind of stable local institution people return to when the old routes stop feeling permanent.
