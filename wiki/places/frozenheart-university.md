# Frozenheart University

Frozenheart University is the [Ice Watch](ice-watch.md) research institution the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) use when local rumors stop being enough.

## Description

The university has books, lecturers, restricted materials, and enough hierarchy to make access expensive. It also has practical advice for language study and dangerous-topic research.

## Campaign Role

The party uses the university to chase the hoof trail, research abyssal connections, and compare what the church knows with what the library will actually let them see.

## Ten Years After

As [Norvindr](norvindr.md) changes, the university becomes more important, not less. It shifts from a local research stop into a reference point for understanding the thaw, the flooding, and the things uncovered by both.
