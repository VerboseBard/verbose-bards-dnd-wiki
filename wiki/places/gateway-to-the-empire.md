# Gateway to the Empire

The Gateway to the Empire is an old dwarven outpost and hidden control facility in northern [Norvindr](norvindr.md), connected to the ancient [Demonic Dwarven Goo](../concepts/demonic-dwarven-goo.md) outbreak.

## Surface Outpost

The surface site includes ruined barracks, a bank, a well, a cliff face, and an old ramp. The [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) first search the outpost while following memories revealed through the [Amulet of Lost Memories](../items/amulet-of-lost-memories.md).

They recover dwarven key discs from the well and surrounding buildings. Darkness and moonbeam reveal a hidden cliffside door where the discs can be inserted and rotated in sequence.

## Hidden Facility

The opened door leads into a deep dwarven facility filled with magical braziers, statue riddles, pressure plates, skull-mouth doors, blowgun walls, acid and lightning traps, magical locks, and portal-routing chambers.

The facility appears designed to protect the deeper control chamber where the dwarves once managed or studied their undead-control goo.

## Control Room

At the heart of the complex is a massive bound gray ooze linked to cables, magical chains, and control apparatus. Ancient memories show that a dwarven commander once reached this room while infected and tried to activate a system that could kill all goo. The master ooze impaled him before he could finish.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 17](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-17.md), the party returns with a living cutting of [Viscus](../people/viscus.md). They insert it into the control apparatus and help [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) convert the master ooze, bringing the wider goo network under [Viscus](../people/viscus.md)'s control.
