# Glacier's Edge Theater

Glacier's Edge Theater is the major performance venue where [Nicole](../people/nicole.md) and [Kendrick](../people/kendrick.md) turn a simple city search into a public music event.

## Description

The theater is built for sound and spectacle. When the weather clears, crowds gather there to hear live music under a magical barrier.

## Campaign Role

The [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) find [Nicole](../people/nicole.md) here, then end up joining the performance long enough to turn the whole event into a blessing.
