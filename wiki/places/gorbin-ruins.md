# Gorbin Ruins / Kreen Gate Site

The Gorbin Ruins are the old gate site on [Driftvale](driftvale.md) where the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) invasion route was discovered and destroyed. The nearby military posting is [Fort Victory](fort-victory.md).

## Description

The ruins are strategically important because they represent one of the clearest links between [Driftvale](driftvale.md) and the [Kreen War](../concepts/kreen-war.md). After the gate was destroyed, the area remained dangerous enough to justify military guard duty.

## Campaign Role

The party is reassigned to guard the ruins for the final months of service. On paper this is a respected, cushy post. In practice, it places them in the middle of [Driftvale](driftvale.md)'s fog economy and [Fort Victory](fort-victory.md)'s corruption.
