# Hall of Sorrows

The Hall of Sorrows is a route through Hell encountered by the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) after an elevator-like transit system drops them away from [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s sixth-layer fortress.

## Description

The hall is tied to an old saying: to ascend through Hell, one must first descend. The guardian explains it as a metaphor for grief: one must go down to the root cause before rising above sorrow.

## Party Route

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 6](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-6.md), the party uses the Hall of Sorrows after learning that the nearby gate is dangerous. They pass a bound draconic threat, manipulate a drained dungeon core, and eventually use the core and a celestial imp to teleport back into [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s throne room.
