# High Temple of Rexel Kar

The High Temple of Rexel Kar is the final temple complex reached during [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md).

## Approach

The party arrives in a magic-dense jungle filled with runes, charged water, heavy vegetation, and a single trail toward the temple. The approach is defended by hundreds of snake people, yuan-ti-like guards, priests, and temple monsters.

[Eve / Eva](../people/eva.md)'s fey familiars scout the path and discover that the outer approach is packed with defenders. The party chooses to fly over the army rather than fight through every rank.

## Outer Temple

The temple's upper approach is guarded by snake statues and dense ranks of cultic defenders. [Eve](../people/eva.md) annihilates most of this outer force with an amplified Meteor Swarm, blowing away the temple roof and exposing the route into the lower structure.

## Lower Temple

The lower temple is saturated with divine and serpent magic. Entrants face temple effects, suppression auras, petrifying gazes, snake guardians, and transformed priestesses. A massive serpent guardian can consume lesser serpents to increase its own power.

The party begins the lower-temple push with Circle of Power, Sunburst, Wall of Fire, dragon firepower, and melee pressure, racing toward the fourth [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragment before [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s final return completes.

The push continues through snake guardians, petrifying gazes, spider-like ceiling monsters, and collapsing stone. The party clears the route with fire, rifle fire, stunning strikes, critical melee pressure, and ceiling-break tactics.

## Final Chamber

The final chamber contains six serpent-head ritual nodes tied to [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s remaining anchors. Destroying or rerouting those anchors lowers the difficulty of extracting [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s fourth fragment.

The party disables enough anchors to make the impossible attempt barely reachable, then spends Wish magic and a captured Hellspire soul to complete the exchange. The fourth [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragment is recovered, and the remaining [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) anchors are pulled into one final manifestation.

Afterward, [Caden / Baba Yaga](../people/caden-baba-yaga.md), appearing as Aiden, arrives and opens the way to the desert battlefield where [Rexel Kar](../people/rexel-kar.md) reborn manifests.
