# Hollowmere

Hollowmere is the central lakeside settlement of [Sunhollow Valley](sunhollow-valley.md) and the meeting place of [the Sunhollow Council](../factions/sunhollow-council.md).

## Description

Built along the northern shore of the valley lake, Hollowmere is the closest thing Sunhollow has to a capital. It is a mixed settlement rather than a single-clan stronghold, with roads reaching outward to every major valley community.

Its location makes it useful as neutral ground. Council gatherings, inter-clan bargaining, and major announcements are most likely to happen here.

The principal council hall is an older circular elven structure rather than a purely beastfolk-built chamber. By [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md), the party confirms that formal council sessions can trigger runic effects that close the doors, broaden language comprehension, and suppress open deceit.

## Political Role

Though Hollowmere is not formally owned by any one clan, the influence of [Ashmane](../factions/ashmane.md) is often felt strongly in its public life because Ashmane still sets the tone of much valley politics.

In [Session 8](../sessions/session-8.md), the party leaves Hollowmere with a Sunhollow escort bound for the [Dead Lands](dead-lands.md) and [Ssar'Velyn Temple](ssar-velyn-temple.md). That departure turns Hollowmere from the site of council debate into the last safe civic threshold before forbidden ground.

After the party's first night in [Burrowroot Warren](burrowroot-warren.md), the next-morning escort to Hollowmere includes:

- [Captain Rovan Halven](../people/captain-rovan-halven.md), Leonin security lead from Ashmane
- [Bram Rusk](../people/bram-rusk.md), Strig watch commander from Windcrest
- [Iven Grell](../people/iven-grell.md), Tortle shield guard from Burrowroot
- [Celia Velen](../people/celia-velen.md), Capran civilian liaison from Burrowroot
- [Penelope Puckle](../people/penelope-puckle.md), Harengon rabbit witness and runner
- [Talin Duren](../people/talin-duren.md), Hadozee old-duty observer from Longbough
