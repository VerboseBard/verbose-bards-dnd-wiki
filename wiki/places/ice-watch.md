# Ice Watch

Ice Watch is one of the two largest cities on [Norvindr](norvindr.md) and the seat of religious power for the icebound world.

## Fifth Age

No [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) material has been added for Ice Watch yet.

## Sixth Age

Shortly before [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), Ice Watch serves as the destination for a church escort mission and the early [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) arc begins to circle around it.

## Seventh Age

The city becomes the center of a longer [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) investigation. The party passes through [Frostfire Harbor](frostfire-harbor.md), stays at [Frostfire Tavern](frostfire-tavern.md), and is redirected toward [Frozenheart University](frozenheart-university.md) and the [Temple of Gorthak's Triumph](temple-of-gorthaks-triumph.md).

Shortly before [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) completes, the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) escort an emissary of [Norvindr](norvindr.md)'s largest church toward Ice Watch. The emissary is meant to help coordinate with the [Conclave of Churches](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md), a gathering of religions preparing for the gods' departure.

Because the real emissary is teleported ahead, the party travels by slower ice-ship with a disguised decoy in hopes of drawing out the assassins.

## Travel

The route to Ice Watch crosses frozen seas by magically tethered ships reinforced with sleds and skis. These vessels can travel without a full crew by locking onto a destination and letting wind and magic carry them across the ice.

## Moon Stone Collectors Era

The city becomes the center of a longer [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) investigation. The party passes through [Frostfire Harbor](frostfire-harbor.md), stays at [Frostfire Tavern](frostfire-tavern.md), and is redirected toward [Frozenheart University](frozenheart-university.md) and the [Temple of Gorthak's Triumph](temple-of-gorthaks-triumph.md).

Later the party follows local clues through performance spaces and private residence politics, which turns Ice Watch into more than a port city. It becomes a stage for church authority, missing people, and outside pressure from the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md).

After the party flees the [Radiant Citadel](radiant-citadel.md), they return to Ice Watch and the surrounding northern route. The ice is audibly cracking and thawing, and the newly joined worlds are visible in the sky.

## Ten Years After

Ice Watch changes from a frozen seat of power into a weather-stressed port and church center.

- Some harbor routes become unreliable as the ice breaks and refreezes in new patterns.
- The old frozen road network gives way to mixed water, ice, and elevated travel.
- The city remains important, but its role changes from static religious anchor to adaptive northern hub.
- As [Norvindr](norvindr.md) floods, Ice Watch becomes one of the places where the old cold order tries to preserve continuity.
