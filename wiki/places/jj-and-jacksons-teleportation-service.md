# JJ and Jackson's Teleportation Service

JJ and Jackson's Teleportation Service is a teleportation business in [Silverbend](silver-bend.md), [Saharun](saharun.md).

## Description

The service uses a large teleportation circle, crystals, and runic infrastructure. It receives travelers arriving from other worlds and offers paid help, brochures, tourist information, caravan services, and local warnings.

## Campaign Role

[Eve](../people/eve.md) arrives here after leaving [Opaal](opaal.md). [Joseph the Goblin](../people/joseph-the-goblin.md) greets her, explains money, and later receives platinum from her.
