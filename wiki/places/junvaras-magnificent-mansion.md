# Junvara's Magnificent Mansion

Junvara's Magnificent Mansion is an extradimensional refuge created by [Junvara](../people/junvara.md).

## Description

The mansion contains a lavish main corridor, translucent servants resembling figures from the past of the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md), a lounge, dining room, many levels, private rooms, and a conveyor-belt-like flow of food.

The lights resemble anti-divination crystals, making communication or observation from outside difficult.

## Campaign Role

The group retreats into the mansion from [Caravan's Rest](caravans-rest.md) to speak privately with [Maeve](../people/mave.md), protect [Lilith](../people/lilith.md), and plan the next stage of the hunt.
