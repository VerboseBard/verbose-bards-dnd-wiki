# Karakum

Karakum is a [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) trade center and the administrative umbrella for the [Silverbend](silver-bend.md) region in the Second Campaign notes.

## Fifth Age

Karakum is the city-state where trade, shipping, and dungeon-adventuring bureaucracy intersect.

- [Silverbend Source Note (Karakum)](silver-bend-karakum.md) is the northern city within the Karakum notes.
- The [Merchant Gild](../campaign/logans-second-campaign/merchant-gild.md) monopolizes trade.
- The [Shipping Gild](../campaign/logans-second-campaign/shipping-gild.md) controls inland transport and river routes.
- The [Dungeon Divers](../campaign/logans-second-campaign/dungeon-divers.md) provide the sanctioned alternative to freelance dungeon running.
