# Kex

The Kex is the true name and identity of the hidden [Crashed Ship](crashed-ship.md) discovered in [Driftvale](driftvale.md) during the [After the War Campaign](../campaign/after-the-war-campaign.md).

## Description

What the party first treats as an unnamed wreck eventually identifies itself as a goblin interstellar trading ship. `The Crashed Ship` is how local narrative first encounters it. `The Kex` is what it actually is.

Session 5 confirms the Kex's core identity through [Kubix](../people/kubix.md): it is not a sea vessel, local airship, or standard spelljamming craft. It is Goblin interstellar technology displaced into [Driftvale](driftvale.md)'s fog.

## Campaign Role

The Kex matters because it changes the scale of the campaign.

It is:

- the source of the party's nanite enhancements
- the site where [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) reveals the ship's inner conflict
- the vessel that recognizes [Jose](../people/jose.md) as its acting captain
- the fabrication and repair base the party leaves hidden in the fog while continuing the [Lost Homeland Mission](../concepts/lost-homeland-mission.md)

After the combat AI is defeated, Kubix estimates roughly 41-45 days to generate enough repair capacity to seal and secure the ship. That makes the Kex a long-term asset, not an immediate escape button.

In Session 6, the party orders that repair cycle to begin and then leaves the vessel hidden. The Kex completes Jose's gauntlets before departure, starts rebuilding damaged drones, and begins sealing hull breaches with nanite-driven metal regrowth.

Two practical limitations remain:

- the shuttle bay cannot be assessed until repairs reopen access to that section
- the ship's armory section apparently broke off during the crash and may still be somewhere in the fog

Those limits keep the Kex valuable but not instantly decisive.
