# Lake of Glass

Lake of Glass is the central lake of [Sunhollow Valley](sunhollow-valley.md), notable for its crystal growths, island center, and its role in the layout of the valley's settlements.

## Description

The lake is the physical center of Sunhollow. Roads curve around it, major settlements anchor themselves to its shores, and crystal growths rising from its waters make it one of the valley's most striking landmarks.

Its island center is not currently documented in full, but it is visually and geographically important to the shape of the valley.

By [Session 7](../sessions/session-7.md), the party learns that the lake's ether crystal growth far exceeds the crystal growth seen on land. The lake bottom may be mostly crystal, and the lake is described as extremely deep, roughly twice the height of the surrounding mountain range.

The waters also hold enormous fish, including a locally remembered ancient "grandfather fish" connected to the [Big Shad](../concepts/big-shad.md) tradition. The fish eat crystals, and the crystals grow slowly, roughly two or three feet per year.

This crystal ecology is not ordinary ley-line behavior. The transcript states that [Driftvale](driftvale.md) has no ley lines and that the lake's crystal growth is caused by ancient divine power tied to Big Shad or his descendants.
