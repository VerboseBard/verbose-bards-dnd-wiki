# Longbough Canopy

Longbough Canopy is the southwestern forest stronghold of [Longbough](../factions/longbough.md) in [Sunhollow Valley](sunhollow-valley.md).

## Description

Built where the forests thicken and the mountain edge presses close, Longbough Canopy is the valley's most visibly vertical settlement. Ropeways, elevated walks, timber platforms, and hidden climbing routes define much of its life.

The Hadozee who live here favor perspective, structure, and controlled movement through height.

## Role

Longbough Canopy is the main Hadozee stronghold of the valley and a natural center for bridgecraft, lookout work, and disciplined watch over the southern woods.
