# Longshore Casino

The Longshore Casino, sometimes called the Long Shadow Casino in table talk, is a major casino in [Big Tusk](big-tusk.md) and the known meeting point for [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s projected table.

## Layout

The casino has multiple levels, including a main floor, upper gambling floor, and basement schematics acquired by the party. Any fight inside is expected to become a multi-level engagement.

## Security

The casino's security is draconic and neutral. They are not aligned with the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md), but they will respond violently if the party starts a fight inside the casino.

[Vince Gambino](../people/vince-gambino.md) agrees to call off or restrain the casino guards if the party must act against [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md), as long as they avoid direct civilian casualties.

## Campaign Role

[Morir](../people/morir.md) says [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) will appear at the Longshore Casino two days after the old mine meeting to receive an update from the captured Viper contact's identity.

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) enters disguised as [Central Toge](../people/central-toge.md). [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s apparent presence proves to be a projection, and the real lead shifts to [Hellspire's Manufactory](hellspires-manufactory.md).
