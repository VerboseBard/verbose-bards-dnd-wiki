# Merchant Gild

The Merchant Gild is the trade monopoly that controls goods moving through [Karakum](karakum.md).

## Fifth Age

The Merchant Gild runs the trade bottleneck in [Karakum](karakum.md). Goods must pass through it, fees are strict, and bypassing the system has consequences.
