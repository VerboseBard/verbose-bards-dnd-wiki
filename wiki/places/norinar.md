# Norinar

Norinar is a village attacked by a dungeon break during [Session 2](../sessions/session-2.md). The party is sent in after [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) are detected in the area.

## Description

Norinar is not a major political site, but it becomes morally important. The party discovers that the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) they expected to eliminate are protecting survivors rather than slaughtering them.

The village had reportedly asked for surrender or negotiation before the assault. Inside the keep, the party finds what looks like a small classroom where a child had been teaching [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) letters, strongly supporting that communication was happening even if the exact role of telepathy or influence remains uncertain.

## Campaign Role

The Norinar incident pushes the party into direct conflict with their own orders. They choose to preserve civilians, spare surrendering [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md), and hide the truth from hostile military channels. This becomes a defining moment for [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md).

The incident kills [Lieutenant Rethiel Kaine](../people/lieutenant-rethiel-kaine.md) and a child, leaves [Sergeant Quill](../people/sergeant-quill.md) psychologically compromised, and allows two Kreen to escape into the forest.
