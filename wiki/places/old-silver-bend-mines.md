# Old Silverbend Mines

The Old Silverbend Mines are a restricted mine complex beneath or near [Silverbend](silver-bend.md). They contain green god-sight-blocking crystals and the former altar or well of power.

## Fifth Age

The mine complex exists as a hidden resource and ritual site below [Silverbend](silver-bend.md), with a third level that contains the old monolith-like power site where two miniature ley-line flows meet.

## Sixth Age

The mine remains a restricted and secretive site, used for hidden conversations and protected meetings. The green crystals continue to block divine sight, making the site useful for secrecy.

## Seventh Age

The third level contains the old monolith-like power site where two miniature ley-line flows meet. Before its collapse, the structure holds a rotating [Possibility Core](../concepts/possibility-core.md).

This level is also where [Wendell](../people/wendell.md) once escaped through a portal after fighting champions.

## Morir Meeting

[Morir](../people/morir.md) chooses the third level for his protected meeting with [Junvara](../people/junvara.md), [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), and [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) because the crystals block outside divine sight.

## Collapse of the Renewable Source

After [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) sings the core's true name, the core enters him and the monolith collapses to dust. The mine no longer grows new green crystals, making existing samples finite.
