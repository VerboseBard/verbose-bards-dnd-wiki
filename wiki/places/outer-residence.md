# Outer Residence

The Outer Residence is the temple-connected private hall where the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) are sent after the temple does not have the answers they need.

## Description

It is a polished, formal space with enough privacy to suggest that the people inside know more than they are saying.

## Campaign Role

The residence becomes one more step in the chain of access, permissions, and hidden politics around [Ice Watch](ice-watch.md).
