# Palewood

Palewood is a forest town on [Driftvale](driftvale.md) and [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s homeland.

## Description

Palewood is surrounded by old forest. A single road connects it to the more populous regions of [Driftvale](driftvale.md), but that road is not always reliable. At times the gloomhaze covers the route, forcing travelers to hire guides or wait until someone who knows the haze can lead them safely through.

The town has a deep changeling history. The first changelings who came to Palewood carried a small number of old artifacts with them, including the twin shapeshifting blades that eventually pass to [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md).

## Personas and Local Identity

Palewood uses or preserves town personas that can outlive a single person. [Beyza / Quaylar the Druid](../people/beyza-quaylar.md), [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s mother, was the third person to use the Quaylar persona.
