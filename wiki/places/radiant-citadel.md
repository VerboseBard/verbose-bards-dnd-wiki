# Radiant Citadel

The Radiant Citadel is the primary teleportation and trade hub of [Unity](unity.md). It sits at a major [ley line](../concepts/ley-lines.md) nexus and functions as one of the most strategically important locations in the post-[Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) world order.

## Description

The Citadel is associated with powerful crystal infrastructure and world-spanning transport. Standard teleportation through Citadel-linked routes is expensive, highly regulated, and difficult to bypass. Unsanctioned long-range travel is increasingly difficult because large teleportations now require fixed circles, refined ether, or multiple mages.

## Importance

Whoever controls movement through the Radiant Citadel controls commerce, military redeployment, migration, and emergency evacuation across the linked worlds.

[Greg's Concurrence Proposal](../concepts/gregs-concurrence-proposal.md) presents the Citadel as more than a travel hub. It acts as the central nexus that receives magical conduit energy from surveyed worlds and stabilizes their resonance during dimensional convergence.

## Citadel Institutions

The [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) arc adds the [First National Bank of the GIF](first-national-bank-of-the-gif.md), [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](lady-penny-hathaways-school.md), [Glacier's Edge Theater](glaciers-edge-theater.md), and [Frozenheart University](frozenheart-university.md) as some of the city's most visible institutions.

The city is so busy during the final approach to the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) that timing, queue position, and travel lag all become meaningful parts of the story.

For the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md), the Citadel is the place where family negotiations, bank access, church politics, portal scheduling, and final-hours pre-apocalypse errands all collide. The city is festive on the surface and deeply strained underneath.

By the time [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md) begins, the Citadel is also a moral battleground. Religious authorities try to kill [Elisia](../people/elisia.md), [Lady Seraphina Blackthorn](../people/lady-seraphina-blackthorn.md) has to defend free will inside the city, and the trap at [Ebonwing Manor](ebonwing-manor.md) proves that hostile powers can stage high-society ambushes inside the same supposedly protected urban core.

In the extracted [War of the Gods, Session 20](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-20.md), the Citadel gains another important layer: a hidden control center inside the crystal itself, where [Veil, Guardian of the Citadel](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) monitors activity, coordinates responses, and quietly manages information far beyond what ordinary residents realize.

In [War of the Gods, Session 23](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-23.md), the Citadel stops being just a strategic prize and becomes an active battlefield. Mind flayer infiltration, infernal portals, scattered dungeon seeds, and external siege forces all hit at once, forcing the defenders to fight block by block while [Veil](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) works from the crystal to redeploy specialists and keep the whole structure from collapsing into panic.

In [War of the Gods, Session 24](../sessions/war-of-the-gods-session-24.md), the battle reaches the crystal itself. [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) attacks the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](radiant-citadel-crystal.md) directly, the party fights him on its outer surface, and the city's survival depends on keeping [Veil](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) at the controls while the assault happens outside. When the released power is successfully channeled back into the system, the shields re-form, the open hell portals across the city slam shut, and the crystal begins firing on the invading fleet. The session also makes the Citadel's deeper role explicit: it is not only a travel hub, but the central anchor needed to pull the fractured worlds back together.

During the final hours before the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), the [Shippers and Delivery Guild Warehouse](shippers-and-delivery-guild-warehouse.md) becomes the staging ground for [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) and Constance's rescue operation into the [Dwarven Egg Fortress](dwarven-egg-fortress.md).
