# Rimefall

Rimefall is the primary city of [Norvindr](norvindr.md)'s northern thaw zones.

## Fifth Age

No [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) material has been added for Rimefall yet.

## Sixth Age

Rimefall is already emerging from collapsing glaciers and flooded ruins as the thaw begins to reshape the north.

## Seventh Age

Rimefall rose from the wreckage left by collapsing glaciers and flooded ruins. It is a city defined by indulgence, wealth, and excess. Ancient districts were swallowed by water, exposing sealed ruins and forgotten technology beneath the new shoreline.

Gladiatorial arenas dominate entire districts. Treasure divers descend into the flood-sunk ruins daily, recovering magitech, artifacts, and relics whose purposes are only partially understood.

Rimefall does not mourn the past. It celebrates survival.

## Ten Years After

Rimefall becomes even more dependent on flood-sunk salvage and rebuilt high-ground districts.

- More of the old city sits underwater, turning the drowned sections into permanent expedition territory.
- New piers, platforms, and upper-ring neighborhoods expand the livable city upward rather than outward.
- Salvage guilds and patron houses grow more powerful as the flooded ruins become the city's long-term wealth engine.
- The arenas shift from simple spectacle to civic identity, because survival in the new [Norvindr](norvindr.md) is itself a form of status.
