# Shili Jungle

The Shili Jungle is a jungle region on [Saharun](saharun.md), believed to be tied to the strongest remaining influence of the [Cobra Cult of Rax'al Karath](../factions/cobra-cult-of-raxal-karath.md).

## Campaign Role

[Junvara](../people/junvara.md) sends two squads of the [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) deep undercover into the Shili Jungle to learn more about the cult. Their orders are to avoid engagement, gather information, and flee if discovered.

One squad goes silent. The second appears to attempt a response, but the message is distorted by interference. [Soul Cage](../items/soul-cage.md) interrogation reveals that the jungle's watcher can see nearly everything entering the region, but "does not have eyes under the water."

At least one of the Black Viper assassins connected to [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) and [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s deaths is believed to be in the jungle.

[Morir](../people/morir.md) identifies that jungle threat as [Sir Hellspire](../people/sir-hellspire.md), an artificer with a specialized workshop. [Morir](../people/morir.md) also says the best illegal route into the jungle is by water, though he fears what lives there.
