# Shippers and Delivery Guild Warehouse

The Shippers and Delivery Guild Warehouse is a massive [Radiant Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) structure used during the final hours of [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) as the staging ground for [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) and Constance's rescue operation.

## Description

The warehouse is several stories tall and large enough to hold hundreds of children, porters, protectors, ritual machinery, and a wall-sized portal sigil.

During the rescue, the portal wall is covered in magical marks, mechanical components, coils, wires, and improvised arcane infrastructure. [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) use the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md)'s peak energy to force open a path to the trapped children and dragon eggs.

## Rescue Operation

[Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) gathers children, porters, and living stuffed protectors in the warehouse. [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) finish the ritual machinery while the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md), [Nicole](../people/nicole.md), [Kendrick](../people/kendrick.md), and other defenders prepare to enter.

The portal opens into the [Dwarven Egg Fortress](dwarven-egg-fortress.md), beginning the rescue of thousands of dragon eggs.
