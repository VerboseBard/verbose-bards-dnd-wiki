# Shipping Gild

The Shipping Gild controls wagons, ships, river transport, and inland porting in the [Karakum](karakum.md) notes.

## Fifth Age

The Shipping Gild works in tandem with the [Merchant Gild](merchant-gild.md) to control transport. All shipping and porting of goods inland or on the rivers and inland sea must be run by a member of the Gild.
