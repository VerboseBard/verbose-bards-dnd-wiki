# Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout

The Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout is a cave system several hours up the coast from the old palace in [Silverbend](silver-bend.md).

## Description

The caves were once one of [Alen](../people/alen.md)'s getaways during her time as the [Raven Queen of Saharun](../people/raven-queen-of-saharun.md). During the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) side arc, cobra-marked Vipers and cultists occupy the site.

## Defenses

The exterior is watched by a sentry and patrols. Inside are Black Viper fighters, serpent priests, mirrors, scorpion-like guardians, and divine serpent manifestations.

The mirrors can call reinforcements from elsewhere, including scorpion-like guardians. During the final battle, [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) blocks the mirrors with Wall of Stone, causing at least one summoned creature to manifest into the sealed barrier and be crushed.

## Campaign Role

The [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) identifies the hideout after tracking the team that attacked the bridge. [Eve / Eva](../people/eva.md) opens the assault with Meteor Swarm, and the group enters the caves to fight the cult directly.

## Hidden Shrine

Beneath the occupied cave is a shrine lined with Infernal and Abyssal inscriptions describing the [Rax'al Karath Prophecy](../concepts/raxal-karath-prophecy.md). The murals show a serpent consuming the world, the world cracking like an egg, [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) consuming the serpent god, and a new coil preparing to consume [Lucian](../people/lucian.md).

## Ritual Room

The deepest room contains anti-divination crystals, a seven-pointed blood design, preserved vampire body parts, living blood vessels, and a protected portrait of [Alen](../people/alen.md)'s earlier life. Disturbing the room triggers active blood magic and nearly seals [Vis / Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) inside after he disrupts it with Anti-Magic Field.
