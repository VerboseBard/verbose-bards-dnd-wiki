# Silver Sage Apothecary

The Silver Sage Apothecary is a [Big Tusk](big-tusk.md) cover identity and business belonging to [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s gnome persona, Ora Gimblewick.

## Description

The shop sells rare potions and jungle-adjacent supplies. Because Ora is a traveling merchant, her absences are normal enough to support [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s espionage work.

Before the casino operation, the party buys or distributes large quantities of healing potions, speed potions, oils, and other consumables through this cover.

## Safe House

The identity includes access to a manor safe house that the party can use while preparing for the [Longshore Casino](longshore-casino.md) operation.
