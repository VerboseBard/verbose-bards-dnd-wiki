# The Singing Sword

The Singing Sword is an inn, tavern, and social hub in [Sin](sin.md), associated with [Granny](../people/granny.md) and the tiefling bard-host [Vaelis Brighttongue](../people/vaelis-brighttongue.md).

## Description

The Singing Sword is the cheaper, louder, and more approachable heart of [Sin](sin.md). It is the town's working tavern, a place for soldiers, adventurers, laborers, drunks, gossip, and passing opportunity.

Its defining feature is the massive ancient sword embedded in stone within the building itself. The blade is enormous, far older than the tavern, and treated with the kind of casual reverence only frontier people can manage. Music resonates through it, making the blade seem to sing along and filling the room with morale-boosting magic that feels welcoming rather than coercive.

The establishment is one of the safer social spaces in [Sin](sin.md), though "safe" is relative. It serves as a gathering point for soldiers, locals, and travelers. [Granny](../people/granny.md)'s hospitality, tea, and sharp judgment make the tavern more politically aware than it first appears.

## Campaign Role

The party uses the Singing Sword for food, conversation, and cautious contact with town life. [Granny](../people/granny.md) develops a fondness for one of the party members over tea and warns them when the [Silk Parlor](silk-parlor.md)'s allies may try to kill them after discharge.

The tavern is where the town speaks plainly, even when no one says anything directly.
