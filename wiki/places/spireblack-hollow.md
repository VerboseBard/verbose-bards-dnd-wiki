# Spireblack Hollow

Spireblack Hollow is a Bronze 3-Star dungeon near [Caldrith's Gate](caldriths-gate.md).

## Description

It is a deep ruin structure with fragmented leyline intersections and multi-tier descent. Ether crystals begin to form in the lower chambers, suggesting the site may be more dangerous than its classification implies.
