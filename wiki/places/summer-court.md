# Summer Court

The Summer Court is one of the major [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) courts.

## Fifth Age

No [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) material has been added for the Summer Court yet.

## Sixth Age

The court is ruled by the Green Man, a powerful leprechaun-like [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) king whose authority comes from deals, trickery, accumulated power, and a strange fondness for mortal-style architecture.

Unlike some [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) courts, the Summer Court uses formal halls, advisers, and holding rooms in ways that feel almost mortal.

## Seventh Age

[Sorrel](../people/sorrel.md) is recalled to the Summer Court after the world's changes. Her grandfather, a Brownie and adviser to the Green Man, names her his successor.

By [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 16](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-16.md), [Sorrel](../people/sorrel.md) has shifted into the winter phase of her [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) life while serving in the Summer Court. She guides the party through court rules and brings them to [Wisdom](../people/wisdom.md).

The [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) petition the Green Man to replace the broken Spring Court link to [Viscus](../people/viscus.md). The Green Man accepts, partly because it lets him one-up his stepdaughter [Belle](../people/belle.md).

The new Summer Court link repairs the immediate wound in [Viscus](../people/viscus.md)'s power and helps protect the [Kuran Grove](kuran-grove.md).

In the finale, the Summer Court also becomes the place where [Wisdom](../people/wisdom.md) and [Julara](../people/julara.md) merge into [Sovereignty](../people/sovereignty.md). The Green Man confirms that he kept Wisdom safe for roughly a thousand years. Because the final merger happens in the [Feywild](feywild.md), Sovereignty remains there rather than entering the mortal realm or ascending into the heavens.
