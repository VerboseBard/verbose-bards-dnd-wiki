# Temple of Gorthak's Triumph

The Temple of Gorthak's Triumph is the [Ice Watch](ice-watch.md) religious complex that receives the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) after the harbor incident.

## Description

The temple is a place of authority, purification, and paperwork. The party is told to bring dangerous artifacts there for review rather than handling them casually.

## Campaign Role

The temple becomes part of the chain that moves the party from the harbor, to the tavern, to the private residence, and eventually into a wider [Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) connection.
