# Underdark / Upside-Down

The Underdark, sometimes called the Upside-Down, is the inverted inner realm beneath and within [Unity](unity.md). It is not a single cave network, but an immense network of territories, caverns, ruins, and civilizations layered along the inner surface of the world.

## Description

At the exact center of [Unity](unity.md) lies the [Nexus](../concepts/nexus.md), where ley lines collapse inward and build impossible pressure. The [Radiant Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) was engineered to stabilize that pressure and make controlled teleportation possible.

Inside the Core, magic behaves incorrectly. Spells overcast. Distances distort. Gravity inverts. Teleportation without containment risks spatial drift, partial arrival, or magical annihilation. The [Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) exists because physics demands it.

The [Aether Sea](../concepts/aether-sea.md) surrounds the [Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) and fills the Core like a vast magical ocean. Flying ships sail its currents, carrying trade, people, and war through stabilized ley flows. Where routes settle, commerce follows. Where they surge, storms tear vessels apart.

The Underdark itself lies beyond the [Citadel](radiant-citadel.md) and below the [Aether Sea](../concepts/aether-sea.md). The Dark Elves control the only confirmed physical route to Topside, while other powers - Dergard raiders, [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) remnants, independent city-states, and ancient machines - contest the rest.

The whole region is widely regarded as a cage. Because teleportation cannot bypass the [Citadel](radiant-citadel.md), major powers spend millions trying to locate or force open physical routes large enough for ships.

## Campaign Role

The Upside-Down contextualizes later drow references on [Driftvale](driftvale.md), especially [Mistress Selyra Vex'ryn](../people/mistress-sila-vexry.md), the [Silk Parlor Network](../factions/silk-parlor-network.md), and old drow artifacts.

It also explains why trade, piracy, and military logistics in [Unity](unity.md) are so tightly connected: whoever controls the route controls the future.
