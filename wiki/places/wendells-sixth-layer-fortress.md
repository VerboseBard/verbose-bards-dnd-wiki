# Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress

Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress is the Hell stronghold controlled by [Wendell](../people/wendell.md) after his rise in the lower planes.

## Description

The fortress sits in a black, volcanic hellscape of obsidian, lava flows, demonic patrols, and tortured captives. Its halls lead down to chambers where prisoners are drained, traded, or broken.

## Maeve's Release

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 5](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-5.md), [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) and [Jarth](../people/jarth.md) enter the fortress to complete a bargain for [Maeve](../people/mave.md)'s release.

[Wendell](../people/wendell.md) honors the exchange but uses the moment to declare that his old leash is gone. [Maeve](../people/mave.md) is released in a dying state, and a captive goblin is allowed to leave as well.

## Hidden Route

A back door between the fortress and the [Dwarven Egg Fortress](dwarven-egg-fortress.md) can be opened with the [Demonic Dwarven Gauntlets and Hammer](../items/demonic-dwarven-gauntlets-and-hammer.md).

## Black Keep Assault

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 6](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-6.md), [Belle](../people/belle.md) melts the main gates and devastates the fortress before the larger allied assault arrives.

The party reaches the fortress through a detour involving the [Hall of Sorrows](hall-of-sorrows.md), a drained dungeon core, and a teleportation circle that drops them into [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s throne room.

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 7](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-7.md), the party fights through a multi-level hall toward [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s cells and kills [Morgrath the Enforcer](../people/morgrath-the-enforcer.md).

In [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 8](../sessions/moon-stone-collectors-part-8.md), the party kills [Lyris the Deceiver](../people/lyris-the-deceiver.md), frees [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) and [Elisia](../people/elisia.md), and withdraws as the fortress collapses.

[Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s soul anchor is destroyed with the keep, and the sixth layer of Hell is lost.
