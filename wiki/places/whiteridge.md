# Whiteridge

Whiteridge is the capital of [Norvindr](norvindr.md) and the seat of its southern cold regions.

## Fifth Age

No [Fifth Age](../concepts/fifth-age.md) material has been added for Whiteridge yet.

## Sixth Age

Whiteridge remains a cold, disciplined stronghold while the thaw disrupts other parts of [Norvindr](norvindr.md).

## Seventh Age

Far to the south, the ice still holds. Whiteridge is built among frozen plains and jagged mountains, and it stands for discipline, restraint, and deliberate governance.

Monster populations displaced by the thaw have flooded into the shrinking cold territories, making hunting not merely profitable but necessary. Entire industries exist to track, kill, and harvest these creatures for materials, food, and arcane components.

Beneath the ice lie ancient ruins and sealed facilities from civilizations long vanished. Their technologies fuel large research efforts, guarded heavily and studied cautiously.

## Ten Years After

Whiteridge remains one of the last major cold anchors on [Norvindr](norvindr.md), but it is no longer the isolated capital it once was.

- The southern ice is smaller, more strategic, and more heavily defended.
- Researchers and hunters rely on Whiteridge as a base for studying what the thaw uncovered.
- As floodlands rise elsewhere, Whiteridge becomes more important as a storage, archive, and control point.
- It is less of a final frontier and more of a guarded stronghold watching a changed world.
