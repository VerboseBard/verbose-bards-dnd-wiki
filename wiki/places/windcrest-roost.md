# Windcrest Roost

Windcrest Roost is the northwestern highland stronghold of [Windcrest](../factions/windcrest.md) in [Sunhollow Valley](sunhollow-valley.md).

## Description

Set near the pass and the upper mountain roads, Windcrest Roost functions as an aerial watch settlement, messenger hub, and first warning point for movement entering the valley from the northwest.

Its people are used to cliffs, shifting winds, and long lines of sight. Birdfolk scouts and lookouts can often spot trouble here before the rest of the valley even knows to worry.

## Role

Windcrest Roost is the clearest territorial base of the Windcrest bird coalition and one of the most strategically important settlements in the valley.
