# Wytebluff

Wytebluff is the cliff-top twin city that overlooks [Corialis](corialis.md) on [Opaal](opaal.md).

## Origin

The cliffs of Wytebluff are said to have risen out of the sea as the broken world re-formed. When surface peoples discovered [Corialis](corialis.md) below, they established a settlement on the heights above it. That settlement grew into the thriving city now called Wytebluff.

## City Life

Built to the cliff edge, Wytebluff overlooks the sea and the undercity below. In the warehouse district, giant cranes regularly raise and lower cargo between the two cities.

The surrounding hills are rocky and poor for large-scale farming, but they support grassy growth and herds of small livestock. The city's population is mostly elves, humans, and halflings, with many other smaller communities mixed in.
