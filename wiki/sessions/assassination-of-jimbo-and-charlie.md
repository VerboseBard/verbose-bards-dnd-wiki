# Side Session: Assassination of Jimbo and Charlie

This side session is a [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) point-of-view prelude to [The Hunt for Black Vipers](../campaign/hunt-for-black-vipers.md). It shows how a Viper cell exploits the chaos of [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), steals [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s old Umbra arsenal out of Hell, and then murders [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) and [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md).

## Overview

On the eve of the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), a hidden Black Viper cell gathers at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md) under the guidance of the [Blackthorn Demoness](../people/blackthorn-demoness.md), who is still operating through Lady Stephanie Blackthorn's identity.

The cell includes [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md), [Sir Hellspire](../people/sir-hellspire.md), [Winrah Holm](../people/winrah-holm.md), and [Morir](../people/morir.md). Instead of fleeing immediately to [Saharun](../places/saharun.md), they choose a two-part strike: first reclaim [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s heritage weapons from [Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress](../places/wendells-sixth-layer-fortress.md), then use the resulting power spike to ambush [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) and [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) before the rest of [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) can regroup.

The session runs parallel to the dragon-egg rescue seen from the heroes' side in [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 5](moon-stone-collectors-part-5.md) and [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 6](moon-stone-collectors-part-6.md).

## Key Events

- The Viper cell meets at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md) and rejects service to [Wendell](../people/wendell.md), choosing instead to rebuild on [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) after one last strike.
- Their demonic handler reveals a narrow opening: [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) is temporarily separated from most of [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md), while [Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) are occupied with the dragon-egg crisis.
- During the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), the Vipers slip through the [Shippers and Delivery Guild Warehouse](../places/shippers-and-delivery-guild-warehouse.md) while [Tony](../people/tony.md), [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md), Constant, Zuk, and allied children and mercenaries open the route into the [Dwarven Egg Fortress](../places/dwarven-egg-fortress.md).
- Using [Smiley](../people/smiley.md) and demonic dwarven key-gear, the cell opens [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s hidden back route from the egg fortress into Hell.
- Deep inside the sixth-layer vault, they solve [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s altar puzzle and recover the Umbra weapon set and armor cache, proving their blood still keys to [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s old arsenal.
- The team narrowly avoids [Belle](../people/belle.md) in the fortress halls after she enters the layer in avatar form and starts tearing fire out of demonic blood.
- Outside Hell, the strike team meets the ancient Viper veteran [Sir Calto Grathpar the Sixth](../people/sir-calto-grathpar-the-sixth.md), hands off most of the arsenal for the [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) fallback, and keeps several blades for the assassination.
- The Vipers ambush [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) and [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) at a forest campsite shortly before sundown, supported by additional sleeper agents and an anti-magic field scroll.
- [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) rewinds the opening exchange once with a time tattoo, but the second engagement goes worse for the defenders.
- [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) is cut down in melee and her soul is captured.
- [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) uses copied magic, invisibility, and time stop to try to recover the fight, but the Vipers finish him before [Junara](../people/junara.md) can intervene.
- [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul is captured and the killers escape by portal to [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) with both the body and the soul prison before [Junara](../people/junara.md) can track them.

## Consequences

This session explains the missing first half of the [Hunt for Black Vipers](../campaign/hunt-for-black-vipers.md) arc. [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) and [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) are not simply reported dead after an offscreen strike; their killers are identified, their method is shown, and the soul-theft becomes a deliberate operation rather than a vague aftermath detail.

It also explains why later Viper cells on [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) still have momentum after [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s fall. They do not retreat empty-handed. They leave Hell with part of [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s old arsenal, the memory of a successful hit on [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md), and two stolen souls that immediately reshape the stakes of the campaign.
