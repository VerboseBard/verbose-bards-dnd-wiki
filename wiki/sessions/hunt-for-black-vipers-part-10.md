# The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 10

Part 10 continues the assault on [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), moving from the first secured site to a jungle facility where [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s agents are redirecting ley-line power through an artificer fortress.

## From Lava to Jungle

After the short rest at the [Desert Well Ritual Site](../places/desert-well-ritual-site.md), the first reinforcements arrive and the party prepares for the next jump. The [Royal Marines](../factions/royal-marines.md) remain behind as guards while the party continues with the Conclave champions.

The ley-line transit carries the group through timeless magical space and releases them into a swampy jungle. Unlike the first site, the portal remains open and anchored behind them, giving the shuttle operator a more stable route.

## Ley-Line Ruins

The arrival point is surrounded by old ruins, serpent-like statues, faded inscriptions, and ancient portal architecture. The party realizes their forced transit has reversed an old system: magic that once flowed from the statues toward the arch now flows backward into the statues.

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) scouts from above and sees deeper jungle ruins and an active structure roughly a quarter mile away. [Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) track recent movement and the group advances stealthily through the forest.

The party finds a strange ley-line junction where multiple lines bend back on themselves rather than continuing naturally. The distortion suggests [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s forces are not simply drawing on the ley lines, but redirecting flow back into the facility.

## Shielded Facility

The group reaches the edge of a clearing around the [Jungle Ley-Line Facility](../places/jungle-ley-line-facility.md). [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) patrol the structure, and a large shield dome burns away ambient magical mist as ley-line power feeds it.

The party identifies three external nodes that could be disabled to weaken the shield, but chooses a faster breach. [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) uses Words of Creation to force a temporary five-foot opening in the shield. The party rushes through before the shield can close, while [Eve](../people/eva.md) tests teleportation portals and the staff survives the strain just long enough to bring Terrence through.

The breach alerts the facility. [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) take firing positions, and buried towers rise from the ground with lock-on beams, necrotic blasts, curse effects, and other escalating defensive routines.

## Tower Suppression

The towers are not ordinary targets. They are heavily protected, fed by ley-line power, and mostly immune to direct damage. [Junara](../people/junara.md) dispels one long enough to stop it from firing, while [Eve](../people/eva.md) attempts a dangerous magical manipulation that [Junara](../people/junara.md) counterspells before it can crash part of the ritual system and risk catastrophic magic damage.

[Eve](../people/eva.md) then identifies the tower control array and uses Teleport to remove the power crystals rather than the physical antenna. The towers lose power and sink back into the ground, ending the lock-on system before it can snowball.

## Mech Battle

Inside the facility, [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) kicks open the front door and confronts a huge mechanical guardian with segmented target points, regenerating shields, rocket pods, grappling systems, chainsaw arms, legs, head, chest, and core.

The boss fights like a layered construct rather than a single creature. [Jack Kenway](../people/jack-kenway.md), [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md), [Junara](../people/junara.md), [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), and the Conclave champions attack different parts while rockets punish anyone still marked by target lock.

[Junara](../people/junara.md) paralyzes the head and rocket pods to stop the worst of the barrage. Jack breaks the shield and damages the head. [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) tears into the construct, then looks down through the broken head and realizes the machine is powered by a soul-cage structure like the one that once held [Charlie](../people/charlie.md).

[Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) recognizes the resonance: the hidden power source is one of the unknown [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragments.

## Second Fragment Recovered

Rather than destroy the mech, [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) reaches into the burning core. Jack helps him pull the soul cage free. Both endure the backlash, ripping the fragment out of the machine.

The mech collapses when the soul-cage power source is removed. The party now has two of [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s four fragments, enough for [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) to be put to rest if no more are recovered.

As the fragment comes free, [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) and Jack sense another [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragment actively being used in the ritual above them. This means the second site contains at least two fragments: the hidden mech fragment and the active ritual fragment.

## Upstairs Ritual

The lower floors and exterior are mostly cleared. The party races upward. [Eve](../people/eva.md) has already found the elevator route and reaches the ritual floor, where an aasimar-like operator, Black Viper defenders, drones, and a Hellspire-like enemy protect the active working.

[Junara](../people/junara.md) brings Callus up through the elevator route so the Conclave champion can be positioned to take over the ritual if needed. [Junara](../people/junara.md) then strikes into the ritual chamber, damaging multiple defenders with Steel Wind Strike while avoiding the ritual operator.

The session ends mid-combat on the ritual floor. The active second-site ritual fragment has not yet been extracted.

This combat and extraction are resolved in [The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 11](hunt-for-black-vipers-part-11.md).

## End State

Two of [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s four soul fragments are recovered. The party has not yet resolved the second ritual site's active fragment, but they are in the ritual chamber and positioned to attempt it next.

The characters are expected to level to 25 after this site is completed.
