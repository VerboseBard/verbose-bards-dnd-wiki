# The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 12

Part 12 concludes the Black Viper hunt, [Jimbo's Soul Ritual](../concepts/jimbo-soul-ritual.md), and the long return of [Lucius](../people/lucius.md).

## Lower Temple Cleared

The party resumes inside the [High Temple of Rexel Kar](../places/high-temple-of-rexel-kar.md), with serpent guardians blinded, burning, stunned, or already falling apart under the group's momentum.

[Junvara](../people/junvara.md) cuts through large snakes with critical strikes, echo attacks, and battlefield control. [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) keep up heavy rifle and dragon-fire pressure. [Jack Kenway](../people/jack-kenway.md) continues his impossible streak of critical hits, while [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) stuns and destroys serpent priestesses before they can rebuild the defense line.

[Vis / Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md) uses ceiling collapse, force cannon shots, and tactical fire support to open the route, while [Eve](../people/eve.md) remains a walking firestorm, combining Immolation, Wall of Fire, and amplified Scorching Rays.

## Aiden's Arrival

During the push, [Eve](../people/eve.md)'s wild magic brands her with the sign associated with [Caden / Baba Yaga](../people/caden-baba-yaga.md). Outside the temple, the released shapeshifter arrives from the sky as a monstrous six-legged form, dropping into the enemy army like an orbital strike.

Rather than bypass the defenders, he tears through them first. This delays his arrival to the party but frees more reinforcements to reach the final battle.

Later, Aiden enters the temple in humanoid form wearing an Infinity Gauntlet-like glove and carrying a goblet of wine. He offers the party peace, power, and a moment of rest before the final confrontation. Those who accept gain the benefit of a long rest, and Aiden makes the purpose plain: they have a divinity to kill.

## Fourth Fragment

The final ritual chamber contains six serpent heads or ritual nodes tied to [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s remaining anchors, blades, and armor. There is no passive ritualist to replace this time. Enemies continue to spawn while the party must either destroy anchors, reroute power, or begin the extraction under pressure.

The party destroys or disables several anchors, lowering the impossible ritual difficulty. Two Wish spells are spent to reduce the difficulty further, and a captured Hellspire soul is used as the replacement component. With support from the party's blessings and a crucial fate-twist on the ritual roll, the fourth and final fragment of [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul is recovered.

All four of [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s fragments are now secured. [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) is no longer merely able to rest or reincarnate; full resurrection is possible, though it is not performed before the final battle.

## Lucius Gathered

The ritual surges after the final fragment is removed. Remaining [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) anchors disintegrate, their power pulled into the spell. Nearby serpents and lingering node-guardians are stripped of life to feed the working.

This is the point of no return. [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s remaining fragments have been gathered into one place, making the final destruction possible.

## Rexel Kar Reborn

Aiden opens a portal to a desert where [Rexel Kar](../people/rexel-kar.md), [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) reborn through the serpent mantle, begins to manifest. The party follows.

[Junvara](../people/junvara.md) opens the fight with Time Stop. During the frozen moments, he uses Wish to instantly cast Hallow on the battlefield and keys the effect to [Lucius](../people/lucius.md), making him vulnerable to fire within the sanctified area. He then prepares himself with Tenser's Transformation before time resumes.

[Lucius](../people/lucius.md) immediately proves why the gods feared a direct strike. He lashes out with legendary actions, poisonous breath, gravity magic, divine spellcasting, and reality-warping pressure. Counterspells blunt some of the worst magic, but the battlefield is still under [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s command.

## Tiamat Freed

When [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) unleashes a Gravity Lance, [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) answer with counterspell work. Zuk's successful counterspell tears at the structure of [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s prison-work and frees [Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md) from the Hells.

[Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md) crashes into the fight in full legend-form. Her fivefold roar empowers the party, and her fire breath lands against the vulnerability [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) prepared. For the first time in ages, the mother of dragons fights [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) directly.

## The Snake Runs

The party hammers [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) hard enough to push him through multiple phases almost immediately. Jack, still under the giant-size potion's effect, inflicts catastrophic damage with Opaal swashbuckling precision. [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) tests Quivering Palm against [Lucius](../people/lucius.md), [Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md) adds fire and force, and [Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md) keeps the pressure on.

[Lucius](../people/lucius.md) responds with a divine Time Stop and flees. He drops a transmuted Meteor Swarm behind him, then teleports to [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md), trying to place himself where the gods cannot smite him without endangering children.

The meteor blast leaves several allies down or badly wounded, but the party survives.

## Annabelle's Refusal

At the school, [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) confronts [Lucius](../people/lucius.md). He swears that he means her and the children no harm, and asks for someone in the school who is no longer a child, invoking the Raven Queen mantle and the dragon sheltered there.

The table's cosmic luck favors the school. [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) refuses the bargain. [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s present restraint does not erase his past, and she snaps the party directly to the school.

## Maeve's Kill

As the party arrives, reality tears open above [Lucius](../people/lucius.md). [Maeve](../people/mave.md) falls through the opening with her two old blades, now reforged and tipped with god-killing material.

She drives both blades into [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s back and ends him.

The final legend remembers the kill as [Maeve](../people/mave.md)'s: the fallen goddess who gave up divinity to protect children, striking down the deceiver who killed gods, hunted her nieces, and tried to turn a school into his shield.

## Epilogue

The tale of the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) reaches its close. [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) is finally destroyed, [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s full soul has been recovered, [Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md) is free, and [Maeve](../people/mave.md)'s divinity is framed as returning through the act of the kill and the need for someone to take [Bridget](../people/bridget.md)'s hand.

Elsewhere in the Hells, a celestial gate opens. A loyal guardian sacrifices himself to unlock it for one of [Julia](../people/julia-julisia.md)'s sister-fragments. She walks through divine fire toward the artifact that once split her and toward another trapped sister.

The [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) war is over, but [Julia](../people/julia-julisia.md)'s fractured story now has three pieces in motion.
