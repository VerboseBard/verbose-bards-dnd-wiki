# Side Session: The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 3

This side session concludes the first [Silverbend Coastal Cave Hideout](../places/silver-bend-coastal-cave-hideout.md) assault and pivots the Black Viper hunt toward [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md).

## Overview

The party finishes the battle against the transformed serpent priest, discovers the deeper shrine beneath the cave, and learns that the [Cobra Cult of Rax'al Karath](../factions/cobra-cult-of-raxal-karath.md) is not merely preserving an old god but also feeding [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s next return.

After the cave, [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) moves [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) to [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md)'s school, where the heroes regroup, interrogate trapped souls, and learn that the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) still have assassins hidden in [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md), the [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md), and elsewhere on [Saharun](../places/saharun.md).

## Key Events

- The serpent priest's final form radiates an aura of suppression, imposing a heavy penalty on attacks, saves, and checks within its reach.
- [Junvara](../people/junvara.md), [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), [Vis / Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md), and [Eve](../people/eve.md) learn the creature is a lesser avatar of Rax'al Karath, not a priest who can be dispelled back into mortal shape.
- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) seals the mirror reinforcements with Wall of Stone, preventing more scorpion guardians from arriving.
- [Eve](../people/eve.md) is swallowed and answers by casting fire from inside the serpent, forcing it to spit her out.
- The serpent's thunderous roar stuns much of the party before [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) arrives and attacks from above.
- The serpent swallows [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md), but [Death's Kiss](../concepts/deaths-kiss.md) activates through [Junvara](../people/junvara.md)'s mark. [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md) is briefly killed, the death is redirected into the serpent, and [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s blessing brings [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md) back.
- [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) captures the serpent soul with [Soul Cage](../items/soul-cage.md).
- The last initiate, [Initiate Thomas](../people/initiate-thomas.md), reveals that he serves the Church of Rax'al Karath, not the true [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md), and names [Bishop Collins](../people/bishop-collins.md) and [Deacon Matthews](../people/deacon-matthews.md) as his superiors.
- [Eve](../people/eve.md) incinerates Thomas after he begins chanting the cult's hunger doctrine.

## The Hidden Shrine

[Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) explores a hidden passage lined with Infernal and Abyssal inscriptions. The murals describe the serpent eating the world, the world cracking like an egg, [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) eating Rax'al Karath, and the serpent or a new coil eventually turning to devour [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) in return.

The hidden ritual room contains anti-divination crystals, a seven-pointed ritual design, preserved vampire body parts, a living blood rite, and a protected portrait tied to [Alen](../people/alen.md)'s lost past. [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md)'s experiments with the shelves reveal a still-living heart, a speaking vampire head, and a blood vessel that leaves him with a restored raven tattoo.

[Vis / Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md) uses an Anti-Magic Field to disrupt an active blood ritual. The choice collapses suspended magical states, destroys manifested bodies, floods the room with blood, and nearly traps him behind a sealing door. [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) holds the exit long enough for [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md) to escape. [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) criticizes the risk, but [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md) privately reassures [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md) that he made the right call.

## The Dragon School

In parallel, [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) decides that the party's protection of [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) is insufficient and moves her to [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md). She awakens [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) doll into a sentient protector called Mini G and demands the divine sand that had been removed from the toy.

The party later teleports to the school, where thousands of children, dragon eggs, young dragons, stuffed protectors, and bonded souls are gathered under [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md)'s protective aura. [Eve](../people/eve.md) meets [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md), reacts like a delighted tabloid reader, and reunites with [Lilith](../people/lilith.md).

## Memory and Revelation

[Mave](../people/mave.md) gives [Eve](../people/eve.md) memory cubes. [Eve](../people/eve.md) regains the memories of her origin, purpose, and shared history with [Lilith](../people/lilith.md), but [Mave](../people/mave.md) warns her not to give [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) the full truth too quickly. [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) receives only the gentler fish-and-goop memories, while [Eve](../people/eve.md) absorbs the harsher reality of what their mother did and what they were made to be.

## Soul Cage Interrogation

[Junvara](../people/junvara.md) questions the Deacon's soul first. It is broken and cryptic but reveals that:

- One Black Viper in the [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md) is enough to matter.
- The jungle's watcher sees nearly everything.
- The watcher "does not have eyes under the water."

When [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) questions the Bishop's soul, [Lucius's Splintered Echo](../concepts/lucius-splintered-echo.md) manifests through it. The echo claims control of the Church of Rax'al Karath, explains that the serpent prophecy now points toward [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return, and confirms that the assassins who killed [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) and [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) remain on [Saharun](../places/saharun.md): one in [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md), one in the jungle, and one returning.

[Lucius](../people/lucius.md) then destroys the remaining Bishop soul before [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) can preserve the last question.

## 442nd Updates

- The [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) teams are in position and establishing safe houses, but have not yet confirmed casino evidence.
- One [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md) team goes completely silent.
- The second [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md) team appears to be trying to report but is distorted or jammed.
- [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md) teams are backtracking the cave operation, checking the old mine lead, and are warned away from [Alen](../people/alen.md)'s blood room.

## Consequences

The fight confirms that the cobra cult can manifest divine avatars through blood sacrifice and willing consumption. The interrogation confirms that [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) is not merely benefiting from the cult; he controls it through at least one splintered echo.

The children's school becomes a likely strategic target because it contains thousands of children, dragons, eggs, and bonded souls: exactly the kind of concentrated life and belief that could power a god-scale return.
