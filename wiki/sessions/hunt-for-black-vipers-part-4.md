# Side Session: The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 4

This side session follows the regroup at [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md), a negotiated meeting with the Black Viper defector [Morir](../people/morir.md), and the shift toward [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md).

## Overview

The party upgrades weapons in Constance's workshop, learns more about [Eve](../people/eve.md)'s recovered memories, negotiates with a surviving Black Viper, and accidentally transforms the old mine's core of power into a major turning point.

By the end of the session, [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s blades consume two [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) soul-anchors, [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) severs the last active influence of [Alen](../people/alen.md) from [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md), and the party relocates to [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) to prepare for [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s casino meeting.

## Constance's Workshop

[Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md)'s forge is set above a lava river, with machinery that lifts lava into a magically regulated forge and plasma-quenching system.

Several items are upgraded:

- [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md)'s brass knuckles gain a raven brand effect that stacks extra lightning or necrotic damage on marked targets.
- [Junvara](../people/junvara.md)'s blade gains an extra attack and reaction attack feature.
- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s Charisma ring improves.
- A wand of sparkles becomes a focused-fire wand of Fireball.
- [Eve](../people/eve.md)'s wand becomes dramatically stronger after [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s blessing, gaining a spell attack increase, Charisma increase, and a volatile extra Firebolt or wild magic effect.

## Eve and Lilith

Back at the table with pineapple and pizza, [Eve](../people/eve.md) processes the memory cubes [Mave](../people/mave.md) gave her. She chooses not to share the full horror of their origin with [Lilith](../people/lilith.md), instead telling her that they have lived before, will likely live again, and are their mother's daughters.

Zuk later brings [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) a dragon egg. [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) bonds with the dragon within, eventually named Gth, and the egg changes from pale white to black as [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s death mantle settles into place.

## Morir's Offer

The next morning, [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) receives a call from the sending stone carried by the silent [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) jungle team. The caller is [Morir](../people/morir.md), one of the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md) hunted by the party.

[Morir](../people/morir.md) offers the lives of the captured [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) members in exchange for a protected conversation and safe departure. The meeting is set at the old mine beneath [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md), among the god-sight-blocking green crystals and the so-called altar of power.

## The Old Mine Meeting

[Junvara](../people/junvara.md), [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), and [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) inspect the third level of the [Old Silverbend Mines](../places/old-silver-bend-mines.md). The site contains a well or altar of power formed by two miniature ley-line flows and a rotating core of pure possibility.

[Morir](../people/morir.md) arrives with neutral high-level mercenaries, returns the unconscious [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) captives, and explains that he wants freedom from [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s oaths. His handler died when the party killed Lady Stephanie Blackthorn or the demon wearing her life.

[Morir](../people/morir.md) reveals:

- [Sir Hellspire](../people/sir-hellspire.md) is an artificer, war-weapons designer, and current holder of [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul.
- Hellspire's primary workshop is in the [Shili Jungle](../places/shili-jungle.md), with a manufacturing setup in [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md).
- [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) is obsessed with destroying the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) and restoring [Lucius](../people/lucius.md).
- [Winrah Holm](../people/winrah-holm.md) is a noble anti-god public speaker who has become a charismatic leader for the serpent church.
- The jungle society contains millions of people, but the Viper-linked zealots are a radical fringe within broader Rax'al worship.
- Entering the jungle without passes is best done by water, though [Morir](../people/morir.md) is clearly afraid of what lives there.
- [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) will be at the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md) in [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) in two days.

[Morir](../people/morir.md) also gives the party two blades containing pieces of [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s essence and explains that each fully manifested serpent avatar becomes another anchor for [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return.

## The Core of Possibility

After [Morir](../people/morir.md) leaves, the full party studies the old mine's altar. [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), drawing on the language of creation, sings the true name of the core. The [Possibility Core](../concepts/possibility-core.md) enters him, permanently empowering him and causing the old monolith to collapse into dust.

The green crystals stop growing. The site is no longer a renewing source of god-blocking crystals. [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s laughter is heard afterward, implying that one of the greatest remaining blocks against divine sight has been removed.

The dust carries unstable potential. [Eve](../people/eve.md) absorbs much of it by playing in the pile, empowering her and causing strange effects. Constance, Zuk, and others preserve some as motes of possibility for crafting or spell use.

## Lucius's Blades

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s ancient blades ask to consume the two [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)-anchor daggers. When fed, they devour both anchors completely, and the party hears [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) scream twice on a psychic level.

The consumption strengthens [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s blades, lowering their critical threshold and granting limited automatic critical strikes.

## Annabelle's Intervention

That night, [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) visits [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md). She removes the raven tattoo and with it the lingering command structure tied to [Alen](../people/alen.md). [Alen](../people/alen.md) is gone beyond return, the Raven Queen mantle fully transfers toward [Lilith](../people/lilith.md), and [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) is freed from the old charge while remaining free to choose protection.

[Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) gives [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) a childlike bracelet that stabilizes him and lets her help him despite his age and history.

## Big Tusk Preparation

The party relocates to [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) through [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s gnome apothecary identity, Ora Gimblewick, owner of the [Silver Sage Apothecary](../places/silver-sage-apothecary.md). The group gains access to a manor safe house and begins preparing for [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s expected appearance at the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md).

The [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) moves additional squads into [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md). Some are tasked with casino surveillance, while others investigate Hellspire's manufacturing lead.

## Consequences

[Morir](../people/morir.md)'s defection gives the party names, locations, and a clear next target. The old mine event removes the renewable source of green crystals, empowers [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), and gives [Belle](../people/belle.md) clearer sight into [Saharun](../places/saharun.md).

The destruction of two [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)-anchor blades weakens [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return, but confirms the larger problem: every fully manifested serpent avatar may create another anchor unless the party prevents or destroys them.
