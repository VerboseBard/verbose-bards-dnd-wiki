# Side Session: The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 5

This side session covers the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) casino operation, the meeting with [Vince Gambino](../people/vince-gambino.md), and the surprise breach of [Sir Hellspire](../people/sir-hellspire.md)'s manufactory.

## Overview

The party prepares to intercept [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) at the [Longshore Casino](../places/longshore-casino.md), hoping to recover [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul. Instead, the casino play reveals that [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) is operating through illusions and remote meetings, while the real targets are at Hellspire's factory.

The group pivots immediately, splits into two strike teams, and eliminates both Hellspire and [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) before either can bring their prepared defenses online.

## Shopping and Big Tusk

At [Silver Sage Apothecary](../places/silver-sage-apothecary.md), the party stocks up on healing potions, speed potions, oils, and other high-end consumables for the coming casino operation.

The group observes [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md)'s culture: clean streets, visible wealth, and near-total obedience to the mob-run order. Theft and street violence are rare not because the city is gentle, but because punishment is swift and public.

## Planning the Casino Play

The group expects [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) to meet [Central Toge](../people/central-toge.md)'s persona at the casino. They discuss several options:

- Pay or negotiate with the casino's owners to avoid a security response.
- Try to move [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) to a private island or off-site location.
- Have [Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) provoke a reaction if needed.
- Use [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s impersonation of [Central Toge](../people/central-toge.md) to draw out information.

The working plan is to use deception first, with violence only if [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) exposes himself or attempts to flee.

## Vince Gambino

The party meets [Vince Gambino](../people/vince-gambino.md), the dark elf leader of [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md)'s ruling criminal order. He first appears disguised as the bartender Toby before revealing himself.

[Junara](../people/junara.md) explains that [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) and the serpent church are trying to restore [Lucian](../people/lucian.md). Vince agrees not to interfere if violence breaks out at the casino, provided the party avoids civilian casualties. He returns [Junara](../people/junara.md)'s money and instead asks for future business and legitimacy between [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) and Constant's school.

Vince also reveals that [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) has many meetings scheduled that evening, including serpent-church contacts, merchants, nobles, and religious figures.

## Casino Operation

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) enters the casino disguised as [Central Toge](../people/central-toge.md). [Junara](../people/junara.md), [Vis / Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md), and [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) enter under cover, while [Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) remain nearby with [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) support.

At the table are several suspicious figures, including:

- The Mother Superior of the serpent cult.
- [Frederick Blackthorn](../people/frederick-blackthorn.md), a lesser cousin of Lady Stephanie Blackthorn.
- A disguised figure whose identity remains unclear.
- The [High Priestess of Leira](../people/high-priestess-of-leira.md), goddess of illusions and trickery.

[Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) appears at the table, but [Vis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) determines that the figure is only a projection. [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) uses the game to test [Central Toge](../people/central-toge.md)'s identity and removes [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) from the table into a private illusory or remote meeting.

## The Private Test

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) successfully maintains the [Central Toge](../people/central-toge.md) deception despite questioning by [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) and a demoness tied to the Blackthorn deception. [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) believes him enough to reveal that the real meeting is at Hellspire's factory.

After several more hands, [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) invites several table contacts to an after-party on the rented island, creating a future chance to capture them. The green disguised figure leaves separately and disappears before [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) watchers can track him.

## Factory Breach

[Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) detect two protected minds at [Hellspire's Manufactory](../places/hellspires-manufactory.md): one high in the building using [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul prison, and one lower underground, likely [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md).

The party splits:

- [Junara](../people/junara.md), [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), and [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) infiltrate through the front and descend toward [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md).
- Constant, Zuk, and [Vis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) attack from above in draconic form to recover [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul and kill Hellspire.

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) bends the magical ward open with the language of creation. [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) disables the trapped door. [Junara](../people/junara.md) sprints through the lower levels and drops an Anti-Magic Field directly on [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) just as he reacts.

## Dark Star Falls

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) grabs [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s blade before it can be drawn. [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) lands Stunning Strike, then Quivering Palm. With [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) stripped of magical defenses by [Junara](../people/junara.md)'s Anti-Magic Field, the strike drops him to zero before he can act.

[Junara](../people/junara.md) strips [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) of items and captures his soul once the field drops. [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s blades consume [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)-anchor blade, weakening [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) again.

## Hellspire Falls

Above, Constant, Zuk, and [Vis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) crash through the upper level. Constant opens with antimatter fire, Zuk follows with lightning and claws, and [Vis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) in amethyst dragon form uses force breath and claws.

Hellspire is killed before he can enter his prepared mech suit, activate defenses, or use [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul further. [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul prison is recovered.

## Consequences

The party eliminates two major Black Viper threats in one operation without triggering the casino guards, factory alarms, or Hellspire's heavier defenses.

[Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul is rescued. [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul remains missing.

The party earns a major level-up milestone after destroying two boss-level threats and another [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) anchor.
