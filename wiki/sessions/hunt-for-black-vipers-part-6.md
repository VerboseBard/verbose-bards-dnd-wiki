# Side Session: The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 6

This side session follows [Eve](../people/eve.md)'s disappearance into [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s hidden prison, the island confrontation with the serpent cult leadership, and the party's retreat to the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md) for containment and interrogation.

## Overview

While the party finishes the [Big Tusk](../places/big-tusk.md) operation, [Eve](../people/eve.md) is pulled into a between-world prison built by [Lucius](../people/lucius.md). There she meets the being remembered as [Caden / Baba Yaga](../people/caden-baba-yaga.md), learns his history with [Alen](../people/alen.md), and escapes only when [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s pain opens a crack in the prison.

Back on [Saharun](../places/saharun.md), the party secures [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul, confronts the Mother Superior of [Rax'al Karath](../factions/cobra-cult-of-raxal-karath.md), and discovers that consuming a [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)-tainted serpent soul is dangerous even for [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s daughter.

## Eve in the Prison Between

[Eve](../people/eve.md)'s wild possibility magic pulls on [Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s Raven Queen mantle and sends [Eve](../people/eve.md) into a prison between realities. In the prison, divinity and magic are suppressed almost completely.

Across from [Eve](../people/eve.md) is [Caden](../people/caden-baba-yaga.md), also known to the githyanki as [Baba Yaga](../people/caden-baba-yaga.md). He tells [Eve](../people/eve.md) that [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) used Mulligan's hammer and divine relic power to create the prison. Mortals who fall in usually die, but [Eve](../people/eve.md) survives because of her divine nature.

Over weeks of prison-time, [Eve](../people/eve.md) reads him her signed romance book and he tells her a much older love story: his life as a shapeshifter, his love for [Alen](../people/alen.md), the hunger within him, the dragon cores, [Mave](../people/mave.md)'s past, his war on the githyanki, and his hatred of Mulligan's betrayal.

## Caden's Escape Gift

When [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s blades devour another [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) anchor, [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s pain shakes the prison. [Eve](../people/eve.md) and [Caden](../people/caden-baba-yaga.md) use the crack to punch a temporary breach. [Caden](../people/caden-baba-yaga.md) pours power into [Eve](../people/eve.md) and sends her out, asking her to tell the daughter of his heart that he loves her and to warn his grandson to be careful with the blades.

[Eve](../people/eve.md) returns beside [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) just after [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s blade is consumed.

## Charlie's Soul

At [Hellspire's Manufactory](../places/hellspires-manufactory.md), [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md), [Vis / Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md), and [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) examine the device holding [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul. The machine feeds magic into the soul prison to sustain it while also enabling questioning.

Constance identifies Hellspire's design logic and safely shuts the system down. [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul prison is recovered without triggering the facility's defense systems.

## Island Confrontation

The party teleports to the rented island where several casino contacts are gathered. Two lesser nobles surrender immediately. [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) confronts the [Mother Superior of Rax'al Karath](../people/mother-superior-of-raxal-karath.md), arguing that her god has been usurped by [Lucius](../people/lucius.md).

The [High Priestess of Leira](../people/high-priestess-of-leira.md) steps aside and refuses to fight. The Mother Superior stalls long enough for reinforcements to teleport in.

## Battle at the Observatory

The Mother Superior splits into two serpent forms and attacks. A spider queen also arrives, briefly controlling [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) through pheromones before retreating after the tide turns.

[Eve](../people/eve.md) uses a twinned, subtle Firebolt empowered by her absorbed lava/fire charge. The spell burns through the Mother Superior's forms, the emerging serpent aspect, and the soul beneath, destroying the cult leader almost instantly.

[Junvara](../people/junvara.md) attempts to capture the soul, but [Eve](../people/eve.md) instinctively pulls it into herself instead.

## Lucius's Bite

The Mother Superior's soul contains a [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)-tainted anchor. When [Eve](../people/eve.md) consumes it without knowing how to purge the taint, [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) tears part of her essence away. She loses power and becomes violently ill.

Later, [Belle](../people/belle.md) meets [Eve](../people/eve.md) in dreams, shows her how to trap the taint in a soul coin, and teaches her how to consume future [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) fragments more safely.

## Citadel Crystal

The party retreats to the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), a nonmagical and divinity-suppressing command space inside the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)'s great crystal. [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) wants a place where [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) cannot easily interfere with interrogation.

The location is safe but painful for [Eve](../people/eve.md), who has just spent weeks cut off from magic and then suffered soul damage. [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md) uses restoration to ease some of her exhaustion before she retreats into [Junvara](../people/junvara.md)'s mansion to recover.

## Other Threads

- [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) declares [Saharun](../places/saharun.md) off limits to the Conclave's interference.
- The [High Priestess of Leira](../people/high-priestess-of-leira.md) agrees to carry that message back.
- [Frederick Blackthorn](../people/frederick-blackthorn.md) and [Timothy Clarence](../people/timothy-clarence.md) are questioned and appear more foolish or misled than malicious.
- Zuk confronts [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) about losing control under the spider queen's pheromones and tells him to address his repressed desires or seek help.
- The next planned interrogation target is [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s soul, with questions focused on [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul, [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s timeline, Rexal/Vexal's summoning, and [Winrah Holm](../people/winrah-holm.md).
