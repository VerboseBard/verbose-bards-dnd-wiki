# Side Session: The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 7

This side session follows the interrogation of [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s soul, [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s trap inside the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), and the discovery of a new way to track [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul.

## Overview

The party decides not to wait for a long rest before questioning [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md). The interrogation reveals that [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul has already been woven into [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s resurrection lattice and that [Winrah Holm](../people/winrah-holm.md) is acting independently and fanatically.

The last question lets [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) use [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s soul, the old wound in the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) Crystal, and hidden contingencies in the prisoners to partially return. The party defeats the fragment before it can destroy the crystal, but the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)'s last perfect blind spot from divine sight is permanently compromised.

## Before the Interrogation

[Eve / Eva](../people/eva.md) rests outside the crystal in [Junara](../people/junara.md)'s mansion after trapping the [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) fragment that had been eating her soul inside a soul coin.

Inside the crystal, [Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) recover from the island fight while Zuk confronts [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) about the spider queen's pheromone control. Zuk frames [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md)'s old vow of celibacy as a potentially dangerous oath made without full understanding and urges him to seek help before repression makes him unsafe.

[Vis / Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md), in [Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) form, receives a non-divine mechanical call from [Greg](../people/greg.md). [Greg](../people/greg.md) warns that the god-blinding crystals were originally [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s design: useful for hiding mortals, but also the shadow that allowed divine betrayal and [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s old plots to go unseen. [Greg](../people/greg.md) still believes small, carefully controlled crystals may be useful, while [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s warning is that recreating [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s blind spots at scale could repeat the same disaster.

## Dark Star's Soul

[Junara](../people/junara.md) and [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) activate the soul cage in a heavily warded interrogation chamber. [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s soul manifests, but [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s features begin bleeding through as the questioning proceeds.

[Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) answers that he no longer knows where Winrah is. He says she has been away for weeks, is more fanatical than the Mother Superior, and is no longer simply working with him.

When asked about [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md), the [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) bleed becomes stronger. [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md) reveals that [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul has been infused into the spell lattice meant to raise [Lucian](../people/lucian.md). [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) is not fully spent yet, because the final seal still needs him intact.

## The Concurrence

Asked where the ritual will happen, the [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)-shaped answer reframes the problem. The location is not only a place; it is a frequency and a when. [The Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) rewrote reality, and Constant's choice to let dragons remain cracked a seal close enough for the serpent-dragon symbolism [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) needs.

When asked when [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) will rise as Rax'al Karath, the answer is immediate: now.

## Crystal Trap

[Lucian](../people/lucian.md) uses a psychic frequency carried through [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s soul to resonate with the old wound where he once stabbed the god-killing dagger into the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) Crystal. The crystal begins to crack.

At the same time, [Timothy Clarence](../people/timothy-clarence.md) and [Frederick Blackthorn](../people/frederick-blackthorn.md)'s hidden soul contingencies detonate. They had not known what was implanted in them. Their soul explosions damage security arrays, wound [Jarth](../people/jarth.md), and allow [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s fragment to flee the soul cage and enter [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s stored armor.

## Battle in the Crystal

[Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s armored fragment emerges in the central crystal structure. He thanks [Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) for making the larger resurrection path possible and begins using gravity, necrotic fields, and crystal debris as weapons.

The party converges quickly. [Junara](../people/junara.md) and [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) cut through his armor and force him through phases. [Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) blast weak points with radiant and antimatter fire. [Eve](../people/eva.md) teleports back into the crystal despite exhaustion. [Vis](../people/vis-jarvis.md)/Jarvis analyzes the shifting phases, protects the party with Circle of Power, and dispels key armor components. [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) closes into melee and burns through [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s legendary defenses with Stunning Strike and Quivering Palm pressure.

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) uses Words of Creation to counter the resonance destroying the crystal. The song stops the shattering from becoming catastrophic and later prevents [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s dying soul explosion from detonating at full strength.

## Fragment Destroyed

When the armor is stripped away, [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s fragment becomes unstable and begins flickering between corporeal and incorporeal states. The party has only moments to destroy it before it detonates.

[Junara](../people/junara.md) attempts to lock the fragment in form with magic, then uses a flurry of attacks and his echo to strike during the brief windows when it can be hit. The final blow destroys the fragment before it can complete its explosion.

The [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) Crystal survives. It remains damaged, and divine sight can now see into it through cracks and breaches, but the heart of authority and [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) neutrality remains standing.

## Aftermath

[Eve](../people/eva.md) tries to reach for the fading soul residue, wanting to test [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s lesson on soul consumption. [Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) stops her long enough for the moment to pass, arguing that she should learn on smaller souls before attempting another [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) fragment.

[Junara](../people/junara.md) senses the [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) fragment trapped in [Eve](../people/eva.md)'s soul coin and trades her a large cache of diamonds, jewelry, healing potions, and an Angel's Tear for it. [Greg](../people/greg.md) later calls [Junara](../people/junara.md) directly and explains that the coin can be used in a ritual to trace the soul fragment back to the concentration of [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s soul powering the main ritual and [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s imprisonment.

[Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) begin repairs on the crystal's internal systems. [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) continues singing to stabilize damaged crystalline structure. [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) helps move debris and rescue the wounded, while [Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) heals survivors with divine access restored through the cracks.

## Consequences

- [Dark Star](../people/dark-star.md)'s soul is gone as an independent captive, overwritten and spent by [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s trap.
- [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul is confirmed to be part of [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s resurrection lattice, but not yet fully consumed.
- The [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md) is still functional but no longer fully hidden from the gods.
- [Greg](../people/greg.md) breaks his own intervention rules to give [Jarvis](../people/vis-jarvis.md) and [Junara](../people/junara.md) a path to [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md).
- The Conclave witnesses the battle through the damaged crystal and decides not to openly defy the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md)' power for now.
- The party reaches level 24 after surviving the trap.
