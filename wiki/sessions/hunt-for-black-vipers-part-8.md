# Side Session: The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 8

This side session follows the long rest after [Lucius's Crystal Trap](../concepts/lucius-crystal-trap.md), the 24-hour ritual to find [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md), renewed bargains with divine and draconic powers, and the Conclave's private offer to [Junvara](../people/junvara.md).

## Overview

The party finally receives a full rest after the crystal battle. That rest does not mean quiet. [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md), [Vis / Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md), [Eve](../people/eve.md), [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md), [Junvara](../people/junvara.md), and [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) each receive major revelations or offers before the ritual to locate [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) completes.

The session ends with the party preparing for the final push. Once the ritual is complete, there will be no more long rests before the confrontation.

## Gregario's Dream

[Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) sleeps peacefully for the first time since [Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) severed his old Raven Queen connection. [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s severed middle fingers resonate with the fragment recently destroyed in the crystal, sating the hunger left inside him.

In a true dream, [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) sees ruined [Silverbend](../places/silver-bend.md) and [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) crying over the belief that he is gone. The dream is not prophecy, but it reveals the shape of her pain.

## Vis and Jarvice

[Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md) speaks with [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md) in a dreamlike version of [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md)'s workshop. [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md) apologizes for treating [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md) as an upgraded [Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md) rather than as a newly created life.

[Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md) offers to separate [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md) into his own body so the young soul is not forced to risk annihilation in the final mission. [Vis](../people/vis-jarvice.md) accepts. [Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md) remains in the warforged body and is invited to become the official Avatar of the Architect.

[Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md) begins [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md)'s 24-hour ritual using [Eve](../people/eve.md)'s [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)-tainted soul coin. The ritual builds a divine-arcane compass that can point directly to the center of the resurrection working. [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) later enhances the ritual through Words of Creation, improving [Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md)'s Arcana work.

## Belle and Eve

[Belle](../people/belle.md) meets [Eve](../people/eve.md) in dreams after [Eve](../people/eve.md)'s soul damage is healed. Stung by [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md)'s criticism of her parenting, [Belle](../people/belle.md) gives [Eve](../people/eve.md) the [Hairpin of Cuteness](../items/hairpin-of-cuteness.md), a golden paperclip-like hairpin tied to [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s own style.

[Belle](../people/belle.md) also lets [Eve](../people/eve.md) watch one of [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s active fey curses play out and explains more about her split aspects. She warns [Eve](../people/eve.md) that [Belle](../people/belle.md) did teach [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) a lesser version of soul-splitting, but argues that her own fragmentation is superior because she divided psyche and identity rather than lazily hiding soul pieces in objects.

[Belle](../people/belle.md) tells [Eve](../people/eve.md) not to eat [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s final divine soul if she lands the killing blow. Doing so would pull [Eve](../people/eve.md) into full godhood and all the politics that come with it too soon.

## Lilith and Gregario

Constance, Zuk, [Eve](../people/eve.md), and [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) return to [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md) after [Gwyne](../people/gwyne.md) warns them that [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) and her dragon egg are in crisis.

[Lilith](../people/lilith.md) believes bonding with the dragon severed or killed [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) because she can no longer feel him. [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) proves he is alive, and the misunderstanding breaks. [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) manifests a new ring and asks for him back.

[Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) accepts the [Ring of Lilith's Favor](../items/ring-of-liliths-favor.md). His vampiric nature is burned away, and he becomes an eight-foot aasimar with black wings: the champion of the Raven Queen mantle that now rests in [Lilith](../people/lilith.md). [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) also ages forward emotionally and physically into the beginnings of a young adolescent.

## Lilith's Dragon

[Lilith](../people/lilith.md)'s dragon hatches as a black dragon with blue eyes and purple-tinted scales, called Greggy. [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) realize the bond between [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) and the dragon is deeper than ordinary dragon bonds and that both child and dragon carry impossible divine implications.

[Lilith](../people/lilith.md) is more than a demigod but not yet strong enough to ascend or fulfill the duties of the Raven Queen. Because she is bonded to a dragon, their futures are now interlinked.

## The Flight of the Ancients

[Gwyne](../people/gwyne.md) takes [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) into the mental assembly of the [Flight of the Ancients](../factions/flight-of-the-ancients.md), where elder dragons meet. There they encounter [Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md), who reveals herself not as a god but as a legend: a being dragons and mortals believe back into existence.

[Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md) explains that dragons were once the guardians and lattice of reality before dungeons took that role. [Lucius](../people/lucius.md), the Raven Queen, and well-meaning heroes helped kill dragon guardians and consume their power to make the old divine solution work, breaking the world in the process.

[Lucius](../people/lucius.md) later bound the reborn [Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md) in the Hells as a guardian. She and other dragons have therefore supported the effort to resurrect all of [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) into one destroyable form, because only then can he be ended and [Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md) freed.

[Tiamat](../people/tiamat.md) asks [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) not to stop [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return prematurely if the final ritual reaches that point. Letting him rise may be the only way to destroy every fragment permanently. She brands them with the [Brand of the Flight of the Ancients](../items/brand-of-the-flight-of-the-ancients.md) and names Constance the future Mother of Dragons for this world.

## The Conclave Meeting

[Professor Hazard](../people/professor-hazard.md) invites [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) and [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) to meet a private faction within the [Conclave of Churches](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md). The meeting includes representatives of several older gods and excludes direct divine champions of the younger gods.

The faction explains that they are not trying to preserve theocracy forever. Their stated aim is to stabilize worlds raised under divine rule, gradually move power into secular hands, and reduce the unchecked influence of the young gods.

They also reveal that some factions allowed or assisted [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return because fully gathering his soul into one place may be the only way to destroy him forever. They identify [Julia](../people/julia-julisia.md), the Conquer / Power fragment later involved in the Julisia merger, as the one who set much of the resurrection game in motion.

## Arbiter Offer

The Conclave faction offers [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) the role of [Arbiter](../concepts/arbiter-of-the-conclave.md), a secular authority empowered to hold gods, churches, and divine servants accountable from inside the system. [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) refuses any divine item or empowerment but is willing to consider a wider adjudicator structure with multiple non-divine voices.

The faction gives [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) a medallion that can summon their champions for the battle against [Lucius](../people/lucius.md).

## Kestrel's Gift

[Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) later meets [Nicole](../people/nicole.md) at his music shop. She gives him the [Shifter's Mask](../items/shifters-mask.md), an ancient [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) artifact recovered from a dangerous temple connected to his people's forgotten history.

The mask is made from material like [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s own changeling nature and carries a dormant mind or personality. [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md)'s ancient blades recognize it from the old temple.

## End State

- [Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md)'s ritual is on course to increase the chance of saving [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) and point directly to the final ritual.
- [Lilith](../people/lilith.md) and [Gregario](../people/gregario-shaq.md) are reconnected through the [Ring of Lilith's Favor](../items/ring-of-liliths-favor.md).
- [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) are marked by the [Flight of the Ancients](../factions/flight-of-the-ancients.md) and warned not to stop [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s return too early.
- [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) has a tentative working channel with part of the Conclave, but refuses divine enhancement.
- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) receives the [Shifter's Mask](../items/shifters-mask.md) and may soon unlock deeper [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md)-linked abilities.
