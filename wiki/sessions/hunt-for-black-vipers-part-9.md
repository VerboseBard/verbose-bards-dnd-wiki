# The Hunt for Black Vipers, Part 9

Part 9 begins the direct assault on [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s resurrection lattice and turns the rescue of [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) into a dangerous ritual campaign.

## Opening State

The session reframes [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s long victory: his real triumph was not survival, but the sealing of the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md). Even dead, his contingencies, soul anchors, sleeper cells, and divine loopholes continued moving.

[Julia / Julisia](../people/julia-julisia.md)'s revenge is also clarified. She is one of six fractured aspects of the devil [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) shattered long ago. By triggering the [Black Vipers](../factions/black-vipers.md), she created a lure that [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) would not notice quickly enough, drawing the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) and their allies toward the resurrection before it became inevitable.

## Departures and Warnings

At [Constance and Zuk's Dragon-Bonding School](../places/constance-and-zuks-dragon-bonding-school.md), [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) releases [Charlie](../people/charlie.md)'s soul at dawn. A felt presence of Gray reassures Constance, and [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) fades with a final smile instead of being held for resurrection.

On [Opaal](../places/opaal.md), [Jack Kenway](../people/jack-kenway.md) decides to return to the [Hell's Bane Heroes](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md)' orbit. His daughter asks him to help settle [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s debt to their people, and Jack brings the artifact hunting harpoon left to him by his wife.

[Eve](../people/eve.md) meets [Belle](../people/belle.md) in dreams and chooses to free [Caden / Baba Yaga](../people/caden-baba-yaga.md) before the final push. With [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s power and [Eve](../people/eve.md)'s memory of the [Prison Between Worlds](../places/prison-between-worlds.md), they find the prison, shatter [Caden](../people/caden-baba-yaga.md)'s bars, and release him to join the fight later.

[Mave](../people/mave.md) privately warns [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) that attempts to save [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) could damage the fundamentals of magic. She asks him not to participate directly in the extraction attempt, but to stand ready to save magic itself if the ritualists lose control.

## Greg's Briefing

Through [Vis / Jarvice](../people/vis-jarvice.md), [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md) explains the resurrection mechanism. [Lucius](../people/lucius.md)'s soul-fragment attack on the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md) activated linked rituals through the [Ley Lines](../concepts/ley-lines.md). [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul has been split into four fragments within that lattice.

The party receives three divine artifacts: one to follow the ritual path, one to contain recovered [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) fragments, and one to travel between linked ritual sites. At each site they must secure the ritual, keep it stable, and either rewrite the magic or substitute another powerful soul for [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s fragment.

[Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md) lays out the thresholds:

- One fragment prevents [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) from suffering eternal ritual torment.
- Two fragments allow [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) to be put to rest.
- Three fragments allow reincarnation without memories.
- All four fragments allow resurrection.

## Allied Waves

[Junvara](../people/junvara.md) accepts aid from the [Conclave of Churches](../factions/conclave-of-churches.md). The first wave includes the party, Zuk, and three Conclave champions: [Terrence of Tyr](../people/terrence-of-tyr.md), [Saraphel of the Dawnfather](../people/saraphel-of-the-dawnfather.md), and Veilen of Valora.

[Admiral Gray](../people/admiral-gray.md) of the Giff fleet also contacts [Junvara](../people/junvara.md), offering the [Royal Marines](../factions/royal-marines.md) against the being responsible for the God Emperor's favorite son's death. A [442nd](../factions/442nd.md) scout becomes the ley-line shuttle operator, while later waves prepare to bring [Nicole](../people/nicole.md), her wolfen guards, the [Royal Marines](../factions/royal-marines.md), and other military support.

## The Desert Well

The first jump drops the party beside a desert well and ruins. The calm welcome from the well-keepers is false; true sight and arcane sensing reveal invisible enemies and a major magical signature in the water.

[Eve](../people/eve.md) opens with a multi-target Scorching Ray, exposing the ambush and destroying an air djinn in a fiery pressure burst. [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) captures the djinn's soul for use as a possible substitute in the ritual.

The battle expands into scorpions, wraiths, demons, and hidden guardians. The party uses gravity magic, radiant fire, lightning harpoons, dragon guns, stunning strikes, and Conclave support to break the oasis defenses and enter the portal beneath the well.

## The Hell Descent

The portal leads into a hellish descent of spiral stairs, lava, flying demons, bone horrors, and layered defenders. [Eve](../people/eve.md)'s radiant Meteor Swarm annihilates much of the lower battlefield and triggers a living-arcana surge that temporarily empowers her magic at personal risk.

The party pushes through waves of demons and undead. Conclave champions nearly die in the fighting, but their interventions matter: Terrence later reflects a necrotic door trap back into the ritual chamber, killing lesser defenders and weakening the main guardian.

## First Fragment Recovered

Inside the ritual chamber, a lich maintains the first ritual and cannot stop without destabilizing the working. [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) recognizes that killing the lich before someone takes over would interrupt the spell and risk damage to magic.

Veilen steps in to maintain the ritual. The party kills the lich, and [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) captures its soul as a second replacement option.

For the first extraction, [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) acts as primary ritualist and [Eve](../people/eve.md) assists. They choose soul substitution rather than spell rewriting, using the djinn soul to replace [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s fragment. The exchange succeeds: the first crystal fragment of [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md)'s soul is pulled into the artifact, the ritual remains stable, and magic does not break.

## End State

The party short rests at the first ritual site. Veilen remains behind to maintain the ritual. About halfway through the rest, the next allied wave arrives, including [Nicole](../people/nicole.md), her guards, and the [Royal Marines](../factions/royal-marines.md).

[Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) now has one of four fragments recovered. Whatever happens next, he will not be trapped in eternal ritual torment.
