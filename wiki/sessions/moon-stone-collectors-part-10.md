# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10: Battle at Ebonwing Manor

Part 10 is reconstructed from the review given at the start of the following session. The original session recording is unavailable, but the recap identifies the main events: the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) breach [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md), fight more than twenty demons, prevent the Deceiver's escape, and end with [Juan](../people/juan.md) turning on [June](../people/june.md) under mind control.

## Door Breach

The session begins from the [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9](moon-stone-collectors-part-9.md) cliffhanger. [Juan](../people/juan.md) and [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) are trapped inside the manor's high-stakes game, while the rest of the party arrives outside.

The doors are breached, and the fight begins against roughly twenty-five demons and disguised enemies inside the manor.

## Brain Shenanigans

The enemy relies heavily on mental manipulation. Several party members are affected, but people repeatedly break free before the control can fully define the battle.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) turns invisible during the fight. [Denny](../people/denny.md) goes after the tooth-themed assassin, beating him badly enough that the table jokes the assassin is nearly toothless by the end.

## Vaelith Escapes

The party begins to gain the upper hand, but [Vaelith the Whisperer](../people/vaelith-the-whisperer.md) warns the others that they have only a few seconds before reinforcements arrive.

Vaelith steps onto a teleporter or portal and vanishes, escaping the battle.

## The Patron Deal

In the final seconds before outside help arrives, several members of the party go down.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md)'s patron offers a deal: both patron and warlock want the party to survive and do not want to lose what they have built. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) accepts. The exact cost and consequences are left unresolved for later discovery.

## Reinforcements

Because of an extremely strong luck result, major reinforcements arrive. [Junara](../people/junara.md) joins the fight, as does Saraphel, champion of the Dawnfather.

Stonework, walls, and coordinated attacks on the Deceiver's clones prevent [Lyris the Deceiver](../people/lyris-the-deceiver.md) or the Deceiver-linked presence from escaping.

## The Deceiver Snuffed Out

The champion of the Dawnfather kills the Deceiver with a blade of divine retribution, snuffing her out of existence.

As she dies, she says, "Worth it." The line foreshadows that the fight was not only about survival or escape. She had planted contingencies across the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) that would activate after her death.

## Juan Turns on June

After the Deceiver falls, [Junara](../people/junara.md) moves to secure the killer of his friend [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md).

[Juan](../people/juan.md), still compromised by enemy influence, attempts to kill [June](../people/june.md) with the dagger. Even with advantage and other benefits, the attack rolls low enough to fail.

[June](../people/june.md) survives, grabs [Juan](../people/juan.md)'s hand, and the session ends on that unresolved moment.
