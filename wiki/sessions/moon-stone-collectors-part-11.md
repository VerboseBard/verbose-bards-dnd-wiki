# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 11: Flight from the Citadel

Part 11 is reconstructed from the recap at the start of the following recording. It covers the aftermath of [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md): the Deceiver's contingencies erupt across the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) is overtaken by [Julia / Julisia](../people/julia-julisia.md), and the party flees to [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md).

## After the Manor

The session resumes after [Juan](../people/juan.md)'s failed mind-controlled attack on [June](../people/june.md). The party convinces the surviving allies not to kill [Juan](../people/juan.md) on the spot.

[Big Bart](../items/big-bart.md) intervenes through [Juan](../people/juan.md), insisting that [Juan](../people/juan.md)'s mind was weak and that he has shut the situation down. Bart also warns that the Deceiver's death likely means more trouble across the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).

## Worth It

When the Deceiver-linked enemy is destroyed in [Moon Stone Collectors, Part 10](moon-stone-collectors-part-10.md), her final words are "Worth it."

Those words prove literal. The Deceiver had planted imprinted commands, memories, and contingency plans in hundreds of people across the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) over the preceding years. Her death activates them. Sleepers murder loved ones, attack civilians, retrieve hidden relics, move toward prepared sites, or sign dangerous paperwork.

The city descends into chaotic magical and political violence.

## Veil Ascends

Elsewhere, [Veil, Guardian of the Citadel](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md), faces the end of his long stewardship. After roughly a thousand years guiding and protecting the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), he chooses not to remain in the crystal or take a new mortal host.

He walks away from the control panels and ascends from a beloved garden. His light grows beyond the crystal, marking the end of his active guardianship.

[Mave](../people/mave.md), meanwhile, prepares to leave and search for the twins she wants to protect in the new world order.

## Return to the School

The party returns to the school to check on [Elisia](../people/elisia.md), [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md), and the other wounded.

The school has been attacked. Civilians, warriors, and mind-controlled attackers lie dead from the front doors to the inner areas. Arnold and Big Delilah are among the dead, having defended the school as long as they could.

The controller for [Elisia](../people/elisia.md)'s [Collar of Demon Subjugation](../items/collar-of-demon-subjugation.md) dies or shorts out as the party approaches.

## Julisia

The party finds [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) standing over [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s stasis pod. A second face is superimposed over hers, and her collar is damaged or burned away.

She identifies herself as [Julia / Julisia](../people/julia-julisia.md). [Julara](../people/julara.md) warns the party not to approach. [Julisia](../people/julia-julisia.md) thanks them, says that their people cared for her and that she cared for [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md), then vanishes through darkness.

The stasis shield over [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) remains intact. Blood has run down the outside of the barrier, implying that [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) or [Julisia](../people/julia-julisia.md) protected him while the school fell.

## Leaving

The party debates whether to stay and face the Conclave or leave. Because they had accepted responsibility for [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) and she is now gone, remaining could result in arrest, execution, or being turned into scapegoats by the Dawnfather's hardliners.

[Luca](../people/luca.md) chooses to stay behind and keep fighting for the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md). Before the party leaves, [Luca](../people/luca.md) receives a divine blessing connected to the struggle ahead.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) sends a message by bee to [Junara](../people/junara.md), saying the party is going to [Ice Watch](../places/ice-watch.md) and that he will still help with the captive assassin if needed.

The party teleports away with [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) and [Juan](../people/juan.md) unconscious or incapacitated, leaving the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) crisis behind.

## Ice Watch and the Chuckling Gecko

The group arrives on [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md), where the ice is cracking and melting under the changed sky. They can now see other worlds hanging like moons around the joined world.

They reach [Ice Watch](../places/ice-watch.md), then the [Chuckling Gecko](../places/chuckling-gecko.md), a kobold-run inn full of dragon-mocking decor and energetic service. The party rests, plans the next leg, and decides to travel toward the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) in hopes of helping [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md).
