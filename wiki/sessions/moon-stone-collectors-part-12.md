# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 12: The Thawing Road

Part 12 follows the party's overland journey from [Frostfire Keep](../places/frostfire-keep.md) toward the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md). The thaw reveals buried ruins, old dwarven outposts, predatory dangers, and puzzles tied to the ancient road north.

## Chuckling Gecko Morning

The party wakes at the [Chuckling Gecko](../places/chuckling-gecko.md). The kobold staff serve breakfast with chaotic precision, backed by older kobolds quietly using small magic to keep the flying dishes and food from becoming a disaster.

Because the party helped reinforce the local teleport circle the night before, the watch covers the meal. The kobolds take advantage of that blank check by serving a full [Heroes' Feast](../items/heroes-feast.md)-quality breakfast.

An adventuring party just back from the north warns [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) that the thaw has changed the old paths. Frozen lakes are surfacing, monsters are moving south, winter wolves are aggressive, giant bears are acting wrong, and old ruins are emerging from the ice.

## Shopping Before the Road

Before leaving, the group buys a sled and supplies for [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) and the journey.

[Zen](../people/zen.md) buys the [Hat of Wishful Thinking](../items/hat-of-wishful-thinking.md), a shape-changing hat that can later receive additional enchantments. The local enchanter, Widow Margaret, adds fire and poison resistance.

[p'Op](../people/p-op.md) visits a trapper's shop and buys [Iron Bands of Bilarro](../items/iron-bands-of-bilarro.md) for nonlethal capture work. The shopkeeper also shows him more expensive magical restraint tools, including rod-like perimeter traps and a whip-and-shackle system.

## Frostfire Keep

The party teleports to [Frostfire Keep](../places/frostfire-keep.md), a rough northern frontier outpost built around the surviving spiral of an ancient dwarven defensive tower.

The area is muddy from the thaw, and the teleport yard is at risk. [Zen](../people/zen.md) uses stone magic and [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) transmutes mud to stone, stabilizing the platform and earning the party a free stay at the [Chuckling Gecko](../places/chuckling-gecko.md) before they head out.

## Road North

The group sets out with their carx mounts, sled, [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md), [Juan](../people/juan.md), [Denny](../people/denny.md), and supplies.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md)'s arctic training and favored terrain help the group keep a strong pace. The sky is clear, warm, and strange, with steam rising from melting snow and the newly joined worlds visible overhead.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) explains that he wants to take [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) to the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md). The grove's chromate fungal network is symbiotic with [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) and his bees. He hopes its wisdom and living mycelium may help [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s absent mind where ordinary healing failed.

## The Wolves' Plea

During the first night's watch, [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) feels a wild, primal mind press against his. A winter wolf pack has surrounded the camp, not to attack, but to ask for help.

The alpha shows impressions of trapped pups, fear, hunger, and danger from the north. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md), [Zen](../people/zen.md), and [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) follow the pack to a cave several miles off the path.

Inside, a wall of force blocks the pups behind a sealed dwarven or ancient door. [Zen](../people/zen.md) identifies the force barrier and is hurled backward by its one-way defensive magic. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) dispels the barrier, then uses stone magic to reveal that the wall is actually a hidden doorway.

The door puzzle uses stone, fire, and water. After the party activates the matching symbols, the door opens and the pack rescues twelve exhausted wolf pups.

The alpha warns them of awakened evils, ice hydras, giant bears acting wrong, old dead soldiers rising, and a vast frozen lake with something evil beneath it.

## The Hidden Worksite

Behind the wolves' chamber is a metal passage resembling a mine shaft or work tunnel. The party finds a set of ancient [Dwarven Mastercraft Tools](../items/dwarven-mastercraft-tools.md) and several old magical light stones.

The tools resize for use and grant exceptional help to crafting. The lights still work after ages, giving off dim red light.

## Gateway to the Empire

On the second night, the party reaches another old outpost. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) recognizes the place as one of the known campsites, but the thaw reveals more of the original dwarven construction.

The site includes an old barracks where the dead are not to be disturbed, a sealed reinforced building, a well, and a magically shaped stone ramp to nowhere.

The reinforced building turns out to be an old bank or vault for the [Gateway to the Empire](../places/gateway-to-the-empire.md), the last outpost of the dwarven empire. The party forces the door, dislodging an immovable rod used as an internal brace.

Inside, they find ancient electrum coins and records. [Paul](../people/paul.md) learns that a dwarven necromancer and undead army once attacked the outpost, and that a special group arrived in a massive gnomish war vehicle, killed the undead, robbed the bank, and escaped over the wall using the ramp.

## The Well Puzzle

[Juan](../people/juan.md) finds an amulet at the bottom of the well: the [Amulet of Lost Memories](../items/amulet-of-lost-memories.md).

Removing it reveals more puzzle pieces: symbol discs tied to stone, water, fire, eyes, a key, a skull, a tree, and a hammer. The party also finds several dwarven stanzas that appear to form a larger riddle.

They do not solve the full puzzle before resting, but the clues suggest a hidden mechanism or key connected to craft, death, sight, stone, and revelation by light.

## Terrence's Judgment

That night, [Terrence of Tyr](../people/terrence-of-tyr.md) reaches the camp. He is a winged justicar sent by the Conclave to judge the party's actions regarding [Elisia](../people/elisia.md), rather than drag them back by force.

He shares food, speaks with [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) about faith, and explains that [Luca](../people/luca.md)'s testimony helped prevent a harsher response. In the morning, Terrence judges that the party acted in good faith: they protected [Elisia](../people/elisia.md), did not take her into danger, and could not have stopped the attack that took her.

He warns that the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) and the churches will become dangerous in the next few years as old gods lose influence, new gods gain reach, and the Conclave struggles for power.

## The Frozen Lake

The road brings the party to the vast frozen lake the wolves warned about. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) uses Water Walk and Pass Without Trace to move the group across while avoiding attention from what lies below.

Under the ice, they see old weapons, armor, ancient corpses, and undead soldiers moving slowly beneath the surface. The party crosses carefully, carrying the sled over weak ice when necessary.

The route takes them into the forest from the southwest rather than [Vylra](../people/vylra.md)'s preferred southeast approach. They make camp at the tree line, now roughly halfway to the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md).
