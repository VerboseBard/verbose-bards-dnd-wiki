# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 14: The Fey-Touched Rescue

Part 14 follows the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) immediately after [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s restoration at the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md). Before the party can leave to confront the [Ancient Ice Hydra](../concepts/ancient-ice-hydra.md), a missing-children emergency pulls them through a mushroom circle and into the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md).

## Rune-Light Study

On the evening before departure, [Zen](../people/zen.md) shows [Paul](../people/paul.md) several ancient dwarven light crystals taken from the old tunnel near the dwarven outpost.

The crystals are ruby or amber colored and still glow after more than a thousand years. [Paul](../people/paul.md) and [Zen](../people/zen.md) determine that the enchantment is built from three interlocking rune effects:

- One grows the gemstone to the correct size and shape.
- One gathers ambient magic and condenses it inside the gem.
- One sustains a light cantrip at the center.

The work is elegant, expensive, and far beyond most modern craftsmanship. [Paul](../people/paul.md) realizes the light rune could theoretically be modified toward radiant output, possibly creating a tool against the [Demonic Dwarven Goo](../concepts/demonic-dwarven-goo.md), but any mistake could release catastrophic stored energy.

## Celestial Demon Responsibility

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) returns [Paul](../people/paul.md)'s stolen summoning salt and raises another concern: [Elisia](../people/elisia.md)'s fate after [Julia](../people/julia-julisia.md) consumed or overtook her, creating [Julisia](../people/julia-julisia.md).

[Paul](../people/paul.md), [Zen](../people/zen.md), and [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) discuss whether the party has a duty to try to restore [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) or separate the fused celestial demons. They recall that the artifact in Hell that originally split the sisters may be one possible path, though reaching it would mean returning to very dangerous ground.

## Home and Chosen Family

The same conversation turns personal. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) asks [Paul](../people/paul.md) and [Zen](../people/zen.md) about home, family, and why they chose dangerous wandering.

[Paul](../people/paul.md) describes learning and exploration as the motive that drives him, but people as the reason he keeps going. [Zen](../people/zen.md) says she likely cannot safely return home and admits she thinks of the group as family.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) offers the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) as a place they can return to, with the warning that staying too long under Blackwood hospitality may make leaving physically difficult.

## Vylra and His Grandfather

Before leaving, [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) speaks with his father, who warns him that his grandfather knows [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) lied by half-truth about how he knew the Ritual of Fated Remembrance. The message is simple: in the grove, if he cannot speak truth, he should not speak.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) then visits his grandfather near the place where the dead are cleaned and prepared for reuse or release. His grandfather looks deeply aged by [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s ritual.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) admits that this was not his first communion with [Viscus](../people/viscus.md). Years earlier, after his chromate-fungus initiation went wrong and Quinn behaved aggressively, [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) showed him a hazy memory of the earlier failed use of the ritual. That older recipient did not survive long outside the grove, and the fragment of [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) within them died as well.

His grandfather suggests that when [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) is older and ready to return, [Viscus](../people/viscus.md) may choose him as one of the heads of the circles.

## The Ancient Mother

As the party leaves the grove, an ancient fox known as the Mother rushes to [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) in distress. With speak with animals, [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) learns that children have fallen and she cannot reach them.

The party follows her to a collapsed hole near the edge of the grove's fields. Toys and disturbed ground show where children were playing. [Paul](../people/paul.md) and the others drop into the hole, but [Juan](../people/juan.md) senses danger and hesitates above.

Inside the collapsed space, the party realizes too late that they are standing in a mushroom circle. Light flares, and everyone below is transported into the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md). [Juan](../people/juan.md) and [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) remain behind.

## Riddles at the Crossroads

The party appears in a bright, strange [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) landscape of vivid grass, mushrooms, impossible trees, and shifting colors.

At a five-way crossroads, a fairy gatekeeper challenges them with riddles before he will reveal the children's path. The group answers most correctly, with help from [Julara](../people/julara.md) and each other.

When the gatekeeper catches [Julara](../people/julara.md) helping [Paul](../people/paul.md), he forces her to answer too. Her failed answer twists [Paul](../people/paul.md)'s navigation in the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md), making survival and navigation harder.

[Soda](../people/soda.md) is also drawn into the trial. The fairy raises her intelligence and grants her speech for the duration of the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) visit, turning her into a fully conversational, very opinionated honey badger.

When the final riddle is solved, the gatekeeper points them toward the froggy lake. [Paul](../people/paul.md) retaliates with a gust of wind as the fairy fades, earning a [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) time curse: when he leaves the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md), he will age several decades.

## Pond of Peril

The path leads to a vast pond full of giant lily pads, enormous frogs, scorpion-tailed dragonflies, and water full of hungry fish.

A flower-dwelling [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) warns them that the children passed through and that the group must survive the trial of leaps and licks. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) casts water walk to protect the party from sinking, but the crossing is still dangerous.

The party leaps from pad to pad while frogs lash with tongues and dragonflies dive from above. [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) is yanked by a frog's tongue, but [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) catches him with grasping vine and [Zen](../people/zen.md)'s silvery barbs helps the party win the contest.

After the crossing, the group continues into a darker [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) forest where unseen creatures watch from beyond the path.

## The Glade Mistress

The trail ends in a beautiful glade ruled by the [Glade Mistress](../people/glade-mistress.md), a powerful [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) noble seated beneath an ancient tree. Dryads sing around her, and the missing children sit nearby, partly out of phase with the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md).

The party realizes the song is not harming the children. It is bringing them into phase with the realm and soothing them into sleep. The group waits quietly for the song to finish.

When the [Glade Mistress](../people/glade-mistress.md) speaks, she explains that the children ate at her table and rested under her protection. In the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md), that kindness creates a debt. She offers to release the children if the party takes the debt on themselves.

The party accepts:

- [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) pays fifty years of life for one child.
- [Paul](../people/paul.md) accepts a debt of strength.
- [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) bargains with an original ballad of the [Glade Mistress](../people/glade-mistress.md).
- [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) offers his ring of fire protection for the last child.

The [Glade Mistress](../people/glade-mistress.md) marks and blesses the children, then grants the party her blessing for twenty-four hours: they cannot be surprised, and their defenses are strengthened.

## Return from the Feywild

The group backtracks safely. The lily pads reshape into a bridge for the protected children, and the wild hunt does not appear.

At the mushroom circle, [Soda](../people/soda.md) threatens the gatekeeper before he can restart the riddle game. He removes her speech and heightened mind, and the group returns to the Mortal lands.

Sixty-five hours have passed outside. Grove rescuers, including [Juan](../people/juan.md), [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md), and the Blackwood cooks, have excavated the collapsed tunnel and waited for the party's return.

[Paul](../people/paul.md) destroys the mushroom circle in rage. The party returns with the children safe, the [Glade Mistress](../people/glade-mistress.md)'s blessing active, and all members of the rescue group touched by [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) magic.

## Rewards and Costs

- The rescue party gains the [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) Touched feat.
- [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) loses fifty years of life.
- [Paul](../people/paul.md) loses personal strength beneath the protection of his artifact belt.
- [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) owes the [Glade Mistress](../people/glade-mistress.md) a ballad and performs it before leaving.
- The party loses nearly three days of Mortal time.
- The hydra mission is delayed, but the group reaches the hunting cabin afterward and remains one to two days from the hydra's territory.
