# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 15: Crown of Fools and Hydra Mates

Part 15 follows the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) after their [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) rescue. The group argues over the mushroom circle, [Paul](../people/paul.md) is marked by [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) aspects, [Julara](../people/julara.md) explains the danger of what he broke, and the party defeats not one hydra but a mated pair.

## Return Tensions

The party returns from the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) after sixty-four or sixty-five hours have passed outside. [Juan](../people/juan.md) and [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) remained in the Mortal lands while the others rescued the children.

[Juan](../people/juan.md) explains that he hesitated because the mushroom circle belonged to a different [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) court than his own and because entering too early could have caused the rescue groups to miss each other across unstable [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) time.

[Paul](../people/paul.md), furious about the [Feywild](../places/feywild.md) ordeal, destroys the mushroom circle. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) points out that the portal itself is already broken and asks him to stop destroying the grove's land.

## Belle's Judgment

That night, [Paul](../people/paul.md) dreams of three aspects of [Belle](../people/belle.md): the Queen of Spring, the Queen of Mischief, and the Queen of Dreams.

The Spring aspect condemns him for breaking a lifeline between the [Kuran Grove](../places/kuran-grove.md) and the Spring Court. The Mischief aspect delights in the chaos. The Dream aspect silences the others long enough to make the consequence clear.

Together, the three aspects mark [Paul](../people/paul.md) with the [Crown of Fools](../items/crown-of-fools.md), a mushroom crown that imposes a [Fey](../concepts/fey.md)-light curse and makes his folly visible. [Julara](../people/julara.md) later realizes this is [Belle](../people/belle.md), the same being whose fire made the assault on [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s Hell fortress possible.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) attempts to dispel the crown with powerful magic. One mushroom withers, but another regrows, and [Vylra](../people/vylra.md)'s hand briefly glows from the contact.

## Julara's Ritual

During watch, [Julara](../people/julara.md) researches a partial workaround. She instructs [Paul](../people/paul.md) to draw a blood circle and drink a potion passed through from her side of the bargain.

The ritual suppresses the crown's Constance glow. It no longer shines all the time, but it will flare when [Paul](../people/paul.md) rages. The crown remains physically present and unresolved.

## Fey Court Lore

On the road, [Paul](../people/paul.md) asks [Julara](../people/julara.md) about [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s divine and [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) relationships.

[Julara](../people/julara.md) explains that [Belle](../people/belle.md) is unusual even among high [Fey](../concepts/fey.md). She is tied to three courts at once:

- The Court of Spring.
- The Court of Mischief.
- The Court of Dreams.

[Belle](../people/belle.md) is also a goddess and her own avatar, allowing her to remain active in the Mortal lands despite the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md). [Julara](../people/julara.md) warns that trying to solve [Paul](../people/paul.md)'s problem by provoking one of [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s enemies would be extremely dangerous.

She also suggests that [Mave](../people/mave.md) may be one of the few beings who could speak meaningfully on [Paul](../people/paul.md)'s behalf, though [Mave](../people/mave.md) has left the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).

## Hydra Intelligence

[Julara](../people/julara.md) also shares more research about ancient ice hydras:

- They are beastlike rather than dragonlike in intelligence.
- Ancient ice hydras do not usually exceed five heads.
- They regenerate fiercely.
- Fire and possibly acid are likely needed to stop severed heads from returning.
- Cold is expected to be useless against the ice variant.

She speculates that the ice hydra survived the [Demonic Dwarven Goo](../concepts/demonic-dwarven-goo.md) by freezing it rather than destroying it.

## Night Watch Goo

During the next camp, [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) spots a gray-goo trace moving under the snow toward the party. He wakes the group, and the party destroys the goo with fire before it reaches them.

The encounter confirms that the infection is still active in the thawing wilderness even away from the original hyo-jak trail.

## Before the Fight

The party reaches the route toward the hydra. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) guides them through the snow and mud, and the group chooses to pursue the known hydra location rather than first clear the collapsed passage.

Before entering the pass, [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) gives an inspiring speech invoking Gorthak's strength, [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s return, and the group's bond as kin.

## Ancient Ice Hydra

The ancient ice hydra ambushes the party from under the snow and gains the first strike. It attacks with multiple heads, cold breath, frostbite screams, ice armor, regeneration, and devastating grapples.

Several party members are petrified in ice or badly injured. [Juan](../people/juan.md) uses inspiration and radiant control magic, [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) and [Soda](../people/soda.md) hold the front, [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) brings fire and healing, and [Paul](../people/paul.md) grows to enormous size.

[Paul](../people/paul.md) sears heads with fire, breaks through the ice armor, and eventually uses his giant strength to throw the ice hydra's body.

## Ember Hydra Mate

When the ice hydra cries out, its mate answers: an ember hydra enters the fight.

The battle becomes a chaotic two-hydra brawl. The ember hydra uses fire breath, lava-like armor, fiery meteor strikes, and ignition effects, while the ice hydra continues fighting even after its heads are severed.

The party uses the hydras against each other. [Paul](../people/paul.md) throws the headless ice hydra onto the ember hydra, knocking both down and pinning them together. [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) attempts to restrain the ember hydra with the [Iron Bands of Bilarro](../items/iron-bands-of-bilarro.md), but the monster breaks free through legendary resistance.

[Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) discovers new power in his restored bow: arrows split into multiple force shots. He later accidentally releases a necrotic burst that hits nearly everyone after shouting for the hydra to die.

## Victory

The party finishes the hydras through a brutal combination of fire, ice, holy water, force arrows, lightning, poison strikes, and giant-strength melee.

The ice hydra's heads are burned away. The ember hydra's heads are frozen, blasted, or destroyed with holy water. Both bodies are reduced to ruined carcasses.

A luck check confirms there is no nest yet. The party has killed the mated pair before they could produce young.
