# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 2: Echoes in the Frost

Part 2 continues the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) arc in the frozen approach to [Ice Watch](../places/ice-watch.md), where the party shifts from travel trouble into local mystery.

## Arrival and the Frozen Blade

The party travels by ice-ship toward [Ice Watch](../places/ice-watch.md), with the cold and the weather doing as much damage as the enemies.

[Soda](../people/soda.md) is saved from necrotic corruption by timely restoration magic, but the party also discovers a buried blade and a dwarf-marked gauntlet in the ice.

The artifacts appear old, dangerous, and connected to a much larger hidden history.

## Frostfire Harbor

The group reaches [Frostfire Harbor](../places/frostfire-harbor.md) and meets guarded authority bearing the marks of local power.

The harbor response is tense but not hostile. The artifacts change the mood immediately, and the party is directed toward the temple district for purification and review.

## Frostfire Tavern

The party rests at [Frostfire Tavern](../places/frostfire-tavern.md), where the innkeeper [Thrum Stonegrinder](../people/thrum-stonegrinder.md) takes the work seriously and the atmosphere is warm despite the blizzard.

[Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) leaves a mysterious note and then vanishes from the rooming situation, leaving only a hidden bow and a growing suspicion that something has gone wrong.

## Temple Business

The party brings the artifacts to the [Temple of Gorthak's Triumph](../places/temple-of-gorthaks-triumph.md).

The priests do not fully solve the problem, but they confirm that the group's strange delivery, payments, and missing person are all tied into a larger chain of local authority.

The inquiry leads onward to a private residence tied to the temple.

## Regrouping

By the end of the session, the party has:

- survived the ice-ship ambush
- preserved the strange blade and gauntlet
- identified the missing [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) thread as a live mystery
- been pushed into the orbit of [Ice Watch](../places/ice-watch.md)'s religious and civic power

## Elsewhere I - The Architect of Worlds

The campaign's first major Elseware shows the other side of the Concurrence crisis: [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md), [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md), [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), [Junvara](../people/junvara.md), [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md), [Charlie](../people/charlie.md), and Jarvis/Jarvice working through the final world-alignment crisis.

This scene culminates with Greg being named the Architect of Worlds.
