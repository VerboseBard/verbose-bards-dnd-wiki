# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 3: The Trail of Hooves

Part 3 follows the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) from the frozen tavern district into research, church politics, and a deeper undercurrent of [Opaal](../places/opaal.md) lore.

## Morning at the Frostfire Tavern

The party wakes at [Frostfire Tavern](../places/frostfire-tavern.md) and resets after the previous session's danger.

The group is still dealing with the missing [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) thread and the strange aftermath of the ice-ship attack.

## Plans Over Breakfast

Over breakfast, the party chooses the hoof trail as the next lead and starts treating the problem like a real investigation rather than a simple rescue.

The group shifts from local crisis response into deliberate clue chasing.

## Frozenheart University

The party visits [Frozenheart University](../places/frozenheart-university.md) for research and access to better information.

The university becomes a temporary hub for lore, restricted texts, and the people who know where to send a problem when it is bigger than a tavern mystery.

## The Empty Seat

A missing figure leaves an emotional blank space in the session.

The party is increasingly aware that people are being pulled out of the story rather than merely delayed by it.

## The Trail and the Gate

The hoof trail leads toward a gate-like threshold and a sealed path into something older.

The investigation becomes physical, with the party following evidence into a place that should not have been easy to reach.

## The Church Responds

The local church takes the danger seriously and begins treating the Abyssal threat as an active problem.

The party's discoveries are no longer isolated rumors. They have become a matter for authority.

## Research in the Depths

Deeper study uncovers more about the trail, the gate, and the way old spaces can hide modern danger.

The session keeps moving from clues to architecture, implying the mystery has a real structural spine.

## The Forbidden Crypt

The forbidden crypt is the danger pivot of the session.

Once the party enters, the trail stops being theoretical and becomes a confrontation with the sealed past.

## Elsewhere I - The Queen Beneath the Waves

The cutaway moves to [Opaal](../places/opaal.md) and the Coral Palace, tying the session to [Mulligan](../people/mulligan.md)'s ancient Commodore history.

[Mira of Opaal](../people/mira-of-opaal.md) speaks with [Aurora, Queen Beneath the Waves](../people/aurora-queen-beneath-waves.md), and the scene flashes through the Shattering, the Rebirth, and the long memory of deep ocean power. The conversation reframes the Commodore as both beloved father and cursed betrayer.

The scene also points toward the [Opaal Old One](../concepts/opaal-old-one.md) and the [Commodore Prophecy](../concepts/commodore-prophecy.md): the Commodore's return means the old seal is failing and the deep threat is close again.
