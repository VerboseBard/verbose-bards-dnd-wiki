# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 4: The Citadel and the Concurrence

Part 4 carries the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) from [Ice Watch](../places/ice-watch.md) politics into the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), where the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), family obligations, banking, and school authority all collide.

## The Bishop and the Missing Wagon

[Bishop Octavian Ironheart](../people/bishop-octavian-ironheart.md) receives the party's report and confirms that the missing wagon was found empty.

He cannot recover the stolen travelers, but he can help the party move faster by arranging access to the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) and its portal network.

## Arthur Saran's Request

[Arthur Saran](../people/arthur-saran.md) approaches the group with a private problem: his daughter [Nicole](../people/nicole.md) has gone off on a music-and-pub crawl and needs to be brought home.

Arthur offers a letter of introduction and uses the request as a bargaining chip for faster access to [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) experts.

## The Theater Crawl

The party tracks [Nicole](../people/nicole.md) and [Kendrick](../people/kendrick.md) through the city and eventually finds them performing at [Glacier's Edge Theater](../places/glaciers-edge-theater.md).

The performance is magical, crowded, and larger than the party expected, but the group resolves the situation without a fight.

## Return to Arthur

[Nicole](../people/nicole.md) and [Kendrick](../people/kendrick.md) are left on workable terms, and Arthur accepts the party's help.

The letter of introduction becomes the real reward: access to [Lady Penny Hathaway](../people/lady-penny-hathaway.md) and the school's portal expertise.

## The Radiant Citadel

The party teleports to the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), where the [Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) is only hours away and the whole city feels like a festival under pressure.

They open an account at the [First National Bank of the GIF](../places/first-national-bank-of-the-gif.md), store most of their money, and keep only a useful amount on hand.

## The School

At [Lady Penny Hathaway's School](../places/lady-penny-hathaways-school.md), [Lady Penny Hathaway](../people/lady-penny-hathaway.md) explains that the expert they need is exhausted but available after rest.

She warns the party that portal lag has cost them time and gives them rooms, food, and a warning about the dangerous residents in the other wing.

## Elsewhere II

The cutaway to Constance and Zuks shows the long consequences of [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md)'s changes to reality.

The scene reinforces that the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md) is also a power gate, that dragonkind has been changed by the world rewrite, and that the party's timing matters far more than they first realized.
