# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 6: The Road Through Hell

Part 6 carries the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) from the aftermath of the egg rescue into the assault on [Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress](../places/wendells-sixth-layer-fortress.md).

## The Missing Mercenaries

As the egg evacuation ends, [Tony](../people/tony.md) reports that one mercenary team has not returned. A familiar sent into the fortress finds a dwarf using bracers to close a portal or back door.

The party concludes that the missing team may have found its own route deeper into [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s operation. The egg portal closes shortly afterward.

## Annabelle's Warning

[Annabelle](../people/annabelle.md) confirms that Constant will still open the party's promised portal, but warns that Hell itself will burn those without protection.

The party purchases rings of fire resistance and prepares for the dive. The city around them has shifted from pre-[Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) panic into exhausted relief.

## Belle at the Black Keep

An Elsewhere shows [Belle](../people/belle.md) entering the Black Keep in Hell. She tears fire from demonic blood, melts the gates, and announces herself to [Wendell](../people/wendell.md).

[Wendell](../people/wendell.md) believes the new [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md) leaves [Belle](../people/belle.md) reduced to an avatar. [Belle](../people/belle.md) proves that he has misunderstood her again. With a mirror device built from [Greg](../people/greg.md)'s work, she reflects the god-killing dagger back into [Wendell](../people/wendell.md) and leaves him maimed inside a ritual circle of [Mave](../people/mave.md)'s divine blood rather than killing him outright.

## Professor Hazard's Workshop

At the school, the party meets [Professor Hazard](../people/professor-hazard.md), a rare non-corrupted dwarf artificer and scholar.

Hazard provides experimental anti-demon tools, including the [Holy-Water Sprayer and Mortar](../items/holy-water-sprayer-and-mortar.md) and unstable [Reflection Mirrors](../items/reflection-mirrors.md).

He also helps [Paul](../people/paul.md) compare giant maps and identifies a lost giant city near [Sorrel](../people/sorrel.md)'s grove, buried within a glacier on the ice world.

## The Temple Blessing

At a small [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) temple of the god of war and struggle, [p'Op](../people/p-op.md), [Soda](../people/soda.md), and [Luca](../people/luca.md) are judged worthy before the statue.

[p'Op](../people/p-op.md)'s lance and [Soda](../people/soda.md)'s claws are blessed with radiant force. [Luca](../people/luca.md) receives a deeper zealot blessing whose full form is not yet known.

## Jimbo and Charlie

Another Elsewhere shows Black Viper agents and demons ambushing [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) and [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) away from the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md).

Both are killed, and their souls are stolen with soul-cage methods tied to demonic dwarven artifacts. This expands the coming Hell assault from a rescue of [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) into vengeance and soul recovery.

## The Succubus Curse

[Paul](../people/paul.md) admits to [Zen](../people/zen.md) and [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) that the cursed library knowledge allows him to summon a succubus for information. They recognize the danger and decline to risk the ritual.

That night, [Paul](../people/paul.md) wakes with the ritual components laid out and nearly casts it under compulsion. He resists, but the effort damages him until [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) uses Greater Restoration to relieve the immediate curse pressure.

## The Hell Assault

By morning, the school is full of allies preparing for war: dragons, werewolves, Arnold's orcs, Gaston's duelists, [Nicole](../people/nicole.md)'s side, and the remaining [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) allies.

[Mave](../people/mave.md), now in a weakened mortal body, explains that [Belle](../people/belle.md) and [Greg](../people/greg.md) have opened the way and that roughly thirty thousand demons remain. Constant opens a massive portal, and several dragons enter first.

## The Elevator Detour

The party follows [Juan](../people/juan.md)'s guidance through the Black Keep, avoiding the worst of the initial fighting. An ancient elevator-like transit system malfunctions and drops them away from the sixth layer.

They arrive in a stranger Hell region with petrified devils, celestial imps beyond a gate, and a guardian who once petrified himself to keep the gate shut.

## The Guardian and the Hall of Sorrows

The party restores the guardian and learns that the gate protects a dangerous radiant artifact that transforms some fiends rather than destroying them.

The guardian warns that the [Hall of Sorrows](../places/hall-of-sorrows.md) is the other route: to ascend, one must first descend. In thanks for the party's unusual kindness, he gifts several [Burning Watcher Skulls](../items/burning-watcher-skulls.md).

## Seventh-Layer Wrong Turn

The party explores the [Hall of Sorrows](../places/hall-of-sorrows.md), avoids a bound draconic threat, manipulates a dead dungeon core, and brute-forces a gate into the seventh layer.

[Juan](../people/juan.md) sacrifices a celestial imp into the dungeon core to activate a teleportation circle. The party lands back in [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s throne room on the sixth layer, exactly where [Juan](../people/juan.md) had been held.

Combat is about to begin.
