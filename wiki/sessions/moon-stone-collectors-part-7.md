# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 7: Morgrath Falls

Part 7 begins with the [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) arriving in a multi-level hall inside [Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress](../places/wendells-sixth-layer-fortress.md), close to the route toward [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s cells.

## Sorrel's Exit

Before the battle, [Sorrel](../people/sorrel.md) is pulled away by [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s fey call through the mushroom token. Her burning skull boon transfers to [Vylra](../people/vylra.md), giving him two orbiting skulls.

## The Battlefield

The party appears inside a teleport circle in a multi-tier hall. Enemies occupy balconies and stairs, with shadow demons, imps, and [Morgrath the Enforcer](../people/morgrath-the-enforcer.md) positioned near the central stair.

Morgrath has already been weakened by previous clashes, including [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s assault through the fortress.

## Opening Control

[Juan](../people/juan.md) opens with Sickening Radiance, turning the hall into a punishing radiant zone that exhausts and burns enemies.

[Zen](../people/zen.md) follows with Vitriolic Sphere, clearing many imps. Shadow demons turn invisible and try to exploit the chaos, but the party responds with Shield, positioning, and radiant pressure.

## Holding the Line

[p'Op](../people/p-op.md) charges from [Soda](../people/soda.md) and drives his blessed lance into Morgrath. [Paul](../people/paul.md) enlarges, rages, and moves to the front, adding elemental force and absorbing hits.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) activates Guardian of Nature and Symbiotic Entity while coordinating from the balconies. His burning skulls begin blasting enemies, and one later detonates inside a demon for massive damage.

[Luka](../people/luka.md) uses Zealous Presence to strengthen the team and stays at the edge of Sickening Radiance while helping [Zen](../people/zen.md)'s flank.

## Morgrath Paralyzed

Morgrath uses a legendary terror roar, but [Zen](../people/zen.md) lands Hold Monster after Morgrath has already spent his legendary resistance.

The paralysis opens the fight. [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) unloads multiple auto-critical lance hits with Action Surge, dealing roughly 276 damage in a single burst. The team piles on, and Morgrath falls.

## Side Lanes

Shadow demons continue trying to strike from the flanks. One is swatted through a stained-glass window, while another is torn apart by [Soda](../people/soda.md).

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md)'s skulls continue contributing damage. One self-detonates into a demon's head, while the remaining skull stays with [Vylra](../people/vylra.md).

## Lyris Arrives

A group luck check goes badly. The failure brings [Lyris the Deceiver](../people/lyris-the-deceiver.md) into the scene.

Lyris, an [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md) illusionist who often appears as a tiefling child, ascends the stairs as the session ends.
