# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 8: The Sixth Layer Falls

Part 8 completes the Hell rescue. The [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) defeat [Lyris the Deceiver](../people/lyris-the-deceiver.md), free [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md), rescue [Elisia](../people/elisia.md), and escape as [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s fortress and the sixth layer of Hell collapse.

## Lyris Enters the Fight

The session resumes inside [Wendell's Sixth-Layer Fortress](../places/wendells-sixth-layer-fortress.md), immediately after [Morgrath the Enforcer](../people/morgrath-the-enforcer.md)'s death.

[Sorrel](../people/sorrel.md) is pulled away by [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s fey summons, and her burning skull transfers to [Vylra](../people/vylra.md). Lyris arrives with illusions, imps, psychic pressure, and charm magic.

## The Deceiver Falls

Lyris multiplies herself, making it difficult to tell which body is real. She charms [Paul](../people/paul.md) and [Luka](../people/luka.md), lashes [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) with a fear vision, and tries to turn the party's frontline against itself.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md)'s burning skull detonates inside one of Lyris's bodies, forcing her concentration to break. [Zen](../people/zen.md) dispels [Luka](../people/luka.md)'s charm, [p'Op](../people/p-op.md) and the others pressure the real body, and [Luka](../people/luka.md) finishes Lyris with radiant claws.

The fight ends with the party having killed two leaders of the [Abyssal Hand](../factions/abyssal-hand.md): Morgrath and Lyris.

## Divine Soup

During the short rest, [Juan](../people/juan.md) considers whether to save or consume the remaining power in [Maeve](../people/mave.md)'s [Divine Soul Coin](../items/divine-soul-coin.md).

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) turns the moment into food. The coin is dropped into the soup, creating a divine meal. Everyone who eats gains permanent vitality, and [Juan](../people/juan.md) gains a deeper change in perception tied to [Maeve](../people/mave.md)'s domains of knowledge, wisdom, art, and beauty.

## The Prison Puzzle

The party descends to the prison level where [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) is held. Morgrath's medallion opens the gate and powers down the security statues.

The room contains several sealed cells, each viewed through magical projection stations. The floor mechanism uses colored rune pieces to configure which prisoners can be released.

[Soda](../people/soda.md) confirms [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md)'s scent in the chamber. Primeval awareness and inspection reveal that one prisoner is celestial, while [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) is curled in a fetal position and unresponsive.

## Elisia

The celestial prisoner introduces herself as [Elisia](../people/elisia.md), a celestial demon hated by demons and devils alike.

The party questions whether releasing her is wise, especially after learning that [Julara](../people/julara.md), the presence touching [Paul](../people/paul.md)'s mind, is one of [Elisia](../people/elisia.md)'s sisters. [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) explains that [Julara](../people/julara.md) is a succubus of knowledge rather than carnal desire.

The party chooses to release both [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) and [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md).

## Phlukk Freed

[Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) appears in the circle in a very bad state. He is unconscious or comatose, and healing only relaxes some of the tension in his body.

[Elisia](../people/elisia.md) warns that he is in a precarious condition and that the party should prepare for the worst.

## Wendell's End

[Elisia](../people/elisia.md) guides the party toward the faster route out. Along the way, they find what [Belle](../people/belle.md) made of [Wendell](../people/wendell.md): a stone-and-fire anchor with a beating heart at its center.

The party alerts the assault force. Constance and the dragons move in, and the final destruction of [Wendell](../people/wendell.md) begins behind the party as they withdraw.

## Collapse of the Sixth Layer

The allied forces retreat through portals back to the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) triage area. The fortress collapses behind them in fire, steam, and imploding stone.

[Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s soul-anchor is destroyed along with the keep. The damage ripples outward through Hell, and the sixth layer is lost.
