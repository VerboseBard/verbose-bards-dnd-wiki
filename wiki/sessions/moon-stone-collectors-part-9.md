# Moon Stone Collectors, Part 9: The Blackthorn Trap

Part 9 follows the aftermath of the sixth layer's destruction. The [Moon Stone Collectors](../factions/moon-stone-collectors.md) return to the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), defend [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) from religious zealotry, place [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) in stasis, learn more about [Julara](../people/julara.md), and end the session in a high-stakes trap at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md).

## Three Days Later

The party exits Hell through a smaller portal than expected. Instead of arriving on school grounds, they emerge into [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) streets full of wounded soldiers, priests, prisoners, and dead.

The party thought only hours had passed during their Hell detour, but the mortal-side assault has lasted about three days. The [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) forces called for aid after discovering that [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s layer had been weakened, and the churches sent soldiers and faithful through the portals to finish the war.

Priests of unfamiliar and half-forgotten gods move among the wounded, hinting that the power structure has shifted after [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md) and the rise of the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md).

## Elisia on Trial Before the Trial

The party treats [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) as a rescued prisoner rather than a captive. That choice immediately creates conflict.

A healer confirms that [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) is physically healed, but mentally absent or buried too deeply for ordinary healing. When the healer notices [Elisia](../people/elisia.md), she reports the celestial demon to religious authorities.

[Cardinal Preceptor Thornvale](../people/cardinal-preceptor-thornvale.md), a servant of the Dawn Father, denounces [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) as an abomination and calls for her destruction. [Juan](../people/juan.md) answers with a parable about two dogs with the same blood but different lives, arguing that actions and nurture matter.

[Lady Seraphina Blackthorn](../people/lady-seraphina-blackthorn.md) intervenes on [Elisia](../people/elisia.md)'s behalf, reminding Thornvale that the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) is not his church and that free will and compassion still matter. Thornvale's angelic guardian, [Morus the Unchained](../people/morus-the-unchained.md), steps forward when the confrontation threatens to turn violent.

The situation resolves when a [Collar of Demon Subjugation](../items/collar-of-demon-subjugation.md) is provided. Thornvale accepts that [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) may remain in the party's custody until the coming Conclave, provided she wears the collar and the party accepts responsibility for her.

## Back at the School

The party returns to the school in a quieter, sadder atmosphere. The assault has cost lives among the staff, guards, and support personnel. Havoc warns that mind healers are still away because of the Conclave and the emergency response.

[Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) is placed in magical medical stasis. [Zen](../people/zen.md)'s deeper mental inspection finds memories but no active presence: [Phlukk](../people/phlukk.md) is alive, but no one seems to be reachable at the surface of his mind.

[Elisia](../people/elisia.md) stays near the party. She admits she has no plan beyond survival, because she was created in Hell and imprisoned for roughly twelve hundred years. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) warns her not to harm anyone and promises that the party will try to find a humane path for her if the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) allows it.

[Elisia](../people/elisia.md) rejects the idea of hiding under a false identity, saying that peace without truth cannot become justice. Her first experiences with food, air, softness, and safety overwhelm her. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) later finds her crying because, for the first time she can remember, she is not in pain.

## Julara's Bargain

[Paul](../people/paul.md) tries to complete another summoning working, but the stolen salt leaves the circle incomplete. [Julara](../people/julara.md) contacts him instead.

[Julara](../people/julara.md) is shocked to learn that [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) lives. She agrees to remove [Paul](../people/paul.md)'s compulsion to contact her in exchange for his memories and account of [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) and the prison rescue.

The bargain is sealed through a small circle made with demonic pepper powder. A hand passes a contract through, and [Paul](../people/paul.md) signs. The compulsion ends, but [Julara](../people/julara.md) gains easier access to converse with him and watch through his perspective when permitted. She says his firsthand view of the end of an age is valuable enough payment for knowledge.

[Julara](../people/julara.md) also helps [Paul](../people/paul.md) identify the lost [Dwarven City of Bar](../places/dwarven-city-of-bar.md), adding key locations to his map and explaining that the dwarves claimed the giant city after the giants vanished at the end of the [Second Age](../concepts/second-age.md).

## The Six Sisters

[Elisia](../people/elisia.md) explains more about herself and her sisters.

[Lucius](../people/lucius.md) once used a stolen divine artifact against a powerful demon. Instead of simply destroying the being, the artifact split her nature into six sisters: demonic and celestial fragments of one original whole.

[Elisia](../people/elisia.md) names herself as one of those sisters, and [Julara](../people/julara.md) is already known to the party through Paul. The other sisters are later identified as [Julia](../people/julia-julisia.md), [Alura](../people/alura.md), [Julessa](../people/julessa.md), and [Sophia](../people/wisdom.md). Some may want reunion; others may fear what reunion would erase.

## Maeve's Warning

[Juan](../people/juan.md) visits the library and finds [Maeve](../people/mave.md), now only [Maeve](../people/mave.md) rather than goddess, headmistress, or divine mistress of knowledge.

She recognizes that the party carries the last of her divine power after the divine soup. She warns [Juan](../people/juan.md) that luck without knowledge is dangerous and that bargains always take something. She also confirms that many powerful observers already know he is [Fey](../concepts/fey.md) because divine and celestial senses reveal what disguises do not.

[Maeve](../people/mave.md) tells him the school may close now that its old protectors and leaders are gone or diminished. She leaves him to the library and says he may take something from the collection.

## Memorial and Politics

The party attends the memorial for the fallen from the Hell assault. [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md), [Charlie](../people/charlie.md), and other dead are honored. The ceremony also gives a public version of [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md)'s departure as the Architect of Worlds: he gave everything for the work, rather than openly naming his divine transformation.

The remaining [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) heroes begin to disperse. Constance and Zuk leave the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) to deal with their own people and responsibilities, leaving the old heroic network fractured.

At the memorial, Lady Blackthorn privately asks [Zen](../people/zen.md) for help. For years, rumors have painted her as a corrupt figure involved in illicit backroom dealings and sinful underground circles. She believes someone is deliberately discrediting her, and the new religious power struggle makes that especially dangerous.

## The Invitation

[Tony](../people/tony.md) delivers an invitation to [Juan](../people/juan.md) in Lady Blackthorn's name. It offers entry to a secret high-stakes game at [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md). The requested buy-in is a look at the dagger taken from [Lyris the Deceiver](../people/lyris-the-deceiver.md), and the invitation hints that [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) would also be welcome.

[Elisia](../people/elisia.md) refuses to attend without a proper invitation and says the situation feels improper. [Zen](../people/zen.md) refuses to hand over the collar's controller without knowing why [Juan](../people/juan.md) wants it. [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) decides to accompany [Juan](../people/juan.md), gives him gambling money, and brings [Denny](../people/denny.md) along in polymorphed ferret form.

## High Table

The manor's protected room is built to block psionics, telepathy, and cheating. At the table, the stakes quickly move beyond platinum into truths, secrets, insight, cosmic jokes, memories, and dangerous objects.

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) absorbs a truth and insight, seeing through the room's disguises. The party is not among friendly nobles. [Vaelith the Whisperer](../people/vaelith-the-whisperer.md), Lyris or a Deceiver-linked presence, and other hostile disguised figures are present. Threads of deception connect people throughout the room.

One truth reveals what [Juan](../people/juan.md) lost for his luck: destiny and legacy. In another timeline he left Feyland as a Fey-touched hero, joined the [Hell's Bane](../factions/hells-bane-heroes.md) orbit, and killed [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) with the dagger on the crystal. That path was stolen or diverted by a deal involving his brother, leaving [Juan](../people/juan.md) with probability-bending luck at a severe cost.

Another revelation frames [Wendell](../people/wendell.md) himself as part of a cosmic joke: the powers that defeated [Lucius](../people/lucius.md) used a diminished undead reject of the gods, empowered him in Hell, and let him believe he had true demon lords under his command. Many of the forces the party fought in Hell were pale or forced imitations, not demon lords risking true destruction.

## Cliffhanger

[Vylra](../people/vylra.md) sends a warning through the sending stone: the Blackthorn at the manor is not the real Blackthorn, but the Deceiver's trap. The message almost fails, but [Denny](../people/denny.md)'s linked watcher-boon helps punch it through.

At the school, the real Lady Blackthorn confirms she is not hosting the event. [Zen](../people/zen.md) gathers the others, [Elisia](../people/elisia.md) stays behind, and the party rushes toward [Ebonwing Manor](../places/ebonwing-manor.md).

The session ends with [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) and [Juan](../people/juan.md) still at the table, [Vylra](../people/vylra.md) placing a truth to keep the game moving: he has already contacted outside reinforcements.
