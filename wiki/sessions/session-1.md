# Session 1: The Bloody Fifth

## Overview

Session 1 begins the campaign in the mud, rain, and confusion of the [Kreen War](../concepts/kreen-war.md). The player characters arrive as rookies, conscripts, volunteers, and shattered veterans, then are assigned to the [Bloody Fifth](../factions/bloody-fifth.md), one of the highest-casualty formations in the war.

The session establishes the campaign's central lens: history remembers heroes, but this story follows the soldiers who were never meant to survive.

## Key Events

- The campaign opens with a narration of the [Seventh Age](../concepts/seventh-age.md), the aftermath of [the Concurrence](../concepts/the-concurrence.md), and the outbreak of the [Kreen War](../concepts/kreen-war.md).
- Rookies arrive through portals into a muddy assembly area after shortened training.
- Veterans from the Eighth Army Group arrive after being routed on [Norvindr](../places/norvindr.md).
- The survivors are assigned to the Eighth Division of the [Bloody Fifth](../factions/bloody-fifth.md).
- The party learns that the Fifth is infamous for extreme casualties and wave tactics.
- Characters receive or retain war gear, including ether rifles, shields, shotguns, conductors, and other improvised equipment.
- The unit deploys to [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md), where fog and magical containment infrastructure dominate the battlefield.
- The party helps hold and then push through a brutal trench battle.
- Their action draws enough [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) away from another front that heroes elsewhere close the reinforcement gate.
- The closure severs the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) hive connection, causing widespread collapse or madness.
- Survivors are selected for a new specialized anti-[Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) unit that becomes [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md).

## Consequences

The party survives a battle that should have killed them. Their actions help enable the closure of the [Kreen](../factions/kreen.md) reinforcement gate, setting up the collapse of the hive link and the creation of [Ash's Hounds](../factions/ashs-hounds.md) in [Session 2](session-2.md).

## Important Conversations

- The General's speech frames the [Bloody Fifth](../factions/bloody-fifth.md) as the army's necessary sacrifice, setting the campaign's core theme that people are cheaper than equipment and history remembers the wrong names.
- Captain Aelira's follow-up makes that doctrine feel human; she speaks like someone who will stand in the same mud and die with the unit if she has to.
- The squad-room talk about scrounging, armor, tickers, and casualties establishes the war as a system of improvisation and attrition rather than heroics.
- The veterans' reactions to the battlefield and the marching orders show that even experienced soldiers know the Fifth is a place where survival is luck, not entitlement.
