# Session 5: The Kex, Nanites, and the Bridge

## Overview

Session 5 continues the party's first major contractor mission into [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) and the hidden crashed goblin ship. After the Session 4 boarding and first nanite enhancements, the party meets the ship's shipboard AI more clearly, fights through the hall and engineering defenses, and claims the ship by defeating its combat avatar.

## Key Events

- The party continues the contractor route / hidden-ship detour established in Session 4.
- They remain trapped by the ship's hostile security logic.
- The medbay and nanite procedures continue to shape the ship sequence after the first enhancements.
- The ship's corridors are laced with blue electrical traps, red emergency lights, pressure plates, and hidden hatches.
- A wand of secrets reveals that some floor plates are real traps while others are system controls.
- The party clears drone and robot defenders while learning to move through the ship's hazards.
- The ship's AI identifies itself as [Kubix](../people/kubix.md), the shipboard mind of the [Kex](../places/kex.md), and explains that a hostile combat/security AI is using the ship's systems against the crew.
- [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) confirms the ship is a goblin interstellar trading ship, not a local wreck, and offers healing injectors plus repairs if the combat AI can be defeated.
- The party receives printed schematics showing how to adapt ship weapons to crystal power and augmentation slots.
- During a short rest, the party attunes to the recovered weapons and studies the ship's armory and stasis storage.
- [Jose](../people/jose.md) is registered by the ship as the new captain.
- The party enters engineering through vents, wins a surprise round, and destroys the ship's combat avatar and its support drones.
- The ship's combat systems shut down, the AI mutiny is broken, and [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) becomes a temporary ally rather than a prison guard.
- In the storage bay, the party discovers research cylinders containing alien spider stock, including one viable young specimen that forms a strange psychic connection.

## Important Conversations

- [Kubix](../people/kubix.md)'s first real conversation with the party defines the crashed ship as a place under attack from its own security logic and establishes that the crew mind is frightened, not just hostile.
- The ship's repeated insistence that the party are `pieces` or `variables` pushes the [Weaver](../concepts/the-weaver.md) thread forward and gives the whole ship sequence a strong fate-versus-choice tone.
- [Jose](../people/jose.md) being recognized as captain becomes a character beat as well as a systems beat, because the ship itself is accepting a new leader while the party fights for control.
- The nanite procedure scenes are not just healing or upgrade scenes; they are a conversation between body, technology, and consent, because the ship keeps trying to decide what the party should become.
- The recovered spider specimen and the alien research cylinders add a quieter but important worldbuilding conversation: the ship was studying life, adaptation, and the kind of intelligence that survives isolation.
