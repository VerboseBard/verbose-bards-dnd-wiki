# Session 6: The Storm, the Music Box, and Sunhollow Valley

## Overview

Session 6 pushes the [After the War Campaign](../campaign/after-the-war-campaign.md) out of the hidden [Crashed Ship](../places/crashed-ship.md) and back into the real contractor job. The party finishes its first round of ship-forged upgrades, leaves [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) and the repair drones to restore the [Kex](../places/kex.md), survives a magical lightning storm and a mutated displacer beast, stumbles into ancient rune-ruins full of trapped spirits, and accidentally proves that the [Music Box Anomaly](../items/music-box-anomaly.md) is far more dangerous than anyone hoped. By the end of the session, the party completes a wagon-passable route and finally reaches the sunlight basin of [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md).

## Key Events

- [Jose](../people/jose.md) remains the [Kex](../places/kex.md)'s acting captain while [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) begins an estimated 45-day repair cycle for the hidden ship.
- The party uses the ship's fabrication systems to receive the completed [Arcstrike War Gauntlets](../items/ship-forged-gauntlets.md), install several ship augmentation modules, and prepare to leave the [Kex](../places/kex.md) behind as a secret long-term asset.
- [Bevar Lurton](../people/bevar-lurton.md) continues bonding with [Gwen](../people/gwen.md), the surviving alien spider specimen recovered from the ship's research storage.
- [Sgt. Jefferson Stone](../people/sergeant-jefferson-stone.md) studies the ship's logical internal layout and watches the nanite-driven hull-repair process closely enough to understand how much damage the [Kex](../places/kex.md) actually took.
- After disguising part of their route back from the ship, the party resumes the [Lost Homeland Mission](../concepts/lost-homeland-mission.md) and starts making real trail choices for a wagon-capable expedition.
- A rare magical lightning storm catches the group in exposed high terrain and drives them into a ravine fight with a mutated displacer beast.
- The party kills the beast, survives its mind-warping poison, and [Bevar Lurton](../people/bevar-lurton.md) harvests several vials of its neurotoxic secretion for later experimentation.
- In a set of old ruins lit by ambient magic, the party encounters spinning rune mechanisms, spectral inhabitants visible through truesight, and evidence that the site still holds active older-world power.
- A chimera attacks in the ruins, forcing the party into a second desperate fight before they are ready.
- [Jose](../people/jose.md) uses the [Music Box Anomaly](../items/music-box-anomaly.md) as a portal-prison, banishing the chimera into whatever lies beyond the box instead of defeating it conventionally.
- The box resists closing, begins drawing local spirits into itself, and exposes [Sgt. Jefferson Stone](../people/sergeant-jefferson-stone.md) to a terrifying malevolent presence on the far side.
- Jefferson's blood activates an altar in the ruins, revealing a surviving fragment of [Hestia](../people/hestia.md), a Fourth-Age goddess of hearth and refuge long thought to have been killed in that age.
- [Hestia](../people/hestia.md)'s fragment leaves Jefferson with a blessing tied to home, refuge, and remembered care, marking the first clear direct contact in the campaign with an older divine power thought lost to history.
- After more routefinding, dead ends, bridge checks, and mountain-path decisions, the party finally reaches [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md).
- The valley is confirmed to be real: a wide, sunlit, fog-free homeland with villages, visible smoke, and beastfolk patrols who first react with hostility and then with curiosity when they see the party.

## Important Conversations

- [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) explains the ship's repair timetable, fabrication capabilities, and the tradeoff between rebuilding the [Kex](../places/kex.md) and manufacturing extra advanced gear for the crew.
- The group's conversation around the magical lightning storm reinforces how unnatural weather remains in [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md) and how quickly rare environmental events can become lethal.
- The party's immediate reaction after the chimera banishment turns the [Music Box Anomaly](../items/music-box-anomaly.md) from a strange curiosity into a locked-down danger that should not be opened casually again.
- [Sgt. Jefferson Stone](../people/sergeant-jefferson-stone.md)'s encounter with [Hestia](../people/hestia.md) reframes the ruins as a place of imprisonment, sanctuary, and very old divine memory rather than just another puzzle site.
- The freed Hestia fragment suggests a wider hidden network of sealed divine remnants tied to older temple wars rather than a one-off miracle.
- Jose calling Kubix or the ship interface `Alexa` appears here as a nickname / possible rename joke, but not yet a stable replacement for the canon names [Kubix](../people/kubix.md) and [Kex](../places/kex.md).
- The final approach to [Sunhollow Valley](../places/sunhollow-valley.md) confirms that the old family stories behind the [Lost Homeland Mission](../concepts/lost-homeland-mission.md) were not just myths.
