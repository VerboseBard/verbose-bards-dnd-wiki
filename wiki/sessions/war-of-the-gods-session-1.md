# War of the Gods, Session 1 (January 7, 2023)

This session is the dated starting point currently preserved for the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md) at the end of the [Sixth Age](../concepts/sixth-age.md).

## Overview

The campaign opens with newly awakened characters being found across the realms by Seekers and brought through giant crystal travel infrastructure to the school. The tone is still exploratory rather than apocalyptic: the party is learning what awakening means, what the school is for, and who now governs their strange new lives.

## Key Events

- The Seekers begin gathering awakened people from different worlds and circumstances.
- [Constance and Zuk](../people/constance-l-coggeshall.md) are found by [Seeker Linda](../people/seeker-linda.md) in the [Elven Lands](../places/elven-lands.md).
- [Greg](../people/greg-p-smith.md) is rescued from isolated tribal goblins on the Elven Lands after Constance and Zuk have already been found.
- [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) arrives a few days later after being found on [Driftvale](../places/driftvale.md).
- [Mulligan / Molokan](../people/mulligan.md) wakes on [Opaal](../places/opaal.md) at a cliff-side shore, washed up as a corpse with no memory. Children help pull him out with ropes, a wind-like air elemental hovers nearby, and [Seeker Lindsey](../people/seeker-lindsey.md) takes him to the school.
- Other arrivals include figures under the supervision of [Seeker Lauren](../people/seeker-lauren.md) and [Seeker Lucia](../people/seeker-lucia.md).
- The group passes through a massive crystal transit station before arriving at the school.
- [Maeve](../people/mave.md), still acting as dean rather than a later divine figure, oversees the opening school phase.
- A testing course and rune-stamped medallion establish the party's first formal magical and martial assessment.

## Consequences

This session establishes the school-and-Seekers opening that the later war material assumes but rarely pauses to explain. It is the real beginning of the New Beginning campaign's surviving dated record.

Mulligan's arrival also plants the later [Commodore Prophecy](../concepts/commodore-prophecy.md) thread. He believes the air elemental later called [Straka](../people/straka.md) returned him to life, but later lore reveals that Straka only recognized him from his prior Commodore life.
