# War of the Gods, Session 11 (September 9, 2023)

This session widens the campaign into labor unrest, infernal bargaining, and street-level [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) politics.

## Overview

Starting as a simple errand run, the session quickly turns into a three-pronged social operation through bazaars, bars, cargo yards, and the Union House. By the end, the party has confronted a manipulative incubus power broker and watched [Belle](../people/belle.md) annihilate devils through 69.

## Key Events

- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), 69, and [Greg](../people/greg.md) visit Old McGinty's.
- Another party branch goes on a pub crawl that includes [Nicole](../people/nicole.md) and an ancient undead smith.
- The crystal station and warehouse district are thrown into chaos by a children's labor strike.
- [Tony](../people/tony.md), kobold workers, and the dock network expose a larger underclass economy.
- The Union House contains hundreds of organized children under cartel-like protection.
- Mr. Cartwright is revealed to be an incubus.
- He tries to leverage souls and bargains to break the strike on his terms.
- [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) absorbs infernal sigils on the walls.
- [Belle](../people/belle.md) manifests through 69 and vaporizes the devils.
