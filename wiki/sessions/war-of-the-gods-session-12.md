# War of the Gods, Session 12 (September 16, 2023)

This session continues the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) urban-investigation phase of the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md).

## Overview

The party follows the aftermath of the children's guild confrontation down to the docks, where Giff sailors, halted deliveries, and changing faction loyalties make it clear that trade and war are now feeding directly into each other.

## Key Events

- The group returns to the children's guild hall and tries to stabilize what was left behind.
- Molokan and 69 openly present religious ideas to the children.
- The party moves to the docks to investigate why freight has stopped.
- Pigfolk and Giff sailors dominate the atmosphere.
- Grey, the dockmaster, becomes a key contact.
- The session surfaces broader faction names and pressures around the Green Man, Go Folk, and dead domains of former gods.
- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) is teleported to McGinty's by Costner and given a spell scroll to return to the school.
- The Seekers are clarified as having sided with the school rather than Diona.
