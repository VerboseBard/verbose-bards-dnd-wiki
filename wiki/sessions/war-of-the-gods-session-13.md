# War of the Gods, Session 13 (September 23, 2023)

This session is one of the school's first full crisis sessions in the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md).

## Overview

An ominous encounter at McGinty's is immediately followed by open attacks on the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) and the school. [Mave](../people/mave.md)'s condition turns catastrophic, [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s side moves on dragon-core logic, and the party is forced into live defense instead of preparation.

## Key Events

- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md), 69, and [Junvara](../people/junvara.md) encounter a Black Viper-looking woman at McGinty's.
- The [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) shield comes under attack from ships.
- The group uses the recall scroll to get back to the school.
- [Mave](../people/mave.md) is dying, while [Belle](../people/belle.md) tries to keep events moving through dragon-core and soul-coin logic.
- [Jinx](../people/jinx.md) and Molokan are involved in the emergency response.
- [Mave](../people/mave.md) dies or ascends in the middle of the crisis.
- Black Viper attackers hit the school directly.
- Gadzooks absorbs much of the damage aimed at Constance and grows stronger.
- Perseus is freed after [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) interferes with the collar magic.
- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) uses his blades in earnest for the first time.
