# Side Session: War of the Gods, Session 16 (November 11, 2023)

This side session is the first extracted transcript page from the [War of the Gods](../concepts/war-of-the-gods.md) portion of the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md). Correlating the surviving dated notes places the actual Session 16 play on November 11, 2023, though the transcript carries a little setup overlap from [War of the Gods, Session 15](war-of-the-gods-session-15.md).

## Overview

The main dated play for Session 16 begins after the island-temple refuge established in [War of the Gods, Session 15](war-of-the-gods-session-15.md). The party pushes into [Thornwall](../places/thornwall.md), turns a stealth entry into a tavern disaster, fights through fog-blighted outskirts, and starts realizing that the town's church and magical corruption are part of a much larger undead-engine problem.

## Key Events

- The party enters [Thornwall](../places/thornwall.md) through the hidden hedge access established the prior session.
- Inside [Thornwall](../places/thornwall.md), the first target is a tavern occupied by hostile dork soldiers loyal to [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s cause.
- The assault escalates from stealth to grenades, dragon charges, fire, and close-quarters fighting until the burning building partially collapses on the party.
- The party fights through a fog-covered area haunted by spiders, corrupted ents, and blighted growth.
- The ruined church becomes the next major objective, and its desecration is recognized as tied to the surrounding magical corruption.
- The group clashes with one of the undead lieutenants in the town center, including charm pressure and fountain-spawned minions.
- After the town-center battle, the party pulls back toward the ruined temple refuge instead of pressing deeper immediately.
