# Side Session: War of the Gods, Session 20

This side session continues directly from [War of the Gods, Session 19](war-of-the-gods-session-19.md) in the [War of the Gods](../concepts/war-of-the-gods.md) arc of the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md).

## Overview

Session 20 opens with the consequences of the previous ship disaster split across multiple timelines of experience. While the main group regroups at the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), the two absent party members receive the missing fallout directly: Constance and Zuk break from the ship entirely and redefine their bond, while [Mullin](../people/mullin.md) nearly dies saving the vessel and severs himself from divine dependence.

After that character-heavy opening, the session pivots hard into war logistics. [Veil](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) finally receives [Junara](../people/junara.md) and [Greg](../people/greg.md) in the hidden center of the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), the party is given portal devices to deploy reinforcements, and the defense of Constance's homeland begins in earnest.

By the end of the session, however, the tactical victory is overshadowed by a quieter disaster: 69 privately consumes the final dragon core [Belle](../people/belle.md) wanted, disappears from the battlefield, and effectively becomes part of her long-running reproductive and divine power scheme.

## Key Events

- The session begins by filling in what happened to the two absent players during the ship disaster rather than rewinding the whole group.
- After Rose's fall, Zuk refuses any more outside meddling, drags Constance off the ship, and flies with her in full adult form rather than his usual limited state.
- During that flight, Constance experiences a major physical and spiritual shift as Zuk pulls future draconic power backward through their bond to keep from losing her again.
- In the mountains, Zuk reveals that in another timeline Constance was his future mate, that he acted rashly to preserve that possibility, and that the two now effectively share one soul across two bodies.
- Their bond is no longer merely dragon-partner affinity. It becomes a soul-sharing, power-sharing draconic link with new combat and survival implications for both of them.
- [Mullin](../people/mullin.md), meanwhile, is caught on the damaged ship when the front section explodes. The Seeker and [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) force an improvised emergency solution by making him the conduit for an unstable portal system and the overloaded magical output.
- Rather than channel that power into [Straka](../people/straka.md) or any other higher power, [Mullin](../people/mullin.md) chooses to keep it for himself and nearly dies doing so.
- He survives only barely, loses a massive amount of vitality in the process, and then fully rejects the divine connection still hanging on him by throwing [Straka](../people/straka.md)'s symbol from the ship.
- [Charlie](../people/charlie.md) helps stabilize him afterward, gives him a heavy weapon of her own design, and frames the whole event as the start of a more self-directed life rather than another bargain-driven one.
- Back at the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md), [Junara](../people/junara.md) and [Greg](../people/greg.md) use [Mave](../people/mave.md)'s directions to reach [Veil, Guardian of the Citadel](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) in the hidden control center inside the crystal itself.
- [Veil](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) demonstrates just how total his [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) monitoring is, reviews the party's movements, gives them future access, and openly positions himself as a non-divine but deeply informed player willing to work with them.
- [Jinx](../people/jinx.md) is present in that same hidden chamber, exhausted from long-running work, while [Veil](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) confirms broader concerns about [Alen](../people/alen.md), the crystals, and the limits of trusting even gods the party currently prefers.
- [Greg](../people/greg.md) also receives his old chassis back through a bizarre automated delivery, restoring a piece of his earlier work and history.
- The tiny surviving dungeon-core remnant from session 19 is destroyed when exposed on the school grounds, where overlapping divine sanctity snuffs it out completely before it can continue acting as a compass.
- [Junara](../people/junara.md) retrieves upgraded blades from the smiths, including a stronger version of his preferred blade and a new replacement offhand weapon.
- The school briefs the party on the battle ahead and gives them five portal anchors to deploy so orc and GIF reinforcements can be brought into the defense.
- The party arrives near Constance's homeland in the middle of a massive battle between undead forces and dragons, with defenders already stretched thin.
- They deploy the first anchors successfully, open the gates, and quickly prove how much stronger the post-session-19 group has become by tearing through an undead dragon and its support almost immediately.
- Constance reunites briefly with Zuk's mother and with her brother [Lucian Coggeshall](../people/lucian-coggeshall.md), whose personality remains exactly as abrasive as expected even under siege.
- While the others focus on the battle and the reinforcement gates, 69 privately receives the final dragon core from the fallen undead dragon and consumes it.
- That act pulls him completely into [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s private space, where [Belle](../people/belle.md), [Alen](../people/alen.md), [Mave](../people/mave.md), Diona, and [Mave](../people/mave.md)'s mother all appear as overlapping divine stakeholders in what is about to happen.
- [Belle](../people/belle.md) reveals that 69's entire growth path has been part of her plan, absorbs him into herself, reshapes mummy as well, and proceeds toward childbirth with [Alen](../people/alen.md)'s bargain fulfilled.
- The session ends with [Belle](../people/belle.md) giving birth to twins, effectively removing 69 from the battlefield and transforming him from party member into part of [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s divine family project.

## Consequences

Session 20 is where the cast stops merely talking about change and actually becomes different people. Constance and Zuk are no longer just bonded; they are metaphysically entangled. [Mullin](../people/mullin.md) is no longer meaningfully a cleric in the old sense at all. [Greg](../people/greg.md) and [Junara](../people/junara.md) now have direct access to [Veil](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md). The [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) itself becomes more than a backdrop and starts acting like an organized but hidden intelligence center.

It is also the session where [Belle](../people/belle.md)'s long game stops being deniable. Whatever softer explanation she gave in session 19, session 20 confirms that 69 was being cultivated toward a very specific reproductive and divine end state. From the party's perspective, that is not just manipulation. It is the effective loss of a companion to a plan they only partly understood until it was already complete.
