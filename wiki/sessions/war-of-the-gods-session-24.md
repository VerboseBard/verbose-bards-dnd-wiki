# Side Session: War of the Gods, Session 24

This side session continues directly from [War of the Gods, Session 23](war-of-the-gods-session-23.md) in the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md).

## Overview

Session 24 is the payoff for the opening [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) siege. What began in Session 23 as betrayal, portals, and divine collapse turns here into a direct battle on the outside of the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md) against [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) himself.

It is also the session where the party learns that saving the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) is not only about winning a fight. The crystal, the seeded [dungeons](../concepts/dungeons.md), the [ley lines](../concepts/ley-lines.md), and the rising [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md) are all parts of the same reconstruction problem. By the end of the session, [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) is down, the open infernal breach is shut, and the survivors are forced to confront what kind of world is now forming.

## Key Events

- The session opens with a recap of the disaster created at the end of Session 23: [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) used [Mullin](../people/mullin.md)'s dropped blessed hammer as the key he needed to bypass school protections and enter the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) directly.
- [Alen](../people/alen.md) had already manifested to challenge him, and [Constance and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) had already destroyed [Wendell](../people/wendell.md)'s undead wyvern before racing back toward the main crisis.
- [Mullin](../people/mullin.md)'s death is confirmed not to be the end of the Commodore legend. An attempted soul-consumption meant to feed him to [Alen](../people/alen.md) fails, which means the prophecy-fed reincarnation logic behind him is still active.
- With the park portals closed, the party uses [Greg](../people/greg.md)'s contact channel to reach [Veil, Guardian of the Citadel](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) inside the crystal's command space.
- [Veil](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) shows them the wider war through the crystal screens and explains the immediate problem: if he leaves the controls, the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) falls, but if no one stops [Lucian](../people/lucian.md), the crystal will be drained and the worlds will not be able to reform safely.
- The party is teleported onto the outside of the [Radiant Citadel Crystal](../places/radiant-citadel-crystal.md), where gravity is altered and the battlefield wraps around the crystal's surface.
- [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) opens with fear effects and layered wall-of-force style control, trying to seal off the god-killing dagger and stall the party long enough to keep draining the crystal.
- The fight becomes a tactical puzzle rather than a simple damage race. The party counters his barriers, pressures his concentration, and forces him to keep choosing between defending himself and maintaining control of the crystal.
- [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md) is the one who physically grabs the god-killing dagger, suffering catastrophic backlash in levels and life force just for touching it.
- [Constance and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md) add heavy ranged and draconic pressure, while the rest of the party chips away at [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s control of the field.
- When the fight deadlocks, [Greg](../people/greg.md) spends one of his precious library favors to force the dagger forward through [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md), plunging it into [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) and breaking the stalemate.
- Mortally struck, [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) drops the hourglass tied to the blade. Its shattering releases an enormous mixed surge of infernal, divine, and death-domain power across the crystal.
- The released power kills or drops multiple combatants, disintegrates [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md), and would have cascaded into a larger catastrophe if [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) had not been one of the only people present capable of handling that kind of overflow.
- With Zuk helping route the energy, [Gregario / Shaq](../people/gregario-shaq.md) channels most of the released power back into the crystal's systems instead of letting it tear the defenders apart.
- The result is decisive: the crystal wound seals, the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md)'s shields re-form, open infernal portals across the city snap shut, and the crystal's weapons begin firing on the invading fleet.
- The party kills the remaining form of [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) present on the crystal. This is a decisive victory for the siege, though later records make clear it was not yet the absolute end of every one of his long-laid contingencies.
- In the immediate aftermath, [Veil](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) receives the party back into the crystal and makes clear that [Wendell](../people/wendell.md) is now a prisoner rather than an untouchable strategist.
- [Junara](../people/junara.md) uses a scroll and stored diamonds to perform a full restoration on [Jimbo](../people/jimbo.md), bringing him back after the crystal battle.
- The surviving defenders also learn that the seeded [dungeons](../concepts/dungeons.md) across the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) cannot simply be destroyed. They are now part of the infrastructure needed to pull the worlds back together and must instead be controlled and pruned.
- On the next morning, [Lady Penny Hathaway](../people/lady-penny-hathaway.md) briefs the party on the larger implications: the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md) is actively reforming, the gods are already being pushed farther away, school protections are diminishing, the routing of souls is now uncertain after the deaths of [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) and [Alen](../people/alen.md), and magic itself may soon require more cost and structure than it did before.

## Consequences

Session 24 is the moment where the [Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) stops being only a crossroads and is openly revealed as a reconstruction anchor for reality itself. The defenders do not just save a city. They preserve the central node needed for the worlds to converge without collapsing into [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s version of the afterlife economy.

It is also the session where several assumptions break at once. [Lucian](../people/lucian.md) can be killed in a battlefield sense without all of his deeper plans being gone. The seeded [dungeons](../concepts/dungeons.md) are dangerous, but they are also necessary infrastructure. The gods are not absent because they do not care, but because the [Divine Gate](../concepts/divine-gate.md) is actively pushing them outward. Even souls are no longer guaranteed a clean shepherding path after the death of [Alen](../people/alen.md)'s manifested form and [Lucian](../people/lucian.md)'s fall at the crystal.

For the party, the session creates a new strategic reality: [Wendell](../people/wendell.md) is no longer the immediate battlefield focus, [Veil](../people/veil-guardian-of-the-citadel.md) and [Lady Penny Hathaway](../people/lady-penny-hathaway.md) now treat world-reconstruction as the central problem, and the surviving heroes are left carrying both victory and a setting-wide systems change.
