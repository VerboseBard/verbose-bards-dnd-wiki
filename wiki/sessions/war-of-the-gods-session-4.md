# War of the Gods, Session 4 (February 11, 2023)

This session continues the prewar development phase of the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md).

## Overview

What starts as another training-and-performance session widens into city exploration, unsettling signs of hidden violence, and the first real shift from school routine into mission deployment.

## Key Events

- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) continues skill training until his dexterity work finally pays off.
- [Maeve](../people/mave.md) personally helps him build stage presence.
- Public performance in the market earns [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) real recognition and money.
- The library begins opening up broader knowledge of the worlds.
- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) wanders far enough through the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) to glimpse how vast and culturally mixed it is.
- He gets lost, is locked out of the school, and spends the night in a park filled with pixies.
- Signs of a quiet killing in the park imply the group is being watched more closely than it realizes.
- By the end of the session, part of the group is assigned to a new off-site mission-training location on a desert world.
