# War of the Gods, Session 6 (March 4, 2023)

This session continues the desert-world arc of the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md).

## Overview

The tone turns darker. Blood-moon imagery, local quest infrastructure, and [Aylinn](../people/aylinn.md)'s influence all frame the next major expedition, as the group mounts up for the mine that will become one of the defining ritual sites of the campaign.

## Key Events

- A blood-moon dream involving Constance and a red figure hints at deeper supernatural pressure.
- [Aylinn](../people/aylinn.md) both rebukes and commends [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) over the stolen book situation.
- Harman Redcliff and his inn become part of the local mission network.
- The party is told there will be no more blood moons for three months.
- [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md) receives a holster for his wand of sparkles.
- Camels are acquired and the group heads toward the mine.
- Surface orcs provide the first combat pressure before the descent.

## Important People

- [Constant and Zuk](../people/constant-and-zuk.md)
- [Junara](../people/junara.md)

## Consequences

This session is the threshold before the mine arc. It sets the mood, the route, and the local political-religious pressure that the next preserved date finally detonates.
