# War of the Gods, Session 7 (June 17, 2023)

This session is one of the first major mythic turns in the [War of the Gods / New Beginning Campaign](../campaign/war-of-the-gods.md).

## Overview

The mine descent reveals that the party is standing in the middle of something far larger than a local dispute. [Wendell](../people/wendell.md), dark-elf ritual work, green crystals, suppressed divine awareness, and [Hawk](../people/hawk.md)'s intervention all collide in one of the campaign's most important early ritual scenes.

## Key Events

- After the surface fighting, the party pushes deeper into the mine.
- [Wendell](../people/wendell.md) appears directly and leads the group farther down.
- Orc thralls and aberrant defenders reveal that the mine is already part battlefield and part ritual structure.
- The lower chamber contains green crystals, a portal, and a dark-elf-led rite.
- A winged guardian is bypassed only narrowly.
- The ritual itself centers on a power focus that draws [June](../people/june.md) toward it.
- [Hawk](../people/hawk.md) arrives with a dark leopard, raises a staff, and speaks a word of power.
- [Aylinn](../people/aylinn.md)'s hold over the area is broken or diminished, and awareness of other deities returns.
- Seeker Lucy arrives with 69.
- The group returns to the [Radiant Citadel](../places/radiant-citadel.md) in a way that should not have been possible through ordinary crystal transit.
- The chant from the mine becomes direct musical inspiration for [Kestrel](../people/kestrel.md).

## Consequences

Session 7 is an early cornerstone for the campaign's later metaphysics. It ties ritual music, ley-like severing, older divine structures, and [Hawk](../people/hawk.md)'s Fifth-Age-linked history into the same scene.
